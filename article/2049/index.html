Kava Kava

This is an archive of the band profile for Kava Kava.

Bookmark and Share

DIY funked up live rock/dance act from Huddersfield featuring the soulful vocals of singer Pat Fulgoni alongside guitars, electronica, brass, strings and beats. They have played clubs and festivals all over EUROPE, in the STATES and in CHINA and their music has been featured in popular TV series such as DIRT, JOHN FROM CINCINNATI, WEEDS etc, films including BOYS & GIRLS GUIDE TO GETTING DOWN, new snowboarding game STOKED, TAP TAP REVENGE 3 for iphone. Various excellent releases on the Chocolate Fireguard label.

BILLBOARD MAGAZINE (US) Despite a hard rock appearance, Kava Kava creates party-worthy music by combining rock, funk, dance and soul.......Kava Kava has already been heard on Showtime series "Weeds" and has been spun by Nic Harcourt on noncommercial KCRW Los Angeles
