Music company samples success

This article was published in 2002.

Leeds-based music production company Joe Williams Music has linked up with Seattle’s LoopLibrary.com to provide content for the website, which offers copyright-free loops and samples for musicians and producers.

Founder Joe Williams said: "More people than ever before are writing and producing their own music, and the majority of them use loops as source material. Online sample libraries are a great way of feeding their creativity, and LoopLibrary.com has something for everyone. We’re delighted to be getting involved with such an excellent resource."

Joe Williams Music specialise in creating custom music for media and commercial projects.
