MonMon unleash Pop Disaster Noise

This article was published in 2013.

Leeds noise trio MonMon have released their latest EP entitled Pop Disaster Noise.

The 3 track EP delivers more of the melodic, dirty rock which has been impressing people at their energetic live gigs as well as bringing airplay from Tom Robinson on BBC 6Music.

The EP is available to download via www.monmon.bandcamp.com as well as on a limited CD version of the 3 track record.
