Downdime release their debut single on Monday...

This article was published in 2005.

On Monday, 25th July Downdime will release their debut single - “Seeds of Hopelessness” - on 7” single through Leeds-based Squirrel Records (SQRL12). The single, described as “melodic noise pop”, is backed with the songs “Existence Is Missing” and “Shine”.

For more information, and to buy a copy online, visit www.squirrelrecords.co.uk.
