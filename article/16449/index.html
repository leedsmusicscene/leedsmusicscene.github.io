Journey's End

This is an archive of the band profile for Journey's End.

Harry Nott - Vocals
Charles Beech - Lead Guitar
Joe Blythe - Bass
Ethan Wainright - Drums

Forming in mid-2011, Journey's End are leading the Wakefield & West Yorkshire Hardcore scene with their unique sound. Taking inspiration from bands such as Brutality Will Prevail, Desolated, Malevolence & Trapped Under Ice - Journey's End produce a chaotic and harsh mixture of heavy & beatdown Hardcore.
Throwing in powerful lyrics with fresh riffs and fantastic drumming - Live performances are packed full of energy and destructive strength. Journey's End are defining West Yorkshire Hardcore as something new and more powerful than ever before. This is our sound..
