Oxjam 2009 looking for a Leeds event manager

This article was published in 2008.

In April, Leeds bands, promoters and venues took part in Oxjam, the month-long festival made up of lots of different music events put on by communities across the UK.

Oxjam are now recruiting a Regional Manager to support the volunteers putting on events in Leeds during March 2009 and this will be a great opportunity to get people involved in the local music scene.

If you are interested in the Leeds Regional Manager role, visit Oxjam or call 01865 472369 for more information.
