The Great Rock N Roll Bash 2007 to take place at Carpe Diem this weekend

This article was published in 2007.

The second annual Rob Galloway's Great Rock N Roll Bash 2007 in aid of St. Gemmas Hospice is to be held at Carpe Diem in Leeds on Sunday, 6th May. Raffle prizes include a Sony Vaio laptop donated by IT Solutions Ltd, and Â£300 worth of studio time donated by Ivolv Media.

The Post Office will also be matching whatever funds are made on the day.

A great line up of local bands will be playing all day from 2.45pm with the bar open at 12.30pm for anyone that wishes to help out with setting up.

Bands scheduled to play include Dan Audio, 4 Letter Holiday, Micky P Kerr, MonMon, Hoover Dams, The Symptom, Hurricane Sunday, Fat Sammy, Rob Galloway, Pushbike Army, The Frenzies, The Moff and the Great Rock N Roll Bash Super Group.

This will be a great day for a great cause and we hope to see as much support as possible from local bands and music lovers alike!
