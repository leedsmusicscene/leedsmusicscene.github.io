The New Unsociables

This is an archive of the band profile for The New Unsociables.

Accomplished songwriters and musicians Nick Waters and Mick Ward served many enjoyable years in 'The Lost 45's'. Andy Walton Played with 'The Ace' and Jonathan Marsden 'The Flying Squad'. Rupert, we don't know much about, what we do know is he can play the bass to virtuoso standard which is nice.

All three bands were often found on the same listings due to a common ground of a raw vintage inspired live sound.

With a shared passion for 60s Soul, Rhythm & blues and Funk, this collective of musicians are taking their shared passion for playing to a new level playing "Soulfull Sonic Grooves with Funky Rhythm & Blues"!.
