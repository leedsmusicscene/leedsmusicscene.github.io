Orchid

This is an archive of the band profile for Orchid.

Ryan - Vocals
Mark - Guitar
Simon - Bass
Kevin - Drums

We are currently a four-piece, although we are still looking to add to our sound. Our music is melodic, yet with an old school rock edge. Our main influences are AC/DC, Metallica and Guns n Roses. Our music is diverse spanning from full out rock tracks to soulful and melodic pieces. Playing live we just want to have fun, play loud and make you want to rock the night through..
