A Day For Heroes

This is an archive of the band profile for A Day For Heroes.

A Day For Heroes have been together since April 2009 and are quickly making their stamp on the music scene. The band have played to great reviews and are now moving forward to higher places after a successful run of gigs across the north of England; recieving a fantastic reaction where ever they go.

The band's sound incorporates a mix of punk and metalcore with soaring guitar solos and melodic harmonies, relentless drumming and experimental basslines, with powerful lyrics that everyone can relate to .
