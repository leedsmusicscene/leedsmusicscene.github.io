Seas Of Furious Lizards

This is an archive of the band profile for Seas Of Furious Lizards.

Formed in early 2011 when a group of guys from Leeds decided to form a band. After much deliberation, they finally settled on the name "Seas of Furious Lizards" when synth legend Richard Barbieri (Porcupine Tree, Japan) expressed that he thought it was a 'good name'. As well as settling on long band names, the Lizards also enjoy writing and playing music. You can hear this music on recordings, or at gigs (the latter is generally encouraged)..
