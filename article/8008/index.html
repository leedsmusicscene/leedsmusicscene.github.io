The Acutes to release their second single on the Bad Sneakers Records label in March

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds-based record label Bad Sneakers Records have this week revealed that the video for the second single by Leeds duo The Acutes is available to view online now.

The rather excellent single "Set On You" can be purchased from Monday, 26th March when it will be released on 7" vinyl, CD and Digital Download formats. The release marks the fifth single from the label, also home to The Sugars and Wild Beasts.

You can watch The Acutes live in Leeds on 24th February (Old Bar), 29th March (Brudenell Social Club) and 31st March (Mixing Tin).
