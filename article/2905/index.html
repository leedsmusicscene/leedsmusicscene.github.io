The Faversham to host charity festival in August...

This article was published in 2004.

Get ready for ethical hedonism. Combining one of the best parties of the summer and raising cash for charity, "Our Festival" presents 14 hours of sensory joy with the best sounds in Leeds.

Seek out ultimate pleasures this August Bank Holiday Sunday with some of Leeds’ most cheerful live acts, talented DJ’s and special guests. These most charitable of individuals are giving up their time for free to help "Our Festival" raise money for Cancer Research.

Share with mates new and old, three rooms of music and an outdoor stage for live acts from 2pm to 4am at The Legendary Faversham. From laid back beats in the early afternoon to a frenzied finale at 4am.

Line up includes: Yellow Stripe Nine, Sepia Tone, Charly Six, The Bilderberg Group and D'Juba.

For more details or to view the line up please visit www.our-festival.com.
