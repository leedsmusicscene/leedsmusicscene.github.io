Leeds-based singer-songwriter Daniel Pearson to release debut album 'Satellites'

This article was published in 2009.

Saint In The City Records will release Daniel Pearson's debut album 'Satellites' on October 5th. The album will be available via iTunes stores worldwide.

Occupying the middle ground between Ryan Adams and Elliott Smith, 'Satellites' contains 10 timeless songs which fuse infectious pop hooks with alt.country and soulful rock n' roll.

Recorded at Leeds studio The Soundmill over an energetic 6 day spell this summer with fast-rising producer Ed Heaton at the helm, the record features a talented backing band alongside Pearson's distinctive voice and perceptive lyrics. Songs from 'Satellites' can now be heard online.
