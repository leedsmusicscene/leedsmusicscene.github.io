The Cubes - Digital Girl

This is a review of "Digital Girl" recorded by The Cubes. The review was written by Andy James in 2005.

A two-track offering from a none-more-indie band from Wetherby. "Digital Girl" isn't a bad song but it does sound a bit like it should be soundtracking a night in the Camden Good Mixer circa 1995. B-side "The Sky Just Is" meanwhile is Coldplay if Chris Martin didn't believe that he could only communicate in whalespeak and is actually the better of the two songs. For those who remember "Park Life" era Blur fondly, worth a listen, for the rest of us... hmmm...
