The Vaccines to appear as part of Live At Leeds 2013

This article was published in 2012.

Organisers of the Live At Leeds Festival have today announced that The Vaccines will be performing at Leeds Millennium Square as part of Live At Leeds Festival on Sunday 5th May 2013.

Tickets to see The Vaccines cost £25 and go on sale on Friday 26th October at 9am.

This is a separate event to the Saturday wristband event which is currently on sale for the early bird price of £15.

All tickets can be purchased via Leeds Gig Tickets.
