Heats announced for Futuresound 2013

This article was published in 2013.

Organisers of the annual Futuresound competition have this week confirmed which 60 bands will battle it out for one of six slots at the Reading and Leeds Festival 2013.

The 60 bands were whittled down from hundreds of hopeful entrants, and will now play in one of 15 dates during June and July.
