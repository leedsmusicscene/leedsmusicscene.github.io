Instant Species have their pies at the ready

This article was published in 2002.

Instant Species will release their 4th album "Meat Pie Arguement" in November (date to be confirmed). The boys have been working once again with Carl Rosamond at The Billiard Room in Leeds and the album has been completed in just over a month. There will be 10 new songs on the album and the band are set to follow it up with an EP in the new year that will contain one of the tracks and even more new material. Rick said "This is our best work to date and will amaze those who hear it as to the sheer quality of the songwriting".
