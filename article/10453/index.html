Leeds Festival 2009 tickets to go on sale in March

This article was published in 2009.

Organisers have today announced that tickets for Leeds Festival 2009 will go on sale on Monday, March 30th at 7pm. The event headliners will be announced on the same evening, so watch this space!

Although ticket prices have yet to be announced, as a comparison (and to ensure you save enough money) the 2008 festival was priced at £155 (weekend) and £65 (day).

Earlier this year Festival Republic reminded music fans to buy their tickets from official ticket agencies only and to avoid the touts and secondary market sellers.

For a list of approved ticket outlets visit our Leeds Music Festival site.

You can also buy your tickets directly from See Tickets and Ticketmaster.
