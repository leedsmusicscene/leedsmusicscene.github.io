Brett Lee's debut EP 'For The Taking' is out now

This article was published in 2013.

After the successful release of his début solo single 'Mistakes' in June, airplay on BBC Introducing, and the release of his new music video, it's onwards and upwards for this new solo artist. After the break up of his band Northern Torch, Brett Lee has bounced back with his own style of music and after a successful period of writing, he's back with his début E.P 'For The Taking', which was recorded at DPS Studios and produced by Elmaroe Music Production.

'For The Taking' is available for download now from bandcamp.com.

The EP will be available on iTunes and Spotify from 1st September.
