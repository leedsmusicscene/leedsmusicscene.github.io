Worriedaboutsatan release a remix album of 'Arrivals'

This article was published in 2009.

Following on from the critically acclaimed debut album ’Arrivals’, released in May, Leeds based electronica duo Worriedaboutsatan returned this week with a 2 CD collection of remixes gathered from fans and friends across the world.

The album was released on Monday 7th December.

What started as a remix competition, which saw the band post remix files on their website, soon blossomed into a full double album’s worth of material, as demand was too great and genres too diverse to pick an eventual winner.

The 25 tracks feature remixes from a diverse set of musicians, bands, producers and collectives, spanning genres as diverse as dubstep, house, ambient, folk and a lot more.

The collection will be mainly released digitally on all major platforms, including iTunes, Beatport and Spotify, and a limited amount of physical copies available from the band themselves via their official website.
