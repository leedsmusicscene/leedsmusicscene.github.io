The Pigeon Detectives to re-release "I Found Out" on November 12th

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band The Pigeon Detectives are set to re-release 2006 single ‘I Found Out’ on Monday, 12th November. Taken from their brilliant album ‘Wait For Me’ the release coincides with the band's biggest UK tour so far.

The Pigeon Detectives are the success story of the year, and ‘I Found Out’ is a raw, spiky guitar anthem. Packed to the brim with great hooks and the un-tempered energy of teenage love, lust and romance it’s a song that everyone can relate to. With a chorus so infectious it’s hollered back at the band whenever the band play live.

The song has been re-recorded by producer Stephen Street (The Smiths and Blur) and is set to be a single of year contender.
