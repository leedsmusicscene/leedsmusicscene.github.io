This Days Fury

This is an archive of the band profile for This Days Fury.

Liam Brett (Lead Vocals)
Matt Stringer (Lead Guitar)
Simon Lee (Lead Guitar)
Joe Prendergast (Bass)
Sam Prendergast (Drums)

This Day's Fury started life in August 2004. A new drummer, many gigs and a debut E.P. later, the band have gone from strength to strength.

Taking influence from the likes of Iron Maiden, Atreyu, In Flames, 18 Visions, Avenged7Fold, TDF combine the harmony-guitar driven sound of metal with fast paced catchy sound of punk to forge their own unique and refreshing style.

The band won their first major support slot in the summer of 2006, supporting American punk/rock festival veterans Boysetsfire in Leeds and have been playing gigs with a whole host of local signed bands since gigging began all those months ago.

Continually writing new songs and developing the stage show, the band have many gigs lined up in the coming months and with further releases planned and a large fan base building the future is looking bright for This Day's Fury..
