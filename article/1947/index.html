Porcelin

This is an archive of the band profile for Porcelin.

Vocals: Daniel Egan
Lead Guitar: Adrian Brearey
Bass Guitar: James Dorree
Percussion: Shane Bullers

Porcelin are a local band who have been working together for over 5 years, played various gigs around Yorkshire and England.

Shane and Adie were members of "Playground" who had a managment deal with HTT records. Playground played at many Leeds venues which included The Duchess (god rest her sole), The Cockpit and Joseph's Well. Non-local venues included London Underworld and Dingwalls, London. The band are currently writing new material after a 1 year break following their success at Morley Battle of the Bands, in which Porcelin came second and James Dorree received the award for best bass guitarist..
