Ols Moore And The Gypsy Dogs

This is an archive of the band profile for Ols Moore And The Gypsy Dogs.

Ols Moore And The Gypsy Dogs is a four piece acoustic gypsy folk band from Leeds, comprising of guitars, vocals, violin, cello, accordion and drums. Balkan gypsy styles are fused with more traditional western blues and folk influences creating a varied and dynamic set.

Since August 2009, the band have played various venues in Leeds, Sheffield, & Leicester, attracting crowds of up to 300 people.

Recording of the Tasteless China EP began in February 2010. The EP is in production and will soon be released. A short launch tour of the much awaited Tasteless China EP is planned for the start of the summer. The band is aiming to return to the studio to record a full length album by the end of 2010.

Ols Moore And The Gypsy Dogs won the 2010 "2Weeks 2Make It" music video competition in April this year. The band hope to use this opportunity to reach a wider audience throughout the UK and beyond..
