Station Lane

This is an archive of the band profile for Station Lane.

Jordan Allsopp - Lead Vocals
Brad Jennings - Lead Guitar
Callum Wild - Rhythm Guitar/Backing Vocals
Kane Overton - Bass
Ben Scargill - Drums

A West Yorkshire Indie Rock Band. We are from Birkenshaw and the surrounding areas of Gomersal and Cleckheaton. The majority of us started playing instruments roughly since Christmas 2010 and we have been together since February 2011 so we are still learning our trades. We aim to get as big as we can in the future and hope you like what you hear...
