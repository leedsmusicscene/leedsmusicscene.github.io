les Flames! announce double 7" single release and UK tour...

This article was published in 2003.

les Flames! release a new 7" single through Wrath Records on Monday, 19th May. The single features the track Mutley, 1, From Essex and is b/w Professional Stalker.

The band are also featured in the first batch of releases on Wrath Records' Super Sevens singles club. Their contribution to the split single is a new track called Commitment To Excellence, with the other side featuring Little Japanese Toy live favourite Finger Mouse.

With these two singles available in May, les Flames! head out on a mini UK tour to promote them. The tour includes a Leeds date on 25th May - supporting US punk band The Briefs at the Cockpit - and a Drowned in Sound gig in London on 27th May. More gigs to be announced.

On Friday 13th June, les Flames! play a charity gig (in aid of Red Cross) at the Brudenell Social Club along with Parva and The Casual Terraces.
