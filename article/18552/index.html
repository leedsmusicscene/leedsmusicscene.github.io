Dancing Years announce new single

This article was published in 2014.

Leeds five-piece Dancing Years have announced the release of a new single 'Places We've Roamed'. Produced by James Kenosha (Rhodes, Dry the River, Pulled Apart by Horses), the double A-side single will be available from Monday 7th July through Leeds based independent label Hide & Seek.

Further highlighting the band's ability for beautifully crafted compositions, 'Places We've Roamed' is a delicate and restrained piano ballad while AA side track 'Borderline' demonstrates the bands ability to move seamlessly between moments of intimacy and surging crescendos.

The official single release show takes place at The Wardrobe tonight (June 25th), with support from Eaves and Jasmine Kennedy.
