Once Upon A Time Lived A Man Named Dave

This is an archive of the band profile for Once Upon A Time Lived A Man Named Dave.

"once upon a time lived a man named dave" is a 2-piece leeds-based band in Leeds playing original material incorporating styles such as dnb,house,jazz,funk,rock and breakbeat. The band was originally a studio project but a return to the live stage this year has seen a string of successful live shows as well as a publishing deal with raw42 for some instrumental film/TV music and a TV performance for "unsigned UK". The band are currently working on a debut album which will be released in August this year and promoted by a UK tour..
