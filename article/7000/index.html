¡Forward, Russia! release "Eighteen" on 24th July

This article was published in 2006.

¡Forward, Russia! release a new single on Monday 24th July. Taken from their début album, “Eighteen" will be available on three physical formats, each with different B-sides, as well as on digital download format.

The CD version features the lead track and comes backed with two songs, “Six" and the Napoleon IIIrd remix of “Eighteen". The first of two 7" vinyl versions will be “Eighteen" b/w “Fourteen", whilst the second 7" version will be a coloured vinyl remix edition featuring the Yes Boss remix on the A-side and the Rollercoaster Project remix on the flipside.

An exclusive :( -aka colonopenbracket- remix of “Eighteen" will be available through most of the usual download sites.

You can order all vinyl and CD formats from HMV (free delivery) or you can visit the iTunes store for downloads.

Having completed T In The Park / Oxegen festivals back-to-back this weekend, ¡Forward, Russia! continue their summer tour (including Leeds residency dates) in the run up to their Leeds / Reading appearances in August.

The band have also been named on the inaugural MTV2 Spanking New Music Tour in November. Along with the Leeds-based four-piece the tour also features Wolfmother, The Maccabees and Fields and arrives in Leeds on Wednesday 8th November with a show at the University.
