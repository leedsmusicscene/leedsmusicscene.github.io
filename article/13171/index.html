The Dark LP

This is an archive of the band profile for The Dark LP.

Rob Long - vocals and guitar
Rich Parker - drums

Originally most of the songs were wrote by Rob on his acoustic guitar, and since The Dark LP formed in November 2009, the band have definitely discovered their own unique style and sound.

We hope to see you at one of our next gigs, and are always happy for any help anyone can offer..
