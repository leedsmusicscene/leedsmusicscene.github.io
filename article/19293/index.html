Nathaniel Rateliff and The Night Sweats Announce 2016 UK Tour

This article was published in 2015.

Currently taking the UK by storm and following an incendiary performance on Later... With Jools Holland, Nathaniel Rateliff and The Night Sweats have announced a nine date UK tour taking in Leeds Irish Centre on the 12th Feb and London's Shepherd's Bush Empire on 17th Feb.

The Night Sweats have had an incredible Summer, which saw the self-titled debut album soar up the US iTunes chart. In the following weeks, current single 'S.O.B' hit the top of the HypeMachine chart and Spotify's Global, the official video now has over 2 million views.

With rave reviews coming in across the board for the both the live shows and album, this tour is set to firmly establish The Night Sweats as one of the most exciting and unmissable live acts around.

Tickets are on sale now, for Nathaniel Rateliff & The Night Sweats at Leeds Irish Centre, on 12th Feb 2016.
