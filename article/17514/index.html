RoundWindow release Sophmore EP "End of an Error"

This article was published in 2013.

On 13th April Round Window will release their 2nd EP "End of an Error." The EP, another truly independent release, will be available to buy online from Round Window's BandCamp page and in a physical format from shows.

This new EP is a 4 chapter tale of mistakes and regrets, which musically sets out the band's stall for the future. The EP also features a special bonus track when purchased via download or on CD as well as exclusive original artwork.

"End of an Error" is now streaming on the band's website www.roundwindow.info.

The new EP is the long awaited follow up to the band's debut release "Pedestals" which is also available from their Bandcamp page where you can "name your price."

For downloads head to http://roundwindow.bandcamp.com
