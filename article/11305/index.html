Road To Horizon

This is an archive of the band profile for Road To Horizon.

Road To Horizon are a four piece pop punk / alternative rock band form Wakefield made up of Danny Kingsbury (Vocals/Guitar) Darryll Moxon (Bass/Vocals) Mark Thirkill (Guitar) Simon Gordon (Drums).

Road To Horizon formed in early 2009 where they hit the practice room long and hard to write the music that they know they're capable of, taking influences from such bands as Rise Against, Finch, Paramore, Funeral For A Friend and many others.

Ever since they've been playing as many gigs to as many people as possible and making people stand up and take note.

Road To Horizon have just recorded their debut E.P titled Love, Loss and Friendship which is available now at every show and also on our online store www.roadtohorizon.bigcartel.com
