Robochrist - Untitled

This is a review of "Untitled" recorded by Robochrist. The review was written by Noah Brown in 2003.

That Chris from Catalyst is a multi-talented lad, and this is ace. Five tracks of... errr... "techno-metal" I suppose you'd call it. The antithesis of po-faced issue-laden Yank techy guitar acts, this stuff has been described as "Metallica meets Aphex Twin at a circus" and that's about right. Processed doom guitars, industrial beats and carnival organs, peppered with samples as ludicrous as Pato Banton, the Werthers Originals ad and that old "Charlie Says" kids' information film, this makes the Ramones look like Radiohead. Great fun. Contact chris@catalyst.com for a copy.
