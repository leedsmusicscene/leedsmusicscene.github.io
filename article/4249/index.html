BBC Radio Leeds looking for artists for Live Sessions...

This article was published in 2005.

BBC Radio Leeds has been running live acoustic sessions for several weeks, every Sunday morning from 10am til noon on the Diana Luke show. Artists so far featured include Waking The Witch, Jon Gomm, Benjamin Wetherill and Nikoli.

They are currently looking for more West Yorks-based bands and solo-artists for the show. Bands are invited to play 3 songs, scattered through the 2-hour show, and are also interviewed.

If you are interested in performing, please email bbcsessions@jongomm.com to have your name put forward. Please include band name, brief description and a website if possible.
