Line up announced for the Play Patterns festival in October

This article was published in 2010.

Play Patterns is a small intimate Leeds-based festival devised by friends, which will run over three days on two stages at The Well.

The event will see over 40 bands appearing at the venue in October, with many local bands taking to the stage.

Weekend tickets (£15) and single day tickets (£6 / £8) are available online from See Tickets, as well as Jumbo and Crash Records Stores in Leeds.
