Dance To The Radio to host opening night at Leeds Festival 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Festival organisers have today announced the return of Leeds independent record label Dance To The Radio as hosts for the opening night party on the BBC Introducing Stage at Leeds 2011.

The label will once again host their "Welcome To Leeds" festivities on the Thursday evening. Festival fans with weekend camping tickets will be able to watch live sets from The Young Knives, Dog Is Dead, We Are Losers, Runaround Kids and Blacklisters to kick off the weekend in style.

Although tickets for Reading are now sold out, there are still tickets for Leeds Festival 2011 available through Ticketmaster, See Tickets and Viagogo.
