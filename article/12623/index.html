Main Stage headliners announced for Leeds Festival 2010

This article was published in 2010.

The official line up for Leeds 2010 was announced this evening with the three Main Stage headline slots filled by Arcade Fire (Friday), Blink 182 (Saturday) and Guns N Roses (Sunday).

Other bands announced for the festival include NME / Radio One Stage Headliners and Pendulum (Friday), Klaxons (Saturday) and LCD Soundsystem (Sunday).

The annual festival will take place on the August Bank Holiday weekend, with live music from Friday 27th August through to Sunday 29th August 2010.

If you are looking for tickets, we recommend buying online from Ticketmaster or from See Tickets.

You can also buy from Crash Records, Jumbo Records or from HMV Leeds Headrow. The HMV store will open from 7am tomorrow morning so get there early to join the queue.

A full set of official Leeds ticket links can be found on the Leeds 2010 mini-site.
