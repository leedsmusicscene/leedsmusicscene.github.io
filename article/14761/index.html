I Drew First Blood

This is an archive of the band profile for I Drew First Blood.

We're I Drew First Blood, a five piece Hardcore/Metal band from Huddersfield, West Yorkshire with influences from The Ghost Inside, The Acacia Strain, Hundredth, For The Fallen Dreams, Thick As Blood.


After forming in 2009 IDFB have always had a problem securing a 2nd guitarist but in 2010 they found someone who was just as committed. After re-writing sets and playing Locally around the Yorkshire area IDFB broke out of Yorkshire and started playing anywhere they can gaining a reputation for their energetic live shows with their mixture of melodic hardore and heavy beatdowns. So far 2011 has been a great year for IDFB and they plan to make it even better with an EP release in June/July and a tour to follow the release in August/September. Also in September IDFB are set to Support Desolated once again and in November are set to support Texas In July (USA - Equal Visions Records)..
