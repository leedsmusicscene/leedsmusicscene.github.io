The Bambinos and The Ran-Tan Waltz release a split EP

This article was published in 2011.

The Bambinos and The Ran-Tan Waltz release a split 7" / download EP through Philophobia Music on 21st February 2011.

The 4 track EP can currently be ordered from Philophobia Music and will also be available from Jumbo, Crash, Piccadilly, Rough Trade, Thee SPC and Norman Records as well as all major download sites.

Both bands will be performing acoustic sessions on BBC Introducing West Yorkshire in the coming weeks: The Bambinos on 17th February and The Ran-Tan Waltz on 27th March.

Both bands will be performing together at Inns of Court, Wakefield on 20th February to launch the EP.

In April they set off on a joint tour; check www.myspace.com/wearetherantanwaltz and www.myspace.com/vivabambinos for full lists of dates.
