Leeds bands to support Oxjam charity on October 17th

This article was published in 2010.

Leeds bands will come together this weekend to raise money for Oxfam charity, via the Oxjam music festival network.

Approximately 25 bands will take to the stage at The Well on Sunday 17th October.

There will be two stages of music throughout the day with one stage focussing on acoustic musicians and the second stage hosting the best in Leeds' rock, indie and pop bands.

On the Acoustic Stage will be: HANNAH TRIGWELL; RUPERT STROUD; JACK JAMES; CLAIRE CAMERON; HAYLEY GAFTARNICK; THE FINEST HOUR; JASMINE KENNEDY; and BEN PARCELL.

The Main Stage line up is: TAG-TEAM PREACHER; HAIL TO THE ESKIMO; SECRET SIRENS; MIMI AND THE LEADERS; ALBERT ROSS AND THE OTTERS; UNSUITABLE PETS; LEODIS; ZENO; SOUND OF SIRENS; MARCH OF DIMES; WE ARE LOS; THE SCANDALS; and APACHE.

The event will also include an auction and raffle.

Entry is open to 14+ (with adult) or 16+ (without). Doors open at 1pm and those arriving before 5pm can pay any donation. Between 5pm and 11pm the entry will be Â£3.

All monies raised will go straight to Oxfam.
