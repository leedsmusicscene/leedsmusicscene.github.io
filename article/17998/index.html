Debut single from Jupiter Falls is out now

This article was published in 2013.

Leeds band Jupiter Falls release their début single on UNIR1 Records just 2 weeks after signing with the US label. The single "World War Three" is available from all online retailers. The single is taken from début album "Revolution" due for release on 16th December.

Visit the band's website for details on how you can win yourself a copy of the album.

The "Revolution" UK & American tours begin in February 2014.
