Instant Species star in top shelf video...

This article was published in 2003.

Instant Species have released their first video in continuing support of their latest album Meat Pie Argument. The video is for the song Top Shelf and is available to watch online at the bands website.

Lead singer Rick commented - "The music industry becomes more of a DIY environment everyday and this is something we've wanted to do for a while to see what the reaction would be... I think it looks pretty cool!"

To watch the video go to www.instantspecies.co.uk and follow the links to TOP SHELF Video.
