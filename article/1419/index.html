Fulc album and single release news

This article was published in 2003.

Fulc return to the famous Sawmills Studios, Cornwall in January 2003 to record tracks for their debut album on Stunted Records, scheduled for release in September 2003. This will be preceeded by a single to be released in April 2003, again on Stunted Records, as a follow-up to their critically acclaimed The Fallen EP. Fulc will again be working with recording engineer Mark Thomas who has worked on all the Muse albums.

A full tour schedule is currently being discussed for April / May to tie-in with the single release, with a Leeds gig already booked for Tuesday, 18th February at Josephs Well.
