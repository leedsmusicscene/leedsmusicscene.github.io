4 Letter Holiday to release a new CD in February

This article was published in 2007.

4 Letter Holiday's new CD release at the end of February marks the pinnacle of the busiest period in the band's history. The demo recorded in Bradford in conjunction with IVOLV media displays a determined effort by the band to demonstrate diversity whilst staying as true as possible to their trademark live sound.

Alongside kicking off the 'Tea Time Shuffle' on Friday 2nd February at the HiFi Club and with notable appearances at the Snooty Fox (Wakefield) and the Mixing Tin, 4 Letter Holiday aim to set their stall out early for 2007.
