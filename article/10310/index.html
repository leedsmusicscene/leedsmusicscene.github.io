Kath and The Kicks

This is an archive of the band profile for Kath and The Kicks.

A menace, a power house; the band continue to poke at the attention of listeners across the North and beyond.

Kath and The Kicks have sent shockwaves through radio broadcasts from Rick Witter's (Shed Seven) show on Minster FM featuring 'All My Time' as track of the week, BBC Introducing airplay of 'The Lethal Parrot' and TV broadcasts on the Made In TV network with 'Alive and Gigging' aired in major cities including Leeds, Newcastle and Bristol.

As the band continues to build notoriety and light the eyes of rock lovers across the nation, make it your mission this summer to scratch your ears with their 'slap in the face' grunge rock music.'

20 Years of Screaming' released in May 2016 features the latest rip-roaring tracks to come from Leeds based rock trio Kath & The Kicks.


Kath Edmonds - Lead Guitar/Vocals
Tom Duffissey - Bass
Lee Proctor - Drums.
