The Cribs release their 'Housewife' single

This article was published in 2010.

After the first public airing of 'Housewife' on Radio 1 on Monday, The Cribs have announced that you can get the single as an official download on iTunes from today, Tuesday 10th August.

If you want it, buy it soon as rumours are that it won't be on there long.

A very special limited edition etched vinyl version of the song will be released on August 30th.

You can watch the video to 'Housewife' now on NME.com.
