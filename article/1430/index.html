BBC Online to award Star Bodixa with album of the week...

This article was published in 2003.

Star Bodixa album And They Danced On Glass has been selected as Album of the Week for the coming week on BBC online. The album is scheduled for release on February 24th, a date that coincides with a low-key acoustic show at LMUSU with Tom MacRae.
