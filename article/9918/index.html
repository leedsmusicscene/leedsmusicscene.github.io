i concur to release a new single in October

This article was published in 2008.

i concur have announced details of a new single and a tour as well as a slot at Reading and Leeds Festivals.

The new single 'Oblige' will be released Monday 6th October on Brew Records. It has been produced by Tom Woodhead of Â¡Forward, Russia! and will be available as a limited edition 7" and download. It will also feature b-side 'Captors'.

A remix of the single by Woodhead's AnteAter project will also be available with pre-orders.

Brew Records will host a special launch night at the Brudenell Social Club, Leeds, on Saturday 4th October featuring Napoleon IIIrd, Dinosaur Pile-up and AnteAter.

Meanwhile, following i concur's Maida Vale session for Huw Stephens in March, the band have been selected by to play on the BBC Introducing stages at both Reading and Leeds Festivals next weekend.
