Kaiser Chiefs team up with the Beano comic for their new single, "The Angry Mob"

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds five-piece Kaiser Chiefs have announced the release of their latest single, "The Angry Mob", and the band have teamed up with the Beano comic in creating a comic strip artwork. The 7" version will even feature a Gnasher Picture Disc

Set for release on Monday, 20th August the CD version of the release will be backed with "Telling Me To Go". A (very) limited edition 7" comes in a poster bag featuring the full Beano/Kaiser Chiefs comic strip artwork as a poster, plus a collectable comic strip illustration of the "Yours Truly, Angry Mob" album sleeve.
