DB4

This is an archive of the band profile for DB4.

We are a four-piece - drums, bass, guitar, keyboard and vocals with harmonica thrown in.

The band is named after the place where the blues was supposedly first discovered - a band leader named WC Handy heard a guy playing 'slide' guitar using a knife on a staion platform in Tutwiler - 'where the Yellow Dog crosses the Southern'.

The band started out playing blues but has now diversified into more mainstream rock and pop. .
