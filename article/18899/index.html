Naomi Coombes

This is an archive of the band profile for Naomi Coombes.

Naomi's music has been described as a 'bluesy, soulful twist to modern songwriting' with 'mature and thought provoking lyrics'.

Despite being only 18 years old, Naomi Coombes has already played well known venues in the South of England such as 'The Troubadour' and 'The Bedford', as well as being awarded the title of the "Best Female Solo Performer" at the 2013 Salisbury Music Awards.

In early 2014 Naomi Coombes got the chance to record seven original songs at the legendary Maida Vale Studios in London for BBC Introducing. She also supported chart artist Ella Eyre in January 2014.

Recently relocated to the vibrant music city of Leeds, Naomi Coombes is making a name for herself in the local music scene by performing at various venues as well as working on an EP to be released in 2015..
