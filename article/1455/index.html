Mr Shiraz are sleeping with the enemy...

This article was published in 2003.

Mr Shiraz release their debut single on the Blunt Monkey Records label at the end of April. Scheduled for a 28th April release, Sleeping With The Enemy will be available throughout the country.

Mr Shiraz play a St Patrick's Day special at Joseph's Well, with support from Dead Pets and Mr Dogg.
