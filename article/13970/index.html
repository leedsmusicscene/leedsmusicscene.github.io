Eyes Of March release new 4-track EP

This article was published in 2010.

This new 4-track release from Wakefield four-piece Eyes Of March is something the band can finally call their own. This CD mixes raw, alternative rock with hints of metal and punk along with somewhat humerous lyrics that the band hopes will set them apart from the local music scene.

This release was recorded and produced at Peak Studios in Bradford and with the great help from producer Roni Szpakowski, Eyes Of March have managed to produce 4 tracks that will hopefully show people what they are about.
