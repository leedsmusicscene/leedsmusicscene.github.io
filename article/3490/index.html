Instant Species to release their second single from five-star album "Plan E" in February...

This article was published in 2005.

Quality Huddersfield alt-pop punkers Instant Species are set to release their second single from the excellent 5 star album "Plan E".

The new single is a double A-side featuring a snappier version of "Night In The City" - described as "pure pop driven guitar that gives it a Strokes' Last Night feel" by Manchester Music - and the lo-fi country lilts of "Scott Free" - "possibly Instant Species' very best song to date" - Whisperin & Hollerin.

Also included are two brand new previously unreleased B-sides.

The single can be pre-ordered ahead of its February release date from the band's website.

Instant Species plan to tour in March, April and May to promote the release.
