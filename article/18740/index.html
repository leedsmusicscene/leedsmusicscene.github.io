Vendettas announce double a-side single launch

This article was published in 2014.

Vendettas are set to release a double a-side single with a launch event on 25th October 2014 at The Wardrobe. The double a-side single features 'What you say girl' and 'Stuck in your World' and will be available at the event.

Support will be from Sam Neil, The Velveteens and Eternal Teenage Syndrome.

The launch will be followed by Bright Beach club night, and tickets purchased for Vendettas' single launch will gain you free entry to the club night.

For tickets get in touch with Vendettas via Facebook or twitter or buy online at Leeds Gig Tickets.
