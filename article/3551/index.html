A Glorious Day - second date announced!

This article was published in 2005.

Embrace have announced a second festival show in Leeds Millennium Square, to take place on May 29th after all 8,000 tickets for the first show on May 28th sold out.

The open-air hometown headlining gig will feature a full supporting bill. Special guests for both shows are still to be confirmed but Embrace hope that some of their favourite bands will be joining them on the day.
