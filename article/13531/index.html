Ten to release a six track EP titled 'Lowlands'

This article was published in 2010.

Ten have this week announced the release of a six track EP titled 'Lowlands'.

Ten is a solo project led by Dominic Deane, who combines electronic keys, shoegaze noise and percussion with the acoustic sounds of glockenspiel and a string quartet to create a beautiful soundscape that moves from slow, eerie and melancholic to pulsating and optimistic.

'Lowlands' follows Ten's debut EP - titled 'Journey' - and is a homage to the nature sounds of the fens, and blends acoustic sounds mixed with classical instruments.

The EP will be released as a limited edition CD and as a Digital Download on Monday, 18th October.

To coincide with the EP release, Ten will be touring North England, Scotland and have scheduled a special one off show in Denmark.
