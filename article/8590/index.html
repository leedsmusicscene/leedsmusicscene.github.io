Stateless release their self-titled debut album

This article was published in 2007.

Newly signed to the Berlin label !K7, Leeds band Stateless have this week released their self-titled debut album.

Available from Monday, 18th June 'Stateless' combines emotional impact with heroic ambition. Its ten songs include the sublime 'Prism', the attention-grabbing 'Exit', the achingly beautiful 'Bloodstream', and the adventurous 'This Language', which incorporates a rap from Solesides member Lateef the Truthspeaker.

The band's next single will be 'Prism', scheduled for release on Monday, 25th June.
