Officers release their debut album

This article was published in 2011.

Officers have today released their debut album, 'On The Twelve Thrones', via the Rough Trade record label.

The album is available as a Digital Download, on CD, Cassette and via a strictly limited-edition 'Book-Set'.

It was recorded and produced in Leeds with final mix touches from Dave Bascombe (Depeche Mode) and Death in Vegas' Tim Holmes. The album also sees a collaboration with friend and contemporary image maker Stuart Semple on the artwork and creative designs.

Very limited edition individual Officers books are also available, which include the album, artwork, lyrics, and stories on the album design process.

You can buy the album online from the Rough Trade online store.
