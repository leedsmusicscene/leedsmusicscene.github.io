This Et Al to make available a limited edition first pressing of their album "Baby Machine"

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds band This Et Al have announced the completion of their debut album "Baby Machine". A fully backed release date will be announced in due course, but initially there are 500 available as an ultra limited edition first pressing. It is available for pre-order now from the band’s website (shipped by Monday 13th November 2006) and will also be available in a limited number of music shops. More details of the release date proper when it becomes known...

The long-awaited 11-track album was recorded and mixed by Richard Green of The Somatics and includes the songs “Wardens”, “Catscan“, “He Shoots Presidents” and “You've Driven For Miles (And Not Remembered A Thing)”.

Catch This Et Al live in Leeds on Tuesday 28th November when they play at the Cockpit venue with Minus The Bear.
