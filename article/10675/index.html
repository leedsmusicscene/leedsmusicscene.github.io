200 tickets left for Live At Leeds 2009

This article was published in 2009.

It’s Live At Leeds today but for anyone still in search of tickets you now only have three options left.

All advance tickets have been sold out, but luckily 200 have been left over to sell on the door. Only three venues will have such tickets on sale: Cockpit (50 tickets), Brudenell Social Club (50 tickets) and Leeds Metropolitan University (100 tickets). These all go on sale at 10am this morning and cost £15.

Live At Leeds is now in its third year after a successful beginning in 2007 for the Leeds 800th Birthday celebrations and the popular annual event has been sold out each year.

This year’s event features 100s of bands and spans the length and breadth of Leeds across a large number of live music venues including the Cockpit, Brudenell Social Club, LMUSU, The Elbow Room, The Library, Packhorse, Holy Trinity Church, Joseph's Well and the Faversham.

On Sunday 3rd May (tomorrow), organisers will be hosting an informal Q & A seminar at the Adelphi from 2pm. The panel of industry professionals taking your questions will include band managers, agents, the PRS, and an A&R representative from Sony BMG Publishing.
