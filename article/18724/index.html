Black Moth tour to commence in Leeds

This article was published in 2014.

To coincide with the release of their second album last week, the Black Moth album tour commences next week starting with a launch event in Leeds.

The 12-track album, 'Condemned To Hope', was produced by Jim Sclavunos (Grinderman, Nick Cave & the Bad Seeds, The Cramps) and is available now. You can watch the video for 'Room 13', the lead single from the album, on YouTube below.



The Leeds album launch takes place at the Brudenell Social Club on Friday 26th September, before the tour then continues on to Liverpool, York and beyond.
