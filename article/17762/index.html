More bands and day splits revealed for Bingley Music Live 2013

This article was published in 2013.

Bingley Music Live organisers have this week confirmed the day splits and made available day tickets for the much-loved Yorkshire music festival taking place at Myrtle Park between August 30th and September 1st.

There has also been a flurry of line up activity with Katy B, Tinchy Stryder, The Neville Staple Band and Frankie and the Heartstrings all being added to the festival line up.

The day splits and line ups are as follows:
