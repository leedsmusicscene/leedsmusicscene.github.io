Siren Lake

This is an archive of the band profile for Siren Lake.

Formed in mid-2006, Siren Lake have worked hard and fast to establish themselves as one of the most interesting young bands Yorkshire has to offer. The Huddersfield post-hardcore quintet have just finished recording their debut EP with producer Mark Mynett (Kill II This) and are ready to shake down venues across the country.

Not afraid to try something new, the band takes everything wonderful in the genres so popular today and creates music in a world of its own. On top of powerful, yet intricate beats lay fat bass riffs aching to get you moving. Along side this, the face-ripping driven guitar licks to compliment the dual vocal hooks battling it out to grab your attention and provide the ear candy that every music lover craves for.

Since completing their first UK tour in April 2007, the band hasn't looked back and is ready to show just what they're made of. With a constantly growing repertoire and buzzing fanbase, Siren Lake intend on making this their year..
