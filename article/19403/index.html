The Paisley Royals announced second single "Keeping off the Track"

This article was published in 2016.

Leeds based indie-rock group The Paisley Royals announced the release of their second single "Keeping off the Track" available from Bandcamp and to stream on Soundcloud this week.

Produced by Jim Ker (Pin Stripe Tuesday, Happy Daggers) at Loom Studios, "Keeping off the Track" is a clear ode to the band's adoring Merseybeat and Brit-pop influences layered with melodic guitar lines, bright rhythm parts and instantly memorable hooks which match the sound and energy of the band's live performances.

Listen "Keeping off the Track" on Soundcloud and get updates on The Paisley Royals from their Facebook page.
