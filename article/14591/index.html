Lifescreen: Video for the new single 'Opting Out'

This article was published in 2011.

The video for our new single 'Opting Out' is here!

Head on over to YouTube to watch it, we'd really appreciate it if you all could help spread the word.

The video shoot took place at Hyde Park Picture House, Leeds.

If you like what you hear, 'Opting Out' is available to download for free from Bandcamp, released on Monday 18th April.

'Opting Out' is taken from the album 'Connexions', which is also available to download from Bandcamp.
