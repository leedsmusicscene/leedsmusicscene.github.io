3,000 experience biggest ever Long Division Festival

This article was published in 2014.

Long Division Festival celebrated its biggest year this weekend gone, with over 3,000 people attending the annual Wakefield festival.

The event saw The Cribs make a triumphant return to their hometown, and music fans experienced the festival with 115 performances taking place at multiple venues across the city centre over the three days.

Dean Freeman, festival director, said: "Long Division 2014 was a great success this year. Many people who have been coming since 2011 have told us it was the best yet, which is great."
