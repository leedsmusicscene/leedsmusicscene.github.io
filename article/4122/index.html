Crew 82

This is an archive of the band profile for Crew 82.

Crew 82 consist of three beatboxers and a CD. Sholto, Vanilla Slice, Dave = beatboxers + DJ Synical

Since winning the battle of the bands 2004 @ Arc in Headingly we have peformed around the UK with acts including Trevor Nelson, Tim Westwood, Goldie Lookin' Chain, Goldie, The Cut Up Bots, Rahzel.

Check the Crew 82 website for finalised UK University tour dates for Sept/Oct and for European dates for 2006. There is also a short promo vid on the site which will introduce us a bit better..
