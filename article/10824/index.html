Trio of local bands added to the Leeds Festival line up on Festival Republic Stage

This article was published in 2009.

Leeds Festival organisers have today revealed the line up for the Festival Republic Stage and a trio of West Yorkshire bands will be appearing.

The local trio are Pulled Apart by Horses, Grammatics and Wakefield's Matt Abbott, aka Skint & Demoralised. The bands join headliners Marmaduke Duke, The Blackout and La Roux.

Other bands set to appear on the Festival Republic Stage this year are Soulsavers with Mark Lanegan, Black Lips, Bring Me The Horizon, Atreyu, Lightspeed Champion, The Rumblestrips, The Big Pink, Broken Records, Bombay Bicycle Club, Marina And The Diamonds, The Temper Trap, The Soft Pack, Hockey, Bear Hands, Amazing Baby, A Place To Bury Strangers, A Day To Remember, Fight Like Apes, Titus Andronicus, Middle Class Rut, Single File, Jack's Mannequin, The Chapman Family, Detroit Social Club, Golden Silvers, Baddies, Go Audio, The Joy Formidable, Magistrates and The Hot Rats.

The winner from Futuresound 2009 will also make an appearance on this stage.

In other festival news, Mariachi El Bronx, The View and The Living End have been added to the Main Stage line up.
