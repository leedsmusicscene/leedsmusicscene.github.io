Penguin to release 'This is Believing' EP via Dead Sober Records

This article was published in 2010.

Penguin have announced the release of their eagerly awaited 'This is Believing' EP through the Dead Sober Records label.

The EP will be available as a Digital Download on iTunes from 8th November, and from other major online distributors shortly later in the month.

The songs are 'New', 'A Vague Interpretation of Everything', 'Where We Are' and 'Just a Well Rounded Person', 4 songs that capture the energy, musicianship and harmonies of this alternative rock trio.

After winning the Centrestage competition in July and earning a place on the BBC Introducing stage at Leeds and Reading Festival, Penguin are a band that are on everyone's lips and they were recently selected to perform at the music and fashion extravaganza Leeds Rocks.

On 13th November Penguin will headline Dead Sober Records launch event at Eiger Music Studios in Leeds [read our gig preview].

'This Is Believing' will be the second release from Dead Sober Records, a new record label that was created by the organisers of Leeds Festival Fringe event and specialises in under-21 bands.
