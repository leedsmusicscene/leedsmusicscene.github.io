Ceiling Demons Set to Release Their Debut LP: Dual Sides 18/12/13

This article was published in 2013.

Ceiling Demons make a unique style of British Hip-Hop, seeking to capture the rawness of human emotion in rhythms & rhymes. Think Mike Skinner meets DJ Shadow and you'll have a rough idea. The line up consists of twin brother MCs Psy Ceiling & Dan Demon, with production by Beat Demon.

Dual Sides is the group's debut LP, which follows on from where 'The Ceiling Demon EP' left off. It is a journey of masochistic escapism that takes you through the darkness in order to appreciate the light. The album was recorded in the heart of the Yorkshire Dales and features BBC Introducing's songbird Pip Mountjoy, and additional production from Euan Kearney.
