Nerve Engine video online now

This article was published in 2002.

Leeds-based metallers Nerve Engine have released a video for their track 'Seven Days'. The video was produced, directed, filmed and edited by the band themselves.

Go to the website at www.nerveengine.com to download the video, you can also watch an instant stream in Windows Media Player.

The track 'Seven Days' is taken from their acclaimed album 'Chrome'.
