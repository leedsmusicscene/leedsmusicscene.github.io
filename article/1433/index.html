CD Central @ Leeds Central Library - get your music heard...

This article was published in 2003.

Are you a solo artist or member of a band, and have you produced your own CD? If the answer to these questions is yes, then you can help yourselves and help CD Central Library get Leeds’ musical talent out of the bedrooms and backrooms of the pubs, and out there into the city and beyond!

CD Central gives local musicians access to a widespread and diverse audience, where their music is available from an easily-accessible central site, and at no cost to the discerning yet adventurous punter.

To submit your CD(s) to the CD Central collection, simply obtain an entry form from the Music Library, or visit the CD Central website by clicking here, and hand the CDs in to a member of staff. They’ll put you in the collection, ready to loan, straight away.

If you provide two copies of your CD one copy will be placed in the Listening Post and they will also advertise your forthcoming gigs. So stop dreaming & start scheming! Send in your CD and gig info today!
