Cyanide Pills to release their debut single on March 2nd 2009

This article was published in 2009.

Cyanide Pills debut single, “Break It Up”, will be released through Damaged Goods on March 2nd 2009 and will be available to purchase on limited edition fluorescent yellow 7” vinyl and Digitial Download formats.

The 7” single is backed with live favourite “Mail Order Bride” and the previously unheard track, “Stick ‘em Up”.

To pre-order a vinyl copy please head over to the Damaged Goods shop.

The single can also be purchased in person from any good record store, including Jumbo Records in Leeds, and the Digital Download will be available on all major download sites including iTunes and E-music.

To celebrate the single release, Cyanide Pills will be playing a special single launch gig at Cardigan Arms on Thursday 26th February.
