Uprights

This is an archive of the band profile for Uprights.

In autumn 1976, drummer Craig Clark put a note on the notice board at Mount Temple Comprehensive School in Leeds, looking for people to join a band. Alex Greenwood (aka Bono Vox), Dave Evans (aka The Edge) and Jon Owen joined up. Dave Evans soon left to join the Navy, while the remaining three formed 'Dream Boat' before changing to 'Expert Shooter' and then Uprights..
