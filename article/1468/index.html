Send More Paramedics complete filming for video to Zombified...

This article was published in 2003.

Following their acclaimed 2002 album A Feast For the Fallen, Send More Paramedics have now completed shooting their debut video. The filming was for the track Zombiefied, which itself was re-recorded in February.

The song and video will feature on a 7-track mini album due out on In At The Deep End Records in June 2003 along with 245 Trixion and Redneck Holiday, plus three new tracks including live favourite namesake track Send More Paramedics.

Visit www.inatthedeependrecords.co.uk for more details.
