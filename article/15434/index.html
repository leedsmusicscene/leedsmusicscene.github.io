Dead Air

This is an archive of the band profile for Dead Air.

Having finalised a full line-up in early 2011, they have already garnered a lot of attention from press and fans around the north of England. Blending a myriad of influences, such as, Born of Osiris, SikTh, Veil of Maya, Cynic and Architects. They intertwine progression with melodic harmonies, an extensive range of vocal work and unrelenting drum sequences. Tech inspired riffs merged with unorthodox bass grooves, help to create a genuinely unique and original sound, separating them from the surge of the uninspired.

Their live performance consists of a punishing yet eloquent display of technicality and colour, that will overwhelm the skeptical. With a promising, and ever evolving sound, their haunting set will lure you in and leave you captivated..
