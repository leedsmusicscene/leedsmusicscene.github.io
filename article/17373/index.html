The Walkmen head second wave of bands announced for Live At Leeds 2013

This article was published in 2013.

Live at Leeds 2013 have this week announced the second wave of bands scheduled to play the festival this coming May. The annual city-wide event will host over 100 bands across 12 stages.

Joining Rudimental, Everything Everything, Alunageorge, Peace and Darwin Deez on the official line up boasting two Brit nominees are The Walkmen, Dutch Uncles, Savages, MØ, Melodys Echo Chamber, Dinosaur Pile-Up and many more.

Tickets for the Saturday multi-venue event can be bought now via Leeds Gig Tickets.
