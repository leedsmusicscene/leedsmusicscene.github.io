New EP in the works for Blue Sky Project

This article was published in 2008.

Blue Sky Project is currently working on its second EP, set for an Autumn 2008 release.

In a change from the band's single 'Masquerade' and last year's EP 'Fenestrae', both released through Salt The Wound Records, this new four-track collection is being created and funded entirely by the band itself. It will feature three brand new songs, as well as a reworking of older track 'Refresh'.

Blue Sky Project's debut limited-edition EP 'Fenestrae' sold out in a matter of months, following a great number of enthusiastic reviews. Leeds Music Scene referred to it as "genuinely mindblowing," while NoTiTLE called it "an accomplished, fiercely imaginative and super-mature piece of work."
