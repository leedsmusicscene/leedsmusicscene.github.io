The Glitterati to release a new album in April 2010

This article was published in 2010.

Leeds band The Glitterati have announced the release of their second studio album. Titled 'Are You One Of Us?' the album is due for release on the DR2 Records label on 19th April.

The rock five piece originally burst onto the scene in 2003 when they released their debut single 'Do You Love Yourself?' on the Poptones record label and their debut album was release in 2005 via Atlantic.

In 2009, The Glitterati began the process of recording their second album with producer Matt Hyde (Gallows, Acroama, Trivium, Slipknot).

The band's comeback single 'Fight Fight Fight' was released in November as a free download.
