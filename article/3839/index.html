Buen Chico to release their debut single on May 30th...

This article was published in 2005.

Buen Chico’s first single - “Killing Somebody” - will be released on 30th May on the new development label Pure Wonder as a one-off single deal. It is a Double-A-Side with “Wait”. The single is a result of recording work done with EMI’s David Kosten (aka Faultline) who has worked with the likes of Chris Martin, Michael Stipe and Flaming Lips, at the renowned Jacobs Studios in Farnham.

Buen Chico will team up with Fleeing New York for a tour around the country, with a show at Leeds' The Vine on Saturday, May 14th.
