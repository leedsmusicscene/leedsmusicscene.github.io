Deuce & Charger release new single 'We Can Beat The Night' on 4 December

This article was published in 2015.

Deuce & Charger bring a massive 2015 to a close with their brand new dance-floor anthem 'We Can Beat The Night'. Released on 4 December on In Charge Recordings, 'We Can Beat The Night' follows a full-on summer of plays on BBC Radio 1 and Kiss Fresh, playlisting on BBC Radio 1 Xtra and a headline debut live set at BBC Introducing/1Xtra Live in Leeds.

Including remixes by RAM Records prodigy Chords and experimental funk/hip hop act Fold, and following hot on the heels of the "emotive and uplifting D&B" [Less Than 3] of their recent 'Tear Down The Barricades' VIP track, Deuce & Charger will be all over club systems and radios everywhere with 'We Can Beat The Night' in 2015 and beyond!
