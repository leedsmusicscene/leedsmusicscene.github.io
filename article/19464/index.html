Thousands of music fans in Leeds this Saturday as Jess Glynne, Corinne Bailey Rae, We Are Scientists and Circa Waves come to town

This article was published in 2016.

Thousands of music fans will be in Leeds this Saturday as Jess Glynne, Corinne Bailey Rae, We Are Scientists and Circa Waves come to town - Live at Leeds is back for its tenth year anniversary celebration!

Celebrating 10 years of breaking the latest and greatest new acts, Live At Leeds 2016 will have over 200 musical acts playing across the city in what is now becoming a must go to music festival. The city sings to a different beat with stages ranging from Leeds University, O2 Academy, the infamous Brudenell Social Club, Leeds College of Music and a new stage on Leeds' famous heritage shopping precinct, Briggate.

The line-up this year features 2015's biggest pop sensation, Brit Award-nominee Jess Glynne, fast rising Liverpool four piece Circa Waves, indie legends We Are Scientists, NME award winner Rat Boy, and a plethora of exciting acts like Ghostpoet, Blossoms, Izzy Bizu, and Blood Red Shoes.

Leeds star Corrine Bailey Rae will be playing an intimate show at the Hi-Fi Club.

Tickets for Live At Leeds 2016 are still available at Jumbo Records via Leeds Gig tickets. Tickets will go off sale on Friday 29th April at 2pm.
