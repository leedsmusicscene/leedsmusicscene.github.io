Corinne Bailey Rae returns with new album

This article was published in 2016.

Leeds singer-songwriter - and two time Grammy Award winner - Corinne Bailey Rae has returned with the release of 'Been To The Moon', the first song from her forthcoming album 'The Heart Speaks In Whispers'.

Written by Corinne and Steve Brown with Paris and Amber Strother from the LA-based future-soul band KING, and recorded in Capitol Records' Studio A, 'Been To The Moon' is a joyous, vibrant and unique track.

The song is available now on all streaming sites and you can watch the video below.



Six years on from her last studio album, Corinne's new, uplifting album 'The Heart Speaks in Whispers' was recorded both at her studio in Leeds, and in Los Angeles, and was co-produced by Corinne with Steve Brown, her collaborator on her critically acclaimed previous album 'The Sea'. It will be released on May 13th.
