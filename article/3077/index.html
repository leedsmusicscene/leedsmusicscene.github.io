10,000 Things to release a double A-side single later this month...

This article was published in 2004.

The next single from Leeds' 10,000 Things will be a double A-side featuring the tracks Titanium and Can't Do Nothing. The B-side is a cover of Damaged Goods by former Leeds favourites Gang Of Four.

The single is scheduled for release on September 27th on the Fiction Records label.

The band will play next on September 9th in London as part of the Hot New Bands club night.
