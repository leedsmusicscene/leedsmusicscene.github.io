Details of Live At Leeds Extra Time confirmed

This article was published in 2015.

Taking place at Goals Soccer Centre on Monday 4th May, "Live at Leeds Extra Time" provides a combination of football, live music, good food and a great atmosphere.

This year the customary Live At Leeds Football Tournament will see 32 teams going head-to-head in an attempt to depose reigning champions ITV.

In terms of music, the event will host an acoustic stage with appearances from Esper Scout, Sons of Great Men, and more to be announced closer to the date.

The acoustic stage is organised through the Live At Leeds' Unconference, and supported by PRS For Music Foundation.

To keep everyone fed and busy on the day, food options come way of The Pizza Guy, while &/or Emporium will provide stalls and pop-up shops, showcasing and selling the work of some of the finest local artists, designers, jewellers, clothing brands and more.

There will also be the chance to play Zorb Football.

The event starts at 12 noon. Entry is free, and monies raised through the activities will go towards Martin House Children's Hospice.
