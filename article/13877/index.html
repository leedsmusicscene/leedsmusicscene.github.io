Ellen and the Escapades set to release a Christmas single

This article was published in 2010.

Ellen and the Escapades have announced the release of a Christmas single, set to hit the shops on Monday, 13th December.

The single is called 'By The Fireside With You' and comes out as a Digital Download via iTunes, Amazon and others.

The single was records and mixed by Stickboy and the video for the Xmas single was shot by Andy Little.

You can listen to 'By The Fireside With You' online at soundcloud.com.

In other band news, Ellen and the Escapades have recently completed an interview with A Negative Narrative.
