Aim A Little Higher

This is an archive of the band profile for Aim A Little Higher.

Steven Mangham - Vocals
Danny Handforth - Guitar/Vocals
Sam Applegarth - Guitar
Gareth Davies - Bass/Vocals
Lewis Thompson - Drums

Aim A Little Higher are 5 lads from Wakefield all with the same passion for music. Infectious pop/punk melodies and a hard hitting rhythm section this band combine elements from Rock, Pop Punk and Hardcore to create a truly unique sound which is sure to get you moving.

Having formed just under a year ago the band have gone from strength to stength playing support slots with the Kerrang Nominated BLITZ KIDS at the Cockpit and Radio One Playlisted KID BRITISH and are shortly to play as support for the awesome Kids Can't Fly as well as Blitz Kids on two dates of their October tour..
