Backyards stream their debut EP

This article was published in 2012.

Backyards' debut EP - If You're Scared - can be streamed at http://www.soundcloud.com/backyards until its release on 1 Feb. From the band:

"As a way to thank everyone for supporting us, we're streaming our EP up until the launch on 1 Feb. We're also proud to announce our Poster EP concept for those who want something tangible from this release. The If You're Scared poster is a high-quality print designed by the wonderful Jenny Evans. Have a listen, have a look! x"

On 15 Feb Backyards will host their live EP launch party at Nation of Shopkeepers with support from Danish touring band The White Album (thewhitealbum.bandcamp.com) and lively Leeds-locals Buffalo Bones (buffalobones.co.uk). The show starts at 8:30 and entry is free. More info at the Facebook event here - http://www.facebook.com/events/252231438180749/
