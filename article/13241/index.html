Napoleon IIIrd to release his 'Christiania' album in November

This article was published in 2010.

Napoleon IIIrd has unveiled the title and cover of his brilliant new album, set for release on November 8th 2010 on the Brainlove Records label.

The album is titled 'Christiania' and is a collection of lushly textured pop songs and evocative, extended textural drones, on the subject of small-town life and escaping its constraints.

The first single to be taken from the album - 'The Unknown Unknown' - will be sent out free to fans in the next few weeks.

To get your free copy of 'The Unknown Unknown' sign up as a fan at www.brainloverecords.com/christiania.

Napoleon IIIrd will tour the album in September and October.
