Overseer provide the soundtrack to Matrix Reload trailer...

This article was published in 2003.

Leeds tekno-angst terrorist Overseer has provided the soundtrack to the Matrix Reload trailer, which was premiered to 80 million in the Superbowl ad-break - and believed to be featured on Film 2003 repeated this Saturday afternoon on BBC2.

Overseer tracks have also been used for the Daredevil trailer, as well as currently being used to advertise Chrysler Dodge cars and the forthcoming Mitsubishi Cars in the US; you can also find the Leeds artist on CSI, GT3, Stuntman, Fastlane and Snatch.

Rob Overseer is "currently putting the finishing touches to his first album for Columbia Records".
