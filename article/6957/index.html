Spin

This is an archive of the band profile for Spin.

Spin began life in late 2003 and three years later I can see the reasons why I started this band in the first place!! The aim? Well, to write and perform big loud rock songs that it seems few artists today either do not wish to or can't. There's been a few line up changes and a load of arguments and musical differences along the way (don't they always say that ?), but this line up of the band I feel is going to stay together and build on what we love doing and that is to ROCK !!! Listen to the tracks posted on Myspace, message us for a CD if you wish, come to the gigs, but most of all I hope you enjoy what we do!!! Look out for an album in the next few months and a load of live shows. See you out there! Karl (March 2006)..
