Deuce & Charger playlisted on BBC 1Xtra

This article was published in 2015.

Deuce & Charger's track 'Stop The Sky From Falling' has been playlisted on BBC Radio 1Xtra this week. The track, featuring Leeds-based artists Bianca Gerald and Weezy Jefferson, will be played on prime-time 1Xtra shows by mainstay DJs such as MistaJam, DJ Target and Trevor Nelson. This follows Deuce & Charger's headline set at the recent 1Xtra and BBC Introducing live showcase at The Belgrave Music Hall hosted by DJ Target and Alan Raw. You can watch the video for 'Stop The Sky From Falling' here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k5XuNuzoDR0.
