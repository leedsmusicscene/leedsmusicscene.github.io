InSatellites announce new single 'Linger'

This article was published in 2014.

Leeds five-piece InSatellites have announced the release of a new single on 4th April 2014 - titled 'Linger' - that will see the band venture into new ground.

Taking their refined mixture of grunge and alt-rock - and combining it with such contrasting influences such as Brand New, Silversun Pickups, Everything Everything and Radiohead - InSatellites are striving for a far more progressive sound, whilst still retaining the catchy vocal hooks and pounding intensity of their debut EP 'No Wave' (release in November 2013, and reviewed here).

The band's latest single consists of two tracks:

1. Linger
2. Nihl (Nil)

Limited edition copies of the single will be available (free of charge) at a special single launch party to be held at The Library on Friday 4th April. Support on the night will ne NARCS, Adore//Repel and Rex Manning Day.

The single will also be available for digital download via the InSatellites page at bandcamp.com.
