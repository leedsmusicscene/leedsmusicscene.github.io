Apollo Junction release new single "Such a carry on"

This article was published in 2013.

The Leeds rockers have released their latest single and video "Such a Carry On". This follows on from the success of their first self titled EP released earlier this year which peaked at number 10 in the Reverbnation charts. Apollo Junction are continuing to ride a wave of successful radio plays being hailed by the likes of Lamacq and Tom Robinson on BBC6 Music and regularly being play-listed by Graham Norton on BBC2. The new video from Apollo Junction was filmed by One Chance Filming featuring over 100+ people from the Yorkshire region including SG Action UK and The York Hornet Cheerleaders. The song is 'Such a Carry On' - Available now on iTunes and many other digital stores
