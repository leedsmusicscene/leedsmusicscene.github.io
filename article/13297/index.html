Leeds Festival Fringe launch 15 track album

This article was published in 2010.

Organisers of the Leeds Festival Fringe - the week long event prior to the Leeds Festival that aims to celebrate the local music scene - have set up a new record label called Dead Sober Records.

Their first release is available now on iTunes. The release is a 15 track album featuring artists playing at the first Leeds Festival Fringe.

The tracklisting is as follows:

1) 'Harriet's Got Herpes' by The Red Pills - playing On the Fringe on Tuesday 24th August at Carpe Diem.

2) 'The Lesson' by The Temps - playing On the Fringe on Friday 20th August at Carpe Diem.

3) 'Amethyst' by Claire Cameron Band - playing On the Fringe on Wednesday 25th August at Carpe Diem.

4) 'One Way Road' by Leodis.

5) 'Everknowing' by The Wick Effect - playing On The Fringe on Monday 23rd at Royal Park Cellars.

6) 'If She Goes Back on This' by Spiders Eat Vinyl - playing On The Fringe on Sunday 22nd August at Carpe Diem.

7) 'These Four Walls' by Acid Drop - playing On The Fringe on Tuesday 24th August at Carpe Diem.

8) 'I Just Don't Like You' by Jen Armstrong Music - playing On the Fringe on Tuesday 24th August at The Well.

9) 'Louder Than Sound' by Mutiny For Hire - playing On the Fringe on Saturday 21st August at Royal Park Cellars.

10) 'The Surge' by The Idles - playing On The Fringe on Friday 20th August at Carpe Diem.

11) 'The Dark Caves' by The Shrinks.

12) 'The Battle' by Secret Circuits - playing On The Fringe on Wednesday 25th August at Carpe Diem.

13) 'Carry Me' by Miranda Versus The Crok - playing On The Fringe on Tuesday 24th August at Carpe Diem.

14) 'This Place' by The Sighting - playing On The Fringe on Wednesday 25th August at Carpe Diem.

15) 'The City of Polaroid Explosions' by The Birdman Rallies - playing On The Fringe on Saturday 21st August at Carpe Diem.
