Deposit scheme for Leeds Festival 2014

This article was published in 2013.

Organisers of the annual Leeds Festival have this week confirmed details of the deposit scheme that allows music fans to purchase their tickets in installments.

Those wishing to attend the festival over the August Bank Holiday weekend can buy their tickets for Leeds 2014 by putting an initial deposit of £50 down, followed by three installments of £53.50. The total price is £210.50, which includes the booking fee.

The deposit scheme is administered at both See Tickets and Ticketmaster, where tickets will go on sale on Tuesday 3rd December at 9am.

The first line-up announcement for Leeds 2014 will also take place on Tuesday.
