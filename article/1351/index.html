Leafeater go on tour

This article was published in 2002.

The first date is the In The City Showcase, an annual event held in Manchester. The event will be held on Sunday 15th at the South club on King Street, Deansgate. This is an extremely important gig for us and would appreciate it if you could make every effort to be there. We need your support! Let us know at studio@leafeater.com if you're interested in going. If there is enough demand we will look into the possibility of running a coach from Bradford.
