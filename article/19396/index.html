Arms of Another new single release from Marsicans

This article was published in 2016.

Marsicans, who are described as upbeat indie meets dirty pop, have launched their latest single, Arms of Another. It is an upbeat, synth-driven track featuring the band's trademark harmonies. The song itself is about the human need for intimacy and how it can sometimes make us act in ways we know we shouldn't. It is an infectious, makes you want to dance, while tugging gently on the heart strings, kind of track.

The single premiered nationally in the UK on Radio X and is out on now on Love vs Fear Records and is the first in a series of Marsicans releases planned for 2016.

Single release:
Arms of Another, available via iTunes and Bandcamp: http://marsicans.bandcamp.com
