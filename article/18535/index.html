Sam Airey releases video for Station Approach

This article was published in 2014.

Sam is a LMS favourite and we have been waiting a long time to hear the news that a Debut Album is and its way. We are excited to say this IS a reality!

In advance of his anticipated debut, Sam is releasing a Single, Station Road, on the 23rd June 2014. This has already received playlisting on Amazing Radio and a fair bit of BBC 6 music and Radio 1 love.

If you want to hear it, please watch via the link below, or even better, go see him. The place to be is the Brudenell this Friday, 20th June, to catch Sam Airey with The Lake Poets, Cottonwoolf and Tea Leaf.
