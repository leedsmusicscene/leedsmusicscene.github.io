The Bluefoot Project awarded "Best of British" in the USA...

This article was published in 2003.

For the second year running Chocolate Fireguard Music are celebrating: one of their releases has again been chosen by Virgin Megastores in the USA for their annual "Best Of British" campaign. Last year it was essential compilation Taster.... Sounds from the funky underground Vol 2 that received the promotion.

This year it is the critically acclaimed debut album Brave from The Bluefoot Project that has been chosen as one of only 8 imported titles to be racked on special "Best Of British" listening posts in the chains six biggest locations: Sunset Boulevard, Los Angeles; San Francisco; Times Square, New York City; Chicago; Union Square, New York City; Boston. The campaign runs throughout June and July.

In other news, The Bluefoot Project will be performing on the Lost Stage at this year's Glastonbury Festival on Friday 27th June, while labelmates Kava Kava have a prestigious gig on the Avalon Stage on the Saturday night.
