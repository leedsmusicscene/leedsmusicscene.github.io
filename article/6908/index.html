On The Mangle

This is an archive of the band profile for On The Mangle.

On The Mangle have been a close knit unit of music creators since 2002 and are off the hook! A new alternative wave of sounds pushing the way forward in a breach of convention. schorching vocals, fast fingers on the fretts, utter madness on drums and hints of popping electro organ synth on keys. Four sparadically talented musicians out and about, aiming to break the conventional alternative scene with 30 new style alternative tracks. Live performance have been described as 'electrifying'..
