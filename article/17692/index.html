Fur Blend release Vicugna Pacos EP

This article was published in 2013.

Fur Blend's debut EP Vicugna Pacos was released as part of Record Store Day 2013. It was (and maybe still is) available from Crash Records, Jumbo Records, Norman Records, Piccadilly Records and Rough Trade. It's also available via bandcamp.com as well as the usual download and streaming sites.
