Embrace release the fourth single from their latest album This New Day on December 4th

This article was published in 2006.

Embrace will release their latest single on December 4th. Taken from their This New Day album, "I Can't Come Down" is full of the kind of heart-bursting spiritual resonance that has become Embrace's trademark and helped them build one of Britain's most loyal fanbases.

"I Can't Come Down" is available on CD, 7" and digital download formats - the limited edition one track 7" includes a special etching by guitarist Richard McNamara. The CD will include the video for the single, featuring backstage and performance footage filmed during the band's recent UK tour, and two new songs recorded live on the tour.
