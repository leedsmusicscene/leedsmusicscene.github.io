Bingley Music Live in the running at UK Festival Awards 2012

This article was published in 2012.

This year has been an incredible year for Bingley Music Live and the popular Yorkshire event is in the running for an award at the UK Festival Awards 2012.

The Festival Awards are the annual Oscars of the contemporary music festival business. Largely voted for by the public, they provide a nationally recognised kite-mark for quality. Over half a million votes were cast last year, making it the benchmark by which festival-goers rate their experiences and a coveted prize for organisers like us.

If Bingley Music Live brightened up your summer please take a minute to vote for them under the 'Best Medium Sized Festival' category.

When you log on to take part in a ballot you can also enter the Dream Summer prize draw to win 2 VIP tickets to every festival that wins an award, as well as a new iPad 3.
