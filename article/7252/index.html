Last Gang to release their debut single

This article was published in 2006.

Last Gang release their début single “Beat Of Blue” on Monday, 30th October. Self-released the single will be available on limited edition 7” format (500 copies only) and comes backed with B-side “Never Be Alone”. Expect the video for “Beat Of Blue” to feature online soon.

Last Gang play at the Faversham in Leeds on Saturday 9th September is what doubles as their single launch party and last Leeds show of the year. The band are also booked in for a Christmas show at Escobar in Wakefield on December 22nd.

Support on Saturday comes from Jack Afro.
