Chicago Joe & The Soul Divas

This is an archive of the band profile for Chicago Joe & The Soul Divas.

We are a seven-piece Soul Band, bringing to you, in the words of Wilson Picket "Sweet Soul Music". We spread the word of many of the soul legends, including: Otis Redding; Wilson Picket; Aretha Franklin; Temptations; Martha Reeves and many more. Our outstanding line up provides a fantastic rhythm section, a horn section to blow your mind and that just leaves our Soul Divas who are enough to leave you shouting for more all night!

We originally formed in 1992, and are based in Yorkshire, but travel further from time to time. We are confident we can deliver the best in musical entertainment and set that will have you all "Dancing in the Street"..
