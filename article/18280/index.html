Suzy Blu releases new EP 'BLU'

This article was published in 2014.

Suzy Blu has this week released her new EP, "BLU", which is available to purchase digitally or on CD.

The tracklisting for the EP is:

1. Blu
2. Heaven Or Las Vegas
3. Wont Be The Girl (Grimiss Mix)
4. Blu (Live @ The House Of Hazard)
