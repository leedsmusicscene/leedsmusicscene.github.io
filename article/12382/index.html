More bands added to the Live At Leeds 2010 line up

This article was published in 2010.

Following the official Live At Leeds launch in January, more bands have been this week been announced.

The following bands have been added to the line up: Gold Panda; Loops Haunt; The Neat; Esben & The Witch; Telegraphs; Is Tropical; Bear Mask; Sketches; Yonderboy; and Wonderswan.

The bands previously announced are: Wild Beasts; 65daysofstatic; Ellen and the Escapades; Johnny Foreigner; Loose Talk Costs Lives; Holy State; Egyptian Hip Hop; Blood Oranges; Frankie and the Heart Strings; and The Horn The Hunt.
