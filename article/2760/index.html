Last Night's TV to release new single in July...

This article was published in 2004.

The next Last Night's TV album, to be titled Letters Home is now being recorded and as a taster for the new material, the band are putting out a limited run single, entitled Nice To Have A History. The CD will also include the new version of Two Views (as featured on the upcoming rejigged version of Too Much In Doubt), and a previously unreleased and recently completed gem from the archive, Angel On Your Shoulder. The final mastering of the songs will be happening in the next few days, and the artwork is being prepared - the band hope to have copies available to buy at Mixing Tin on 4th July.
