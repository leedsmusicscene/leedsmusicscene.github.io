Young Amphibians to release 'Thought Control' in April

This article was published in 2014.

Alternative Rock band Young Amphibians will be releasing their debut EP at the end of April 2014. The EP which was recorded at The Animal Farm, London will be titled 'Thought Control' and will be the band's first release since founding in January 2014.

"We're unbelievably excited to get the release out, we've put a lot of time and effort into making this EP & we really hope it shows. The support we've received leading up to it has been great, long may it continue". Thom. P Smith

Young Amphibians are playing 360 Club, Leeds on 19th April with The Puppeteers, The Reds & Hidden White Noise.

Thought Control will be available to pre-order in the coming weeks. Head to www.facebook.com/youngamphibians for more information & updates.
