Jon Doe's land in trouble...

This article was published in 2002.

Congratulations to the boys in the Jon Doe's who recently played their unique style of pop, punk emo at The Verge in Camden, London alongide The Lawrence Arms, Jerry Built and The Take.

The guys have made it to the final of Trouble TV's "Trouble Wants U" with their video for My Girlfriend is an Alien.

In order to lodge your vote for the bands Video which is currently being played on Trouble TV simply text the word JON to the number 07786 201234 (normal text charges apply).
