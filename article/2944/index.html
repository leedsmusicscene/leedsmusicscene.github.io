The Scaramanga Six to release new single...

This article was published in 2004.

Impending single release: from the forthcoming long player, Cabin Fever, The Scaramanga Six have cherry-picked We Rode The Storm as the next single. This should be out accompanied by a set of mystery B-sides on CD format in the next couple of months.

For more details of this, plus some shots from the newly finished video for the single, visit the band's website.
