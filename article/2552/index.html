Black Wire receive NME "Single of the Week" for their debut single...

This article was published in 2004.

Black Wire were awarded this week's "NME Single of the Week" for their debut single Attack! Attack! Attack!, writing amongst other things; "Great band name, great song title, great tune. Piece of piss" and "Black Wire make being in a band seem like the best idea in the world and piss all over their peers" before calling the single "a killer debut".
