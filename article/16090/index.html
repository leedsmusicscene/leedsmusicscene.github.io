Remember the Hurricanes release their debut single

This article was published in 2012.

'Blow My Mind', the debut single from Leeds band Remember the Hurricanes will be released on 4th February 2012 via all major download sites.

The single has been played numerous times on BBC Introducing and was presented as Huw Stephens' "tip of the week".

You can listen to the track below:

Blow My Mind by rememberthehurricanes

Remember The Hurricanes have been described as having "fresh and interesting electro sounds with Beatles catchy pop melodies".

To celebrate the release of their debut single the band will host a special launch event at the Cockpit in Leeds on 4th February.
