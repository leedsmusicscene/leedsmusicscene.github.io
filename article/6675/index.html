Grand Charity Showcases scheduled for both May Bank Holidays

This article was published in 2006.

The Irish Centre in Leeds will host two Bank Holiday Charity Showcases in May. The first, on May 1st, is for the benefit of the MacMillan Cancer Nurses and the other (29th May) is for the British Heart Foundation.

The events will, fittingly, be tribute events with the May 1st event featuring The Rob Dylan Band (Bob Dylan), Green Mac (Fleetwood Mac) Keep It Cash (Johnny Cash) and Rob Nichols (Nick Drake).

The second charity event at the Irish Centre on May 29th will feature Iron-On Maiden (Iron Maiden), Bon Jov’ish (Bon Jovi), Ozzyfied (Ozzy Osbourne) and Tallica (Mettalica).

All profits go to charity, tickets are priced at £10 advance or £12 on the door.
