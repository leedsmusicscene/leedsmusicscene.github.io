Cinerama to release a new single on 8th June...

This article was published in 2004.

Cinerama will release a new single on 8th June 2004 titled It's Not You, It's Me. The single is available on 7" format only and will be limited to just 1,500 copies. The B-side is Erinner Dich, a cover of the German disco hit from the Kraut-pop wonders Klee.
