Embrace to release a new album in April

This article was published in 2006.

Embrace have this week announced details of a new album to be released on 3rd April, with a single (“Nature’s Law”, taken from the album) scheduled for release on 20th March.

Following their resurgence in 2004, culminating in over 600,000 copies of the album "Out Of Nothing" being shifted in the UK alone, it’s little wonder that the album - titled “This New Day” - is the sound of a revitalised band bursting with renewed confidence!

With Youth once again taking care of production, “This New Day” was recorded both at his studio in Granada (Spain) and Olympic and Britannia Row studios in London.

In other news, tickets for Embrace’s 2006 Forest Tour are now on sale - as part of the tour, the band play Dalby Forest (North Yorkshire) on 25th June.
