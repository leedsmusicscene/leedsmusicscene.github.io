Leo Brazil and his Twitch release their new album

This article was published in 2010.

The self-titled release "Leo Brazil and his Twitch" is the new full length album from Leo Brazil.

The album was recorded in the band's home studio and will be the first release from Huddersfield's new independent record label Dead By Dawn, which was set up recently by Mike from Mr Shiraz.

"Leo Brazil and his Twitch" will be available on Spotify and from all Digital Download sites. Limited edition physical copies will be available soon.

The Parish in Huddersfield is hosting a launch party for both the album and the record label on Saturday 13th November and you can catch Leo Brazil and his Twitch in Leeds at the Dry Dock on Wednesday 10th November.
