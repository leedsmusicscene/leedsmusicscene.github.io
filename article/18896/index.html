Porl Whos

This is an archive of the band profile for Porl Whos.

Porl Whos are a four piece alternative band from Leeds aged 18 to 20.

With their set of fast paced all original tracks nailed, they are gaining notoriety. Their sound been likened to that of "The Cramps" and "Dinosaur Jr" with hints of "Captain Beefheart".

In their first few months alone, Porl Whos graced the stages of such venues as The Wardrobe, The Packhorse and The Key Club in Leeds..
