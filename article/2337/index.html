Uptight to release their Pink African Violets EP...

This article was published in 2004.

Uptight have almost completed their new Pink African Violets EP. As well as the title track the CD will contain the tracks Hypnotised and Blood Soaked. The band are also in the process of compiling their earlier EPs into a 14-track album. The album will include recordings from The Hitman EP, Smut Rock EP and Slightly Psycho EP, as well as a couple of songs from their very earliest demos.
