Futuresound 2007 winners revealed: six bands to appear at Leeds Festival

This article was published in 2007.

The winners of Futuresound 2007, the annual competition to find six local bands to appear at the legendary Leeds Festival, were announced this week.

Over a hundred bands initially applied for Futuresound 2007, with thirty lucky finalists making their way through the first selection process and onto the stage at the Cockpit venue in Leeds. Ten heats later and and the winners were revealed...

Overall winners of the competition, and set to appear on the Carling Stage at this year's Leeds Festival will be Wintermute.

The other five winners, all to appear on the Unsigned Stage, are New Vinyl, Rochelle, International Trust, Micky P Kerr and The Debuts.
