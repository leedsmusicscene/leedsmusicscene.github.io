Serf

This is an archive of the band profile for Serf.

KIERAN STOKES - VOCALS
JAMES ARMITAGE - GUITARS
DOMINIC BERESFORD - BASS
AARON GILES - DRUMS

SERF have been chipping away at the edges of the music industry’s consciousness for the last few years.

Since their inception in the spring of 2000 SERF have earned their stripes, wowing audiences from John ‘O Groats to Lands End (well, Glasgow to Brighton to be brutally honest!), with their blues - driven, guitar rock and their exciting and enervating live shows, building a loyal and enviable following on the way..
