Harry Lewis

This is an archive of the band profile for Harry Lewis.

Harry is a vibrant, exciting, moving performer who never fails to satisfy his audience. His love and passion of his chosen instruments, Piano and Guitar, accompany the mellow acoustic sound of his voice beautifully and he always uses this to great effect.

His style of music is very eclectic ranging from acoustic covers of well known chart songs by artists such as The Kings of Leon and The Killers, as well as classic songs by classic artists such as Oasis, Goo Goo Dolls and John Mayer.

Harry's talent for performing and love of the stage set him in good stead as a performer and he is always looking for gigs and events to perform at..
