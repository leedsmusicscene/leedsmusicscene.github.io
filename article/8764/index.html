The Weekends to launch their EP at Cockpit

This article was published in 2007.

New Leeds band The Weekends introduce themselves with a launch gig at the Cockpit, Leeds on Friday 14th September. Their limited edition CD will be available to purchase on the night.

The CD was recorded and produced by Carl Rosamond at his Billiard Room Studios, Headingley. Previous bands to have worked with Carl include Wedding Present, Kaiser Chiefs, Shed Seven, Dead Disco and many more...

The band are set to support Bez' new band Domino Bones at Dry Bar, Manchester on 8th September to prepare their set for the launch back home in Leeds the following week.

Tickets are now available to purchase via paypal at The Weekends' official site.
