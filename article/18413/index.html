Sam Airey and Post War Glamour Girls added to Deer Shed Festival line up

This article was published in 2014.

Organisers of Deer Shed Festival have confirmed a couple of Leeds acts that will be appearing at the festival in July.

The festival, which is headlined by Johnny Marr, British Sea Power, and Stornaway, will see Sam Airey and Post War Glamour Girls making the short trip across from Leeds to the North Yorkshire festival.

Sam Airey has recently announced the release of a new single.

Alongside the music the site will play host to an array of family-friendly workshops focused on science and creating, with this year's theme of Time Travel.

Deer Shed Festival 5 takes place this summer at Baldersby Park, Topcliffe over the weekend of 25th to 27th July.
