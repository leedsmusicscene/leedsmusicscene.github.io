Leeds band Dinosaur Pile-up added to NME Radio One Stage at Leeds Festival

This article was published in 2009.

Leeds Festival organisers have today revealed a number of Main Stage and NME / Radio One Stage additions to the line up for Leeds 2009, with the regional highlight being the inclusion of Leeds band Dinosaur Pile-up.

The Leeds band will open up the tent on the Friday morning.

Additions to the Main Stage include New Found Glory, Alexisonfire, Madina Lake, Sonic Boom Six, The Rakes and Fightstar. Other acts announced today for the NME / Radio One Stage include The Horrors, Little Boots, The Airborne Toxic Event, The Virgins, Manchester Orchestra, Dananananaykroyd, You Me At Six, Patrick Wolf, Metric, Spinnerette, Delphic, Crystal Castles, Passion Pit, Metronomy, Frank Turner, Lethal Bizzle, In Case Of Fire, and Broadway Calls.
