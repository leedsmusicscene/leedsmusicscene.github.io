iLiKeTRAiNS to release "A Rook House For Bobby" in October...

This article was published in 2005.

iLiKETRAiNS have announced this week that their second single – the follow up to the acclaimed "BEFORETHECURTAiNSCLOSE" - will be released on October 17th on the new record label Kids. The single – titled "A Rook House For Bobby" - will be available on 7" picture disc vinyl (restricted to just 500 copies) and as a download.

To coincide with the release, iLiKeTRAiNS will head out on a UK tour throughout October playing at Leeds' Brudenell Social Club with Redjetson on Monday 24th.
