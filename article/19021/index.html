Leeds noise-rockers, Himself, feature in Rock Sound and The Radio One Rock Show

This article was published in 2015.

Leeds perennial rock-underdogs, Himself, have enjoyed an exciting start to 2015...

Last month they were featured in national music publication, Rock Sound, as well as having their track, The Cursed Song, on the magazine's cover CD.

Last weekend the band were invited by Radio One DJ, Daniel P Carter, to record a session at the world-famous Maida Vale studios. There they recorded four tracks which went out on the Radio One Rock Show on Sunday night with Carter proclaiming it one of the best sessions the programme has had.

The band play a hometown show at The Fox & Newt on Saturday 30th May alongside Bristol's Iran Iran and Leeds' Tall Talker.
