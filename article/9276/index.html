The Pigeon Detectives announce the release of their second album

This article was published in 2008.

The Pigeon Detectives have today announced the release of their second album, the follow-up to their 2007 debut ‘Wait For Me’.

The band release ‘Emergency’ on May 26th on the Leeds-based record label Dance To The Radio.

The album was recorded at Monnow Valley studios in Wales with producer Stephen Street (Blur / The Smiths) and mixed by Cenzo Townshend (who also worked on Wait For Me).

The album will be preceded by a brand new single. 'This Is An Emergency' will be released on May 12th.

The Pigeon Detectives head out on a UK tour in May, with two back-to-back dates at Leeds’ Millennium Square (both dates sold out). Tickets still remain for other dates, check See Tickets for the full availability.
