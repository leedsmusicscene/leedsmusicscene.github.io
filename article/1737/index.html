Tacoma

This is an archive of the band profile for Tacoma.

Tacoma are a local four piece made up of

Kenny Harkis:-guitarist, songwriter
Marc Britton:- Vocals, Bass
Jonny Clarke:- Drums
Andy Hollings :- Keys

Over the last 12 months things have started to pick up for Tacoma. With serious interest from all over Europe and gigs all over the UK, this band have been described as the "New school of Britpop". Their live performances have been said to have "an air of big stage Glastonbury", and the latest CD as "a cut above the rest"..
