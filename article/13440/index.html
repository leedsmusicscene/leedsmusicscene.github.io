Chickenhawk's new album 'Modern Bodies' to be released on Nov 1st

This article was published in 2010.

On November 1st Chickenhawk will release new album 'Modern Bodies' through Brew Records

The new album includes tracks from their limited edition self-titled album, the A. Or Not? EP and the track 'Scorpieau'.

The album has had a variety of guitar and vocal re-workings and has been mixed by Kevin Vanbergen (80's Matchbox B Line Disaster, The Pixies, Gallows) and mastered by Dick Beetham at 360 Mastering (Black Sabbath, Jeff Beck).

The full tracklisting is as follows:
