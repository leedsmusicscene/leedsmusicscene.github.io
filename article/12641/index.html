Quack Quack to release their debut album - 'Slow As An Eyeball' - in May

This article was published in 2010.

'Slow As An Eyeball' is the d√©but album from Yorkshire-based instrumental trio Quack Quack and will be released on the Cuckundoo Records label on 10th May 2010.

The band worked with Spectrum's Richard Formby (Wild Beasts, Hood and Mogwai) at his predominantly analogue studio in Leeds and the album was recorded over a three-year period. Recording was straight to tape, with very little in the way of post-production, as Quack Quack aimed to capture the live sound and vibrant energy that dazzled crowds at gigs with the likes of Deerhoof, Jeffrey Lewis and Scout Niblett.
