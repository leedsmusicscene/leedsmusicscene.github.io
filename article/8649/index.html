Robert J Langley

This is an archive of the band profile for Robert J Langley.

Rob has been playing around Leeds for several years as a member of Hyde Park Times, more recently with Feluka and on his own, playing such disparate styles as pop, folk, soul, rock, jazz, funk, world, ska, reggae, psychedelia, avant garde and pretty much anything else you'd care to mention.

Rob is a self-taught multi-instrumentalist who plays guitars, bass, keys, drums, mandolin, appalachian dulcimer, harmonica and musical saw. He has extensive performing experience on stages of all shapes and sizes from back room of the Chemic to the Glastonbruy Jazz World stage.

Rob is currently looking to branch out and expand his repertoire as far as possible and would be greatly interested in backing or collaborating with musicians of any description in any situation..
