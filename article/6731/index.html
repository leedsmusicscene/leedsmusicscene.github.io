Corinne Bailey Rae releases "Trouble Sleeping" on May 29th

This article was published in 2006.

"Trouble Sleeping" is the new single from Leeds’ Corinne Bailey Rae. Released on 29th May the single is available on 2xCD and 7” formats. The b-sides include a cover of “Munich” by Editors which was performed on Jo Whiley's Live Lounge on Radio 1.

The second CD includes an additional two live tracks (“Till It Happens To You” and “Breathless”) and comes enhanced with the new video.

Pre-order the single now for just £1.99 (free P&P).
