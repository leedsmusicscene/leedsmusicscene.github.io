Bradford Music Week 2008 to take place in May

This article was published in 2008.

Bradford Music Week will take place in and around central Bradford between Monday 26th and Saturday 31st May 2008. The event will feature seminars, exhibitions, workshops, special events and activities and plenty of gigs! There will be concerts from national breaking bands and also importantly opportunities for local artists to play in everything from small acoustic performances to full blown gigs at various venues in and around the city centre.

Full details have yet to be revealed, so keep checking here or the Bradford Music Week 2008 website.
