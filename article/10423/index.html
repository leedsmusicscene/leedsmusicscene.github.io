FOG : You'll Know Everything We Play!

I set up the covers band 4 years ago & after several early personnel changes we have been settled with the same 4-piece for the past 2 years:

BAND LINE-UP: Chris Berry Lead Vocalist & Rhythm Guitar
Andy Myers Vocalist & Lead Guitar
Pete Goacher Bass
Mick Clark Drums

We play pubs & clubs throughout Yorkshire - including Leeds, Shadwell, Scholes,
Thorner, Otley, Doncaster, York, Beverley, Long Marston, Whitley,
Ackworth, East Keswick, Selby
We are also regularly booked for weddings, parties & corporate events

SET LIST INCLUDES: Everything from 60s to Recent Hits
From Brown Eyed Girl to Valerie - Beatles, Stones, Who, Kinks
REM, U2, Eurythmics, Jam - Zutons, Kings of Leon, Stereophonics,
Razorlight, Scissor Sisters, Kaisers

ALREADY 45 gigs booked for 2009 - we may not be huge but if you like good music that you've heard of played & sung well & live then we're the band for you!

WE ALSO PRESENT .... CMC ... CLASSIC MUSIC COVERS ... an electro acoustic version of FOG for smaller venues - without drummer, but with female singer CHARLOTTE HESP.
