YSN to release More on Wrath Records...

This article was published in 2005.

Following their recent signing to independent label Wrath Records, YSN have announced that their first release for the label will be a brand new version of the track 'More', recorded during the band's recent sessions in London. The single will be available from Monday 13th February on strictly limited 7" as well as digital download via Karma Download.

To accompany this release, YSN will embark on their first UK tour. The band's Club DeccaDance will travel the length and breadth of the land, making a special stop for a Victorian bordello-style launch party at The Faversham in Leeds on Saturday 11th February. Presented by Bad Sneakers, this night promises to be a theatrical extravaganza: replete with live dancing girls and other suitably bawdy performers.
