Fox & Keys

This is an archive of the band profile for Fox & Keys.

Fox & Keys are a piano and vocal duo that perform at hotels, wedding receptions and private functions throughout the UK. The duo have a large repertoire including popular music, jazz, soul and motown. A talented partnership, with unique soulful vocals alongside evocative arrangements and impressive piano skills, Fox & Keys will bring you the perfect atmosphere for a range of functions, from wedding receptions and corporate events to sophisticated dinner parties. The band perform as a duo but can also be booked as a trio, quartet with vocals or instrumentalists. For more information, please contact the band via this site or the myspace..
