Sam Airey returns with a new single

This article was published in 2014.

Sam Airey has announced the release of a new single titled "Station Approach", which will be available on 9th June 2014.

The single is the first to be taken from his debut album, and will be released through the Hide & Seek Records label. It was produced & mixed by Whiskas and mastered by Tom Woodhead.

"Station Approach" is about capturing the spirit of youth and holding on to it while you still can. With crashing guitars, atmospheric drums and an explosive finale, it shows a greater scope than heard previously on Sam's records.

The single is backed with "Stars", which lays Sam's voice bare, enhanced only by the gentle rocking guitar line.

As well as being available digitally, the single is being released on limited 7" vinyl. It is available to pre-order now at samaireymusic.bandcamp.com.



Sam Airey plays Leeds Town Hall today (Saturday 3rd May) as part of Live At Leeds 2014, and has also been added to the Deer Shed Festival line up this July.

A launch gig for the single will take place at Brudenell Social Club on 20th June.
