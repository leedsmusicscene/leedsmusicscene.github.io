Initial line up for Moor Music Festival announced

This article was published in 2010.

Moor Music Festival has this week made their first line up announcement for the 2010 event.The festival takes place at Heslaker Farm, Skipton between 12th-15th August 2010 and .

The organisers have revealed that the Leeds-based collective Ketoloco will be hosting the Moor Social bar on Friday 13th August, playing alongside Freerange Records head honcho, Jimpster, BacktoBasics resident Buckley plus Filth resident and one to watch for 2010, Luke Pompey.

On the Saturday, the Moor Social bar will be hosted by York's longest running underground House night, Freakin. Spinning alongside the Freakin residents will be Luke Unabomber, co-founder of Manchester's legendary Electric Chair parties, and Sheffield's Chris Duckenfield.

The Homespun Stage will be making its presence felt this year with one of the best Dubstep and Drum and Bass line ups the area has ever seen.

Friday on the Homespun Stage will be hosted by Herbal Mafia and will be seeing Dubstep's hottest property Joker take to the decks. Supporting him will be Emalkay who is one of the most exciting talents to emerge on the scene in recent times.

Saturday is hosted by Base and Tropical, and will see two of the drum and bass scenes biggest acts smash the dancefloor: Subfocus and Dj Friction, both with vocal support from MCID.

Sunday is hosted by High Pressure Sound system and will be a day of easy vibes with a blend of roots, dub, ska and hip hop.

The line ups for the Earl Hickey stage and Green Room line ups will be released very soon. Last year the stages saw artists such as King Creosote, Wild Beasts, Animat, Thomas Truax and Rothko so expect an exciting line up for Moor Music Festival 2010.
