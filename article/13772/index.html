Leeds Festival 2011 tickets to go on sale on 1st December

This article was published in 2010.

It has been reported [read more] that mobile phone company Vodafone is set to offer all of its subscribers exclusive access to buy pre-sale tickets for Leeds 2011.

Vodafone VIP has revealed to its mailing list that it will be selling its allocation of tickets from Wednesday 1st December and the deal is set to extend the existing partnership between Vodafone and Leeds organisers Festival Republic.

Tickets are priced at Â£180 for a weekend pass. These tickets include camping.

If you are not an existing Vodafone subscriber you can sign up at Vodafone VIP.

Leeds Festival tickets will be available exclusively to Vodafone VIP customers for 48 hours. At 9am on Friday 3rd December they will go on general sale from the official ticket agencies See Tickets and Ticketmaster.

As per normal, the main public release of Leeds 2011 tickets is expected to be at the end of March next year to coincide with the official line up announcement.
