Mr Dogg

This is an archive of the band profile for Mr Dogg.

Started in 2000
Played Leeds Festival 2001 thanks to the Futuresound competition
Headliners at Green Head Park All-Dayer festival for Huddersfield City Council
2nd place in the 2002 HiFi Club Battle of the Bands (Leeds)
Invited to headline Motorcross Freestyle Competition 2003 at Shefield HallamFM Arena.
