Luna Kings EP out at the end of January

This article was published in 2015.

Exciting news....

Luna Kings E.P "Have we met before?" will be released at the end of January on a limited 100 CD run.

This includes 5 tracks that the band have been working on to bring to you.

You can catch Luna Kings at the end of January playing at The Packhorse on Saturday, 31st January playing at End of The Trail Records Record Label Showcase.

Doors 7pm, £5 OTD or contact one of the bands to be added to the £4 guest list.
