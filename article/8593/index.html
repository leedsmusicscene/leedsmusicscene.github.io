Glissando to release 'Loves Are Like Empires' on July 2nd

This article was published in 2007.

On July 2nd, Gizeh Recordings' sister label Loom will release Leeds duo Glissando's longest release to date: 'Loves Are Like Empires'.

This release brings a few loose ends together, and is a collection of the band's work to date as the duo prepare for their debut release-proper on Gizeh later in the year.

Glissando play live at the Packhorse on July 14th, with support from Her Name is Calla.
