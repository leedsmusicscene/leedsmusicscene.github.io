NARCS to release double A-Side on April 1st

This article was published in 2014.

The final single taken from NARCS' debut album 'Two Birds, One Stone Later' will be the double A-side 'Coast To Coast' and 'Souvenir', which will be released on April 1st through Clue Records.

'Coast To Coast' is the swansong of an old couple who refuse to face modern reality. It bursts to life within seconds with the whole band crashing in around a soaring guitar riff, while 'Souvenir' is a visceral, rasping, in-your-face piece.

The double A-side is backed by 3 live session bonus tracks, recorded at Leeds College Of Music with Reckless Curiosity late in 2013. These bonus tracks are 'Sandchild', 'Collisions' and 'Tall Grass'.



NARCS will play live at The Library on Friday 4th April.
