SUPER Friendz Presents THIS MUST BE THE PLACE on Monday 30th May

This article was published in 2016.

A brand new event bringing together music, film, art, food and drink is taking place on Bank Holiday Monday 30th May 2016. You can find THIS MUST BE THE PLACE under the collective roofs of some of our newest and most popular venues, Belgrave Music Hall, Headrow House and Live Art Bistro.

With a fantastic line up featuring The Wytches, Tom Vek, The Japanese House, Kero Kero Bonito, Joanna Gruesome and Bill Ryder-Jones. This is really something you really do not want to miss!

Other bands featuring throughout the day are:

Dilly Dally / Diet Cig / Trust Fund / Weirds / Julien Baker / Cowtown / Pixx Let's Eat Grandma/ Harkin / Julia Jacklin / Lail Arad / Two White Cranes / The Orielles / Chest Pains / Dirtygirl / Oh Peas + more to be announced.

The event also features art from I Am Collective, Cinema from Dawson's Arthouse and street food from the award winning Dough Boys and Patty Smiths.
