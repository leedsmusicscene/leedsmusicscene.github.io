The Monk

This is an archive of the band profile for The Monk.

The Monk are a bald headed indie rock outfit from West Yorkshire, based in Huddersfield, Leeds and Bradford.

Formed from the ashes of Vulgar Dolls when Cam left to join the excellent Edward Molby. Joined on drums by Dave's brother Smirk, Dave moves to bass and vocals. If you like bone headed lyrics, loud melodic guitar riffs and going to the pub for a few drinks then this is the band for you..
