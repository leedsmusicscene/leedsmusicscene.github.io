Nonpareil

This is an archive of the band profile for Nonpareil.

Nonpareil is a Leeds based progressive rock band. Simon is the main vocalist and guitarist, Logan is on bass and backing vocals with Marc on drums. The music deals with dynamics as a growing progression. Through structure and context the music aids the listener to peer into new found emotional discord and harmony, from beautiful heartfelt melodic to the raw menacing discomfort. We have recently enjoys producing our own 4 track CD "Changing Tides". Nonpareil: the word originates from France, translating 'not equal' or an entity that has no parallel..
