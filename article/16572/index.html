Square Figures

This is an archive of the band profile for Square Figures.

Jake Alder (Guitar/Vox)
Ollie Grant (Drums/Vox)

Square Figures came together early 2012. Jake (Guitar/Vocals) and Ollie (Drums/Vocals) met at Leeds College of Music, miles away from their southern hometowns. They were united by a love of experimental and alternative music with influences including bands such as One Day as a Lion, Mars Volta, The Dead Weather, Deerhoof and Jimi Hendrix. They found their own versatile sound, an eclectic mix of genres with varied tempos, rhythms and grooves; itchy guitars, agitated drums, catchy choruses and vocal melodies, sewn together into a weird and wonderful fence of sound..
