The Scaramanga Six to release 'Walking Through Houses' on 19th May

This article was published in 2008.

The Scaramanga Six have announced the release of a new single on 19th May. Available on the Wrath Records label, the 5-track CD is headed by live favourite ‘Walking Through Houses’ and includes ‘I Can See a Murder’, ‘Last Roll Of The Dice (live session version)’, ‘Vesuvius (Live session version)’, and ‘Walking Through Houses (live session version)’. You can also get your hands on the single on 7” vinyl and Digital Download formats.
