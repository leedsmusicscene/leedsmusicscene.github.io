Shaheen

This is an archive of the band profile for Shaheen.

Mixing her talents as soulful singer/songwriter and conscious rapper/poet, Shaheen was first heard with Leeds based nu-jazz band Today's Mathematics. Played on Radio 1, such singles as 'Butterfly' were acclaimed by such gurus as Trevor Nelson, leading to later collaborations with Tru Thought's Nostalgia 77. Produced by Homecut's Testament, 'Perfume' was released in 2007 by First Word Records, and now heading solo, Shaheen's stunning harmonies and melodies are to be showcased in 2009, and is the new British talent to watch and listen out for....
