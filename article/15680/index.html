Pulled Apart by Horses to release a new single 'V.E.N.O.M'

This article was published in 2011.

Pulled Apart by Horses will release a new single on Monday 14th November.

The single, titled 'V.E.N.O.M', will be available as a Digital Download from the usual online download stores.

The band will also be launching a very limited run of 7" vinyl copies, which will come backed with two new tracks 'Motivation' and 'PWR'.

This physical format of 'V.E.N.O.M' will be available to buy on 16th January 2012, with the band's next album 'Toughlove' released one week later on 23rd January.

The album was recorded in Wales in August with producer Gil Norton (Foo Fighters/Pixies).

The Leeds band will head out on a UK tour in February to coincide with the single and album releases. You can buy Pulled Apart by Horses UK tour tickets at See Tickets.
