Full line up for Slam Dunk Festival 2015 revealed

This article was published in 2015.

Just two weeks after announcing more bands for Slam Dunk Festival 2015, event organisers have today confirmed the full line up.

Slam Dunk will take over Leeds City Centre on Saturday, 23rd May 2015 and with a massive increase in capacity the event now spans stages around Millennium Square, O2 Academy and Leeds Beckett University.

Today's announcement includes Architects, who will be playing a UK festival exclusive. From front man Sam Carter: 'It's going to be one to remember for us and the crowd'

Joining them will be legendary Californian post-hardcore band Finch, who are currently on the road supporting the excellent album 'Back To Oblivion'.

Also added to the bill today are We Are The Ocean, Set It Off, As It Is, Cartel, Roam, and Moose Blood, plus many others.

The full line up for Slam Dunk Festival 2015 is:
