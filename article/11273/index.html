Moody Gowns to release debut EP 'Snakes & Horses' on 5th October

This article was published in 2009.

Moody Gowns are to release their debut 4-track E.P. on Monday 5th October on new label Odd Rod Records.

Recorded and produced by the band, ‘Snakes & Horses’ includes ‘Just Another Body in the Snakepit’, ‘Misery Guts’, ‘Drink Tea All Day’ and ‘Snakestep’, a bonus dub-step remix from bassist Liam Kane, better known as Grubby Gnome.

Snakes & Horses will be available to buy on Monday from independent stores across the country including Leeds stores Crash Records and Jumbo Records. It will also be made available to buy online from iTunes and the band’s online shop.

Moody Gowns will be playing their next gig at FORM @ The Elbow Rooms on October 16th supporting Kid ID.
