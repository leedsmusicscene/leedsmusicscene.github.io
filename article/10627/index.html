Ingi & Jamie

This is an archive of the band profile for Ingi & Jamie.

We're Ingi & Jamie, two young musicians from sunny Yorkshire having fun playing music. It all started one day when Jamie broke his finger and was forced to surrender his solo acoustic gig to Ingi, another solo acoustic guitarist. However, when the doctor gave the all-clear earlier than expected, Jamie asked if he could have a piece of the action, so he helped Ingi play to a fantastic crowd of Primrose Pub people. The gig went so well that we decided to do it again, and again, and, well, here we are! So come on down to one of our gigs and we promise you won't regret it! Thanks for reading! x.
