The Maccabees release 'Spit It Out' ahead of their return to Leeds

This article was published in 2015.

The Maccabees have received well-earned Critical acclaim for their fourth album 'Marks To Prove It', released on 31 July 2015, with many claiming it is their best record yet.

Today, the band reveal the third video, from their Joseph Connor directed trilogy. Taking the viewer on a poignant trip of the now destroyed David Bratby murals in the underpass of Elephant and Castle roundabout, the visuals provide the perfect accompaniment to the band's current single and live favourite 'Spit It Out'.

The Maccabees are currently touring America and will return to the UK, playing Leeds O2 Academy on 25th November, before heading out to Australia and finishing early next year in Europe.
