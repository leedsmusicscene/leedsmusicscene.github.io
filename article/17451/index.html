Heart-Ships premiere video for 'Five Forks of Lightning'

This article was published in 2013.

After strong support from Amazing Radio and XFM, Heart-Ships have revealed the fantastic animated video for 'Five Forks of Lightning'.



Featuring a fine blend of hand drawn animation and artistic flares of CGI, the video is set in a strange world, with an Iron Giant character chasing a much smaller, more agile foe, with a rather surprising conclusion.

The band announced their debut EP release last month and the track is taken from it. 'EP 1' is scheduled for release on 29th April 2013.

Heart-Ships will play live as part of the Live At Leeds weekend on Saturday 4th May. The band have also announced they are part of Manchester's now infamous Record Store Day day party on 20th April.
