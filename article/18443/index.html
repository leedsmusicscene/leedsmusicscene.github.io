Four more bands join Slam Dunk Festival line up

This article was published in 2014.

Slam Dunk Festival has this week confirmed the addition of 4 more artists to the annual 3 day festival, which is now into its 9th year.

Joining 2014 exclusive headliners All American Rejects will be much-loved Indiana 4 piece The Ataris, London ska punkers The Skints and Cambridgeshire pop rockers The First.

Across on the acoustic stage is Chas Palmer Williams from Lightyear.

The 7 stage event at Leeds University uses a combination of indoor and outdoor stages across urban sites to create a totally unique festival experience.
