Brand new festival to hit Leeds this weekend

This article was published in 2014.

Taking over the whole of the Brudenell Social Club on Bank Holiday Monday 26th May 2014, the inaugural GOLD SOUNDS will host 20 bands over multiple stages as well as its own market place with stalls hosting record labels, screen prints, artists, books, magazines, merch, food and more.

GOLD SOUNDS is a celebration of all things noisy, fuzzy, slack, punk and just all around awesome. Headliners are CLOUD NOTHINGS, who will be supported by the vicious punk of Canadians WHITE LUNG, local slacker favourites SKY LARKIN, fuzzed out garage pop from PINS, noise pop from JOANNA GRUESOME and some brilliant 90's inspired melodies from the likes of PAWS, MENACE BEACH, EUGENE QUELL and POLTERGHOST, upcoming NME favourites KAGOULE, LOOM and THEO VERNEY as well as West Yorkshire's finest, POST WAR GLAMOUR GIRLS, IMP, THE SPILLS and many more.

Stalls will be hosted by the UKPA - bringing an exhibition of the best screenprinting from all around the world, local literary tastemakers COLOURS MAY VARY as well as food from the brand new, and hella tasty TACO WALL.
