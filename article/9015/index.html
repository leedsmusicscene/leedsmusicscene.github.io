Oporto to host charity acoustic event

This article was published in 2007.

Fun-lovin’ music bar Oporto are helping spread some Christmas cheer by hosting a live acoustic evening to help raise money for Great Ormond Street Hospital Children’s Charity.

Taking place on Sunday 9th December, the evening will see a whole host of acoustic acts including sets from soul singer Amy Stead and from the mesmerising talent of Tom Hunt, along with the announcement of another two special acts still to be confirmed...

Paul Lane, manager of Oporto says, “This is the first charity acoustic night we’ve held for Great Ormond Street Hospital Children’s Charity but we’re really looking forward to it. We love putting on live music nights, especially when it involves local musicians and the response is always fantastic. I just hope we manage to raise lots of money for the GOSH Children’s Charity.”

Starting at 7.30pm, entry is only £4, all of which will go to the Great Ormond Street Hospital Children’s Charity.
