Full line up revealed for Long Division Festival

This article was published in 2011.

The full line up for the inaugural Long Division Festival have been revealed, with bands now confirmed for the three-day multi-venue event.

The Long Division Festival takes place in Wakefield from Friday 10th June to Sunday 12th June.

The event is organised by indie fanzine Rhubarb Bomb and Wakefield music venue The Hop.

The opening night (Friday) will take place at The Hop. The event is free entry, and will be headlined by Brighton's Shrag.

Saturday's festivities take place across six venues in the city. The venues taking part are Mustangs, The Hop, Henry Boons, the Town Hall, Graziers, and the Cathedral.
