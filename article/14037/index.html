ZX Story

This is an archive of the band profile for ZX Story.

once upon a time in the cloud fortress paul t was having one of his nostalgia fixes playing treasure island dizzy on his trusty sinclair zx spectrum.

after finding that elusive 30th coin behind a bush on his not elusive enough 30th birthday the music had well and truly become stuck in his head and was not going to leave.

this gave him an idea...

he decided to try and recreate something similar but add in his liking to punk, metal and rock and roll as well.

a couple of hours later some basic backing tracks were created, but now paul t had a problem... he was a bit poo when it comes to playing guitar...

so he contacted his long time friend who also has a similar liking for retro video gaming and therefore lena was recruited to add her mad guitar skills and also teach him to play better...

after a couple of years of pissing about, changing minds, laziness on paul t's behalf, holidays, a man in a gorilla suit, the transformers/batman movies coming out and dead rising 2 followed by weekly noise making in a rehearsal studio they are now ready to unleash their noise upon you!

now it is the beginning of a fantastic story! let us make a journey to the cave of stude... i mean... monsters...

join us... become a part of the story....
