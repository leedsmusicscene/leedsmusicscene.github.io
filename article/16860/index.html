Braveface to release debut single with launch gig at Escobar

This article was published in 2012.

On Saturday 18th August, new to the scene pop rockers Braveface are to release their debut single 'Jilted Navigation' on Bearfoot Records with a launch gig at the Escobar on the same date.

The gig is free in and features support from Lucky Boy Browne and The Soul Catchers and New York Tourists.

The EP comes backed with 'Wires', 'Parasite' and 'Chase The Winter'.
