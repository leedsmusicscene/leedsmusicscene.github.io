D-Railed

This is an archive of the band profile for D-Railed.

D-Railed have been on the backburner since April 09 but is now rehearsing again with a new guitarist/keyboard player. With luck we will be back playing again early in the new year with a much broader, less rockier set list so hopefully our appeal will span to a larger audiance. The band now comprises of: Jez, Lead Vocals. Chris, Rythmn Guitar.Graham, Bass and Backing Vocals. Gillan, Keyboards and Lead Guitar. Vacancy, Drums. John is our superb sound man who we use at larger gigs. We play Rock, Pop and Blues with Soul. Ideally suited for pubs, parties, weddings etc...we adjust our sets to match whatever is required (we even do a few cheesy songs!!). We are currently working towards moving into the corporate, weddings and functions market and are currently adding new numbers more suited for those type of venues.
