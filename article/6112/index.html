The Sunshine Underground release "Commercial Breakdown" on January 16th...

This article was published in 2005.

The Sunshine Underground complete a very successful year with a NYE show at the Faversham and kick off the New Year with their second single on the City Rockers record label.

Available on 7”, CD and Download formats the Leeds four-piece release "Commercial Breakdown" (backed with the excellent "The Way It Is") on Monday January 16th, with a headline gig at the Cockpit the following Friday as part of a 20-date UK tour.

A busy live schedule, including their recent show-stopper at this year's In The City, has seen the profile of the band increase during the 2005 and this latest single was picked and played by Zane Lowe as his "Hottest Record In The World".

A three track Lamacq Live session from Maida Vale will air on January 9th, the same week as another session for XFM will go out on John Kennedy’s Exposure show.

Pre-order the single now from HMV.co.uk (free P&P).
