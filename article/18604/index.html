GirlsOnDrugs win Centre Stage 2014 to book slot at Leeds Festival 2014

This article was published in 2014.

Another place on the Reading & Leeds Festival line ups was confirmed this week following the final of the Centre Stage 2014 competition.

Twelve bands competed in the Centre Stage 2014 final at the O2 Academy Leeds in Leeds on Wednesday, 23rd July and the talent on show from all the young Leeds musicians was incredibly high.

All bands put on exceptional shows, but there could only be one winner, and that accolade went to GirlsOnDrugs, a two-piece electro from Selby who were described as "deep bass synths and sultry vocals".

GirlsOnDrugs will play at the annual Leeds Festival this August.
