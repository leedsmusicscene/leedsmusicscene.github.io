Wolfe Trap

This is an archive of the band profile for Wolfe Trap.

Wolfe Trap are a three piece indie/folk band from West Yorkshire. We specialise in three part harmonies and intricate folk-style finger picking.

Our main influences come from artists such as Mumford and Sons, Simon and Garfunkel and Ben Howard.

In just six months Wolfe Trap have gone from success to success with achievements such as supporting well-known folk singer Rachel Sermanni at Unity Works in Wakefield, appearing on Made in Leeds' Alive and Gigging and being chosen by the company MAS records to be one of their unsigned artists.

We're currently working on our debut EP and writing and recording new material having just returned from our 2016 Germany tour..
