Berretta

This is an archive of the band profile for Berretta.

An enormous diversity of musical influences, from rock to electronica, pop to jazz, are brought together to create the unique sound of new band Berretta. They were formed in 2001 and have experienced a couple of line up changes, but the core remains.

Berretta are: Grace Gold (Vocals), Bobby Oven (Bass), and Joe (Guitars)

Berretta are here to play good music, and to inspire their audiences with something truly extraordinary! Check the website for more information and updates..
