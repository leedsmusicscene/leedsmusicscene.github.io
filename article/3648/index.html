The Cribs to release "Hey Scenesters!" ...

This article was published in 2005.

The Cribs release a new single on Monday, 4th April. Titled "Hey Scenesters!" the single comes on a very limited 7" format and also on CD. The band will travel to Austin, Texas for the SXSW Festival, after that you can catch them on tour with the Kaiser Chiefs and Bloc Party through April including a show at LMUSU on Wednesday, 13th April.
