Leeds Festival 2016 confirm final headliners

This article was published in 2016.

Organisers of Leeds Festivals 2016 have confirmed their final headline act - and it will be another co-headliner, with Biffy Clyro and Fall Out Boy sharing the top spot. So that's FIVE headliners for August 2016, with the new pairing joining the existing headline trio of Foals, Disclosure and the Red Hot Chili Peppers.

Melvin Benn, commented: "This year really is a big one for Reading and Leeds. Five headliners across three days - it's a first for us and the UK Festival scene. We're always looking to give Reading and Leeds fans something new and this year isn't going to disappoint. Biffy Clyro and Fall Out Boy are going to be incredible."
