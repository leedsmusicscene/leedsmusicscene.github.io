Holy State present their first music video

This article was published in 2011.

Following on from the free download earlier this month, Holy State have presented their first music video for 'Lady Magika'.

The gritty post-punk merchants formed in 2008 and offer a constant array of loud & warm dynamics. They recently joined the Brew Records roster in December and have been busy recording their debut album.

The video to 'Lady Magika' (by Robin Fuller) is a visual feast, combining different media elements to create a richly detailed, dream-like world. You can watch it below:



You can download a copy of 'Lady Magika' for free from the band's bandcamp.com page.

A full length LP (titled 'Electric Picture Palace') and UK tour is planned for April 2012.
