Winners of Futuresound 2016 announced - six bands to appear at Leeds Festival

This article was published in 2016.

Monday night saw the conclusion of the annual Futuresound competition with six winners confirmed to appear at Leeds Festival 2016.

Overall winners were Fighting Caravans, who will now play on the Festival Republic Stage this coming August Bank Holiday.

Runners up - who will each appear on the BBC Introducing Stage - were The Indigo Project, Vexxes, Dusk, Adore//Repel and Night Owls.

The winning bands were selected from 40 bands who had been battling it out at Futuresound 2016 this month.
