Bright Light Syndrome

This is an archive of the band profile for Bright Light Syndrome.

Genre

Indie Rock / Electropop

Press

''The brilliant Bright Light Syndrome!''

Alan Raw, BBC Introducing.

"Combining killer grooves with soaring vocal hooks, Bright Light Syndrome deliver a wall of sound miles ahead of their contemporaries on the scene. After reaching the final 16 bands in the 2011 Fast Track to Feis competition, the lads are now in the process of recording their debut EP due for release in the new year - though this won't be your typical collection of well crafted pop tracks; these songs have heart, soul and conviction. This, combined with influences ranging from Placebo to The Fray, results in a potent and intoxicating blend of electro-pop and rock that'll have you revisiting these tracks time and time again."

Matt Benatan, Leeds Music Scene.

"'Somewhere Out There' is a sublime piece of indie/electropop that shows off the incredible talents of BLS. Everything about this tracks screams HIT!"

Richard Wilson, Rewind Podcast.
