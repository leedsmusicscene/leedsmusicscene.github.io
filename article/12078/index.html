Moor Music Festival 2010 dates announced

This article was published in 2010.

Details have this week been released for the 6th annual Moor Music Festival, which will take place this year between Thursday 12th and Sunday 15th August 2010.

The Moor Festival 2010 will feature an extra day, and will run through until the early hours of Monday morning.

Early bird tickets are on sale now at £65, a bargain for a fun-packed weekend of camping and frivolity. These tickets will go off sale in early February and normal prices are set at £85. Buy your Moorfest 2010 ticket at the official ticket page.

The Moor Music Festival is held at Heslaker Farm, Skipton and has a capacity of 2,500.
