Quack Quack join the Cuckundoo roster and announce new shows

This article was published in 2010.

Local label Cuckundoo Records have this week announced that Yorkshire-based instrumental trio Quack Quack have joined their roster and will release their debut album - titled 'Slow As An Eyeball' - later this year.

The band have also announced some new shows.

On 27th January Quack Quack will be playing a show at The Library in Leeds with their new label mates Vessels. The band will also be appearing with So So Modern in February and as part of the British Wildlife IV Festival in March.

Tickets for the gig with Vessels can be bought now for just £4.50 from See and We Got Tickets, and for the So So Modern show for £5 from We Got Tickets.
