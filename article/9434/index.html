The Music announce the release of a new single in June

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds four-piece The Music have today announced that their next single will be released on Monday, June 9th 2008. Taken from their third album ‘Strength In Numbers’, the single (which is the title-track) will be released through the Yes Please / Polydor label.

The album will be released the week after, on June 16th 2008.

The band are currently in the middle of their ‘Four Cities’ set of dates, the concept of which involves returning to play the same cities in consecutive months, but each time returning to a bigger venue. The dates, including two dates in Leeds in April (Cockpit, 16th) and May (LMUSU, 14th), are now sold out.

The Music have also been confirmed for the Isle of Wight festival.

Finally, The Music have revealed details of a full UK tour, including a show at Leeds Town Hall on Wednesday 2nd July. Tickets are available now at See Tickets.
