Stag & Dagger Festival line up revealed

This article was published in 2009.

Organisers of the three-city Stag & Dagger Festival have this week revealed the line-up for the Leeds leg, which is scheduled to take place across a number of Leeds’ live music venues on Friday, May 22nd.

Early Bird tickets allowing access to all venues are currently still available for £11 from See Tickets. Early Bird prices are only available until Monday 13th April.

The Leeds line up is as follows:

Brudenell Social Club: King Creosote, Twilight Sad, Pictish Trail, Player Piano, Cursive, and Girls.

The Library: Micachu & the Shapes, Telepathe, Dananananaykroyd, Vivian Girls, and Wavves.

TJ’s Woodhouse Club: Cold War Kids, The Mae Shi, White Denim, Abe Vigoda, Wintersleep, and Crystal Antlers.

Packhorse: Tubelord, Phantom Band, Male Bonding, Miles Benjamin-Anthony Robinson, and Screeming 66.

Faversham: Casiokids, Delphic, Everything Everything, Youth Attack, Raised by Wolves, Mumdance, Tomb Crew, Casper C, Heads Up, Jeuce/Spill the Noise, Skull Juice, and Tom Grainger (Bad Robot).

All Stag & Dagger wristband holders will get an additional bonus on Saturday 23rd and Sunday 24th May. The town centre sees the arrival of a new pub and venue, Nation of Shopkeepers, which will feature an array of live music events, DJ nights, pub quizzes and record fairs. Wristband holders will be entitled to free entry to the party on Saturday, which features DJ sets from Lovefoxxx, Skull Juice and Casper C, and to the event on Sunday, featuring Nathan Fake w/Allez Allez.
