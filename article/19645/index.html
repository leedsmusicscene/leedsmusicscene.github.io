Sorry Escalator

This is an archive of the band profile for Sorry Escalator.

Ryan D. Welsh - Rhythm Guitar/Vocals/Space
Sean Graham - Drums
Shaun Lockwood - Lead Guitar/Sounds
Jamie Hogg - Bass Guitar

A noisy, poppy, gazey four piece based both in Middlesbrough and Leeds. Formed in 2012 as a solo project of guitarist Ryan Welsh the bands sound developed over the next four years with the additions of Sean Graham, Shaun Lockwood and Jamie Hogg, Sorry escalator released their debut EP in the summer of 2016. The band are now writing new music in order to release a full length LP. So come wander..
