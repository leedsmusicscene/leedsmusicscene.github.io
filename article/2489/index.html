Dead At 27

This is an archive of the band profile for Dead At 27.

Dead at 27 formed late in the month of December 2002. As the nights got darker sooner, and the sensible animals hibernated, four guys with separate, yet equally magnificent talents came together and played in unison, producing some of the worst sounds known to mankind. They quickly developed a sense of musical unity and wowed crowds all over the town. Well, the gigs came slowly through 2003, until the word was spread, and offers came thick and fast as they reached their first anniversary. A few new venues and some outstanding fans bring us to present day, 2004, where the band are looking to expand their repertoire and rock the audiences further afield. .
