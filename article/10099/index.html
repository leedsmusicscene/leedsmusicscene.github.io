Bradford bands and musicians offered free support through Factory Street Studios

This article was published in 2008.

In conjunction with Bradford Council and Access to Employment, “Factory St Studios” is a new creative music hub opening soon in Bradford and their team are offering musicians free services to help them take their music to another level.

Factory St’s mentoring team consists of Amy Eden, Alex Eden and Richard Ferdinando, and having access to an extensive contact portfolio of venues, promoters, designers, photographers and collaborative support the team at Factory St can give Bradford bands and musicians the direction needed towards getting the right gigs, technical advice on performances, improved song writing skills, more confidence and any other support they may need.

Note that there is a Bradford district selection process.

Contact Amy for more information on amy@factorystreet.co.uk.
