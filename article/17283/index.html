Breezersletjes

This is an archive of the band profile for Breezersletjes.

Hailing from the seaside port of Whitby, Breezersletjes relocated to Leeds where, through their shared admiration of British shoegazing and American lo-fi bands of the 80s & 90s started writing songs together in late 2010. This resulted in the release of their self-produced debut E.P. '1987' on Blush Empty Records the following summer. Having only played a handful of live shows they intend to spend the coming year doing as many gigs as possible in the hope that people like what they hear.

David Featherstone (Vocals/Guitars)
Andrew Tiltscher (Bass)
Simon Harrison (Drums).
