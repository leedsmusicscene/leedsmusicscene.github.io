Western Front, The

This is an archive of the band profile for Western Front, The.

The Western Front are a 6 piece band that blur the lines between indie, punk, rock and other forms of guitar based music. Combining the intuitive and technical guitar sound of Television and The Smiths with the honesty and intensity of the Clash and the Pistols, they are the musical equivalent of shock and awe, with their short, explosive, but always melodic songs, containing layers of guitars, militaristic drumbeats, direct lyrics and driving basslines. Rather than be content with being a poor pastiche of their musical ancestors as too many modern bands are, this band promises to surpass them with the whole world watching..
