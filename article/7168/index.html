The Sunshine Underground break into the Top 40 Singles Chart

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds-based four-piece The Sunshine Underground celebrate this week after breaking into the Top 40 Singles Chart announced yesterday. Their re-release of “Put You In Your Place” on the City Rockers label reached number 39.

The band's debut album - “Raise The Alarm” - is set for release on 28th August, with a launch party at Cargo in London on Monday 4th September.

Catch The Sunshine Underground live at Leeds Festival (Carling Stage, Saturday 26th August).
