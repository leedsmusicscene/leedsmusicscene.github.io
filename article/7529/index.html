Leeds hosts a Musicians Networking Day – "The Future of UK Music" - at Host Media Centre

This article was published in 2006.

Host Media Centre in Chapeltown, Leeds, is holding a Musicians Networking Day on Saturday November 18th. The event will run from 11.30am to 6pm at the state-of-the-art New Media centre, which is a short walk north from Leeds city centre.

To book a place at the Networking Day email nicepeople@hostleeds.com. The event is free although places are limited.

The day will bring together big names from the national and local music industry including Alan Raw and Dave Cross who will be available to give advice to artists and bands looking to make a career out of music.

There will be stalls from the Association of Independent Music, ClothCat, the Arts Council and The Princes Trust, as well as advice sessions, a ‘demo jury’ with Alan Raw and a music technology ‘studio masterclass’.

It will also be a great networking opportunity for bands and artists to meet other members of the local music scene.
