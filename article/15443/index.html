NGOD release 'Bait Head' single

This article was published in 2011.

Bradford experimental rock/pop four piece NGOD have announced the release of their latest single, 'Bait Head', via the Enjoyment Records label.

The single, which comes backed with 'Probably Not', is available to download now from bandcamp.com.

Alternatively, you can also buy 'Bait Head' on a limited edition 7" black vinyl. This format will be available from late October, although pre-orders are now being taken from the Enjoyment Records online shop.

Creating the perfect blend of melodic hooks, technical musicianship and progressive song structure, NGOD are a band whose talents are only matched by their consistency to impress. Appealing to the indie crowd and tech-rock fans alike, this double sided single sits comfortably as a cornerstone for the band and a strong example of fun yet intelligent music.

You can also purchase the single on CD directly from the band at any live show.
