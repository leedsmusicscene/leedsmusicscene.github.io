The Rosie Taylor Project to release their debut album in May 2008

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds five-piece The Rosie Taylor Project will release their debut album on Monday, 5th May 2008. The album is titled ‘This City Draws Maps’ and will be released on CD and Digital Download formats via the Leeds label Bad Sneakers Records.

The album is preceded by a single, ‘A Good Café on George Street’. Taken from the album, the single was released on Monday this week and its video is currently play-listed on MTV2’s 120 Minutes show and can be viewed online on You Tube.

The album launch party will take place on 16th April at Leeds’ Brudenell Social Club. Support comes from Tiger That Talked, Dinosaur Pile-Up and Michael Rossiter.
