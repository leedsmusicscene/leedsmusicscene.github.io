The Pigeon Detectives confirmed for Crooked Ways 2013

This article was published in 2013.

Crooked Ways 2013 has added The Pigeon Detectives to their line up for the summer in an announcement made by festival organsiers this week.

Also joining them are Fenech Soler, Yashin, The Lafontaines, China Rats, Jim Lockey & The Solemn Sun, and Middleman, who were all added to a bill already featuring The Enemy and Grandmaster Flash and many more.

Crooked Ways is an annual independent one day festival that is held in Pontefract, West Yorkshire. This year's event will take place on Saturday 1st June 2013.

Ticket prices are £35 for adults or £17.50 for under 13's from Yorkshire Gig Guide.
