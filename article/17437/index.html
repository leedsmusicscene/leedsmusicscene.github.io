The Birthday Kiss release debut 7" single

This article was published in 2013.

The d√©but single from The Birthday Kiss - titled "Can You Keep A Secret" / "Worth It" - will be released on April 8th on hazy blue 7" vinyl and digital download through LA-based Death Party Records.

The Birthday Kiss were formed in 2012 by songwriter Ben Siddall, brains behind indiepop band The Lodger who released 3 albums on Angular and Slumberland Records and also rhythm guitarist of This Many Boyfriends; and singer Sarah Williams ex of The Research, separated by the M1 between Leeds and London.

This is their first single release following their Christmas song "Sentimental Christmastime" which received some UK airtime on BBC 6Music at the end of the year.
