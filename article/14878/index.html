Work In Progress

This is an archive of the band profile for Work In Progress.

We a group of five friends who got together about 18 months ago just to see where it would go. Seems to be working out so far!

Bands covered include The Strokes, Blondie, KOL, The Hives and, wait for it, The Human League! Fun stuff that moves your feet.

We will get some music uploaded as soon as we have some decent recordings.

Hope to see you around soon..
