Wild Beasts to release a single on Leeds label Bad Sneakers Records

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds-based record label Bad Sneakers Records have this week announced the release of their third single. “Brave Bulging Buoyant Clairvoyants” by Wild Beasts will be released on 20th November, and follows recent releases on the label by The Sugars and The Acutes.

The single feature the tracks “Brave Bulging Buoyant Clairvoyants” and “The Old Dog”, and will be available on 7” vinyl, CD single and digital download, distributed by Vital UK. The video has been directed by Nick Scott, the graphic designer responsible for The Cribs single and album artwork.
