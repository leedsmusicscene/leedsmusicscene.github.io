Little Japanese Toy announce launch party for new single...

This article was published in 2003.

Little Japanese Toy release their second single Calling Disused Numbers on March 3rd through Star Harbour - Shifty Disco’s single’s club - with nationwide distribution from Universal.

To launch the single, Little Japanese Toy play Escobar in Wakefield on Wednesday 19th February with support from Watermelon Beat.

Since releasing their first single Little Boy through Sickroom GC early 2002, the band have received critical acclaim for their sparse lo-fi ambient sounds. Everett True is certainly a fan, and has interviewed the band for a forthcoming feature in the next issue of Careless Talk Costs Lives.

The evening at Escobar starts at 8pm and is £2 entry on the door. The bands will be on 9 till 11, but DJs keep the party going till 2am, for more info visit www.littlejapanesetoy.com
