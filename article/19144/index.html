Auricle EP from Those Delta Wolves out now at Jumbo Records

This article was published in 2015.

Those Delta Wolves have announced that their Auricle E.P. is out now at Jumbo Records in Leeds.

Fans of the band can also pre-order very limited edition signed copies which comes with a bonus print.

The 3 track E.P. follows brilliantly on from their last release with some some heavy riffage infused with great vocals that brings to mind Seattle grunginess, yet although it's ramped up it has a clear eye on melody and songwriting.

Tracks are 1) Hide And Seek 2) SGMC 3) Yellow King.
