Insect Guide release their new single on March 22nd

This article was published in 2010.

The new single from Insect Guide - titled 'Dark Days & Nights' - is released on limited edition CD and Digital Download formats on Monday 22nd March 2010.

'Dark Days & Nights' is released on Dead Penny Records and is taken from the forthcoming album of the same name. The band's album will be released on Squirrel Records in May 2010.
