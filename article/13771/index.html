December 12th sees the release of a new single from Middleman

This article was published in 2010.

December 12th sees the release of "Chipping Away", the infectious second single by Middleman.

The single will be released as a Digital Download through Blip Records / Universal and features a Napoleon IIIrd remix. It is taken from the band's debut album set for release in March 2011.

In November, "Chipping Away" will deservedly be the Best Of Myspace - Single Of The Week.

Leeds-based Middleman have swiftly gained a fervent live following through tight, energetic live shows. The band's debut single gained airlplay from Radio 1's Huw Stephens, was playlisted at XFM, and the band also recorded exclusive sessions for Eddy Temple Morris and John Kennedy.

The debut single by Middleman, "Its Not Over Yet", has been included on the prestigious soundtrack for the leading basketball game: NBA 2K11.
