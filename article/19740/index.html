Dusk release their debut EP 'Visions'

This article was published in 2017.

Dusk will release their debut EP 'Visions' as part of Independent Venue Week at the 360 Club, Leeds on 27th January.

After releasing their debut single 'Dali' in September, Dusk now offer up an EP that perfectly emits the dreamy and distinct sound the band possess.

Dusk is a combination of off-kilter synth sounds backed by a violent rhythm section, all encompassed by an ethereal vocal. Their entrancing set has captured the eyes of audiences up and down the UK, and led to them being invited to play at Reading and Leeds Festival 2016.
