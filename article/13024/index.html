Dinosaur Pile-up to release their debut album in September 2010

This article was published in 2010.

Leeds trio Dinosaur Pile-up are set to release their highly-anticipated début album in September 2010, with the lead single from the album set for release on 26th July.

The album is titled 'Growing Pains' and will be released via Friends Vs Records.

The single - 'Birds And Planes' - will be released on both Digital Download and limited-edition 7" vinyl formats.

The band's début single was released in 2009 and was followed by a second single, 'Traynor'. In the summer of 2009 Dinosaur Pile-up released 'The Most Powerful EP In The Universe', which featured songs such as 'Summer Hit Single', 'Beach Bug' and 'Cat Attack'.

The band will appear live at the Cockpit in Leeds on September 24th 2010.
