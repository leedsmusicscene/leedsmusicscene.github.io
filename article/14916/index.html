Zook

This is an archive of the band profile for Zook.

Zook are an original rock band from Bradford. We have been likened to a heavy Stereophonics as our style blends a bit of punk, indie and rock.

Zook have gone from strength to strength after a slight change of line up in 2009, now with the final piece of the jigsaw in place in Durm our bassist the good gigs are coming in.

Zook play most of the good venues in Bradford and will be playing further afield soon as their infectious catchy huge chorus ridden songs are heard.

With a major evening slot secured at the Alchemy Festival 2011 Zook are a band to watch!.
