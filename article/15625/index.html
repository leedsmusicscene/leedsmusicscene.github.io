The Do's

This is an archive of the band profile for The Do's.

The Do's are a duo from Wakefield,West Yorkshire.

Elliot Oldroyd (Guitar vocals)
Matthew Oleary (Drums,Vocals)

The bands debut EP "Slide" has just been released through the independent record label Philophobia Music.It was recorded at Greenmount Studios in Leeds by
Jamie Lockhart (mi mye) and Rob Slater (The Spills).and is available in Jumbo Records,Crash Records etc.... and through The Philophobia Music Bandcamp.
