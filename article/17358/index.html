Raise the Roof Festival 2013 takes place this weekend

This article was published in 2013.

Raise the Roof Festival 2013 will return to the VOX Warehouse in Leeds this weekend after a triumphant event last October.

Revellers on Saturday 23rd February can expect more live music, guerilla theatrics and costumed tomfoolery as a theme based around interstellar adventures will form a surreal backdrop to a colourful cross-section of bands and DJs.

Ska and reggae champions The Beat are set to headline the event with the 10-piece band providing a full hour-long set.

Supporting acts include explosive pop quartet Middleman, an extended DJ set from the renegade crew Dub Pistols, and a performance from the live afro-beat/hip-hop collective Nubiyan Twist.

Also on the bill are Benson, who open the show with brass-backed rock n roll reminiscent of Madness.

In between live acts there will be DJ sets encompassing roots and reggae, old school hip-hop, breaks, 2-tone, rocksteady, dub-step and plenty more.

Tickets for the event are still available from Crash and Jumbo Records via the Yorkshire Gig Guide website.
