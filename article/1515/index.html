LSK album launch party announced for The HiFi Club...

This article was published in 2003.

LSK launches his new album Outlaw (Sony) at The HiFi Club on Saturday 5th July. The Leeds UK soul/reggae singer will be appearing backed by a live band including his sister and pop diva Rhianna on backing vocals. Outlaw is a mix of reggae, ska, soul and spoken word, featuring the massively popular The Takeover as well as the DJ dancefloor favourites 70's/80's and forthcoming single Rap Starr.

LSK rescently shot a ‘La Haine’ style video for Rap Starr. The video, scripted by Leigh and filmed on the streets where he grew up, includes a cameo by The Iration Stepper Sound System.
