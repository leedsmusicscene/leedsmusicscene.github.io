Biscuithead And The Biscuit Badgers release 'Soy Milk'

This article was published in 2013.

Biscuithead And The Biscuit Badgers have announced the release of their new single 'Soy Milk' on July 7th.

For six years the Leeds band has been gathering a reputation as oddball tubadours who will leave you singing of land hermit crabs and cheese.

During that time their albums, 'Interspecies Disco' and 'The Greatest Show on Toast' have catapulted them to the edge of obscurity and now they present their new single, 'Soy Milk' which sees Sam's tuba, Bob's piano, Matty's drums and Dean's ukulele and melodious voice come together to make the unique Biscuit Badgers sound.

You can watch the video for 'Soy Milk' below.



To celebrate the release, Biscuithead And The Biscuit Badgers will play live at Fox & Newt in Leeds on Saturday July 6th.
