Beacons Festival Announce Stage Times

This article was published in 2012.

Beacons Festival 17th -19th August 2012
Funkirk Estate,
Skipton, The Stunning Yorkshire Dales.

After many months of careful planning and with less than two weeks to go Beacons Festival are pleased to announce the stage times for this year's event.

Please see below for current stage times, the Clashfinder link is also here: http://clashfinder.com/s/beacons2012/

Tickets are now very close to selling out but a small amount are available to purchase from the Beacons Festival website, Skiddle, HMV, Seetickets, Resident Advisor, Leedstickets.com, Jumbo Records and Crash Records.
