PaperPlane announce 'Rebuild' EP

This article was published in 2013.

Halifax prog-post-punk trio PaperPlane are happy to announce that their latest EP 'Rebuild' will be released on 12th August 2013.

The 5-track EP track includes the songs 'Strings Like Ribbons', 'Live Her', 'All With Rivers', 'Chariot' and 'Rose' and can be purchased online here.

To celebrate the news the band are also sharing a live session of previously unreleased new track and epic EP closer 'Rose'. The track was recorded live at X-Church session.



Recently completing a run of European headline dates in April, the band are now gathering themselves to release a video for second single 'Strings Like Ribbons' in conjunction with the Rebuild EP release on 12th August.

PaperPlane will play live at The Library in Leeds on 11th August to launch the new EP.
