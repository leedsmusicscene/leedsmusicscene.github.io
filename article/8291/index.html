Jack Afro to release their debut album in July

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band Jack Afro have this week revealed details of their début album.

Having signed a deal with Japanese label Vinyl Junkie, home of fellow Leeds band The Invention, the band's album will be titled "Once More With Feeling" and will be released in the UK on Monday, 2nd July. The band's first long player includes their 2006 single “Take Me Home”.

Jack Afro support The Pigeon Detectives in Liverpool and at Manchester Academy on 16th and 17th May respectively.
