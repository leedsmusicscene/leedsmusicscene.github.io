Ajanta to release a double A-side single in October

This article was published in 2006.

Having already shared a stage with renowned acts such as The Groundhogs, Stateless and Captain Beefheart’s Magic Band, young Leeds virtuosos Ajanta release their debut single this October: a double A-side of ‘Destroy The Voice’ and ‘Peanut Butter Sandwich’.

Released on Monday 9th October 2006 on GrooveStealer Records the single will also include a third track, “Destroy alt. (feat. Iggy)”.

To support their debut single, Ajanta are currently in the process of organising a short UK tour, which will commence towards the end of this year.
