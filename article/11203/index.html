The Medusa Snare will release their debut album on 21st September on Squirrel Records

This article was published in 2009.

The debut album from The Medusa Snare will be released on the Squirrel Records label on Monday 21st September.

The Medusa Snare began as the solo project of Adam John Miller, formerly of The Manhattan Love Suicides, and was later fleshed out to a full five-piece band.

The 10-track album - titled "Cinderella" - was recorded over the summer of 2008. It is available now on pre-order with the official release on Monday.

The Medusa Snare will be performing live at the Nation of Shopkeepers on 3rd October in a gig that doubles up as their album launch party and fellow Leeds band Wonderswan’s single launch party – so double the reason to attend. The Acutes complete the line up.
