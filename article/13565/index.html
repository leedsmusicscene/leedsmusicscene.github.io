Vessels release 'Meatman' single on November 8th

This article was published in 2010.



Vessels have announced that they will return with the enigmatically-named single 'Meatman, Piano Tuner, Prostitute' on 8th November.

'Meatman' is the first single to be taken from the band's second album 'Helioscope', which is due for release in February 2011 and is the follow-up to acclaimed debut 'White Fields and Open Devices'.

The download-only single includes the album version and radio edit of 'Meatman', alongside an experimental remix by Border Community techno producer avus.

You can download the album version of 'Meatman' at the Vessels - SoundCloud page.

The final track is live favourite 'Ornafives', an uplifting instrumental song which has been part of the band's live set for the past two years.
