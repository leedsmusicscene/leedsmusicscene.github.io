Lock Up and Dance Stage line up revealed for Leeds Festival 2007

This article was published in 2007.

Carling Weekend: Leeds Festival organisers Mean Fiddler have today revealed the line ups for the Lock Up Stage and the Dance Stage at this year's August Bank Holiday weekend music festival.

The Lock Up Stage will feature two days of bands, Friday and Saturday, whilst the Dance Stage will make its appearance to close the festival on the Sunday. The stage headliners have been revealed as New Found Glory (Friday) and Jimmy Eat World (Saturday).

They will be joined by Turbonegro, Gallows, Against Me!, The Dwarves, From Autumn To Ashes, Cancer Bats, The Casualties, Ignite, Municipal Waste, Sonic Boom Six and Ghost Of A Thousand (all Friday).

Other bands appearing on the Saturday are The Living End, Thrice, Mad Caddies, The Bronx, Capdown, Big D and The Kids Table, The Draft, Street Dogs, The King Blues, Riverboat Gamblers and The Grit.

The Dance Stage headliners are Unkle. They'll be supported by !!! (Chk Chk Chk), Peeping Tom, Digitalism, Datarock, Lethal Bizzle, Metronomy, Dan Le Sac vs Scroobius Pip, Does It Offend You, Yeah?, Crystal Castles, and The Teenagers.

More bands have been announced for the rest of the festival. Billy Talent make an appearance on Friday 24th August, and Paramore take to the Main Stage on the Sunday.

On the Radio One / NME Stage, Friday's line up now boasts Devendra Banhart and Pull Tiger Tail. The Sounds, Aiden and The Scare have been included on Saturday's line-up, and Sunday will now welcome Brakes and Dogs to the Leeds Festival.

Over on the Carling Stage, Maps and Make Model will make an appearance on Friday.

Day Tickets still remain for the Saturday, buy now from See Tickets.
