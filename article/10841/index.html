Ian Williams' "Bible Black Heart EP" is out now

This article was published in 2009.

Adventure Club Records have just recorded and released the debut record from Alt-Country songwriter Ian Williams. A review from the Press Association describes a record "which, lyrically at least, tip the Stetson to Leonard Cohen". They go on to suggest potential listeners should "musically, think Ryan Adams at his most introspective".

The entire record is available for streaming at Ian Williams' MySpace page.

The EP can be purchased from Jumbo Records, iTunes or direct from www.bibleblackheart.com.
