Knievel

This is an archive of the band profile for Knievel.

The roots of Leeds-based band Knievel lie in vintage rock and roll with classic rock inspired originals and high performance live gigs. Consisting of brothers Adam and Lee Webber, Richie Jevons and Dan Mizen.

They have been together for 2 years, gigging up and down the country and even as far as France. The music is influenced by, The Black Crowes, Led Zeppelin, The Rolling Stones and The Faces ect.... so as you can tell from the influences, the band have a 70's rock edge about them, but the delivery of the music is bang upto date. The band have a reputation amongst its growing following for fusing heart thumping rock and roll, great songs and inspiring stage presence and attitude. Knievel have been described as the best unsigned / undiscovered Live band in the North of England..
