Andrea

This is an archive of the band profile for Andrea.

I am a singer, songwriter and performer. I have my own recording studio. I have performed in UK and abroad including Pakistan where I sang to 10,000 people including their government. I performed at the Leeds Mela in 2005. My music video is being played on TV in Pakistan and my songs have been played and I have performed live on BBC Radio Leeds several times.

I have over a 3 and half octave range (up to a F (6)) and can sing anything from classical and ballads, to pop to rock, to dance. The glossy magazine 'Asian Woman' featured an article about me in their February 2006 edition. This is distributed to 1 million readers.

I have just completed my debut album, which is an eclectic mix of genres, from adult pop, ballads, light rock, classical crossover, featuring me singing in English and also featuring an English and Urdu fusion called 'Smile', a English/Hebrew fusion called 'Resonance' and a song featuring me singing 'backwards'..
