Uptight to feature on US compilation CD 'Whole Lotta Shakin'

This article was published in 2003.

Rochester, NY USA: Whole Lotta Shakin is coming to CD. Not the great rocker that Jerry Lee Lewis scored with back in 1957, but the radio show which has been airing in Rochester, New York since 1984.

Rochester's Garage-Pop Records label are now releasing a full-length compilation CD of many of the bands heard on the show, including Leeds' Uptight. The CD is scheduled for release this summer, so for more information visit the Garage-Pop Records site at www.garagepoprecords.com.

The radio show can also be heard via the internet at www.modernmusicandmore.com every Sunday starting at 8PM (GMT).
