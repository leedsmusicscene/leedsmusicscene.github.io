10,000 Things record debut EP for Domino and head out on UK tour...

This article was published in 2003.

10,000 Things have just completed the recording of their first EP for Domino Records. The EP - for release late 2003 or early 2004 - was produced by the legendary Phill Brown (worked on classic Stones, Bob Marley, Hendrix).

The band will head out on a UK tour in November alongside American band Living Things (labelmates of the Hiss and Soledad Brothers), in what is billed as the "Night of the Things" tour.

The tour takes in Leeds' Cockpit venue on November 20th, while the band are also scheduled to appear at The Wardrobe on December 11th with The Blueskins.
