The Downfall compilation and album recording news...

This article was published in 2003.

New track from The Downfall - Centre of Our Tension - will be released on a Positive Impact compilation CD. The track will also be available for a limited period as a completely free MP3 download on the band's website.

New material for the forthcoming album due for release in Summer 2004 is coming together well and the band are putting together the finishing touches to two as yet untitled new songs. Guitars/voclas Dunk Bray commented "We're really trying to push out the boudaries of our sound. I think people can expect some suprises from the new material whilst also being able to say 'Hey it's still The Downfall'".
