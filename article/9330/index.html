The Music reveal third album and third leg of their 'Four Cities' tour concept

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds band The Music have revealed that they will release their third, and as yet un-titled, album through Yes Please / Polydor in early summer 2008. The album, recorded in late 2007, was produced by Paul Hartnoll (ex Orbital) and Flood (Killers / U2).

Before the release, The Music have announced the third leg of their ‘Four Cities’ set of dates, the concept of which involves returning to play the same cities in consecutive months, but each time returning to a bigger venue.

The Music play at Leeds Metropolitan University on 14th May.
