Resonation

This is an archive of the band profile for Resonation.

Yorkshire band Resonation have been best described as a bluesy wah wah drenched funky roots rocking Reggae band.

The band have a 6 piece line up and the full debut album is due for release in early 2010 through independent label 'Planet Gecko Reckords' and available to download universally through Reggae distribution giants 'Zojak Worlwide'.

The band have played all over the UK including The Jug of Ale in Birmingham, The Trades Club in Hebden, The Brook in Southampton and most of the popular northern music venues.

They have also proven to be very popular at summer festivals having played at various events including a slot on the Glastonbury Glade stage (as an entry in their 'New Talent' competition), Birminghams Artsfest, Huddstock and WOYD (all in 2008), and the highlight of 2009 festival tour was a slot at Kendal Calling alongside The Streets and The Zutons..
