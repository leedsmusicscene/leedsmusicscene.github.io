Maggie8 release 'Charming Lady EP' this month

This article was published in 2012.

Total Confidence Records has teamed up with the folks from Leeds label Dance To The Radio to release Maggie8's new EP 'This Charming Lady' on gorgeous 12" vinyl on 30th January 2012.

The tracklisting for the EP is:

1. Charming Lady
2. Damaroo
3. 4 Whacks
4. Ghar Aah
5. Khet
