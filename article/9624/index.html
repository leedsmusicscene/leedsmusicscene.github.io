Dorien Starre

This is an archive of the band profile for Dorien Starre.

"The four piece's heavy indie sound was backed brilliantly by the lovely drum rhythm and deep bass, the swooping guitar riffs were excellent and the vocals from Leo Derevonko's very unique and very rangy voice. The up and down, hook filled, catchy indie music was very good indeed from this very talented group."
~ Stereoboard

"A tuneful, bouncy lot with a flair for the catchy and a croony stylish singer making things even better."
~ Fly Regional

"Their opener flowed from a simple guitar riff to a satisfyingly beefy chorus."
~ Sandman Magazine.
