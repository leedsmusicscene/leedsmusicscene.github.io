Rise & Fall

This is an archive of the band profile for Rise & Fall.

Mollie Targett- Vocals
Simon Shields- Lead Guitar
Tom Cullen- Drums
Alex Papuga- Rhythm Guitar
Josh Reynolds - Bass Guitar

We are a 5 piece band from Leeds who at the moment are playing our own songs, we basically have a full album now, just need to get into recording. We have a few gigs on the way aswell. We are mainly a rock and metal band, our songs have taken influences from the more rock/metal bands like Lacuna Coil and Motley Crue although we have also taken further influences from bands like Papa Roach and The Gathering..
