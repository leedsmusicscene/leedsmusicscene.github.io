Kram to release a new single in March

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds three-piece Kram will release their new single through GrooveStealer Records on Monday 12th March.  The double A-side 'Sides’ and ‘Where Were You?' will be available as a limited edition CD release, and as a downloadable single from all good online distributors.

Kram have been causing a buzz for the last couple of years, resulting in prolific slots with yourcodenameis:milo, ¡Forward, Russia! and LostAlone.  The band's last EP sold out on its day of release following a fantastic press reaction, with LMS ourselves urging fans to "go see Kram while they're still local".

To coincide with the release, KRAM are currently organising a UK tour in March and April this year. The band will play on the 18th February (Cockpit), 12th March (The Wardrobe) and 17th March (Carpe Diem).
