The Sugars to release 'Black Friday' on June 16th

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds three-piece The Sugars will release a new single on Monday, 16th June.

Released through the Bad Sneakers Records label, ‘Black Friday’ will be available on limited edition 7” coloured vinyl, CD and Digital Download formats, and is the third single to be taken from the band’s forthcoming debut album ‘The Curse Of The Sugars’, released 7th July. The single was produced by Will Jackson (The Cribs / The Pigeon Detectives) and comes backed with a cover of ‘Isle Of Sirens’, a Curtis Mayfield penned track.
