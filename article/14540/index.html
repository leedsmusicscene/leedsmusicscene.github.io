Stalking Horse to release 'Heathen Head, Howling Heart' on Too Pure

This article was published in 2011.

Stalking Horse will release 'Heathen Head, Howling Heart' via the Too Pure singles club on 9th May 2011.

The single was recorded with producer James Kenosha and will be available as a limited edition numbered heavyweight 7" vinyl.

Stalking Horse will play live on 30th April at the Leeds Metropolitan University, as part of the Live At Leeds weekend.
