Forever Cult release 'Suntrap' as free download

This article was published in 2014.

Forever Cult have released their latest track 'Suntrap' as a free download via Clue Records. Their music blends the brutal moments of 1990s Seattle grunge with the jaunty pop-minded lyrical charm of mid-00s British indie.

'Suntrap' is backed by 2 live acoustic session tracks, 'The World Turned And Left Me Dizzy (Again)' and 'I Hate Your Eyes'.



The single has been released as part of Independent Venue Week, a 6 day long series of nationwide gigs running from 28th January through to the 2nd February 2014. The Leeds gig takes place at 360 Club in The Library on Friday January 31st. Playing live alongside Forever Cult at the event are fuzz junkies Allusondrugs, Fizzler and Perfect Crimes.
