Using The Enemy

This is an archive of the band profile for Using The Enemy.

Long story short... we all eventually met at Leeds College of Music, we started playing music together early on really. Lewis was already in a band, within time the outfit would branch out we started inviting people such as Shaun to come along and play with us. Fairly soon the idea of creating an entirely new band came about, and with Shaun, Lewis, Damian, Liam and James the new group was complete. Now altogether, we are rocking out playing a variety of genres from hard-rock, to acoustic even! A churning out a good load of our own music, so far the future's looking pretty darn good..
