The Horn The Hunt release 'Raptor' on November 1st

This article was published in 2010.

The Horn The Hunt have announced the release of their 'Raptor' single on 1st November 2010. The single will be available on both 7" vinyl and Digital Download formats through the White Label Music label.

Two remixes for the single come by way of renowned producer Dean Honer (iMoster/Human League).

'Raptor' is the first single to be taken from the band's second album 'Depressur Jolie' which is set for release in 2011, also on White Label Music.

The Horn The Hunt's self-titled debut album was released independently in August of 2009.
