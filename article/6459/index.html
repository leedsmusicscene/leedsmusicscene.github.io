Stanford

This is an archive of the band profile for Stanford.

Stanford are the fresh sound which is erupting into the city’s local venues and beyond. Hidden away these five diverse talents combine to produce songs jam packed with variety and excitement. Fusing instrumental training with self-taught song writing abilities and an innate ability to write perceptive lyrics. Dense guitars meet subtle piano interludes riding effortlessly over heart wrenching vocals.

Their songs have grabbed the attention of local radio DJ’s and magazines alike and already created a buzz amongst Leeds’s most intuitive promoters and venues (Transmission, Leeds Vibrations and Panama Promotions). Cutting their musical teeth into the developing Leeds music scene they have spread into Manchester and London responding to their ever increasing fan-base..
