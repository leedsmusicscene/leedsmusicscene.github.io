Beacons Metro Launches at Headrow House

This article was published in 2015.

Beacons Metro, the intercity festival kicked off last weekend, with East India Youth and Ghost Culture opening Leeds newest venue, Headrow House.

Evolving from a weekend festival held in Skipton, to Beacons Metro is a festival that runs over 12 weeks, with 90 bands, playing more than 45 shows. The venues taking part are Headrow House, Belgrave Music Hall, Wharf Chambers and Canal Mills. Hosting a diverse range of music, from Dance to Grime, Alt Rock to Post Punk, there is something for everyone.

Tonight, 5th October, Beacons Metro continues at Headrow House with a Free Entry show, doors at 7pm for Nai Harvest with support from: Bloody Knees, Abattoir Blues and Forever Cult.
