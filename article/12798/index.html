Kath and the Mighty Menace

This is an archive of the band profile for Kath and the Mighty Menace.

In the interest of bringing explosive sound to the music loving people of Leeds, lead vocalist, Kath Edmonds of Just Kath fame and the dynamic duo of former Japanese Fighting Fish - Steve Ramsden (Bass) and Paul Crowther (Drums) are picking up their musical weapons to form Kath and the Mighty Menace; a mind blowing Jekyll and Hyde musical tussle that'll have your audio-networks tingling. This new found band format deliver a passionate and convincing rock/pop performance, each track bursting with energetic honesty and diversity. Kath and the Mighty Menace formed in the early parts of 2010; a collaboration of like minded musicians and a trio that entirely compliment each other ready to take Leeds by storm..
