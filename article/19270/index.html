The Omega Era launch their Debut EP 'Alpha' on 18th September at 360 Club

This article was published in 2015.

The Omega Era are a band that leave you in no doubt where you stand. There is enough variation in their set to span most of rocks sub genres. Offering pounding rhythm sections layered with soaring leads and anthemic vocal melodies that make you want to sing along at full volume; these are the base ingredients for The Omega Era sound.

"Rock is not dead, we want to make sure you know it" Gav Ramsay - Bass Guitar.

In ALPHA, the debut EP from The Omega Era there are no half measures. This barnstorming debut release with massive drums and pounding bass lines, sets the tone from the off and you get the notion of a band that mean business. Each track demonstrates powerful guitars and piercing riffs, that all make for a perfect wave of sound for Lead Singer, Jonny Skinner's, huge vocals to come soaring down. The gritty lyrics throughout this EP will be sure to leave its mark, ALPHA will be officially released 18th of September at the launch night at 360 Club in Leeds at the Library venue, free hard copies will be available on the night and it is also available for free download and streaming from:

www.Reverbnation.com/theomegaera
and
www.Soundcloud.com/theomegaera
