Emmerdale actor rises from the grave to star in new music video

This article was published in 2014.

Emmerdale actor Matt Healy, whose character Matthew King met a tragic end through a car windscreen, returns to the screen to star alongside local actors in a music video by award winning singer/songwriter Dom Robinson, to première at Unit One in Leeds on Monday 22nd September 2014.

Matt Healy returns to play a dark shadowy character in the music video for the song 'Growth', almost as if he had returned directly from his tragic demise. 'Growth', alongside the song 'Fawn', are the title tracks to the period drama VIII which premièred at this years' Cannes Film Festival.

Both songs are written and performed by local singer / songwriter Dom Robinson, who was the winner of the Leeds Talent Showcase 2013.

'Growth' is taken from Dom's third EP, which you can listen to below.



The videos were produced by Leeds based Rossi Pictures as part its IPM Acting Academy module. They will be premièred at Unit One on Burley Road at 7pm on Monday 22nd September 2014 at an event where Dom will perform a live set.
