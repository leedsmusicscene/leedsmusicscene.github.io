The Canolas to release debut single

This article was published in 2012.

The Canolas are set to release their debut single 'She'll Leave You Behind'.

Priced at Â£3 and available on CD or via download, 'She'll Leave You Behind' is offered with a bonus track entitled 'The End' - an atmospheric and rarely heard song in the three piece's repertoire.

Hosted by NoTitle Magazine's regular 'Under The Radar' night at Carpe Diem, the Leeds based trio will be churning out their infectious blues/rock at the single's launch this Saturday 28th April at 8pm, which is free entry and sees support from CRUYFF and JBW & THE RAKERS.
