Kaiser Chiefs release album, single and announce HMV in-store show in Leeds...

This article was published in 2005.

Kaiser Chiefs release their debut album - titled "Employment" (B-Unique) - on March 7th. The album will follow the re-release of their debut single "Oh My God" which hits the shops on February 21st.

The 12-track album includes single "I Predict A Riot" as well as live favourites "Everyday I Love You Less & Less" and "Saturday Night". You can pre-order it from Amazon.co.uk.

The single release coincides with two in-store shows at HMV Sheffield and HMV Leeds (city centre). These two gigs take place on February 21st, and the Leeds event kicks off at 5.30pm. The band have a full UK tour booked in the for the Spring, with both Leeds dates (13th and 26th April) now sold out.
