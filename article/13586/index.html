Holy State are set to release a new AA-side single

This article was published in 2010.

Holy State have announced that they will be releasing a new AA-side single to coincide with a UK arena tour with Biffy Clyro and a tour around Europe with Blood Red Shoes.

Recorded with Bratwell (Rory Atwell), formerly of Test Icicles, at the end of summer 2010 the new double A side single shows Holy State in typical ferocious and eclectic form.

The single is released on the Leeds-based Dance To The Radio label on the 22nd November, and is available on both Digital Download and CD formats.

The tracklisting is A- 'Medicine Hat' and AA- 'Sultan of Sentiment'.

You can listen to 'Medicine Hat' on the Dance To The Radio - SoundCloud page.
