Tytania release their debut album on January 29th

This article was published in 2016.

Leeds band Tytania are releasing their debut album on January 29th and are hosting a launch party to celebrate with friends, family and supporters.

For the launch band have chosen the Parnaby Tavern in Hunslet, voted best pub in the region by Yorkshire Evening Post readers, stating that no venue has ever made them feel more welcome.

The event will be free entry and feature live performances from Tytania and special guests The Fiasco and Dave Hanson.
