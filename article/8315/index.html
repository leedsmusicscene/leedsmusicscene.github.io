The Finnlys

This is an archive of the band profile for The Finnlys.

The Finnlys are a four piece band from the massive city of Leeds. Bringing an unbelievable live performance to the table, upper class recorded tracks, and a fan base to be proud of.

Taking the idea of twisting darker, more direct lyrics with beautiful guitar lines and choruses to Producer Ed Heaton of The Soundmill (leeds) and letting him influence the tracks that became "The Money Tree EP" 2009. selling out ltd 500 hard copies, and a capacity launch show at Leeds university.


"You get those beautiful riffs and harmonies, slow then fast, appealing to everyone. The only problem i have with this EP, is that it leaves you wanting more. i definitely want more."
Vibrations Magazine.

With sold out shows locally, and touring the uk, summer 09 saw The boys asked to play a stint in Holland, visiting Amsterdam & Rotterdam. Returning to play some of thier finest shows. Supporting the likes of The Rifles, The Bluetones, Backhanded compliments and Joe carnall & The Book Club.


The End of 2009 Sees the lads releasing debut Single "For Love Nor Money" (produced by Ed Heaton) with a single tour & launch show at The Cockpit..
