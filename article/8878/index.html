Authors of Malicious Code put out a fourth free release

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds based Authors of Malicious Code have released their fourth EP of 2007, as always for free download, on the band's MySpace.

The Part Four EP, compromising of tracks "99%" and "How A War Is Won", was recorded, mixed and mastered in mid-September at Pravda Studios in Headingley.

The release follows even more positive press regarding the band's efforts this year, in particular for their Part Three EP.

AOMC plan to have free limited printed copies of the Part Four EP at their forthcoming live appearances.
