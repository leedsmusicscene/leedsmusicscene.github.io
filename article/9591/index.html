Wintermute will release their debut EP on the On The Bone Records label

This article was published in 2008.

Wintermute will release their debut EP on the Leeds record label On The Bone Records.

Released on Monday, June 16th on CD and Digital Download, ‘Fun With Wizard Stencils’ is a collection of some of Wintermute’s most prolific new material and proves their status as an up and coming original guitar band in the UK.

The EP was recorded by James Kenosha (Grammatics, This Et Al) in Bridlington.

A launch party for the release will take place on June 18th at the Brudenell Social Club in Leeds, with support coming from These Monsters and Pulled Apart By Horses.
