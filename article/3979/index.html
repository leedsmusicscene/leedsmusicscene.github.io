Livestock Meeting

This is an archive of the band profile for Livestock Meeting.

Born through a love of all things cattle, a jam was a perfect accompaniment to a good glass of apple juice. With apples being as they are, we decided to peel off our Granny Smith's badges and stick them to our foreheads alongside a geriatric, obscene postman who bequeathed to us a legacy of forlorn livestock. The Faversham was also part of this apple / cattle fuelled adventure, later resulting in spontaneous gallons of tea and haircut spotting extravaganzas! Thus giving us valuable time to get our heads round our jams and turn them into songs. Songs that stick like a giant lump of Velcro against a velvet wall, songs that make you want to climb statues in the dead of night.

So what does this all mean?

Why is the earth round? Two questions which would result in a serious debate. Seriously, what does it all boil down to?

A nice tasty stock that can be used in a variety of rice and pasta dishes, with enough left over to feed the dog with ... and listen to.

Livestock Meeting are: Preech - Vocals / Djembe
Matinau - Death Bass
Nelly – Drums
Misterook – Guitar
Jeff - Zombie.
