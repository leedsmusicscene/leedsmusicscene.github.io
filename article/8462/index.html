International Trust release a new EP

This article was published in 2007.

International Trust have this week released their latest EP, "Talk of the Town". Out now through Red Lace Records, the follow up to the band's debut "Bruce Lee" can be purchased through the International Trust website.

As well as the title track, the tracklisting for the new EP includes the songs "Disneyland", "I can't believe you fell in love" and "Show Me the Money".
