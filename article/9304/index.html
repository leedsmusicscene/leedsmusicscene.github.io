Huddersfield band Kava Kava included on TV soundtrack

This article was published in 2008.

Yorkshire band Kava Kava will have three songs on the new season's soundtrack to FX TV show Dirt (featuring Friends star Courtney Cox Arquette).

The dance rock act are no strangers to US Film and TV having already been spun in Golden Globe winning series Weeds and cult Hollywood movie Boys And Girls Guide To Getting Down. Last year the band were featured in the soundtrack to John From Cincinnati (alongside Muse, Elvis Costello, Joe Strummer, U2, David Byrne, Kasabian, TV On The Radio and Bob Dylan).

The songs selected for the new season of Dirt are the single ‘Tic’, the soulful ‘Terrorists’, and the psychedelic 'NFA'.
