King No-one to launch 'Millennium EP' at Belgrave Music Hall

This article was published in 2014.

King No-one have been rather busy since they played the BBC Introducing stage at Leeds and Reading festivals last year. Their music has been played on all the Introducing stations in the UK and on BBC6Music. Their new single, Millennium, has been played on several radio stations reaching as far as the U.S, Germany and Africa.

Having recently supported Embrace, on their sell out UK 02 Academy tour, which included Shepherds Bush Empire, King No-one have been playing to huge crowds. To top it all off, the headlined a sold out, home town show at Fibbers in York.

So what is next for King No-one?

Well, the y are looking forward to a few big festival slots, such King of the Mountain Festival and Galtres festival.

But first they are releasing the new E.P Millennium. The Launch night is at the Belgrave Music Hall on the 13th of June. This is set to be a great night with support from Oliver Pinder and Field studies.

Check out Millennium which was recently featured on C4's Sunday Brunch.
