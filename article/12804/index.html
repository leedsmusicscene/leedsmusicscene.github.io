Lock Up Stage line up revealed for Leeds Festival 2010

This article was published in 2010.

The line up for the Lock Up Stage at Leeds 2010 was official revealed last night and the headliners have been announced as Bad Religion and Alkaline Trio, who will play on the Friday and Sunday respectively.

The headliners will join Guns N Roses and Blink 182 to top the bill on the official Leeds 2010 line up.

As usual the line up was announced live by Mike Davies on BBC Radio 1 and once again there are plenty of old and new bands to attract music fans. The full line up for the Lock Up Stage 2010 is as follows.

Friday 27th August: BAD RELIGION, The Get Up Kids, Cancer Bats, Zebrahead, Crime In Stereo, RX Bandits, Trash Talk, Paint It Black, Off With Their Heads, and Moral Dilemma.

Sunday 29th August: ALKALINE TRIO, Sick Of It All, Against Me!, Hatebreed, Streetlight Manifesto, Strike Anywhere, This Is Hell, Static Thought, Civet, The Skints, Crazy Arm, and Blood Or Whiskey.

The Lock Up Stage spans two days at both Reading and Leeds sites (the third day being the Dance Stage) and has always been a popular event.

If you haven't yet bought your ticket for Leeds Festival 2010 then you can still get your hands on a ticket via Viagogo, the official ticket partner of the Leeds festival.
