Forever Cult release new free download single

This article was published in 2015.

Leeds-based fuzzed-up grunge pop trio Forever Cult have today announced the release of a new single, the first of three singles the band have planned for this year.

With a solid 2014 behind them including appearances at Leeds & Reading Festival, Live At Leeds and their first UK tour, the Leeds trio are set to unleash three free downloads over the coming months.

Titled 'Winter's Glow', the first free single will be out on 30th March via Clue Records. You can listen to it below.



Forever Cult are taking their high octane raucous live show on the road again, embarking on a UK tour throughout early April.

On 5th April they will play with Allusondrugs at Warehouse 23 in Wakefield, followed by a gig in Leeds with Valours at The Key Club on 18th April.
