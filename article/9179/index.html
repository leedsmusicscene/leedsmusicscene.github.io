Shakinouts

This is an archive of the band profile for Shakinouts.

Shakinouts are a Leeds based band formed in the early part of 2007 from the ashes of two other bands. They simply took the best looking/best musicians and become the lovely Shakinouts.

Shakinouts in 2009 performed at Party in the Park; supported Shed Seven at Leeds Academy; headlined the main room at the Cockpit 3 times; released a digital single; were featured on TV twice; played live in session on a number of radio shows; and recorded a brand new EP set for release in 2010.

Shakinouts, Jamie, Matt, Daimo and Alex, are currently writing new material in a little hole together somewhere in Leeds.
