Wild Beasts release a new single on November 14th

This article was published in 2011.

Wild Beasts are to release 'Reach a Bit Further' as the next single from their widely acclaimed top twenty album, 'Smother'.

The single will be available on 14th November via digital download and on limited edition coloured 12" vinyl, collecting together the b-sides from the previous singles on Smother, some of which were only available on limited or digital only formats, as well as a new track, 'Thankless Thing', recorded with Richard Formby at the beginning of the Smother sessions.

The tracklisting is as follows:

A1 Reach A Bit Further
A2 Smother
B1 Catherine Wheel
B2 Thankless Thing

You can pre-order the 12" now from the Domino Records online store.
