Leeds bands to feature on a Silver Rocket compilation CD...

This article was published in 2004.

Leeds bands The Scaramanga Six and les Flames! will feature on a compilation by London's Silver Rocket Club, a monthly night at The Garage. The compilation, a celebration of their 50th monthly event, will feature 50 songs by 50 of the bands played and cunningly is to be called "SR50".

The Scaramanga Six have contributed "The Electricity Bill" and les Flames! have supplied the song "Une, Deux, Trois, Quatre...".
