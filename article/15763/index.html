The Eight Track Heroes

This is an archive of the band profile for The Eight Track Heroes.

The Eight Track Heroes are:

Lead guitar - Josh Benson
Bass guitar - Graham Klassen
Guitar - Matt Markinson
Drums & Vocals - Daz Tomlinson
Vocals - Shaz Byrne.

We can bring a support act if required.

A feel good covers band that spans 40 years of music, from Otis Reading and Chuck Berry to Florence and the Machine, The Killers and Kanye West!

Capable of entertaining any audience and proud to have a 100%re-booking record makes this band our band a must for your venue or festival.

Type of music we play can change according to the venue. Chilled acoustics to hard metal.

As the great Status Quo said... 'whatever you want' !.
