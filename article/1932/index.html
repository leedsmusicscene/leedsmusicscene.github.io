Decoration

This is an archive of the band profile for Decoration.

John Peel favourites with a sound a that shares a cramped box room with The Buzzcocks and The Smiths Decoration's recent recordings have recieved airplay at both home (including a Peel Sesion) and abroard - (Ireland, Germany, BBC World Service... ) Reviews pending in the US with magazines Magnet and The Big Takeover.

After an all-guns-blazing slot at Fierce Panda's Club Fandango and a headline at Manchester's Life Cafe, Bolton's finest indie exports, Decoration, are set to headline their next Fierce Panda night before heading back up the M1. Previous gigs at London's 93 Feet East and the ICA saw the band perform two highly charged sets to a growing fan base, including Radio legend John Peel, who aired the band's debut session earlier in the year.
