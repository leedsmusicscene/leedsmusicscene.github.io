Vessels announce new album and release new single

This article was published in 2014.

Vessels have this week announced details of a new album, 'Dilate', which will be released in March 2015 and has been described as "a brave and brilliant new sound, masterfully fusing the widescreen builds and elegant breakdowns of their previous work with altogether more physical electronic rhythms".

The band have also released a new single 'On Monos', which is available now. Featuring the crystalline vocals of fellow Leeds-based singer Snow Fox (Naomi Cousins), you can find the track from bandcamp below.



Pre-orders of the album from bandcamp will come with an instant download of the album version of 'On Monos'.

Below you can watch the mesmerising video for 'On Monos' that was created using footage produced by Professor Joseph Hyde from a hacked 1980s MB Vectrex games console, edited by Jacek Zmarz.
