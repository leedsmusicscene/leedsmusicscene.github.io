Weave

This is an archive of the band profile for Weave.

Weave were formed in 2013 by Jo Nettleton (Vox), Tim Hardcastle (mandolin, percussion), Paul 'Gibby' Giibson (bass) - all are former members of the group 'The Little Darlings' - Charles Hardy (acoustic guitar), formerly of 'Siamese Twist', and Christine Avis, who is cellist with Huddersfield based cinematic art rock band 'Stems'.

Weave write their own songs and deliver them in both acoustic and semi-acoustic style with melodic vocals from Jo Nettleton.

Their recently released EP 'Skimming Stones' produced by Leeds based musician James Grunwell and mastered by Tim Thomas Music Leeds is now available..
