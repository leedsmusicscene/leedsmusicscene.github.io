The Sunshine Underground release new single and video

This article was published in 2014.

The Sunshine Underground has this week revealed a new music video for "Start", which was filmed in Spain in the summer.



Fans can purchase the radio edit, plus brand new B-side "All Your Luck", on iTunes.

The Sunshine Underground begin their UK tour this week and will play live in Leeds on October 10th. Tickets are currently available through Leeds Gig Tickets.
