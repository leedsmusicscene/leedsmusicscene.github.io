More bands added to Leeds Festival line up... while Brand New forced to pull out

This article was published in 2013.

Organisers of the annual Leeds Festival confirmed a handful of additions to the line up today, while also announcing that Brand New would no longer be appearing.

Today's additions include EarlWolf, Uncle Acid & The Deadbeats, Bag Raiders (DJ Set), and Crowns.

And in related news, Brand New announced they would no longer be playing the festival this coming August Band Holiday weekend:

"Brand New regretfully announce that due to insurmountable personal issues, they have been forced to cancel their August European tour, including appearances at the Reading and Leeds Festivals. Sincere apologies to everyone we are letting down."
