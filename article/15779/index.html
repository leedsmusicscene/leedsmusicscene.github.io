Wot Gorilla? to release a new single 'is'

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds mathsters Wot Gorilla? are to self-release their new single 'is' on the 30th November as a free download via bandcamp.

The band will be promoting the single with a free launch event at a Nation of Shopkeepers on the same day with support from Gunning for Tamar and Richard Parker.

Wot Gorilla? will go into the studio soon to record their first full album with James Kenosha (Grammatics, PABH, etc). The album is set for a 2012 release.
