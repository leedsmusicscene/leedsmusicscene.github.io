Hard Shoulder

This is an archive of the band profile for Hard Shoulder.

Hard Shoulder is a West Yorkshire based covers band established over ten years ago and is well known for its massive and diverse song repertoire that can be tailored to suit any venue, function or occasion.

The band comprises of four dedicated and professionally minded musicians committed to making every show a memorable one.

Hard Shoulder uses all top quality professional equipment and has liability insurance, we can even provide DJ services as well as the band if required.

To find out more about the band, its members, the songs we play and our upcoming shows please visit our website..
