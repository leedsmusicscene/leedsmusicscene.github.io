Brew Records to release 10" split single

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds independent record label Brew Records have this week announced the release of their upcoming split 10" single.

Set for release on 18th July, the record features brand new tracks from Kong, Castrovalva, Shield Your Eyes and That Fucking Tank.

Following the recent success of their Record Store Day split 10" with fellow Leeds label Dance to the Radio, Brew have returned with another split 10" record which boasts even more rock power than the last.

Alongside Kong ('Ribbons') and Shield Your Eyes ('Jessica') on the tracklisting are Leeds legends and firm Brew favourites That Fucking Tank, who contribute the track 'NWONWOBH', and Castrovalva, who close the single with the track 'In Our Prime'.

You can pre-order from the Brew Records store.

Each purchase of the 10" single will include a download card featuring all 4 tracks plus a bonus 65daysofstatic remix of Kong's 'Ribbons'.
