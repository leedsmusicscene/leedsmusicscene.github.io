Live At Leeds 2011 wristband exchange details revealed

This article was published in 2011.

Organisers of the Live At Leeds 2011 festival have today revealed details of the wristband exchange process for tomorrow.

You cannot access the venues with your ticket so make sure you exchange it for a wristband before you go to watch your first band of the day.

To exchange your ticket for a wristband between the hours of 10am and 5pm please go to Leeds Metropolitan University, Calverley Street Entrance, LS1 3HE (opposite LGI).

To exchange your ticket for a wristband between the hours of 5pm and 10pm please go to Leeds Metropolitan SU Box Office on Portland Way, LS1 3HE (near Dry Dock).

The event is not fully subscribed (yet!) and you can also buy a ticket from Leeds Metropolitan University SU tommorow morning from 10am.

If you have not yet planned your day, you can review the Live At Leeds 2011 timetable.

While you're out and about tomorrow you can use Twitter to keep you informed of the latest news and gossip, as well as critical information about queues and which venues are filling up throughout the day.

Make sure you follow @liveatleeds2011 and @leedsmusicscene to find out the latest goings on today and tommorow, and use hashtag #liveatleeds2011 in all your posts.
