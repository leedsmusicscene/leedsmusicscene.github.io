Ten Floors

This is an archive of the band profile for Ten Floors.

Ten Floors - an original hybrid of indie piano rock - are a fresh new band from the north of England who have quickly established themselves as one of the most dynamic unsigned indie bands today.

Likened to early Athlete and Coldplay, Ten Floors - are described as "a great band"¬ù (BBC Raw Talent) and "an explosion of piano indie" (The Mag) with "an incredible sound" (Rhythm and Booze).

With a digital single available on iTunes, an EP recorded with Embrace's Mickey Dale and high profile appearances in 2007/2008 behind them - including Sheffield's Don Valley Bowl and Chelmsford's Hylands Park - Ten Floors are currently hard at work in the rehearsal room writing tracks for a new release in 2009 soon to be confirmed..
