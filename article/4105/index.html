Red Stars Parade to release their debut album on August 22nd....

This article was published in 2005.

Red Stars Parade's debut album - titled "Disko" - will be released on 22nd August through Dry Run Recordings. Recorded last year with Dave Chang at Philia Studios, the album features 11 tracks including the songs "Zemanova" and "El Retardo".

For more information visit the band's website, or you can listen to tunes from the album @ www.myspace.com/redstarsparade.
