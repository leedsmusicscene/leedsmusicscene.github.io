Leeds bands announced for Carling Stage at Leeds Festival 2004...

This article was published in 2004.

Carling Stage bands have been announced this week for Leeds Festival 2004 and the line up includes a number of local bands. Kaiser Chiefs, The Glitterati, The Cribs, 10,000 Things and Infrasound will appear over the weekend's schedule (exact dates to be announced) and join a line up that also includes The 45’s, Agent Blue, Amplifier, Autolux, Blanche, Danko Jones, The Departure, Devendra Barnhart, Dios, Do Me Bad Things, The Duke Spirit, The Features, The Golden Virgins, The Living Things, Madrugada, Mclusky, My Red Cell, The Others, Pink Grease, Riff Random, Roxy Saint, Terra Diablo, TV On The Radio and Yourcodenameis: Milo.

Headliners for the Carling Stage are The Kills / Soundtrack Of Our Lives (Friday), Kasabian / The Shins (Saturday), and Mark Lanegan / Mondo Generator (Sunday).

The line up for the Concrete Jungle Stage has also been finalised, with the following additions being announced: Goldfinger, Dillinger Escape Plan, Bouncing Souls, The Starting Line, Coheed & Cambria, Flogging Molly, Allister, Alexis On Fire, and The Holiday Plan.

The other announcement this week is that The Distillers are Satuday's Main Stage “Special Guests”.

For up to date listings and to buy tickets please visit the Leeds Festival site.
