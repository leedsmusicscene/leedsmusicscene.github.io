The Music release new single in November

This article was published in 2002.

The Music will release a new single through Hut Recordings on November 18th. Getaway will be available on enhanced CD (featuring the new video) and limited edition picture disc 7". Both formats of the single will be deleted on the day of release.

The band are currently touring in mainland Europe before heading off to the US and Canada for their debut North American dates in October and November. The world tour continues with sold out shows in Japan in December before returning to the UK for the dates in January 2003.
