Quidrophenia returns to Oporto for Round 6!

This article was published in 2007.

If you’re one of the unlucky music lovers who missed out on this year’s Carling Festival tickets, don’t fret! Quidrophenia 6 is back and is bigger than ever. Taking place at Oporto on August Bank Holiday (27th August), Quidrophenia 6 will make sure you don’t miss out on all the fun and festivities, with an unforgettable 14 hour bandfest!

Entry is a ‘quid’, hence the name Quidrophenia, and all proceeds go to Cancer Research UK.

Showcasing the best of Leeds’ music scene, Quidrophenia attracts music lovers from far and wide to witness the best in up and coming Leeds’ talent and putting Leeds on the music map – where it deserves to be!

Since beginning, Quidrophenia at Oporto has been raising money for Cancer Research whilst providing a platform to boost the profile of local bands and the Leeds music scene in general. This year's line up includes The Invention, Micky P Kerr, New Vinyl, The Voltz, Hurricane Sunday, Vinyl Collide, The Little Blackhearts, Racine, Jonjo Feather, Tenderloin, Pushbike Army and Hannah Wilson.
