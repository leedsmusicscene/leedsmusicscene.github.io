Royal Park Cellars launches listings broadsheet...

This article was published in 2003.

Panama Promotions have launched an in-house listings broadsheet for the Royal Park Cellars - the first print run for which has already been completely taken up.

The publication is presented as an A3 sheet folded down to A5. When unfolded, one side carries news and info about the venue, while the other is a full A3 listing of gigs at the venue with a brief description of each.

Promoter Steve Kind said:
"The Internet is all very well - and it's certainly transformed the way we book and promote our gigs over the last couple of years. The Leeds Music Scene Website in particular has been of enormous help to us an other venues - but we think there is still a place for good, solid, stick'em-on-the-wall listings - and the fact that the first 500 copies we placed in the venue and other locations have already gone shows that maybe gig-goers agree with us".

A re-print of the October issue - with updates to the gig listings - is being produced this week and will be available at the venue and other selected locations around the City.

Listings for the Royal Park Cellars are updated regularly at: www.royalparkcellars.co.uk.
