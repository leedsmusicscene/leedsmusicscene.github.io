Holy State unleash new single on 30th January

This article was published in 2012.

Leeds four piece Holy State have announced that they will be releasing a new single - 'Dial 'M' For Monolith' - on Monday 30th January 2012 through the Leeds label Brew Records.

Three years after the release of their first demo,'Dial 'M' For Monolith' is their second single taken from their upcoming debut album. It was recorded at Lightship95, moored on the River Thames, and produced by Ben Phillips.

You can stream the single now from bandcamp.com.

The band's debut album will be released on 30th April 2012.
