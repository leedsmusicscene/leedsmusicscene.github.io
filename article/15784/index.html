La Machine release the 'Spirit Level EP' in December

This article was published in 2011.

La Machine is the new project from Lee Morris and Natalie Bolton, formerly of electro-goth duo Lovebites.

The duo's debut single will be the 'Spirit Level EP', which will take the form of a free download available from Bandcamp and Soundcloud.

The EP will consist of the title track and three remixes by prominent figures within the Leeds music scene.

The 'Spirit Level EP' is to be one of several free download EPs to be released by the duo over the next few months.

The EP will be available to download on Monday December 12th.
