Cinerama release new single and live album...

This article was published in 2003.

The Scopitones label released Cinerama's new enhanced CD single this month. Having decamped from West Yorkshire to the West Coast at the start of the year, David recorded this new release with Steve Fisk in his new home town of Seattle.

The single features three brand new songs, along with a bonus CD Rom video of the "Careless" single from last year's "Torino" album. Tracklisting: "Don't Touch That Dial" - "The One That Got Away" - "On / Off".

Also just back from the CD pressing plant is the second Cinerama live release, "Cinerama: Live In Belfast", a sixteen track album that was recorded in 2002.
