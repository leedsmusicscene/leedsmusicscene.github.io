Fossil Collective rise out of the ashes of Vib Gyor and release debut EP

This article was published in 2010.

Following the split of Leeds band Vib Gyor last year, singer Dave & drummer Jonny have regrouped under the name Fossil Collective, and have promptly released their debut EP entitled Honey Slides this month on iTunes.

The EP show a change of direction from their previous incarnation, veering towards a more folky sound, influenced by artists such as Neil Young, Bon Iver, Fleet Foxes & Midlake to name a few.

Lead track On & On is accompanied by an animated video by Ashley dean who is renknown for his videos for iLiKETRAiNS and more recently his sledgehammer homage for Lone Wolf.
