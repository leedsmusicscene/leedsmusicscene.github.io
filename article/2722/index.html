Themselves/Hood split 7" to be released on July 6th...

This article was published in 2004.

This long awaited picture disc featuring Hood will be unleashed on July 6th on the Rocket Racer record label. There is one track from each band. The Themselves track is a re-working of the Hood track. Themselves is Dose One (cLOUDDEAD) and Jel.

As well as the initial 1,000 pressing, there will be a limited edition of 100 additional pressings with extra artwork which you can only buy direct from Rocket Racer.

For more details visit www.rocketracer.us.
