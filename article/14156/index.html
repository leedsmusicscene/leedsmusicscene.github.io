Scars On 45 release their debut EP

This article was published in 2011.

Scars On 45 have announced the released of their our debut EP through the Chop Shop/Atlantic Records label.

The 'Give Me Something' EP is available as a Digital Download and includes four tracks:

1. Give Me Something
2. The Way That We Are
3. Loudest Alarm
4. Don't Say (Acoustic)

The title track will also be featured on the band's forthcoming debut album, expected out later this year. The EP also includes 'Loudest Alarm', a b-side from the album recording sessions, and an acoustic performance of the album track 'Don't Say'. The collection is rounded out with a newly recorded song, 'The Way That We Are'.

Scars On 45 made their inaugural stateside performance at SXSW 2010 in Austin, TX and recently completed their first-ever US tour.
