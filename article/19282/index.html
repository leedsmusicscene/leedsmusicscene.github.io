Allusondrugs announce Leeds show on 27th November 2015

This article was published in 2015.

Probably the best news we have heard for a long time!

One of our favourite bands, psych-pop-grunge rockers, Allusondrugs are returning to play 360 Club (Lending Room at The Library), Leeds on the 27th November. This is one gig you will not want to miss, with support from Treason Kings and Adore/Repel, it will be a raucous night of brilliant music.

This year Allusondrugs have been really busy with high-profile support tours and shows with Enter Shikari, Marmozets, Lonely The Brave and Pulled Apart By Horses. Having also completed a very successful and extensive period of headline tours through April and May, multiple festival appearances throughout the , culminating in a short co-headline run with fellow Yorkshiremen Brawlers, they have built an impressive and loyal following. .

Alongside all this, the band are also proud to announce a partnership with the excellent folks at Too Pure to bestow upon the universe a double-A-side 7" single in September. We predict big things for Allusondrugs and having caught the eyes and ears of the likes of Kerrang, Rock Sound and Radio One, it appears that the industry sources agree!
