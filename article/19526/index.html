What Howls

This is an archive of the band profile for What Howls.

Ben Gausden (Drums)
Tom Gausden (Bass)
Joe Kerry (Guitar / vocals)

A three piece from Leeds - born out of listening to far too much guitar music and new to the scene - expect distorted guitars, smashed drums with well-crafted melodic vocals..
