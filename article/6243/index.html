Hex

This is an archive of the band profile for Hex.

Formed back in the winter of 2003, the Bradford 4-piece band known as Hex have been entertaining the stage with their punky songs since late 2004. Meeting many people along the way, even managing to perform with big star bands from both sides of the pond. Now with experience under the belts, they're ready to unleash their own material on to people's Hi-Fi systems. With the debut EP already out and recordings for the album soon to be finished, Hex have gathered a popular crowd as their following and are gearing up to embark on their 1st full UK tour this year!!.
