Sunny Philadelphia

This is an archive of the band profile for Sunny Philadelphia.

James Debroy - Vocals
Rob Orange - Guitar

Sunny Philadelphia are James Debroy and Rob Orange, a song writing team from Leeds.

They are currently working on a set list of original songs which range in style from soulful blues to uptempo funk.

James and Rob can be seen performing at open mic nights in the Leeds area, venues include Carpe Diem/The Grove Inn..
