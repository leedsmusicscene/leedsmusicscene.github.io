Laika Dog to release debut album in February 2005...

This article was published in 2004.

Laika Dog, featuring former Terrorvision singer Tony Wright, have announced the release of their debut album "Forever And A Day" out on Monday, February 7th on Townsend Records.

The 10-track CD is also available for pre-order now from www.townsend-records.co.uk.

In related news, April 2005 sees Terrorvision reforming for "one week only" to play a series of shows around the UK - including Leeds University on Saturday, April 16th - whilst former Terrorvision bass player Leigh Marklew's new band Maliby Stacey can be seen every night on popular music show "Scuzz" between 9-10pm where their "Invasion" video is currently playlisted.
