Feeds

This is an archive of the band profile for Feeds.

In a world of imitation artists, Feeds sound like everything and yet nothing you've heard before. From the four corners of the UK their tale began in rainy Huddersfield, where they honed their sound in cheap practice rooms and crampt bedrooms. After settling on a line-up in late 2011, Feeds have since self-released 2 EPs progressing from dimly lit pubs to brightly lit stages, across the country. Carefully crafted and lovingly made, their songs constantly evolve and develop as you journey through each story. Their eclectic brand of prog-pop is a combination of throw-back harmonies and soaring melodies, hot riffs and cool rhythms..
