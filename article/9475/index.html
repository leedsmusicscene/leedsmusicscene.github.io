Hadouken! to appear live at HMV to promote their debut album 'Music For An Accelerated Culture'

This article was published in 2008.

Hadouken! mark the release of their debut album, ‘Music For An Accelerated Culture’, with a series of free live shows and signing sessions at stores up and down the country - the band will appear live in Leeds at HMV (City Centre) at 4.30pm on Friday 9th May.

The band’s debut album is released on 5th May 2008 and includes the songs Smashed Gate Crash; That Boy That Girl; Declaration Of War; Game Over; Mr Misfortune; Crank It Up; What She Did; Driving Nowhere; Liquid Lives; Spend Your Life; and Wait For You.

Until 4th May, you can order the album on MP3 format for just £5 from 7digital.com.

Hadouken! play live at Leeds University on Friday 9th May. Tickets are still available at See Tickets.

In other news, Hadouken! are now fully confirmed to play Leeds Festival 2008, making their appearance on Friday 22nd August.
