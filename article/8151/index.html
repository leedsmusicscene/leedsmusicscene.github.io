Wild Beasts to release a new single on Bad Sneakers Records

This article was published in 2007.

On 23rd April, Leeds band Wild Beasts will release their second single on the Leeds-based Bad Sneakers Records label. Titled "Through Dark Night", the single comes backed with the B-side "Please, Sir", and will be available on 7", CD and Digital Download formats.

Wild Beasts have just been signed by Domino Records (home of Arctic Monkeys and Franz Ferdinand) and this will be their last Bad Sneakers Records release before joining Domino for an album release later this year.

Wild Beasts write intelligent, quirky, original and incredibly catchy pop music, and are lined up to support Maximo Park at their high profile May London shows at Shepherds Bush Empire and London Forum on 11th and 12th May. The band play at the Faversham in Leeds on 21st April, for what will be their single launch party.
