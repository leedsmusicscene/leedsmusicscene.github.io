Allusondrugs announce début EP release

This article was published in 2014.

Psychedelic alt-rock grunge band Allusondrugs have announced that their self-titled EP will be released on 21st July via local label Clue Records.

The EP was recorded and produced in one week in January 2014 at Greenmount Studios in Armley, Leeds, tracked live to analogue tape as band members all set up and performed together in one room in an attempt to try and capture the band's explosive live energy on record.

The band's first three singles - grunge-laden 'Nervous', the beautiful shoegaze pop gem 'Plasters' and the stoner drone of 'MyCat'- all sold out and growing whisperings amongst press and promoters alike of their high energy, visceral live performances recently led to them being invited to play Live At Leeds and to headline the Leeds leg of Independent Venue Week.

The E.P will be available online and in physical format through Clue Records. There will also be 100 limited edition cassettes available through Pinky Swear Records.
