Silverlode release free 5 track collection

This article was published in 2011.

Throughout 2010, Silverlode released various new recordings to members of their mailing list.

Along with new recording of acoustic favourite in 'The Garden/On The Mound', four these varied songs have been collected together with artwork for a free 5-track digital download named 'Silverlode And His Amazing Friends'.

The collection is available now for free at silverlode.bandcamp.com.

The band are presently recording a new batch of songs which will appear in the spring.
