Colour Of Spring release new single 'Pillow'

This article was published in 2016.

Watch the video below for Colour Of Spring's new single 'Pillow'.

It's a beautifully shot piece with the shoegaze swoon perfectly sound tracking the adventures of two good friends on a cold night together in Leeds.



The band recorded 'Pillow' with Alex Greaves (Forever Cult) and partnered up with York label Swirly Records for a limited run of hand-cut 7" singles.
