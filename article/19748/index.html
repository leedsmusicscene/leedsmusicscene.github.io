Young Amphibians Release 'City Charm' Video

This article was published in 2017.

Leeds based Alternative Rock band Young Amphibians have released their brand new music video for 'City Charm'.

The video, which was directed and produced by Callum Scott-Dyson of CSD Videography marks the first release from their upcoming second EP 'Junked Up Heart' set for release on Friday 24th February 2017.

The band will celebrate the release with an EP launch at 360 Club - The Lending Room - Leeds on 24th February with performances from The Potenzas, Northern Comfort & REGAN.

The video is now available to watch below or on Facebook at www.facebook.com/youngamphibians.



Tickets for 'Junked Up Heart' EP Launch are available from Crash Records & Jumbo Records or via Young Amphibians.
