96.3 Radio Aire to showcase Leeds music

This article was published in 2009.

From January 10th 2010, Leeds musicians will get a chance to shine on a brand new show on 96.3 Radio Aire.

The show - presented by Larry and Paul - will be on every Sunday night between 7pm and 10pm, straight after the Big Top 40 Show.

Focussing on Leeds life, Larry and Paul will take weekly looks at the Leeds music scene, night life, sport and culture.

Paul said, "Like many, I came here as a student, and now simply cannot move away. Leeds is such a great city, and I'm delighted to be starting a show on a radio station that reflects that."

Each week Larry and Paul will discuss gigs and new releases, as well as playing music from local bands.

Larry said, "We know how important it is for local radio stations to support local music, and every year, we get right behind the Local and Unsigned competition. We hope that through the new show, we can go some way towards giving talented Leeds musicians more of the exposure they deserve."

Leeds bands or musicians wanting their music considered should send a CD to

Larry and Paul
Radio Aire
51 Burley Road
Leeds
LS3 1LR

Don't forget to tune in to 96.3FM from 7pm on January 10th.
