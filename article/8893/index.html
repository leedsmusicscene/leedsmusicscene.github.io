Grammatics to release "Shadow Committee" on 22nd October

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds-based band Grammatics will release their first single on the Dance To The Radio record label on Monday 22nd October. Available on limited edition 7” vinyl and Digital Download formats, and distributed by VITAL, “Shadow Committee” follows the band's first commercially available release “The Shipping Forecast”.

Grammatics play Leeds Faversham on November 3rd.
