This Is Happening - a new podcast for Leeds music is launched

This article was published in 2013.

A new podcast has been launched this month.

This Is Happening is a new monthly Leeds-based podcast hosted by Whiskas along with regular contributors Simon Fogal and Dan O'Dell. Each episode will include additional guests from the local music scene and beyond.

The hosts will talk irreverently about the music they're listening to, the gigs they're going to and the bands they are involved in.

This Is Happening will be published in the first week of the month and the first instalment was recorded on Sunday 30th June at Brudenell Social Club, hosted by whiskas with Simon Fogal, Dan O'Dell and guest Nathan Clark.

In the first edition, the hosts talk about Glastonbury, Destroy All Monsters, Merchandise, Department M, Post War Glamour Girls, Swimming Lessons, the new Leeds Arena and much more.
