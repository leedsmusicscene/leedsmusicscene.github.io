3milehigh to make live TV appearance on BBC...

This article was published in 2004.

Following a successful jaunt down to the Isles of Scilly to play at the Camel Rock Festival on the island of St Mary’s, which culminated in an interview and having the most requested song on the Isles of Scilly radio station, 3milehigh will make their television debut. The band will appear and play some live tracks on BBC (Yorkshire) at 8:15am on Monday, 1st November as part of the Today Show.

In other news, the band have recently recorded an interview for BBC Raw Talent and have since been invited back to do a live session on January 6th.

Finally, 3milehigh's next Leeds gig will be a HMV Showcase appearance at Joseph’s Well on Thursday, 11th November.
