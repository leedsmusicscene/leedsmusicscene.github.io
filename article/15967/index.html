The 27 Club

This is an archive of the band profile for The 27 Club.

The 27 Club are a blues/rock band hailing from Keighley, West Yorkshire. The band have been described live as 'getting pelted with cannon balls' yet 'delicate and melodic' in the same sentence.

Influences derive from Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, Pink Floyd, Bob Dylan, Red Hot Chilli Peppers . Yet the songs are put together in their own unique way.

After playing several high profile gigs such as Bingley Music Festival, Batley Variety Club and Headingly Stadium the band have gone from strength to strength and are at the top of their game.

Radio stations have also started to take interest in The 27 Club and airplay has been received numerous times.

The 27 Club have recently been recording at Loom Studios and have an EP called 'I Taught Myself Everything I Know'. These songs can be heard on the page..
