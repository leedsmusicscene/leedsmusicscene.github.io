Kaiser Chiefs confirmed to headline at Harewood House this summer as part of the O2 Wireless Festival 2007

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band Kaiser Chiefs will been confirmed as headliners for two shows at the O2 Wireless Festival taking place in London (Hyde Park) and Leeds (Harewood House) this summer.

02 Wireless Festival takes place over four days in London (14th to 17th June) and the weekend in Leeds (16th and 17th June). The five-piece, who this week celebrated their single 'Ruby' hitting the top position in the UK Top 40, have yet to confirm which dates they are playing in which city. A further announcement is expected on March 12th, when tickets are to go on sale.

The band have also been confirmed for Glastonbury 2007.

In other news, Kaiser Chiefs will play tonight as part of the 2007 NME Awards Show at London's Shepherds Bush Empire.
