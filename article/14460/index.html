Charge of the 1337 Brigade

This is an archive of the band profile for Charge of the 1337 Brigade.

Charge of the 1337 Brigade (COT1B) are a 5 piece Pop Punk/ Ska band from Bradford, West Yorkshire.

We are a group of mates who decided that instead of doing something productive with our lives we were going to be in a punk band and so far the decision has not paid off at all! We recorded a 5 track EP in February called 'COT1B- EP' with Luke Yates of Sounds of Swami and The Human Project. We have spent the last few months rehearsing, writing songs and partying like a proper band. Now with our new EP behind us and our first few gigs booked we look forward to spreading our music to anyone and everyone who will listen!.
