Diesel-U-Music is inviting Leeds' next generation of DJs and MCs...

This article was published in 2003.

Diesel-U-Music is inviting Leeds' next generation of DJs and MCs to come out of their bedrooms to express their musical abilities in association with Sugar Beat at the legendary Elbow Rooms in Leeds (26th April).

4 Music will be filming the event for inclusion in their breaking talent section of the forthcoming June and July six part series.

Headlining the night will be DJ Skully, the 2002 DIESEL-U-MUSIC Award Scratch Champion, who releases his 100% exclusive debut mix album 'Champion Sounds' on April 21st.

Diesel-U-Music is looking to champion local talent and are offering the opportunity to DJ alongside DJ Skully and Tim Utah Saints.

To enter the competition a half-hour tape or CD can be delivered to the Leeds Diesel Store on 57-59 Vicar Lane in Victoria Quarter (Tel: 0113 242 1719) or visit www.diesel-u-music.co.uk

All entries will be submitted into the final event that will take place in London in early July 2003. Winners receive record release, tours and management.
