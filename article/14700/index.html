Wild Beasts announced for Constellations Festival 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Following last year's inaugural Constellation music festival, which saw Les Savy Fav, Four Tet and Broken Social Scene appear at Leeds University, organisers have this week confirmed the festival will return to the venue again later this year.

The line up for Constellations 2011 already includes Wild Beasts, Yuck and The Antlers and many more bands are to be announced between now and November.

The event will take place on Saturday 12th November 2011 and will see the introduction of the Constellations Exhibition Hall and Constellations TV, an exhibition of submitted art plus film screenings throughout the day, and the return of some of last year's hugely popular collaborators.
