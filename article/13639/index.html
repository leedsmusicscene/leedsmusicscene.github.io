Sunday For The Suspect

This is an archive of the band profile for Sunday For The Suspect.

Formed in June 2009, after a chance meeting of both Danny & Jordan and quickly joined by both Luke and Matt, Sunday For The Suspect were complete.

From Barnsley, Wakefield & Bradford the band takes its influences from a mixture of different places so making its music and sound entirely its own.

It is the band's mission to deliver the goods at all times whether on stage in an energetic live performance or laying down our tunes for all and sundry to rock out to.

In the short journey so far Sunday For The Suspect have gigged regularly in and around West Yorkshire attracting positive plaudits from Headlining bands they have supported such as Hitomi, The Headstart and Koopa.

The year 2010 promises to be an exciting year as the band look to hit the recording studio with a view to releasing their debut 4 track EP whilst continuing to gig and build up a loyal fanbase..
