Escort Knights announce the release of their new single

This article was published in 2012.

Following the successful release of their first single 'I Don't Know Your Name' in 2011, Escort Knights are back with their second single 'Heartstrings' including B-Side 'Kiss From A Rose' the band's unique take on Seal's 90s classic.

'Heartstrings' is a double shot of audio endorphins with thumping four to the floor and Daft Punk esque retro synth sounds.

B-side 'Kiss From A Rose' is a dance-floor cover of the classic Seal track; one of the biggest selling songs of the nineties and already a live favourite.

A limited run of 1000 CDs will be printed and hand numbered by the band, featuring a remix by legendary house DJ Lance Lewis (Ministry of Sound).

The single will be released on 14th February 2012 through the Waltzer Records label. You can also buy the single as a digital download.
