Pretty Riddles

This is an archive of the band profile for Pretty Riddles.

Guitar/Vox - Paul Lodge
Guitar - Barry Edwards
Bass - Shaun Swallow
Drums - Chris Lodge


Pretty Riddles are a Leeds band that is swiftly building a reputation as ones to watch, with their live shows creating a buzz in all the right places. Recently described by No Title Magazine as lying "somewhere between good era Bob Dylan and great era Iggy and the Stooges" Pretty Riddles have a live set full to the brim with energy. Recently supporting such acts as The Horrors & Various Cruelties these are a band you must not miss..
