The Glitterati to release a new single on November 23rd

This article was published in 2009.

Leeds-based rockers The Glitterati will be releasing a free download single on Monday 23rd November. The release, titled ‘Fight! Fight! Fight!’, will be available to download from the Global Music website.

Following the single, The Glitterati will be heading out on a full UK tour supporting W.A.S.P. The band will join Blackie Lawless and crew at Leeds O2 Academy venue on 30th November – tickets are available now via Leeds Gig Tickets.

In other news, the band are adding the finishing touches to their second studio album, due out in February 2010.
