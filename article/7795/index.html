Leeds bands Armstrong and International Trust to release a split single

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds bands Armstrong and International Trust have announced details of a split single scheduled for release on 29th January 2007. The double A-side CD single will also be available to pre-order from www.recordstore.co.uk.

Armstrong follow up their début single's chart placing (#23 in the UK Indie charts) with the song “Re:Invent The Scene” whilst the International Trust side will include their song “Bruce Lee”.

Both bands are touring throughout January in order to promote the single. The Leeds single launch event takes place on Saturday, 20th January at Mine (Leeds University) with additional support from The Tommys and Futuro plus special guest DJ Whiskas (Forward Russia).
