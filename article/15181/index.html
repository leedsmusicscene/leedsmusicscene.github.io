Ryan Mitchell-Smith

This is an archive of the band profile for Ryan Mitchell-Smith.

Ryan Mitchell-Smith is a singer/songwriter and blues harmonica player based in Wakefield, UK. Ryan is a multi-instrumentalist and gigs where and when he can in various disguises, occasionally performing with talented artists such as; Antonio Lulic, Dr Blue, Ruby Macintosh, Sebastian John, and I Like Mike. He is as at home spanking the cajon as he is slapping the bass but his love for guitar and pushing its boundaries along with his harmonica playing are what set Ryan apart. Due to health issues Ryan sometimes struggles to gig very often so if you are lucky enough to see a gig date, it is worth trying to get along. It may be to his cost, but Ryan isn't shy of energy when the music gets going!.
