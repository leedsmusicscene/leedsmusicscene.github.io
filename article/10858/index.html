Ossfest '09: local football club to host charity music festival

This article was published in 2009.

On Saturday August 1st Ossett Town Football Club will host a charity live music event at their Ingfield Stadium. The event will be for the benefit of Wakefield Hospice and is intended to be an annual occurrence.

There will be a full programme of bands and entertainment for the region that is sure to be a huge success.

This event is one of several as part of the growing relationship between Ossett Town FC and Wakefield Hospice. The club has provided the Hospice with free shirt sponsorship for next season and there are continued efforts to raise the profile of the charity to enable it to continue its superb support to families affected by terminal diseases.

This will be the showpiece music event of the Summer for the area and Ossett Town Football Club are very hopeful the region will turn out in force to support the bands, the charity and the club on the day.

Bands currently on the bill include:

White Belt Yellow Tag, The Faff, New Vinyl, Above Them, The Antiks, Tresmacacos, The Headstart, Voo, Brand New Analogues and The Wireless Stones.

A very special headliner is to be announced soon.
