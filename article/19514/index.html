Vexxes

This is an archive of the band profile for Vexxes.

Tom Fieldhouse - Vocals
Maax Oliver - Guitars & Vocals
Shreedhan Vaidya - Drums
Sam Whittle - Bass & Vocals

Following a year of extensive gigging, UK/EU touring, global releases and sell out shows, a few friends decided it was time to work on something new and exciting. From this VEXXES were formed.

After months of writing, VEXXES headed to Decimal & Stakeout Studios in London (Fightstar, Charlie Simpson, We Are The Ocean, Reuben, YM@6, Arcane Roots, FFAF), to record their debut EP with the extremely talented producer Chris Coulter (Arcane Roots, EMP!RE & In Dynamics).

VEXXES then released their debut music video for single Everything I've Ever Known on the 22nd of March 2016, with a premier on Upset Magazine. The band then announced a May '16 release of their debut EP 'Common Ground'.

Following this, Ticketmaster launched a premier of an exclusive track 'ENEMIES', where they were likened to bands such as Lower Than Atlantis, You Me At Six and Deaf Havana. They also highlighted on the bands bold lyrics and beautiful riff-work..
