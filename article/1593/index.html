A Day Left to launch new EP at Brudenell Social...

This article was published in 2003.

A Day Left are set to release their new EP - Everything Grows Light - at a very special launch night at the Brudenell Social Club on 5th December.

"The EP is the culmination of a year of much touring and gigging. I reckon it's good indication of how far the band has travelled since our first outing..." said singer Oli Renton.

The new material leans towards the band's heavier and darker influences with more than a hint of the epic.
