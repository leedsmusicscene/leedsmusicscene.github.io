The Research release a new single - "The Way You Used To Smile" - on 17th October

This article was published in 2005.

The Research release their third single on Monday, 17th October, titled "The Way You Used To Smile". The single features the title track as well as "The Hard Times" (CD version) and "Hey Tornado" and "I Made A Promise" (7" version).

The Research play a free gig at St George's Hall in Bradford this Friday.

You can pre-order the single from HMV for just 99p (and free delivery).
