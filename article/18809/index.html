Hawk Eyes announce new album

This article was published in 2014.

Hawk Eyes have just announced details of their brand new album 'Everything is Fine', which is set for release on 9th February 2015.

Writing for the record begun back in 2012 with producer Andy Hawkins, along with additional engineering from Ryan and the band's live sound engineer Giles Stocks.

Listen to 'Die Trying' below.



Having supported the likes of System of A Down at arena shows and playing the biggest music festivals across Europe, as well as SXSW over in the States, Hawk Eyes are poised to take over the world of heavy music.

The band will also be touring in February to support the release, ending in Leeds at the Brudenell Social Club on Saturday 21st February.
