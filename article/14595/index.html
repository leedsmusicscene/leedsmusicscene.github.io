First bands announced for Bingley Music Live 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Bradford Council has today announced the first round of bands for Bingley Music Live 2011.

The bands announced today are Fun Lovin' Criminals, Maximo Park, Feeder and Eliza Doolittle. More top names are set to be announced in the coming weeks.

The annual event takes place this year on 2nd, 3rd and 4th September 2011 in Myrtle Park, Bingley. Last year's event attracted 42,000 music fans over its three days.
