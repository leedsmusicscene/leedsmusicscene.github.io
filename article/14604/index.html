Yonderboy announce release of double A-side single

This article was published in 2011.

Yonderboy have announced a new double A-side release, 'All Bob Minor' / 'Too Easy', available from 30th April 2011 online and in selected record stores.

Following last year's limited edition 7" vinyl release with fellow Leeds indie popsters Just Handshakes (We're British), Yonderboy have this week announced their eagerly anticipated second release will be available at the end of April.

The single was recorded with producer Andy Hawkins at Cottage Road Studios, occasional home to Leeds natives Pulled Apart By Horses and The Pigeon Detectives, and showcases Yonderboy's newly honed sound and subtle, eclectic influences ranging from Talking Heads and The Smiths to Steve Reich.

The release coincides with a prominent appearance for the third year running at Live At Leeds 2011, and will be further promoted via a series of gigs throughout the summer including an appearance at the Deer Shed Festival in North Yorkshire.
