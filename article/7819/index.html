Sub45

This is an archive of the band profile for Sub45.

Sub Forty Five are purveyors of some of the finest British Rock, in many years. Described in the local press as possessing ..'a sound not unlike early Red Hot Chili Peppers' they continue to entertain growing crowds on the live music scene.

Formed in January 2006, The four lads take influences from Led Zeppelin & Rush through to Biffy Clyro and Foo Fighters. Their autobiographical lyric content, narrative of life in modern times is complemented by classic guitar patterns and influential drum fills.

Sub Forty Five continue doing what they do best, putting on a show and giving a crowd exactly what they pay for...

Bashing - Matt Laffey
