Micky P Kerr to release 'The Joke' on Monday

This article was published in 2009.

The new single by Micky P Kerr - titled 'The Joke' and produced by Dan Hulme (The Coral, Tricky, Delphic) - is released on Monday 16th November through the Modern English record label. You can watch the video to the new single below:



‘The Joke’ is Kerr at his best - a heartfelt plea, a vulnerable song about loneliness, underling just how diverse his music is.
