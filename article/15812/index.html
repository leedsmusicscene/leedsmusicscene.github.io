The Asa Hawks release debut EP

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds band The Asa Hawks are pleased to announce the release of their debut EP, Roadkiller.

The 3-track recording, with added bonus instrumental track, is now available to buy via The Asa Hawks website or at all upcoming gigs. If you'd like to get your hands on a very limited copy, please visit www.theasahawks.com and click 'Buy'.

Track listing:
Roadkiller
Four left feet
She can give you what you need
Roadkiller (bonus instrumental track)

The cd will also be available to buy at The Asa Hawks Christmas gig at The Fox and Newt on December 16th.
