Timetable for Constellations Festival 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Organisers of the Constellations Festival 2011 have revealed the running order and timetable.

The event takes place on Saturday 12th November across 5 venues inside the Leeds University Union building: Stylus, Riley Smith Hall, Mine and Pulse, with films in the Terrace Bar.

Headlining the event are Wild Beasts (Stylus, 21.30-23.00), The Big Pink (Riley Smith, 22.00-23.00), Three Trapped Tigers (Mine, 21.45-22.30) and 2:54 (Pulse, 21.15-21.45).
