Esper Scout to release 'Illuminate, Incinerate' EP in October

This article was published in 2011.

Esper Scout have announced the release of their self-released EP 'Illuminate Incinerate' which will be available from 14th October 2011.

The band have been busy writing and recording their latest E.P following the release of their debut, 'Kids & Pets', in Spring 2011. During recent months the band has supported Vessels, This is my Normal State and Bleech. Their sound has been compared to Auf der Maur, Queens of the Stone Age, Jack Off Jill and Thrice.
