That Fucking Tank to release a 7" picture disc single

This article was published in 2007.

The astounding behemoth 2-piece rock riff outfit That Fucking Tank have finished recording sessions for their new release just this month to be released this year through On The Bone Records.

Tank's new release will be their first ever A-side/B-side single on 7" picture disc vinyl and is currently titled 'The Horse & Magnet'. The track also has surprise guest vocals from Giles Bailey formerly of Kill Yourself and Dananananackroyd which is a first for the riff ready instrumental duo.

The release will also come packaged with a free 30 minute DVD of there recent European tour from June and will also feature the music video for the A-side from the single. The release will be backed with a tour of the UK in October and is their first release since their highly acclaimed album 'Day Of Death by Bono Adrenalin Shock' in August 2005.

It's set for release in mid October via On the Bone Records in Leeds and will be available from all major download sites and good indie record stores across the UK.
