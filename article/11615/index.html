12:27

This is an archive of the band profile for 12:27.

Alt Rockers 12:27 hail from West Yorkshire, England. Formed in 2010 the band have been making their mark on the scene performing at venues across West Yorkshire including the 360 Club, Barnsley Metrodome, Escobar Leeds and more notably at the O2 Academy in Manchester and Liverpool.

12:27 have built a loyal following over the years and it's easy to see why, Not only do they have a unique and refreshing talent they're Charismatic, Charming, Youthful, Energetic and always leave you wanting more.

"If Joy Division and The Libertines gave birth it would have probably been born at 12:27"

The band consists of Thom. P Smith on Lead Vocals and Rhythm Guitar, Ash Ingham on Lead Guitar, Alun Davies on Bass and Billy Stowell on Drums.

12:27 recently embarked on independently releasing a full 12 track album titled 'Something Yet To Learn'. The album contains new material as well as early 12:27 material which is available on Spotify, iTunes and Amazon UK.

"Brilliant young band, writing music beyond their years!"

"12:27 have a fantastic range of songs. I have seen them perform several times at different venues and each time they have given a spectacular performance. The band is fuelled by outstanding young talent and will always give 100%. Utterly fantastic!"
