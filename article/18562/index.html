Fold win an Independent Music Award for Salvation

This article was published in 2014.

Fold are pleased to announce that they have won an IMA (Independent Music Award) in the 'spoken word with musical accompaniment' category for their single 'Salvation', as judged by the IMA industry panel. The full EP, Salvation, also received a nomination under the Album Category - Spoken Word with Musical Accompaniment.

Independent Music Award (IMA) is an International programme. It boasts over 1 billion fans worldwide and a panel of industry experts, acclaimed musicians and more who judge and awards. They run annual award ceremonies and take submissions from across the globe in a variety of different categories. These categories all fall under two subheadings, Best Album and Best Single. The panel of industry experts then determine winners in each category and artists are notified.

Fold are a highly recommended live band and do check out their latest release 'Be Water My Friend' which has been picking up big praise with press and radio worldwide. Featured on The Guardian Music Blog, gaining support from BBC 6, featured as Dave Rowntree's record of the week on XFM radio. Not stopping there, Fold are reaching out across the oceans to influential trend setter Kara Manning in New York on WFUV Radio, as well as features in Berlin, Zagreb, Mexico and more.
