The Blanche Hudson Weekend release debut album

This article was published in 2011.

The debut album from The Blanche Hudson Weekend is now available.

Released on Monday 10th October via Leeds label Squirrel Records, 'You Always Loved Violence' takes the band's fuzzed up, lo-fi pop and pushes it that little bit further.

You can buy the album now from the Squirrel Records online store.

'You Always Loved Violence' contains 14 tracks:

1 Intro
2 Union Square Blackout
3 The Bitterest Clash
4 Then You Tell Me
5 Coming Home
6 River's Edge
7 Screen Test
8 Permanent Ink
9 Different Again
10 No Contest
11 Getting Faster
12 The Last Ride
13 One Last Kiss
14 Blue Exit Down
