Happy Daggers

This is an archive of the band profile for Happy Daggers.

Happy Daggers are a Leeds based quintet, who have been described as 'One of the best live bands coming out of Leeds right now.' Happy Daggers have a sound and stage performance that is as energetic as it is infectious - filled with music to make you move.

Members:
Sinclair - Guitar/Vocals
John - Bass
Jordan - Keys
Danny - Lead Guitar
Tom - Drums/Vocals.
