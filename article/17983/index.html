Forever Cult release 'Fuxx' EP as a Free Download

This article was published in 2013.

Forever Cult are a 3 piece alternative rock band based in West Yorkshire. Their music blends the brutal moments of 1990s Seattle grunge with the jaunty pop-minded lyrical charm of mid-00s British indie. After spending some time as a two piece, Kieran (guitar and lead vocals) & Aaron (drums), have been joined by Alex (bass) to record & release their debut EP 'Fuxx'.
