Come Play With Me singles club announce new split 7" vinyl

This article was published in 2016.

Come Play With Me, the brand new Leeds singles club, have announced the second split 7" vinyl in the series will be released on 19th February.

Aiming to showcase the most exciting artists from the Leeds City region the first single released in November 2015 featured tracks from Harkin (Sleater-Kinney, Sky Larkin) and Cinerama (The Wedding Present, David Gedge).

Now the latest single from the label looks to continue the trend of highlighting the awesome diversity of great music coming out of this part of the world with new tracks from Fizzy Blood ('Sweat and Sulphur') and Officers ('Attack').

You can pre-order the split single from the Come Play With page on Pledge Music.
