Threekseven

This is an archive of the band profile for Threekseven.

threekseven are Jason Howard, Karl Greenwood and Krishna Thiruchelvam. We formed at Leeds University in 2002, through studying music together.

threekseven demonstrates the fusing of our diverse musical tastes and different musical backgrounds.

Drawing our influences from the like of Brian Eno, Martin Hannet, Stockhausen, Vincent Gallo, Michel Gondry, Ralf Hutter and Florian Schneider, we use the studio as an instrument to expand our creative boundaries.

Whether our music is plucked or programmed, brutally fragile or ethereally intense, a mutual dimension is created between ourselves and our audience..
