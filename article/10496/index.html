Elephants On Acid to release their 3rd EP on 17th March

This article was published in 2009.

Elephants On Acid are to release their third EP, 'High And Dry', on 17th March 2009.

'High And Dry' includes the track 'Can You Hear?' which will be included on 'Trip in Time volume 4' (Psychedelic Rock Compilation - due for release in June 2009 Germany).

The 'High And Dry' EP is on sale from 17th March from Crash Records and Jumbo Records in Leeds. The EP launch is on 17th March at Joseph's Well.
