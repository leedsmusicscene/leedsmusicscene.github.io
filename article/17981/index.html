Octernal Music Festival Saturday 19th October

This article was published in 2013.

Octernal is a brand new all day event carefully curated by a group of promoters, who want to rock your socks off! They say 'We all have our favourite artists that we want to share with you and Octernal Festival is our serendipitous vessel that will make that happen'

Taking place on the 19th October, across 3 stages; Brudenell Social Main Room, Brudenell Games Room and Left Bank Church, there will be a menagerie of acts ranging from bazouki rock to caveman battle doom, from psych folk to slacker indie, from drone to post punk. Every earholes a goal!
