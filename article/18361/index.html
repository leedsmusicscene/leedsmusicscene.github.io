The Vitasound Projects debut album 'American Werewolf in London' available with syncopated film

This article was published in 2014.

The Vitasound Projects' debut concept album 'American Werewolf in London' is now available to watch on YouTube. The complete syncopated videos that influenced the writing process have been released to allow listeners to appreciate the audio visual experiment.
