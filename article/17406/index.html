The Enemy and Dinosaur Pile-up confirmed for Crooked Ways Festival

This article was published in 2013.

In an announcement from the organisers of the Crooked Ways Festival, The Enemy, Grandmaster Flash, Peter Hook, Mallory Knox, Dinosaur Pile-up and more were confirmed this week for the Yorkshire festival.

Crooked Ways is an annual independent one day festival that is held in Pontefract, West Yorkshire. This year's event will take place on Saturday 1st June 2013.

The first line up announcement includes The Enemy, Grandmaster Flash, Peter Hook, The Dot, Mallory Knox, Dinosaur Pileup, Rolo Tomassi, The Defiled, Gallops, Sam Forrest, Above Them, and Allusondrugs.

More acts to be announced on 26th March.

Ticket prices are £35 for adults or £17.50 for under 13's from Yorkshire Gig Guide.
