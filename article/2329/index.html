Infrasound to release single in March...

This article was published in 2004.

Having recently inked deals with Versity Music (based in Sweden) and BMG Publishing, Leeds based band Infrasound are currently recording their debut single, scheduled for release on March 29th. The single is titled Fire In The City and is being produced by Andy Gill. A video for the single is also in the making.

The band support Black Rebel Motorcycle Club at Blank Canvas on February 14th and headline the Cockpit on February 8th.
