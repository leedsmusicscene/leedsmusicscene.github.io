Afraid Like You

This is an archive of the band profile for Afraid Like You.

Joe - Lead Vox & Guitar
Ford - Synth & Backing Vox
Chris - Bass
Mark - Drums & Backing Vox


Afraid Like You are four 20-22 year old lads from the outskirts of Leeds who love Indie, Rock and Dance music. In December 2009 they managed to fuse the three together into a fast paced, hook driven, melody machine that claims to produce more rhythm than the whole of Brazil. To get an idea either visit our myspace or put an old 90's dance music tape into a blender. Either way you will get the picture....
