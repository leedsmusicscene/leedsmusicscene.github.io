Live At Leeds 2010 full line up and timetable announced

This article was published in 2010.

To complete a week of festival line up announcements, the full and final Live At Leeds 2010 line up was this week confirmed. And the organisers have released the event timetable and programme so that you can plan your day's venue-hopping well in advance.

Over 150 bands will be appearing at 17 venues in Leeds as part of the festivities on Saturday 1st May 2010 and tickets for the day-long city-wide event are still available for £15 from See Tickets. Tickets are exchangeable for wristbands prior to the event.
