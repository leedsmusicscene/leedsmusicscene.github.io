96.3 Aire FM continue to offer airplay to local bands via its Music Express show...

This article was published in 2005.

96.3 Radio Aire is continuing to show its commitment to the up-and-coming music scene in Leeds in 2005 with the continuation of a weekly Music Express feature.

Now in its fifth month, the show is aired every Thursday between 8 and 9pm, and presenter Andy Philpott said, “We have featured some excellent tracks from local bands and musicians and aim to bring more of the same in 2005”.

Music Express features news and information on local bands and also have a highlighted “Track Of The Week” which gives up and coming acts the chance to have their recorded material played out on the air.

Any bands or solo artists that would like their music to be be considered for airplay or have a gig they want plugging should send the info and material to the address below or email the studio at musicexpress@radioaire.com.
