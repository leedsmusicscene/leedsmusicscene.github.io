The new EP from The Wick Effect will be released on 16th August

This article was published in 2010.

The Wick Effect are pleased to announce the release of the "Everknowing EP" on August 16th 2010, on iTunes, Amazon, Napster and many more digital download sites.

This EP features the songs "Go", "Been Let Down" and "Everknowing"; these songs can be previewed now on the band's official website.

"Been Let Down" has been nominated for Woho Music's Track Of The Month award for July 2010, and "Everknowing" will be featured on the debut compilation album by Dead Sober Records, celebrating the first ever Leeds Festival Fringe event, taking place during August 2010 across the city of Leeds.

All songs were recorded in May 2010 at Cottage Road Studios, Leeds. They were written and arranged by The Wick Effect and produced by Andy Hawkins.

A number of live dates and a BBC live acoustic session have been scheduled to coincide with this release. See the band's official website for more details.
