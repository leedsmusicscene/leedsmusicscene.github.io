Leeds Festival 2009: full Dance Stage line-up revealed

This article was published in 2009.

Just hours after confirming Faith No More as headliners of the NME / Radio One Stage at Leeds 2009, organisers Festival Republic have this evening announced the full line-up for the Dance Stage.

The Dance Stage takes place at Leeds Festival on Saturday 29th August (the Lock Up Stage is scheduled for Friday and Sunday).

The full line-up is as follows: DEADMAU5 (headliners), VITALIC, MSTRKRAFT, CHASE AND STATUS, LADY SOVEREIGN, THE CRYSTAL METHOD, THE QEMISTS, DAS POP, MIXHELL, CHIPMUNK, 3OH!3, MASTER SHORTIE and L'AMOUR L'AMOURGE.
