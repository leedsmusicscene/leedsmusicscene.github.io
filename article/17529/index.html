More acts announced for Bingley Music Live

This article was published in 2013.

The Fratellis, Loveable Rogues, Lucy Spraggan, The Lake Poets, The Temperance Movement and Charlie Brown are the latest acts confirmed for this year's Bingley Music Live.

They will join Primal Scream, The Cribs and The Wonder Stuff, who were announced for Bingley Music Live back in March.

Last year Bingley Music Live 2013 was shortlisted for the UK Festival Awards Best Medium Sized Festival award. This year the event will be held at Myrtle Park between Friday, 30th August and 1st September 2013.

Three-day weekend tickets are currently available via Yorkshire Gig Tickets priced at £45 (adults) and £30 (children).
