Frenetics give away free download ahead of debut EP

This article was published in 2012.

Frenetics have today announced that they will be giving away the lead track from their forthcoming debut EP.

The 'Broken Hand' EP will be released on 11th June 2012 and you can download the track 'Ella' now from Soundcloud

You can also watch the video for 'Ella' below.



The tracklisting for the EP is:

1. Ella
2. Satellites
3. See You On the Other Side
4. Swing Kids

You can watch Frenetics at their EP pre-release party at 360 Club at The Library in Leeds on 19th May and then again at Carpe Diem on 2nd June.
