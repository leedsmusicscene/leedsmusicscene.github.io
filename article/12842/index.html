Cissy to tour UK in July 2010 to promote new white label 12"

This article was published in 2010.

Cissy will tour the UK in July 2010, to promote the release of their 3-song white label 12", a self-release and the band's first set of songs commited to vinyl. The tour will be Cissy's third in as many years, the band having toured the UK and Europe with Origami (San Francisco) in 2008 and Das Boton (Chicago) in 2009.
