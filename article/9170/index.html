i concur to release 'Lucky Jack / Build Around Me' on March 10th 2008

This article was published in 2008.

The new double a-side from i concur, 'Lucky Jack/Build Around Me', will be released by Brew Records and distributed by Cargo on 10th March '08. It will be available on 7"vinyl and iTunes download.

Forest of Sound and Brew Records will be putting on a very special launch gig at the Brudenell in Leeds on the 8th March. As well i concur you'll also be able to see the superb Her Name Is Calla, Wintermute and The Pattern Theory. You'll also be able to get the single early on the night...

To pre-order the single visit www.myspace.com/brewrecords. You can also listen to both tracks in full. Also those that pre-order this single will receive a free remix of Lucky Jack by Bracken.

The band will also be embarking on a UK tour which will include about 6 shows with Vessels.
