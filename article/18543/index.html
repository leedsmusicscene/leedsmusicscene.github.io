The Dukes of Bevington reveal new track and video

This article was published in 2014.

'HOME.' is the brand new offering from The Dukes of Bevington and the the contagious indie-pop-rock track can be downloaded now via bandcamp on a pay what you want basis.

The track is taken from the band's forthcoming EP, a yet to be named release due out later this year. Watch the video:



Download the track here:



The Dukes of Bevington recently released 'Days & Nights' which saw the band receive continued support from BBC Introducing and local press.

Being likened to bands of a charming indie-pop ilk like Two Door Cinema Club and Temper Trap, The Dukes of Bevington showcase their own version of catchy indie-pop music with killer melodies.

The Dukes of Bevington take their place in the Futuresound heats at The Wardrobe on Friday 18th July in the hope of winning a place on the line up at this year's Reading & Leeds Festivals.
