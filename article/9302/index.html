Your Vegas sampler to be given free to Bloomingdale's (US) customers

This article was published in 2008.

New York (via Leeds) five-piece Your Vegas are to partner with Pan Am for the release of their Spring/Summer ‘08 collection of fashion accessories sold in Bloomingdale’s stores across the USA and the band will this month appear live in-store at the New York launch event. The promotion will provide every Pan Am customer with a free CD sampler of the band.

Your Vegas, whose music the Los Angeles Times calls “anthemic rock that rise above the pedestrian,” and whose forthcoming 2008 debut album, “A Town and Two Cities”, was chosen by Playboy magazine as “One To Watch in 2008,” originate from West Yorkshire and have recently made New York City their home.

The band’s debut album will be released in the US via Universal Republic Records on May 20th. However, there are no dates as yet for a UK launch.
