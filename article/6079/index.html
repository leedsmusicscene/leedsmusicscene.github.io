Xi to release limited-edition DVD single "Montauk" ...

This article was published in 2005.

Leeds rock band Xi have announced that their DVD single "Montauk" will be released at a special show on Saturday, December 10th at Joseph’s Well - just in time for Christmas! The DVD will feature two previously unreleased tracks and three videos, all produced and directed by Kevin Petch.

The single comes packaged as a numbered, limited edition (100 copies) and will also include a special website link to a “secret” web site full of features, un-released downloads and more!

Playing with Xi at their "Xmas Xtravaganza" will be Bruise, The Adventures of Loki, Rik Lee and new Leeds band British Fiction. Tickets are available now, with advanced ticket holders automatically entered into a free prize draw to win a copy of the DVD and other merchandise from all five acts, including CDs, t-shirts and badges.
