Three Peaks Split EP by Martin Plock, The Matt Burnside Band & Steph Stephenson

This article was published in 2012.

What happens when three Yorkshire based singer-songwriter acts get together? This excellent split EP from The Matt Burnside Band, Steph Stephenson and Martin Plock answers this question.

In order to re-enforce the Yorkshire connection, this EP has been called "Three Peaks" and will be available for download direct from their respective Bandcamp sites from the 31st March for a mere Â£3.

It will feature 2 tracks from The Matt Burnside Band: new track "Well, Well" and the previously released "Sandcastles"; Steph Stephenson has contributed a new song "Train"; while Martin has included a new banjo based song "So Low" as well as a remastered version of "Hometown Scene" from his debut album.

Each of the artists new tracks contain various collaborations between them including extra vocals and instrumentation.
