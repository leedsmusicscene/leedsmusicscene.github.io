With influences such as Joe Meek and Leonard Cohen, Paul Thomas Saunders fashions his own brand of alternative song that is poignantly intimate yet majestically arranged.

His EP was recorded over four nights in his Leeds basement and encompasses both his love for far-out production and good old-fashioned song writing.

Following an enthusiastic reception and a UK support tour with Blue Roses, Paul Thomas Saunders has a string of releases and dates lined up for 2010. He is featured on the new Dance to the Radio compilation 4x12" Volume 4 and was recently played on Lauren Laverne's 6Music show.
