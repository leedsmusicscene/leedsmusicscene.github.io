Out Of Focus

This is an archive of the band profile for Out Of Focus.

Formed in 2006, Out Of Focus, were definitely going to be something different. Combined with their music, and crazy-ass attitudes; a new type of band was formed: a talented, mentalist, determined and frequent hospital visitor of a band. All of this adding up to 100% pure entertainment! If you haven't seen or heard of these guys, you're missing out! The cleverly chosen name "Out Of Focus" describes the nature of this band perfectly. They're not just a band, they are born entertainers that will do anything to keep you at the edge of your seat, and I mean pretty much anything....
