Eureka Machines to release their third album in March

This article was published in 2013.

Eureka Machines are currently gearing up for their third album release in March 2013, following their enormously successful ongoing Pledge Music campaign.

The album, tentatively named "Remain In Hope", is produced by Chris Catalyst and Andy Hawkins (Pigeon Detectives, Hawk Eyes, Pulled Apart By Horses) and will be released to Pledgers in March 2013.

There will be a commercial release later in the year.

The band are also raising money for Cardiacs frontman Tim Smith, who remains in constant rehabilitation following a stroke in 2009, and 10% of funds raised via Pledge Music will go to Tim.

To coincide with the album release Eureka Machines head out on a UK tour this Spring and the tour includes a gig on Saturday 11th May at Brudenell Social Club with The Scaramanga Six (buy tickets).
