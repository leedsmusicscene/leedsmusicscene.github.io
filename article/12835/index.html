Nature Of The Beast

This is an archive of the band profile for Nature Of The Beast.

Nature Of The Beast is hairy rapcore monster made up of 4 musicians from Leeds-6. Marcus (vocals), Shua (guitar) & Liam (bass) who met through a love of riffs and facial hair. They found Alessandro (drums) hiding in a bat cave where he lived with Marcus. They got together and made music with mighty mighty sludgy green molten riffs and schizophrenic lyrics about Libyan dictators, Lil' Jon & crying yourself to sleep in the toilets of an Indian restaurant. Just imagine if komodo dragons could play guitar? And imagine if you had a pie in your hand right now?.
