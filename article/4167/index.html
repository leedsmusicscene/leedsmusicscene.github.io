Leeds Festival 2006: tickets available now!

This article was published in 2005.

With the best ever Carling Weekend: Reading and Leeds Festival still fresh in everyone’s memory (or if not, check out our Leeds 2005 Reviews) you can start to plan for next year already!

From now until September 10th (subject to availability), you can buy tickets for next year’s festival at 2005 prices (subject to a booking fee). This means that for only £125, you can be back next August Bank Holiday for more of the best bands at the UK’s best festival.

Tickets are available from the ticket hotline on 0870 060 3775 or log onto www.seetickets.com to get ready for next year now.
