Sidewinder

This is an archive of the band profile for Sidewinder.

Sidewinder, a 4-piece rock band from North/West Yorkshire. After previously performing cover gigs in and around Yorkshire the joining of Neil in the late summer of 2006 the band decided to created an original rock band writing and peforming at every opportunity. They are now performing many shows throughout the North of England and are planning a UK tour during July '07. Recently Sidewinder scored very high at the first heat of the WTP Battle of the Bands, scoring 24 points out of 25!

Nick Robinson - Vocals / Guitar
Lee Kitson - Guitar
Chris Robinson - Drums / Vocals
Neil Fitchew - Bass / Vocals .
