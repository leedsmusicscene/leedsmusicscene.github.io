Calls Landing announce details of their debut album

This article was published in 2015.

Leeds-based four-piece Calls Landing will release their debut album 'Heirlooms' on 11th May, 2015.

The first track to be revealed from the full-length is titled 'Belgium' and the its video can be viewed below.



'Heirlooms' is a journey through issues such as anxiety and distrust, alongside experiences of both important and transient personal relationships but ultimately is a beacon of positivity and hope in life. Full to the rafters of hooks and potential singalong favourites, Calls Landing's unique brand of emotive melodic goodness is poised to take them far.

Watch Calls Landing in Leeds at the Packhorse on Tuesday, 17th March.
