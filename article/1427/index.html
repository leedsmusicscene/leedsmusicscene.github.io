Little Japanese Toy all set for late-February single release on Shifty Disco

This article was published in 2003.

Little Japanese Toy release a new single on Monday 24th February through the Oxford-based label Shifty Disco; limited to 1,000 copies the single includes re-mastered versions of Disused No's, Now I'm Better and I Sunset. The 'Toy are scheduled to play a showcase at the Notting Hill Arts Centre on Saturday.

The band's next Leeds show will be at the Packhorse on Tuesday, 4th February when they play alongside Mama Scuba, Blank Space and Palo Alto. A launch party is expected late February.
