Monte Carlo make available the first part of their free album trilogy...

This article was published in 2004.

The first part of the FREE Monte Carlo album trilogy is nearly ready. A six-song taster of the album is now available at the band's website, and the full album will be available for download by the end of the month.

Parts 2 and 3 will be available at the end of April and May respectively and will be accompanied by free gigs: Packhorse (April 29th) and Dr Wu's (June 9th).
