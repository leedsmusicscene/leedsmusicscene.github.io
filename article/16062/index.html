Blacklisters unveil new single Trickf*ck

This article was published in 2012.

Blacklisters have today announced that their new single 'Trickf*ck' is available to stream online now. Listen to the track below or via bandcamp.com.


The single is released as a digital download on Monday 27th February 2012 through the Leeds label Brew Records.

'Trickf*ck' is the most visceral and heaviest from the upcoming debut album, dealing with the subject of handsome and yet incredibly stupid men.

The band's debut album will be released on Monday 4th April 2012 and was produced by Ross Halden and mastered by Carl Saff (Young Widows, Oxes, Melt Banana).
