Dance To The Radio announce a limited edition series of four 12" singles

This article was published in 2009.

To celebrate their 50th release local independent record label Dance To The Radio have this week announced the release of 4x12”, a series of four limited-edition 12” singles for 2009.

The first of the series, 4x12” Volume 1, features Leeds bands Pulled Apart by Horses, Wonderswan and Broadcast Society, plus New York’s hotly tipped Bear Hands.

Volume 1 is available to pre-order now from www.dancetotheradio.com and those that order one of the 500 limited-edition 12” singles will also receive free MP3s with the Wonderswan track downloadable immediately as part of the pre-order.

The first release comes with Wonderswan (“Hey Nature”) and Broadcast Society (“Behind Your Back”) on Side A, and Pulled Apart by Horses (“E = MC Hammer”) and Bear Hands (“What A Drag - Cale Parks Remix”) on Side B.
