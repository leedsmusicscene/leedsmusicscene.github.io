Grammatics to release a new single 'Double Negative' on October 26th

This article was published in 2009.

Grammatics will release their new single – ‘Double Negative’ – on October 26th via the Leeds-based Dance To The Radio record label. The single will be available on 7” vinyl format and comes backed with a cover of The Good Life’s ‘Notes In His Pockets’.

The single follows on from the band’s 2009 debut album, which was hailed as one of the year’s great arrivals and recently finished runner-up in Drowned in Sound’s Neptune Music Prize awards.

The Leeds quartet will join Bloc Party on tour next month, playing Leeds O2 Academy on 11th October. The band will also appear as part of the Brainwash Festival IV on November 1st.
