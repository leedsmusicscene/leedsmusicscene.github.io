Blacklisters to release 'I Can Confirm'

This article was published in 2011.

Blacklisters have announced the release of a new single, 'I Can Confirm', which is set for release on the Brew Records label on 12th December. The single will be the band's first release on the Leeds record label and is taken from their forthcoming debut album.

The single was produced by Ross Halden and mastered by Carl Saff (Young Widows, Oxes, Melt Banana) and follows on from their free download earlier this year.

To coincide with the release fans can purchase a limited 'I Can Confirm' t-shirt or screen print which were designed exclusively for the band by Drew Millward.

You can order the single from Brew Records.
