Pulled Apart by Horses release a new single in April

This article was published in 2012.

Following the over-whelming response to the release of their second album 'Tough Love' through Transgressive Records In January, Leeds band Pulled Apart by Horses will release 'Wolf Hand' as a digital only release on 23rd April.

According to the band 'Wolf Hand' is "our very own piercing tale of forgetting about ageing and staying forever young".

One of the album's many highlights, the track shows exactly why many are calling Pulled Apart by Horses the best British rock band in many years.

The album's previous single 'V.E.N.O.M.' received unprecedented support from Radio 1, 6 Music and XFM and 'Wolf Hand' looks certain to carry on the tradition, hot on the heels of an incredible UK tour that climaxed at a sold out show in London last month.
