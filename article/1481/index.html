Juxtaposition announce video and recording details...

This article was published in 2003.

Juxtaposition are busy rehearsing four new songs with Chantal Acda and Marissa van Mourik of Dutch/Belgian band Chacda before Chantal goes off to Iceland where she will be breaking-in wild Icelandic horses.

Some of the finished work (music, not horses) will be recorded on video in Darlington for work connected with a new TV outlet ... more of this news will be announced later.

In other Juxtaposition news, the video to the Take a Holiday single is now available from www.capturedmango.com.

For more information visit www.juxt.co.uk.
