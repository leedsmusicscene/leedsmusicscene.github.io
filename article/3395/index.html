The Hightones

This is an archive of the band profile for The Hightones.

The Hightones are bringing back rhythm and blues (the rock and roll kind) to the circuit adding a modern touch on the way. The current line up is Alex Dolata (Guitar/Vocals), Tom Lea (Guitar/Harmonica/Vocals), Mike Holman (Bass/Guitar) and Luke Jones (Drums). Drawing on their influences such as The Small Faces, Dylan and The Yardbirds they have created what is trully a unique sound amongst modern bands. The Hightones have been playing gigs in and around Leeds and received rave reviews when they headlined the Cockpit in September. They have just put the final touches to their debut EP, The Attic Tapes, and any enquiries about it should be made to Alex Dolata..
