Poor Man's Plunder

This is an archive of the band profile for Poor Man's Plunder.

In the month of July 2011 Poor Man's Plunder was born. Combining many years of past experience ranging from playing in a garage to playing live shows all over the country. The band has drawn influences from the entire range of musical genres from Dan's interests in Punk Rock right up to Chris' love of Death Metal. The band draws these together to provide a range of covers that any audience can sing along to whilst adding an array of original work with catchy riffs and lyrics from the heart and souls of the members. The band has one joint passion and that is to play and write music, this passion has driven the band forward from being a side project in a garage to being a powerhouse of music.

Daniel Harper - Percussion
Matthew Pocock - Guitars and Vocals
Chris Stead - Bass.
