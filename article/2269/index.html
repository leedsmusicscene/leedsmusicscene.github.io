The Cribs to release "You Were Always The One" in February...

This article was published in 2004.

The Cribs head out on tour in January/February with fellow local bands The Blueskins and 10,000 Things. The first 50 punters at each show will receive a limited edition sampler CD featuring all 3 bands, with the Leeds show taking place at Brudenell Social Club on 30th January.

On 23rd February The Cribs will release a follow-up single to their 2003 debut. On the Wichita Recordings label the new single will be titled You Were Always The One and you can expect a video to be appearing very soon. The band will also be featured live in the BBC Radio 1 studio on January 26th.
