Dance Tent headliners and line up announced for Leeds Festival 2004...

This article was published in 2004.

The Dance Tent line up has now been announced for Leeds Festival 2004.

The Friday evening will see a headline slot from Squarepusher, with the following also announced today: Ty (with full band), DJ Krush (Live), Radio 4, Fingathing, Buck 65, Client, Loose Cannons, Ivory, Chromeo and Roger.

The Saturday headliners of the Dance Tent will be Roots Manuva, supported throughout the day by 2 Many Djs, !!!, LCD Soundsystem, Shystie, Zoot Woman, Black Strobe, DJ Format & MC Abdominal, Blue States, Boom Boom Satellites and Brave Captain.

As usual, Sunday sees the Dance Tent turn into the Concrete Jungle Stage.
