Stu Hamm Masterclass comes to Leeds on 25th May

This article was published in 2014.

Legendary bass player Stu Hamm will be in Leeds this month delivering a multi-instrumental masterclass at Music Nation Leeds.

Stu Hamm is regarded by many as one of the greatest bass players of our time with a musical CV including working with Joe Satriani, Steve Vai and Frank Gambale to name a few. Stu is also the director of bass programs at the Musicians Institute in Hollywood and has toured the world as a clinician and instructor.

The masterclass promises to be a once in a life time opportunity to learn from one of the most respected musicians in the world, you'll have the chance to chat and play with Stu and listen to some great stories!

This event will be held at Music Nation Leeds at Cape Industrial Estate on Sunday May 25th from 16:30 - 18:00.

Tickets are limited so grab one while you can at musicnationleeds.com.
