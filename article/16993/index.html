Inspades

This is an archive of the band profile for Inspades.

The scars of past experience are worn proudly on the sleeves of INSPADES. Set amongst a backdrop of uncertain times and the desire to connect with something meaningful, INSPADES deliver a brand of challenging, textured modern metal.

Inspired by their home town's dark history, this powerful trio blend a beautifully absorbing clash of styles to produce a sound that is so much more than the sum of its parts. The road INSPADES has travelled has rarely been easy, but it is this perseverance and strength of conviction shown in the face of adversity that provides the emotion in their song writing.

Their focus is firmly on channeling their incredible chemistry into creating some of the most innovative and exciting music of their career.

Incendiary drumming, imposing bass, vocals that scream and soar, and guitars to rattle your ancestors. INSPADES cut through the noise with an explosive cocktail and a work ethic that's second to none.

So sever your ties, let the ghosts of bad times run free. Watch it all unfold and let the engineers of fresh meat cut through the din with raw power and energy.

Join the revolution and eat your heart out..
