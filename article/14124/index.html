Hadouken! to release their Oxygen EP in February

This article was published in 2011.

The Oxygen EP, the latest release from Hadouken!, will be available from iTunes on 14th February 2011.

The EP is backed by 'Mecha Love' and comes with three remixes of 'Oxygen', including Gemini, Alix Perix and Slugz & Joe London, and a remix of 'Mecha Love' from Nova.

'Mecha Love' was originally released as a single last year on the band's Surface Noise record label.
