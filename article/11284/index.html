Doctor Dot

This is an archive of the band profile for Doctor Dot.

Max - Guitar, Vocals
Tash - Guitar, Vocals
James - Bass Guitar
Andy - Trumpet
Stevie - Drums

Doctor Dot is an exciting, original new band formed at the University of Leeds in September of 2008. Their influences are as far ranging as ska, folk, progressive rock and metal, and this is clearly identifiable in their unique sounding compositions. Their songs are diverse and individual, varying from the powerful melodies of Chance Me and Invisible, to the intelligent fusions of salsa rock (The Stranger) and disco ska (Control). They played their first gig at the prestigious Northern Monkey venue in Leeds city centre, to an enthused audience. Doctor Dot's primary aim as a band is not only to create great music, but also to get people tapping their feet and filling the dance floor wherever they go..
