Furr reveal video for debut single

This article was published in 2016.

Leeds quartet Furr have revealed the video for their debut single 'Think Sharp Kid'.

The video sees the band rocking out amidst a neon glow stick haze with a performance that perfectly complements the angsty riffs, pulsing grooves and contagious chorus of 'Think Sharp Kid'.



Furr (formerly known as Humans As Ornaments) recently played with Vukovi and Fizzy Blood at the Brudenell Social Club, which was reviewed here on Leeds Music Scene.

The single was released on 3rd June through Riff Pop Records.
