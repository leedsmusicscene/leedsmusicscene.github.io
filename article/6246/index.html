The2nds

This is an archive of the band profile for The2nds.

The2nds are ferilised on Cleveland way in a small bedroom belonging to a fella named Beevers. Creating repetitive light hearted tunes, the three piece then known as all play no work went through song after song of Teenage traumatic toss before sttling down as 'No Credit!' Playing small venues, methodist halls and WMCs. But much has happened since we sucked our last dummy and we've started to move onto bigger challenges and the Yorkshire music scene, also signing on with Wirral based Indie Label Chromium Records. And plus, at 14/15 years old, we've still time ahead of us.......
