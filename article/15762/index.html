Tomorrow We Sail release 'The White Rose'

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds seven-piece Tomorrow We Sail have announced the release of 'The White Rose' on November 28th 2011. The single will be available on limited edition CD and download format from November 28th.

Tomorrow We Sail is a collection of musicians that formed in 2009. They have spent the past two years developing and morphing their sound and songs into what is now a vast landscape of guitar, piano and orchestral textures.

After self-releasing debut EP 'The Common Fire' last year, the group retreated to their basement studio and rehearsal space to create the foundations of a new single release 'The White Rose'. Once again self-released the latest release features two tracks: 'The White Rose' and 'Leningrad'.
