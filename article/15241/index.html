Double Muscle & TwoDucksDisco team up for unique release

This article was published in 2011.

Best Enemies Records have announced details of the first edition of Art Department, a unique digital singles collection accompanied by handmade original prints by talented artists.

'Tommy' by Double Muscle will be released on Monday 26th September 2011, backed with 'Lungs' and a exceptional cover version of Minutemen's 'Cut'. Accompanying the single will be a limited edition of handmade prints by TwoDucksDisco (Cameron Steward) and a short documentary showing the creation of these unique pieces of art.

Fans can pre-order to secure a print from Monday 12th September 2011 - at which point they will receive an immediate download link for the single prior to its release.

On that same date the band support Screaming Females at The Brudenell Social Club, a very limited amount of prints and CD copies of the single will be available to buy and takeaway on the night.

This second release from the Leeds based scuzz punks will lead up to a tour and full-length release in Autumn 2011.

Best Enemies Records hope to announce details of the second edition of Art Department soon.
