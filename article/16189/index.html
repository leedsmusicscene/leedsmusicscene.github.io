Leeds Festival line up to be announced on 12th March

This article was published in 2012.

Festival Republic have today confirmed that tickets will go on general sale at 7.15pm on Monday 12th March 2012, when the headliners and many more names for the festival will be officially revealed.

The line-up announcement will take place live on air on BBC Radio One.

Ticket prices for Leeds 2012 have not yet been announced but are expected to be in the region of Â£200 for a weekend ticket.

Festival fans have the option to pay for their tickets in full or by using the ticket instalment plan across either two or four equal payments in order to spread the cost.

You will find a list of approved Leeds ticket sellers via: bit.ly/leeds2012tickets.

Members of Vodafone VIP will be able to buy their tickets from 9am on Friday, 9th March and there will be an exclusive 1 hour pre-sale at HMV Leeds from 18.10pm on 12th March.

As is the case every year the internet is running wild with Leeds Festival rumours, the main rumours being Foo Fighters, The Cure, Green Day, Kaiser Chiefs and Kasabian amongst many others.
