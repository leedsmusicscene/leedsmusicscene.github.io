StrangeForms 2015 line-up announced

This article was published in 2014.

The first wave of bands for 2015's StrangeForms Festival has just been announced. After a successful first year showcasing post-rock, post-metal, math-rock and leftfield acts from around the UK the festival is back, again being held at Wharf Chambers in Leeds over the Easter Bank Holiday weekend (Saturday 4th April and Sunday 5th April).

Announced today were:

Her Name Is Calla
Telepathy
Alright The Captain
Tacoma Narrows Bridge Disaster
Falls
Waking Aida
Tomorrow We Sail
Worriedaboutsatan
Trojan Horse
Envoys
Civil Protection
Fly On Byrd, Fly On
