Bingley Music Live 2012 add Nero, Kids In Glass Houses and more

This article was published in 2012.

Bingley Music Live 2012 organisers have this morning announced that the following bands will join the line up in Myrtle Park in September:

NERO
KIDS IN GLASS HOUSES
KATZENJAMMER
KING CHARLES
SKINNY LISTER

They join an existing line up that included The Pigeon Detective, Hard-Fi and The Charlatans amongst many others. You can view the current line up on our Bingley Music Live page.

Bingley Music Live 2012 will feature over 60 acts across the 3 stages with loads more national, international and local talent to be revealed over the coming weeks.

The event attracts over 40,000 music fans over its three days and is funded, organised and managed independently by Bradford Council.
