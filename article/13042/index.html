Mimi & The Leaders

This is an archive of the band profile for Mimi & The Leaders.

Based in Leeds and York, Mimi & The Leaders are a 3 piece band with a big infectious sound.

We are currently in the studio perfecting the new songs and hope to have a CD ready for distribution in the next few months.

Amy - Lead Vocalist/Songwriter has featured on Rhythm Code's single "Pushin Me" and is set to impress with her vocal style on the new tracks for Mimi and the Leaders.

Charles - Songwriter/Drums/Samples has played for many bands in the UK and has performed at prestigious venues, such as Hed Kandi, Glastonbury, and the Federation Club in Leeds, The HiFi Club and The Brudenell Club.

David - Guitarist/FX/Bass has performed alongside Charles in various bands and also played for The Bluefoot Project who have played Glastonbury and recently obtained Radio 1's album of the month.

Johnny - Guitar FX is The youngest and most enthusiastic member of the band he fills out the sound well and his solo's impress.

Mimi & The Leaders are set to storm the UK music industry and will have you tapping your feet immediately..
