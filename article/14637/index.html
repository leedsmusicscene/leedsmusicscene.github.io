Lock Up Stage line up revealed for Leeds Festival 2011

This article was published in 2011.

The line up for the Lock Up Stage at Leeds Festival 2011 was announced today by Mike Davies. The presenter of The Punk Rock Show on BBC Radio 1 and curator of the Lock Up Stage revealed the line up this morning.

Leeds Festival takes place over the August Bank Holiday weekend, from Friday 26th August to Sunday 28th August 2011. Headliners of the Lock Up Stage on Friday will be Descendents and on the Sunday will be The Mighty Mighty Bosstones. The Saturday will be the Dance Stage instead of the Lock Up Stage.

The full line up on Friday is:

Descendents
Flogging Molly
Face To Face
Hot Water Music
Bedouin Soundclash
Off!
The Black Pacific
The Menzingers
Your Demise
Spy Catcher
Mouthwash

The full line up on Sunday is:

The Mighty Mighty Bosstones
King Blues
The Bronx
Capdown
Leftover Crack
Comeback Kid
Boysetsfire
Street Dogs
Teenage Bottlerocket
Title Fight
Sharks

There are more bands are still to be announced, including the line ups for the Festival Republic Stage and the BBC Introducing Stage. Keep your eyes on the Futuresound 2011 announcement, which will provide Leeds bands with the opportunity to join the line up at both Reading and Leeds sites.
