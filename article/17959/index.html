Esper Scout release 'Poet Curses' on Monday 14th October

This article was published in 2013.

Esper Scout release their debut mini-album 'Poet Curses' this Monday, 14th October. The album was recorded, produced and mixed by the band with previous collaborator Paul Crompton at Eiger Studios and their own Hut 57 in Leeds.
