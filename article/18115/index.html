New video single from Jon Gomm out now

This article was published in 2013.

The new video/single from Jon Gomm - titled 'Wukan Motorcycle Kid' - is out today.



'Wukan Motorcycle Kid' is the second single taken from Jon's new album 'Secrets Nobody Keeps'. It is about the Wukan Uprising of 2011, and today marks 2 years since the death of Xue Jinbo.

The song, album, guitar tab, signed CDs and gift packs are available now from jongomm.com/store.

Jon has a rare guitar clinic coming up in Leeds in February 2014, and you can get tickets from musicnationleeds.com.
