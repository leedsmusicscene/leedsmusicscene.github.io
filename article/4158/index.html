Nikoli to release their third single - "Stay Safe Tonight" - in September

This article was published in 2005.

Nikoli release their third single on 2nd September, the first of two to be released this year on their own East Park Records label. The single - Stay Safe Tonight - will be available at gigs over the next few weeks. The band play a launch night at Mixing Tin on the day of the release and again at the "The Music Guru" event at the Brudenell Social Club on 24th September.
