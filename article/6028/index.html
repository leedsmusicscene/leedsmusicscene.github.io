Young Professionals make new album "Pleasure Time" available for free download

This article was published in 2005.

Young Professionals have released an album entitled "Pleasure Time", which was written and recorded at their own Hope House Studios in Leeds. The album documents eight months in the lives of a band living in Leeds before they go their separate ways.

Formed (albeit temporarily) from the ashes of The Bilderberg Group, Young Professionals was an experiment, an attempt to contradict the way in which their predecessors wrote, recorded and played by doing everything together at the same time - hence "Pleasure Time" is almost entirely a live recording!

The entire album can be downloaded for free from the band's website, along with 2 albums and 3 singles by The Bilderberg Group.
