fallaroundhere

This is an archive of the band profile for fallaroundhere.

fallaroundhere are an indie band currently based in Bradford in the North of England. Having been about in various manifestations for a few years, the band finally settled on a permanent line up in 2004, releasing "136 miles E.P." in May 2005. Playing music thats is pitched somewhere between mainstream indie such as Coldplay and Doves, and bands such as Mogwai, Cursive, Arab Strap and Bright Eyes, the band are currently establishing themselves as a formidable live act, picking up new friends and fans with each show.....
