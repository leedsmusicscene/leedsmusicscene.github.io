Afraid of the Dark announce new recording plans

This article was published in 2002.

Afraid of the Dark are set to start recording new material in November, although details are sketchy at this stage. It's not been too long since the band released their mini-album The Sun Is Your Enemy during the summer, although they have announced that their new material will take on "a darker, rockier style that really represent the band well".

Catch the band live in Leeds when they appear at the Packhorse on 18th November (with Four Planes In Four Days) and 14th December (with Itch).
