Lizardmen

This is an archive of the band profile for Lizardmen.

As with all plains of surreal notions the time came for it to be expressed - it came in the form of Lizardmen. Three different varities of nature come together to explore what interests them most, influence coming from books, film and sound. Welcome to the Lizardmen. A Sci-Fi-Rumble come exploration into the recesses of whats possible. Heres the legend, as told by a dear friend... "The name, to extend this process of filling, derives from a theory concocted by a crackpot colleague at the call centre of death, in York. He was, and remains, convinced that such a species toured the Earth pre-humankind. He has since gone to Mexico and wedded a Central American filly, but his memory lives on. Love x" Louis Baamberrr..
