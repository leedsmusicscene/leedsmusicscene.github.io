Calls Landing to play Leeds Festival as winners of Futuresound 2015

This article was published in 2015.

Thursday night saw the conclusion of the annual Futuresound competition with six winners confirmed to appear at Leeds Festival 2015.

Overall winners were Calls Landing, who will now play on the Festival Republic Stage this coming August Bank Holiday.

Runners up - who will each appear on the BBC Introducing Stage - were Happy Daggers, NARCS, Samuel S Parkes, Treason Kings and Where Fires Are.

Well done to all bands - we'll see you at Leeds Festival!
