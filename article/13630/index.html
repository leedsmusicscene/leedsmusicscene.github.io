Eskimo Sandwich

This is an archive of the band profile for Eskimo Sandwich.

George Boomsma - Vocals, Keyboards, Trumpet
Richard Hounsome - Bass Guitar, Effects Pedals
Alasdair McDougal - Drums & Percussion, Backing Vocals, Saxophone
Sebastian Rab - Guitars, Backing Vocals, Keyboards

Inspired by the likes of Muse, Radiohead and The Music, the four-piece from Leeds/Northallerton are developing fast in the music maze. Front-man theatrics, effects pedals and afros are the order of the day at an Eskimo Sandwich gig, with the Eskimo experience gaining an enthusiastic fan base, reaching ever further from home.

After playing North Yorkshire's Festival 07 featuring Lil' Chris and The Holloways, winning the Hambleton District Battle of the Bands and playing live on BBC Radio, Eskimo Sandwich recorded their first 3-track EP - "My Sunlight".

In July of 2008 Eskimo Sandwich played the main stage of CultureShock Festival, supporting Boy Kill Boy and Utah Saints. The band set out in 2009 with the release of their second EP - "Which Way To Go?" along with an expanding list of live dates..
