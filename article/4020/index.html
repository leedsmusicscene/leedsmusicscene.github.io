Kid iD

This is an archive of the band profile for Kid iD.

Leeds based Ralph played some gigs as a solo artist for about a year before starting Leeds Uni and jamming with fellow musos josh and will. Jamming lead to a few gigs and after a while the group grew with an extra percussionist Joe and later Pete and Ian with the brass section. It was a slow progression of sound, moving from the solo folk song writing of Ralph to a fusion of sounds including Jazz, Funk, Ska and Rock :-)

One of our songs, The Waiter Song is currently on a TV Ad (digital only) so keep your eyes peeled.

We are continuing to play the Leeds circuit so hopefully we'll see you all a gig sometime soon..
