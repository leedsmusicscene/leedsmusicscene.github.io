The Pigeon Detectives release "I Found Out" on November 6th

This article was published in 2006.

The Pigeon Detectives release a new single on Monday, 6th November. Available through the Leeds-based Dance To The Radio record label, live favourite “I Found Out” is released on 2 x 7”, CD and Digital Download formats.

The B-Sides for the single are “Left Alone” and “Hello (My Old Friend)”. The CD also comes with the video to “I Found Out“.

The Pigeon Detectives play at Leeds Metropolitan University on November 7th before heading out of the country to support Kaiser Chiefs on some of their European tour dates.
