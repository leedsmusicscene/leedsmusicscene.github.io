The Spills release their 'Smoke Signals EP'

This article was published in 2010.

Following their previous double A-side 7" single, Wakefield band The Spills will release their 'Smoke Signals EP' on Monday 19th July.

The 5 track EP was produced by Jamie Lockhart and Lee Smith at Greenmount Studios and will be available on CD from Jumbo, Crash, and Norman Records.
