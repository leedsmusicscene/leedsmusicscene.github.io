T.O.Y.S

This is an archive of the band profile for T.O.Y.S.

T.O.Y.S are:

Dave (Acutes)
Adam (Manhatten Love Suicides)
Eddy (Downdime)

T.O.Y.S are a three piece indiepop band formed in Leeds in 2010. David Kitchen (The Acutes, Angry Sandwich) plays a baby Yamaha keyboard through a mighty speaker, Adam John Miller (Manhattan Love Suicides, The Medusa Snare, The Wednesday Club) plays fuzz bass and Eddy Lines (Manhattan Love Suicides, Downdime) plays drums. A collaboration project that quickly took on momentum, the result is an uptempo blend of lo-fi pop, krautrock and dancefloor indie..
