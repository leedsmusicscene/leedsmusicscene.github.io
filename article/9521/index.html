Wild Beasts release their debut album this summer

This article was published in 2008.

Wild Beasts will release their debut album, 'Limbo, Panto' through the Domino Records label on 16th June. The album will be followed a fortnight later by new single, 'The Devil's Crayon' (backed with 'Treacle Tin').

The album tracklisting is 'Vigil for a Fuddy Duddy'; 'The Club of Fathomless Love'; 'The Devil's Crayon'; 'Woebegone Wanderers'; 'The Old Dog'; 'Please, Sir'; 'His Grinning Skull'; 'She Purred ,While I Grrred'; 'Brave Bulging Buoyant Clairvoyants'; and 'Cheerio Chaps, Cheerio Goodbye'.
