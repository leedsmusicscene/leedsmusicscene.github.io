Gary Stewart to release his debut album

This article was published in 2010.

Gary Stewart has announced that this debut album - titled 'Boy Cries Wolf'- will be released on September 13th 2010 through the Babaganoush Records label.

Gary has been working on his album for over a year. It was recorded at his house, with one microphone and a little portable mixing desk, and features a mixture of old and new songs.

The album includes guest appearances from Ellen Smith (from Ellen and the Escapades), Martyn Roper (Lovesick Cowboys/Old Time String Band) and Rosie Doonan.

The launch for 'Boy Cries Wolf' will take place on Thursday 16th September at the Brudenell Social Club. Tickets are £6 - or £10 with a copy of the album.
