The Music to feature on Channel 4

This article was published in 2002.

The Music will be featured in the Channel 4 "4 Play" series this Wednesday night (Thursday morning) at 1.00am. The mini documentary includes exclusive interviews with the band together with live footage filmed in Japan and studio footage shot during the recording of the album.

The band are currently on tour around the UK, heading off around Europe and the US in October and November. A further UK tour is scheduled for January 2003.
