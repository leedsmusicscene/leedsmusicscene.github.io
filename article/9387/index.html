Medicine For The Meek

This is an archive of the band profile for Medicine For The Meek.

Medicine For The Meek formed in early 2007 when singer songwriter Shara Meek collaborated with some of Leeds Finest musicians. This unmistakable hybrid of country rock, folk and Spanish influences, blended with compelling vocals and embracing melodies, brings you the unique sound that no other band has. Medicine For The Meek is without a doubt a captivating live band with an unusual twist. Be prepared to be enticed into their western world where the tales of the unexpected come true.

Shara Meek - Rhythm Guitar / Lead Vocals
Louis Stanton-Linder - Lead Guitar
Rob Reed - Rhythm Guitar
Alaric Lewis - Bass
Mark "Marshall" Lister - Drums and Percussion
Jeff Barry - Harmonica.
