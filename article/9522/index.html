Pifco will release their debut album on July 7th

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds duo Pifco will release their debut album on July 7th.

Released on the Run Of The Mill Records label, ‘Pifco a go go’ is the band’s first official release since they began producing music together in 2004 - although they have featured on many compilations and have released a tape EP in that time.

Already well established within the Leeds music scene with a constant run of gigs supporting the likes of Pram, Scout Niblet, The Long Blondes, a.P.A.T.t and No Age, Pifco’s grunge sound is reminiscent of early Sonic Youth, with a heavy dose of jerky punk rock similar to Coachwhips and a splash of electro indie pop akin to The Fall.

The 11-track album includes the songs ‘Jumping Sickness’; ‘Snorkle Stalker’; ‘Jonny No Name’; ‘Aluminium Shoes’; ‘Mixer’; ‘Get!!’; ‘The Long Island Incident’; ‘The Embassy Dinner’; ‘Race Course Race Horse’; ‘Face Wall Paper’; and ‘Cliff Jumper and Hound’.

Pifco play to embark on a UK tour in September.
