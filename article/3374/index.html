Nerve Engine announce album launch party details ...

This article was published in 2004.

On Friday 3rd December Nerve Engine take their unique brand of anthemic rock to the Fenton to launch their new album Underneath The Sprawl. Copies of the album (recorded by the band themselves in keeping with their "fierce DIY attitude") will be onsale direct from the band for Â£5.

Joining the party are local favourites Burst Rondo and new all-girl goth-rockers Blood Sundae.
