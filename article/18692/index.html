Thirty Hearts

This is an archive of the band profile for Thirty Hearts.

Formed in 2014, The band is are a four piece who span the pennines with members basedin Manchester, Bradford and Leeds.

With a growing catalogue of music, an energetic live performance and a recent string of successful gigs, Thirty Hearts are putting a big emphasis on getting their name out there. Not just to get people listening but also to network with other bands, promoters, radio stations and venues.

Musically, the band strive to find a balance between heavy, dark riffs and light, melodic pop moments - often delivered with an element of surprise..
