AC & The Funtime Band

This is an archive of the band profile for AC & The Funtime Band.

AC & The Funtime Band are an eight-piece Soul / Motown Band which started in 2010. We are a group of young, talented and experienced professional musicians from around the Leeds area. The band's repertoire includes hits from many of the great soul legends such as Stevie Wonder, Martha Reeves & The Vandellas, Otis Reading, Amy Winehouse, Aretha Franklin and many more which have the crowd singing and dancing... and always shouting for more! Our usual line up includes Vocalist, Trumpet, Tenor Sax, Baritone Sax, Guitar, Bass, Keyboards, and Drums. Playing That Funky Music at a venue near you soon!.
