Star Bodixa single update

This article was published in 2002.

Star Bodixa played an acoustic set on LBC (London Radio) on Saturday to launch their new single "Fairytailed" which is released on 30th September. They play an acoustic set on Talk Radio (James Whale show), and on Friday shoot off to Wales to film an acoustic set for Cable TV (BBC2 Wales see p28 of this month's Q magazine).

A dream team comprising Julian Spear (Red Shadow national radio plugger), Liam Walsh (Red Alert regional radio plugger), Dave Clarke (Planet Earth national press), and Sarah Crowther (Pomona - regional press)has been put together to work on "Fairytailed", and later the album release. Concorde International Agency are now handling their tour booking.

The album "And They Danced On Glass" has finished production and advance copies will be sent out shortly. Release date set for February 2003.
