Weirds

This is an archive of the band profile for Weirds.

Weirds are a psych rock quartet formed in early 2013 whose debut single was released on Nottingham based Denizen Recordings. The band from Leeds, UK consists of Aidan Razzall, Matthew Vaughn, Zachary Thomas and David Nash.

Crocodile/Yolk on Denizen Recordings released 28th May 2013.
Off The Hook/Heavy Rain on Too Pure Singles Club to be released 13th April 2015.
