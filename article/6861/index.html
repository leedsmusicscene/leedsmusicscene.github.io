Lewis Denby to release 'Lookout Posts and Telescopes' in July

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds musician Lewis Denby has this week announced details of his new CD.

Released on July 16th on the GrooveStealer label, 'Lookout Posts and Telescopes' is a seven-song stripped-down acoustic record.

To promote the release, Lewis is embarking on a short tour of the country this summer and plays Mixing Tin in Leeds on Wednesday 2nd August.
