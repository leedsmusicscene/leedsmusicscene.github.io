Codego annouce release of new EP

This article was published in 2012.

Codego have announced the release of their new EP titled 'Leopard' which is due for release on Fri 6th July.
The EP features four tracks all recorded and mixed at Loom Studio with producer Grant Henderson and will be available on cd and digital download from bandcamp & iTunes, Amazon etc..
