Urban Fusion 2: more opportunities for local musicians...

This article was published in 2004.

Are you be part of an existing music group or an individual looking to become involved in the local music scene? Then Urban Fusion 2 is your opportunity to shine!

Urban Fusion 2 is an exciting opportunity for communities in the Harehills Neighbourhood Renewal area to join together and share cultures, heritage and traditions through music.

In collaboration with the St James Partnership this project will offer the chance for music groups from all cultures and communities to create music with the help of professionals in the industry, record the track in a professional studio to create a CD and take part in a live tour of the area.

For anyone who thinks they may be interested, come to our drop in sessions where you can meet us, the artists you could be working with and generally find out if this project is right for you.

Drop in sessions take place at Hall Place Music Studios on Tuesday, 27 January (7-9pm) and Saturday, 31 January (1-4pm)

For more information contact Leeds City Council's Arts & Regeneration Unit on 0113 247 8049 or email arts@leeds.gov.uk.
