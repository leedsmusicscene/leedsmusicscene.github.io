Want to win £2,000? Leeds Battle Of The Bands details announced...

This article was published in 2006.

Taking Liberties - who run students nights across Leeds city centre venues - have announced a Battle Of The Bands competition starting in September.

Although the venue has yet to be finalised, details of the prize money has… and the winning Leeds band stands to win £2,000.

Sponsored by Stella, the competition will be run as a series of heats and an overall Grand Final. If you’re a band or musician in Leeds and wish to enter then post your demos to:

Battle Of The Bands,
The Stable,
6 Bainbrigge Road,
Leeds LS6 3AD

The deadline for demos is 13th September.
