Vendettas - What You Say Girl

This is a review of "What You Say Girl" recorded by Vendettas. The review was written by Parcs in 2014.

At close to just a year as a unit, Leeds' own 60's inspired trio Vendettas continue to pump out hits which force you off from the sofa and onto the rock and roll dance floor.

Set to a backdrop of "Woah's" a-plenty, What You Say Girl rams home the infectious sound that characterises the mod rock style which Vendettas adopt. The intensity of the track fails to let up at any moment, heightened by a neat guitar solo, and sweetened by a perfect vocal outro. Clocking in around the 3 minute mark, What You Say Girl is simple, and clearly makes an impact. Yet more solid rock and roll from the local three-piece.

The timing of this release is perfect, as the boys are set to open The Wardrobe's stage at this year's Live at Leeds event. Catch them there at 12:00pm on 3rd May.
