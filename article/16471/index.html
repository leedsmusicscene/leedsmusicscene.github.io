Live At Leeds 2012 timetable revealed

This article was published in 2012.

With less than 3 weeks to go organisers have today confirmed the final acts and stage times for the 6th annual Live at Leeds.

The event boasts a huge line-up of music over the May bank holiday weekend including The Enemy, Marina & The Diamonds, Los Campesinos!, The Subways, LadyHawke, Chew Lips, Alkaline Trio, I Like Trains, Ghostpoet and many more.

Organisers have also announced two Hangover Events, both to be held at Leeds Brudenell Social Club. The first takes place on Sunday 6th May 2012 and will be headlined by Dan Sartain, with support from Matadors, The Mcgowns, and The Reacharounds.

The following day, on Monday 7th May 2012, it will be the turn of Male Bonding, Young Magic, Au Palais, Azores, and Double Muscle to take to the stage.

You can gain free entry to both events if you have a Live At Leeds Wristband (note however that priority will be given to ticket holders).
