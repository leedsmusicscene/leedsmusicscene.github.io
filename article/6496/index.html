Shut Your Eyes And You'll Burst Into Flames to release their debut single on April 10th

This article was published in 2006.

A recent burst of Dance To The Radio single releases is completed on Monday 10th April with the release of Leeds five piece Shut Your Eyes And You'll Burst Into Flames' debut single.

Available as a limited edition 7" (500 copies), the single features the songs "Signal Noise" and "Kingdom Of Goats" and is available for pre-order from the Dance To The Radio website.
