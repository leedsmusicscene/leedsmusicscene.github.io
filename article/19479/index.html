Vaynes, The

This is an archive of the band profile for Vaynes, The.

Leeds based sleazy rock and roll band hugely influenced by Johnny Thunders. First release was in 1986. Previously The Dead Vaynes. Stevie Vayne kept the name after their split to form Stevie Vayne & The Slaves.

Ever present members ...

Steve Hulme (Stevie Vayne) - also played in The Dead Vaynes, Stevie Vayne & The Slaves, To Be Continued

Mike Ingham (Mick Vayne, Prince Michael Vayne) - also played in The Dead Vaynes, Shake Appeal

Other members through the various incarnations of the band ...

Adam Pearson

Craig Adams (Craig Super Hero, Space Cadet No.1) - also played in The Alarm, The Dead Vaynes, The Mission, Sisters Of Mercy, Spear Of Destiny

Gerard Campbell (Gerry Famous) - also played in The Expelaires

Ian Hargreaves (Haggis, Uncle Haggis) - also played in Bastard, The Dead Vaynes, The Honeyslides, Stryker

Jessica Fischer

Paul Harrison (Harry Harrison, Sir Harry Vayne) - also played in Abrasive Wheels, The Dead Vaynes

Tony Nevison (Nev Nevison) - also played in Abrasive Wheels, The Dead Vaynes, Zuma.
