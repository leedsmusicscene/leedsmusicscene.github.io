Corinne Bailey Rae to re-release "Like A Star" on October 9th

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds solo artist Corinne Bailey Rae will re-release “Like A Star” (originally her debut single) on Monday, 9th October. Available across 7", CD and maxi CD formats the single comes with two new tracks: "Emeraldine", and a cover of Aretha Franklin's "Daydreaming". The release also includes the Amp Fiddler remix of "Enchantment" and the video to “Like A Star“.

Corinne recently won two awards (Best UK Female and Best UK Newcomer) at the MOBOs in London and is nominated for Best UK & Ireland act at this year's MTV Europe Music Awards.

Corinne Bailey Rae appears live at Leeds University on October 8th.
