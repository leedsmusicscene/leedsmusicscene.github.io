Castrovalva will release 'Thug Poetry' on 2nd November

This article was published in 2009.

Castrovalva will release their new single on 2nd November 2009 via the Brew Records label. The release - titled 'Thug Poetry' - sees the introduction of Leemun Smith on vocals, who was responsible for the artwork on the band’s debut selt-titled mini album.

The single includes the tracks ‘Thuglife’ and ‘Outlawz’ and can be pre-ordered now from the Brew Records website.

Castrovalva will play live at The Library on Friday, 30th October as part of the Brainwash Festival IV.
