Stalking Horse to release 'Waterhole' on 7th November

This article was published in 2011.

Stalking Horse, the new project from This Et Al frontman Wu, has announced the release of a digital single, 'Waterhole'.

The single will be released on the ILR record label on November 7th and comes backed with 'Broken Brain'. ILR (I Like Records) is the label set up and run by Leeds band I Like Trains. 'Waterhole' is the record label's first release to feature another band.
