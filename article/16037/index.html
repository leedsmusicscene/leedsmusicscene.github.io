I Like Trains enter the studio to record new album

This article was published in 2012.

I Like Trains have this week announced that they have entered the studio with Mercury Prize nominated producer Richard Formby (Wild Beasts) to record their as yet untitled third album.

The band hope that Formby, best known for his recent work with Wild Beasts on the albums 'Smother' and 'Tiny Dancers', can push and evolve their sound already developed over the previous two albums.

The news follows the band's recent unveiling of the epic video for the track 'Sea of Regrets' which has racked up over 37,000 views within a matters of weeks.
