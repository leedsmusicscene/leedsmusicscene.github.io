O Fracas release a limited edition 7" single on 12th September...

This article was published in 2005.

One of the highlights of this year's Lee Sounds Stage, O Fracas have announced the release of their debut single "Zeroes And Ones" b/w "What Jim Hears". The limited (only 500 copies) edition 7" single is released on the Marquis Cha Cha label on Monday, 12th September and can be pre-ordered now from www.puregroove.co.uk.

O Fracas have a launch party for the single on the same day at the Faversham venue with Bromheads Jacket.
