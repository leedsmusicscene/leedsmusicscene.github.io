Siamese Twist - Adored

This is a review of "Adored" recorded by Siamese Twist. The review was written by Sam Panasuik in 2011.

Siamese Twist have the right vocals and musical attitude to grab peoples' attention and make them rock out with them.

Their 4 track EP "Adored" is a great soundtrack for the warm British summer we're having, as I listened in my garden I knew the five piece were special. The vocal drawls of Rebecca are infectious, and after fourth track "Sympathy Riot" has come to an end, you are left craving more.

First track GO! (Upside Down) is the stand out track for me. With its lead guitar chords heavy yet with a dancey beat, this will be on my iPod all summer, and the vocals are excellent for a sing along session.

Brilliant album from Siamese Twist. Hopefully the Yorkshire 5 piece will be back with more spectacular tracks soon!
