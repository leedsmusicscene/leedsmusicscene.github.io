Galaxians debut US release is out now

This article was published in 2014.

GALAXIANS debut US release - a self-titled 4-song vinyl 12" - is available to buy now.

The record has been released by growing Atlanta dance label Rotating Souls, run by Pittsburgh-born Curt Jackson, and features four songs from the band's current live set.

The band came in contact with Rotating Souls Records in 2013 when drummer Matt Woodward bought a number of titles from the label. The initial connection was made through RSR recording artists East Liberty Quarters.

The new EP is available online at juno.co.uk, with vinyl copies soon to be available in selected UK shops, and through the band's Bandcamp page - galaxians.bandcamp.com.

Details:

Title: GALAXIANS RSR EP
Label: ROTATING SOULS
Cat: RSR X
Track list: A1) Blasting Off // A2) My Shit Is Custom B1) Nightlife B2) Fire & Magic

Galaxians also have a second US record scheduled for release in the coming weeks on New York label Dither Down records.
