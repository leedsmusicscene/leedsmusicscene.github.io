Leeds bands to feature on Tip Toe Records podcast

This article was published in 2010.

Tip Toe Records have today announced details of their second podcast, which is available for download now from iTunes. The latest podcast from the label includes songs from a number of bands from Leeds.

The full tracklisting is:

Buen Chico - 'Happiness is Important'
Fran Rodgers - 'I See Horses Flee'
Hail! the planes - 'At Least They're Playing the Beatles'
Insect Guide - 'Dark Day + Nights'
Mechanical Owl - 'Snowdonia'
Milk Wood - 'The Passenger'
Olfar - 'Harbour Hymn'
Wingman - 'Skating'

You can also get the podcast from tiptoerecords.jellycast.com/podcast/feed/5.
