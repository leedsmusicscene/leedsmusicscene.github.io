Yellow Stripe Nine to release new single on May 23rd...

This article was published in 2005.

Yellow Strip Nine have chosen "One Look (It's Love!)" as their third single for Decadent Recordings. The three-track single will include "The Greatest Game" and brand new song "I'll Sing With All My Heart". The single is scheduled for release on Monday 23rd May.

Yellow Stripe Nine play at the Tea Time Shuffle at The HiFi Club on Friday 6th May.
