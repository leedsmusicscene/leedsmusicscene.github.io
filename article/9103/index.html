Men Of A Certain Age

This is an archive of the band profile for Men Of A Certain Age.

Biography: Formed in 2007 with the intention to be a band to brighten their audience's day through good music and comedy aimed at themselves. Playing a mixture of covers spanning 30 years to the present and including some lampoons together with original material exploring life experiences, they aim to have you singing or dancing or grinning or even all three! See, hear, laugh, participate and go home feeling younger (than the band) "A great night out" - catch them between their hip replacements now!.
