Galaxians second US vinyl release announced!

This article was published in 2013.

The second of Galaxians' upcoming US vinyl releases will be on Atlanta-based label Rotating Souls. The 4-track 12" EP will be released before the end of 2013 and will feature songs recorded during the bands recent sessions at Ghost Town studio in Leeds.

The band also talked about both new vinyl releases on Dither Down (NYC) and Rotating Souls during a video interview with music bloggers The Shepherd, recorded at Beacons festival after their live set on the 'You Need To Hear This' stage.
