The Music to release a special edition version of their album "Welcome To The North"

This article was published in 2004.

The Music have announced the release of a special edition version of their second album "Welcome To The North", available from January 17th 2005. Originally a Top 5 album in 2004, the re-release will feature new cover artwork, an additional disc featuring extra live tracks and the means to access live footage of the bonus tracks.

The album follows the release of their new single, Breakin', on January 10th.

The six live tracks were recorded at the Liverpool Academy.
