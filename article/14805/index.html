Finalists announced for Futuresound 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Now into its 11th year, the Futuresound competition has become a regular feature on the Leeds music scene.

The annual competition offers six bands and musicians from around the West Yorkshire region the opportunity to appear live at both sites of the prestigious Reading and Leeds Festivals.

The last few days have seen music industry judges listen to submissions from the many hundreds of applicants all hopeful of taking one of the slots on offer on the Festival Republic Stage and BBC Introducing Stage.

That is now complete and organisers have announced today the glittering array of 44 established and up-and-coming bands who will appear in a series of heats at the Cockpit in Leeds in June and July.

Here is the full list of bands and their gig date:

Panic, Redwire, The Ridings, Secret Circuits

Monday 4th July:
Arthur Rigby & The Baskervylles, Hannah Trigwell, Hail Animator, Imp

Wednesday 6th July:

Thursday 7th July:

Monday 11th july:
Bear Mask, I Swim With Sharks, Piskie Sits, The Weeknights

Wednesday 13th July:
Alvin Purple, Dorien Starre, The Hello Project, Matt Bentley

Thursday 14th July:
Black Moth, The Majors, Post War Glamour Girls, The Spills

Monday 18th July:

Wednesday 20th July:

Good luck to all the bands chosen and we wish you all the best.
