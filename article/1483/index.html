Worm release their debut album "Integral Virus"

This article was published in 2003.

The debut Worm album Integral Virus (Voltage Records) is now available at gigs and via the band's website: www.wormed.info. The album has been re-mastered and partially re-recorded from the original CD-R copies that were doing the rounds and will soon be available in shops once distribution is finalised.

Recording for the second album, titled Hate, is already well under way and the release is scheduled for November 2003.
