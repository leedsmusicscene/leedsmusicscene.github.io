Ashley Hicklin & The Troubadours sign a worldwide publishing deal with EMI

This article was published in 2008.

Ashley Hicklin & The Troubadours have this week announced a worldwide publishing deal with EMI.

The band were previous winners of the council-led initiative Bright Young Things and have been featured on Look North and BBC Leeds.

The eagerly awaited single ‘Home’ is due for release on November 3rd 2008, with a launch-party and tour-dates to be announced shortly.
