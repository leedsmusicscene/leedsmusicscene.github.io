Astro Circus

This is an archive of the band profile for Astro Circus.

Astro Circus are a five piece ensemble, formed towards the end of 2009 from Wakefield. Each member has a vast collection of influences and experience, drawing from each of these helped create an explosive and intriguing fusion of musical style which can only be described as "Progressive Rock".

After minor lineup changes, the band are back on track in 2011, with an arsenal of proper rock songs.
They are heading into the studio late July/August to record their anticipated debut 7 track album..
