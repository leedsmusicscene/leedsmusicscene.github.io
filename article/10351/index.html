Casino

This is an archive of the band profile for Casino.

Casino are a four piece indie band from Leeds who have been playing music from a very young age. We have been playing collectively as Casino since mid 2008 and we all enjoy performing together. The band consists of Ryan who sings and plays bass guitar, James who also sings and plays lead guitar, Michael who plays rhythm guitar and Carl who is the drummer.

We write all our own lyrics and music to songs and this makes for very good music (in our and our fans' opinions!!).

Feel free to check out our MySpace and you can make up your own minds about our music. Add us as a friend as we would love to here from you!

Catch you all later!!.
