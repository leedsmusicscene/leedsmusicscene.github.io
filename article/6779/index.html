Yes Boss release a double A-side in June

This article was published in 2006.

It's World Cup year, and this summer Yes Boss will unleash their own anthem as a double A-side single on the Leeds-based Dance To The Radio record label. "More Or Less / They Think It's All Over" is released on Monday 19th June as a limited edition 7" and digital download.

John Kennedy recently aired a session from Yes Boss and made their last single his X-Posure Big One. He has invited Yes Boss to play with Spank Rock and Sway at London Barfly on May 23rd.

Yes Boss appear on "Road to V" - Channel 4, from June 21st for nine weeks.
