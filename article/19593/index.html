Mi Mye release second single from upcoming album 'The Sympathy Sigh'

This article was published in 2016.

Following on from 'I Think Everything's Going to be Fine' (which received radio plays from Tom Ravenscoft, Heavenly Jukebox and BBC Introducing, as well as a video premier from Clash Music), 'Determan the Extent' is the second single to come from Mi Mye's new album 'The Sympathy Sigh'.

The single will be available to buy digitally from July 22nd.

The album 'The Sympathy Sigh' will be released on 12" vinyl and digital download through Philophobia / Hide & Seek on August 5th 2016.

To launch the album, Mi Mye will be appearing at The Red Shed in Wakefield on 5th August, followed by the Brudenell Social Club the night after.
