Glass Caves hit the road

This article was published in 2016.

Pontefract's Glass Caves are set to embark on a 10-date tour kicking off at the Brudenell Social Club on 14th April. The tour launches hot on the heels of a couple of headlining warm-up dates in London and Brighton and a surprise support slot on Cast's current tour at Manchester Academy 2 on 9th April.

The band have recently been showcasing new material destined for release on their forthcoming follow-up to their superb self-financed debut album 'Alive' which was released at the end of 2014 (still available folks!).

As well as Brudenell Social Club on Thursday 14th April, Glass Caves will be in York on Saturday 14th May.

Those tour dates in full:

Glasgow - Hard Rock Cafe, Friday 15th April
Edinburgh - Sneaky Pete's, Saturday 16th April
Sheffield - Rocking Chair, Friday 22nd April
Birmingham - The Sunflower Lounge, Friday 6th May
Barnsley - Opium 10, Saturday 7th May
York - Fibbers, Saturday 14th May
Liverpool - Zanzibar, Saturday 21st May
Ayton - Hemelvaart, Saturday 4th June
Nottingham - Bodega, Saturday 18th June
