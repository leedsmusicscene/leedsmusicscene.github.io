A Destructive Issue to feature on "Un-Scene" compilation CD...

This article was published in 2004.

Leeds teenagers A Destructive Issue are to feature on the upcoming Punktastic compilation CD entitled Un-Scene due for release at the beginning of August. They will be the youngest band on the CD and one of only five unsigned bands. The track selected is entitled This Could Kill and was recorded as part of the band’s demo at Purple Pro Audio, Bramley earlier this year.

This is the first release by ‘Punktastic Recordings’ who say “We wanted to showcase new songs from bands that we love, and also give some exposure to bands we really like that you might not have heard of….Thanks to our linkup with Sorepoint the compilation will be available in all good record stores, from HMV to your local indie. The release will also be available outside the UK, in places like Europe, the US and beyond.”

Other bands to feature include Sonic Boom Six, TANAOU, Route 215, Appease, Matthew Reynolds, 3dBs Down, Phinius Gage, Echofreddy, Stories and Comets, Not Katies, Short Warning, The Killerest Expression, The Mono Effect, and My Awesome Compilation. For more information visit www.punktastic.com.
