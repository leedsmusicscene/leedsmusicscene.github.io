Temple Works to host 'kin Hell Fest in April

This article was published in 2013.

In April, Temple Works in Leeds will be the host for 'kin Hell Fest, a 3-day festival of music, merchandise stalls and art exhibits.

The event takes place over the weekend of Friday 26th to Sunday 28th April 2013 and features a vast array of local and national bands.

Live music on the Friday night begins at 6pm, and the headliners are Winterfylleth. Also playing on Friday are Diascorium, Kastrated, Gore, Ravenage, Magpyes, Acolyte, and Rectal Implosion.

Saturday's headliners are Rompeprop with the music starting at the 11.30am. Other bands on the Saturday include The Afternoon Gentlemen, Bong, Alkerdeel, Palehorse, A Den Of Robbers, Ishmael, Flayed Disciple, Cancerous Womb, The Atrocity Exhibit, Regurgitate Life, Prolefeed, Sloth Hammer, Nu, Pogodi!, and Masochist.

Man Must Die will close off the festival on the Sunday, supported by Foetal Juice, Lazarus Blackstar, A Forest Of Stars, Gout, Iron Witch, Wode, Sufferinfuck, The Day Man Lost, Horsebastard, DSDNT, and Envoys.

A full weekend ticket will cost £35. Individual day tickets are also available at £9, £16 and £11 respectively. Head over to kinhellfest.webs.com to buy your ticket.
