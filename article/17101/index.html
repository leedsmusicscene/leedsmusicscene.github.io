The Leeds Music Hub set to host free Music Industry sessions

This article was published in 2012.

A range of free music industry support sessions for 2012 at The Leeds Music Hub are now open for booking via the Music Industry Yorkshire website.

These events led by industry professionals and are perfect or anyone aspiring to work in the music industry. Places at these events are FREE but limited so reserve a place now before it is too late.

The sessions are:

8th November 2012 - 7pm: Money and Music / Band Finances
22nd November 2012 - 7pm: Music Law Essentials
6th December 2012 - 7pm: Music and Video

Joining Music Industry Yorkshire - Yorkshire's music industry development network - is still free for people or businesses in Yorkshire.
