Liquid Pickles release Christmas charity song

This article was published in 2014.

Liquid Pickles, a project including James Smith (Post War Glamour Girls), Grant K. Fennell (Cottonwoolf) and producer Rob Slater (Greenmount Studios), have released a download only Christmas single on Bandcamp.

You can pay what you want for the single, but every penny you spend will go on to Simon On The Streets, a charity which provides help to the homeless.

The song itself is an experimental noise/dub reworking of Kate Bush's 'December Will Be Magic Again', using a combination of baritone guitars, analogue synthesizers, drum machines and found sound.



Liquid Pickles will continue to create music throughout 2014, aiming to collaborate with various artists in and around the North of England.
