Runaround Kids new singles

This article was published in 2012.

Today we can announce our most ambitious project to date. Following on from their acclaimed debut album 'Linked Arms' Runaround Kids are wasting no time in bringing you some new music with a series of releases throughout the year.
First up, is the release of a T-shirt and a song in April, the song is called 'You'd Feel The Same'. This will be followed in June with a 12" split with We Are Losers, and a cassette with The Spills later in the year. We'll be compiling these (plus a few others) onto a record at the end of the year.

You can pre-order the T-shirt with a CD of the single, plus sign up to get all the records and T-shirts and stuff, at our bandcamp - http://philophobiamusic.bandcamp.com We'll be throwing in some badges, stickers, scans of lyrics, handwritten stuff from studio sessions from the last couple of years, and anything else we can think of that will fit into an envelope... The T-shirt and single is £8, the bundle (T-shirt, 12" vinyl, tape, compilation CD, God knows how much music...) is just £12.

Hope you like the idea. We're pretty excited.
