Tytania

This is an archive of the band profile for Tytania.

Formed in Leeds UK by singer Luke Goodwin and guitarist Adam Winnard, Tytania came together through a love for music, an unfathomable list of shared interests and an insatiable desire to have fun.

Together with Drummer Charlie Turner and bassist Jack Briers, Tytania have amassed a loyal and dedicated following by taking their undeniably infectious and upbeat live show to every major town and city throughout the UK.
