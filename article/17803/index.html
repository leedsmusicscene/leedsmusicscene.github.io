Club Smith release their new single "The Green Room"

This article was published in 2013.

Leeds three piece Club Smith release their new single "The Green Room" through Tri-Tone / PIASUK on September 2nd.

Delivered by Dave Bascombe (Depeche Mode) and Lance Thomas (Ladytron) this single mix sees the band employing a more electronic approach to reflect the darker themes of human challenges running through "The Green Room"; the title itself a metaphor for the prolonged feelings of uncertainty that surround a loss.
