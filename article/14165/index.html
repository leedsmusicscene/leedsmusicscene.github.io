Wot Gorilla? release their 'New Arrival' EP

This article was published in 2011.

Wot Gorilla? have released their second EP, the 'New Arrival' EP, via the Idle Hands Records label.

It comprises of 4 brand new tracks that were recorded with James Kenosha (Pulled Apart By Horses, Dinosaur Pile-Up, Grammatics) in August 2010.

The tracklisting is:

1. Fear Of Flying
2. 3 DABS And A Chang
3. Interlude
4. Shoes For Traction

Based in Halifax, Wot Gorilla? formed in 2008 and the band topped off a successful year in 2010 with an appearance on the BBC Introducing Stage at the Reading & Leeds Festivals, along with a slot at the well known Brainwash Festival in Leeds.

The new record shows a strong development and progression since their first EP, and shows the true potential and promise that this young four piece have.
