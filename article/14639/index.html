Club Smith announce the release of their their debut single

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds band Club Smith have announced the release of their their debut 7" single, out on 6th June through new Manchester label Front Wall Records.

The single is a double A-side vinyl release, featuring "No Friend of Mine" and "Young Defeatists", and will be available in independent record shops, including Crash Records and Jumbo records in Leeds, as well as all the normal online outlets.

The single follows a successful support tour opening for The Pigeon Detectives in April, and comes with a video filmed by Leeds based Grizzly Bros (Danny North and Tim Blackwell). You can watch the video below:



The band will launch the single with a special free gig at Nation of Shopkeepers on 11th June.
