Brawlers reveal an official video for 'Mothers And Fathers'

This article was published in 2014.

Brawlers' debut single 'Instagram Famous' has garnered radio play from the likes of Radio 1's Zane Lowe and Huw Stephens, support from XFM's Ian Camfield and John Kennedy to name just a few.

The guys have been pretty busy with a recent run of dates with Nine Black Alps and are just heading off to play shows up and down the UK as special guests to Decade. In addition, the band has been confirmed for a number of UK festivals including Y Not in Matlock.

They have still had time to release their debut EP, 'I Am A Worthless Piece of Shit' (currently available through http://ilovealcopop.bigcartel.com) and now Brawlers have just revealed an official video for 'Mothers And Fathers'.

Watch this and if you haven't seen them live yet, you are missing out!!
