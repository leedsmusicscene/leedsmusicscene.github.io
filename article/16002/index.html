The Future Is Not Ours, Comrade EP to be released 13th February

This article was published in 2011.

One Day, After School... release their first EP on Philophobia Music on 13th February. ODAS have been working on the periphery of Wakefield music for a number of years. The bedroom based project of writer / musician Dean Freeman, ODAS have released a string of home demos through various labels and institutions (Philophobia Music / Geek Pie Records / Rhubarb Bomb) over the past few years. These recordings ranged from ambient downbeat, to crunchingly lo-fi alt rock but with this new record, their first 'proper' one, ODAS has found a more upfront, dynamic sound that more accurately represents the live approach of the band as a whole.

The EP will be launched at The Hop in Wakefield on Saturday 18th February. Supports to be announced shortly.
