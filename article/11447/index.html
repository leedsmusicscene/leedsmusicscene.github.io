The Blanche Hudson Weekend to release their debut single on 23rd November

This article was published in 2009.

The Blanche Hudson Weekend will release their debut 7” single – titled ‘The Letters To Daddy EP’ – via the Leeds-based Squirrel Records on 23rd November 2009.

The Blanche Hudson Weekend feature Caroline and Darren (ex-The Manhattan Love Suicides) along with a bunch of other ‘Weekenders’, and bring to you all kinds of noise, fuzz, melody, dissonance, rawness, killer tunes and buzzsaw pop.

The band’s debut limited edition 7" single is pressed on the most jet black of jet-black vinyl and includes the tracks ‘Crying Shame‘, ‘The Last Ride’ and ‘Noise And Fury’.

The EP is available for pre-order at £3.50 (inc. P&P) from the Squirrel Records website.
