Little Big Men release new single on Monday...

This article was published in 2002.

Little Big Men release their new single Long Black Legs on Monday 4th November. The CD single is limited to 250 copies and each CD is individually numbered. The track listing is Long Black Legs, Habit and live favourite Nonsense. The release coincides with the launch of the bands new website www.littlebigmen.net.
