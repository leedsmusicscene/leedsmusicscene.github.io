Apollo Junction making waves across the BBC

This article was published in 2012.

Leeds electro pop quintet Apollo Junction have recently featured across the BBC with various DJ's including Steve Lamacq, Tom Robinson and Alan Raw. They can currently be heard on the BBCiplayer with Tom Robinson on his BBC6 introducing mixtape on the following link:

http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b01p3lj6


They have confirmed their first major London show at the Half Moon in Putney in February '13 and will be announcing a summer tour in 2013 to coincide with their first single release in March. Including dates in Leeds // Manchester // Sheffield // Liverpool // Edinburgh // Glasgow // Nottingham // London // Brighton and many more.
