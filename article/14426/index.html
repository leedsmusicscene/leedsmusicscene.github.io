Plugged In Yorkshire to host music industry conference in Leeds

This article was published in 2011.

On March 30th 2011, Plugged In Yorkshire will host Music Futures, a regional music industry conference for Yorkshire.

The music industry's most influential and inspirational figures will deliver a full day of discussion and debate around new ways of doing Music Business and the future direction of the Music industry.

The event takes place in Leeds at The Carriageworks and the format will be a series of talks, networking opportunities and interactive sessions aimed at sharing experience and insider tips, to find new focus for the region's music businesses.

Topics covered will include access to finance, partnerships between music and brands, and relationships between labels, managers and artists.

For more details visit Plugged In Yorkshire.
