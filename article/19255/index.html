Everything Everything to headline Beacons Metro Leeds

This article was published in 2015.

Beacons have revealed the final acts taking part in the inaugural Beacons Metro this autumn and have confirmed that Everything Everything as headliners on Saturday 17th October at Canal Mills [tickets].

Beacons Metro Leeds will host over 30 events and 100 bands at the soon to open Headrow House, Canal Mills, Wharf Chambers and Belgrave Music Hall.

Also playing as part of Beacons Metro Leeds, which takes place over a 12-week period, are Mac Demarco, Waaves, Factory Floor, Titus Andronicus, East India Youth, Ghost Culture, Lone Lady, Traams and many more.
