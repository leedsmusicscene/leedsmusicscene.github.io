Casper Hauser releases his debut LP, "It's About Time"

This article was published in 2006.

Casper Hauser released his debut LP on September 1st. Titled “It’s About Time”, the album features 11 tracks spanning 45 minutes.

You can download the LP from iTunes, Virgin, HMV and Napster from £7.99 (or 79p per track).
