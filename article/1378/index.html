Police seek Leeds Festival rioters

This article was published in 2002.

PRESS RELEASE FROM MEAN FIDDLER:

The West Yorkshire Police have released photographs of 24 men that they would like to trace in connection with the disorder at this year's festival. The event was marred by a small minority and gave a distorted perspective of what music fans and music festivals are about.

The Mean Fiddler does not condone these illegal actions and are appealing to the public to help the police identify the culprits.
