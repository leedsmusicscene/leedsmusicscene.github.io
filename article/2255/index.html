Bodixa to release new single and announce tour with Adam Masterson...

This article was published in 2004.

Bodixa are set to release their latest single - titled California (b/w Sleepless - early this year and head out on tour in support to Adam Masterson in February. The tour takes place between 15th-29th, and comes to York Fibbers (closest gig to Leeds) on 22nd February. As reported earlier in the week the band play LMUSU on 5th February in a live radio broadcast for BBC Raw Talent.
