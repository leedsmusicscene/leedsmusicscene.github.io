The Lost 45s UK to release a limited edition double 7" single pack

This article was published in 2006.

The Lost 45s UK release a double pack 7" vinyl single later this month with a launch party scheduled to take place at Baby Jupiter on November 25th.

Issued on the Purple Sound imprint, the first record kicks off with the melodic soul stirrer “God Given” and is backed with the raw garage groove of “Psychotic Rhythm”. Record two has the Stax influenced sound of “Take Your Time” and the cooler, more contemplative sounds of “Ghost In The Noonday Sun”.

You can purchase the limited edition vinyl double pack at Baby Jupiter.
