Runaround Kids release new single on 13th September

This article was published in 2010.

Following up their appearance at Leeds and Reading Festivals Runaround Kids will release their new single 'No Dreams / Falling Into Better Hands' through Philophobia Music on 13th September.

The AA side single will be available on 7" and digital download from the usual places.

You can listen to the single and order the vinyl now at philophobiamusic.bandcamp.com.
