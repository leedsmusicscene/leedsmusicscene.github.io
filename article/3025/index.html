Fifth Goodbye release a free download EP...

This article was published in 2004.

Following their recent tour, Fifth Goodbye are back in local surroundings with a new EP that is available as a free download from the band's website.

The band are also recording a split 7" with Between Lines, which will be out shortly.

Fifth Goodbye's next Leeds appearance will be on 29th Sepember at Joseph's Well supporting thisGirl.
