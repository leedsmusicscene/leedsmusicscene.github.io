Corinne Bailey Rae to release 'The Love EP'

This article was published in 2011.

Corinne Bailey Rae has announced details of a brand new EP containing cover versions that will be released on Valentine's Day, 14th February 2011.

Called 'The Love EP', it comprises of some of Corinne's favourite songs, recorded around the world as she toured in support of her recent album 'The Sea'.

Tracks include Prince's 'I Wanna Be Your Lover'; Belly's 'Low Red Moon'; a soulful reading of the Bob Marley classic 'Is This Love?'; Paul McCartney's 'My Love'; and a Sly & The Family Stone-inspired live version of Doris Day's 'Que Sera Sera'.

You can purchase 'The Love EP' from iTunes.
