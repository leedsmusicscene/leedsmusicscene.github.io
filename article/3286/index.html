The Dragon Rapide to release a CD at Easter ...

This article was published in 2004.

The Dragon Rapide will release their second recording on the Leeds-based label collective, Chinchilla, in the Spring of 2005. The CD has the working title of "Real Bronx Cheer" and will include 8 new songs.

The band will tour the UK to coincide with the release, and are set to be supported by German three-piece Tripolis.
