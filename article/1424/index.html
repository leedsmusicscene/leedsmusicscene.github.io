four day Hombre playlisted at Radio One...

This article was published in 2003.

four day Hombre have been selected by the Radio 1 music panel to be playlisted from today:

"If you listen to Radio 1 you may just catch us being played alongside the likes of Blue feat. Elton John or the mighty Christina Aguilera feat. Redman. Thanks to those of you who voted for us and we look forward to being ridiculed by Mark & Lard on Monday.

Here's the slots that we'll be getting played in : Jo Whiley Fri 10/1 (10am - 1pm), Nemone Sat 11/1 (1pm-3pm), Mark & Lard Mon 13/1 (1pm-3pm), Scott Mills Weds 15/1 (4am-7am), Dave Pearce Thurs 16/1 (6pm-8pm)."
