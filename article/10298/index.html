Pippa Lloyd

This is an archive of the band profile for Pippa Lloyd.

Pippa Lloyd is a singer songwriter based in Leeds. Pippa has been writing songs since an early age but it wasn't until she came to University in Leeds that she starting performing regularly. She met fellow student, Abby Mottram in Halls of residence and so formed 'Decree'; a successful acoustic band becoming a finalist in Bright Young Things 2007. Pippa has now moved on to focus on her solo career. She plays both solo acoustic sets and with a full backing band. Pippa expects to have a new recording out early 2009..
