Leeds bands come together this Christmas for charity

This article was published in 2012.

With the aim of raising valuable funds for local homeless charity Simon on the Streets this winter, Leeds based music-videographers Anthologies, and record label/promoters Hide & Seek have collaborated on an ambitious festive music project unveiled this December.

Entitled This Christmas (with Anthologies and Hide & Seek) it brings together many of the finest musicians from the flourishing melting-pot that is the Leeds music scene, as they capably tackle the challenge of performing a "Christmas song" in their own vastly varying but equally brilliant ways.

The project will feature unique performance videos of these songs from some of the musicians, such as Blue Roses and Grant K. Fennell, filmed by Anthologies and available to view on their site.

Everyone who donates to the project will automatically receive a link to download the compilation which brings together recordings from 15 of the bands involved.

Everyone who donates will also be entered into a very generous raffle.
