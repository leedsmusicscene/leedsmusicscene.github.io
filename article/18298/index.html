Issimo

This is an archive of the band profile for Issimo.

After Abi won Rock The House 2013 at The House of Commons, she paired up with 2012 runner up Marc and formed ISSIMO along with a band of rogue jazz musicians. They were spotted at The Bedford in Balham (performing acoustically) by an organiser from Festival Republic and were offered a slot at Latitude Festival where they received an overwhelming response from festival goers. ISSIMO went on to play Beat-Hearder festival and Bingley Music Live the same year. Since then they have been in the studio working on their debut EP with producer Roni Szpakowski which is set to be released in summer 2014.

ISSIMO are strongly supported by BBC introducing and Alan Raw (West Yorkshire Presenter) is a great advocate and classes himself as a big ISSIMO fan. Popular tracks "Pretty Simple" and "Things About You" received radio play on the BBC Mark Forrest Show which is aired nationally across BBC stations. After recently playing a sell out gig at The 360 Club @ The Library in Leeds they are raring to go.
