Mumford & Sons confirmed as Main Stage headliners for Leeds Festival 2015

This article was published in 2015.

Following November's announcement of Metallica exclusive headliners for Leeds Festival 2015, festival organisers have today confirmed Mumford & Sons as their second Main Stage headlining act.

The band last appeared at Leeds back in 2010, when they appeared on the NME/BBC Radio 1 Stage.

Also announced today were Royal Blood, Bastille, Deadmau5, Rebel Sound, Catfish and The Bottlemen, Years & Years, Wolf Alice, Pretty Vicious, Jack Garratt and Hannah Wants.

Weekend tickets for Leeds 2015 are available now and can be purchased for £205 (plus booking fees) through Ticketmaster and See Tickets. A ticket payment plan is also available, with an initial payment of £50 followed by monthly installments.
