Duncan McFarlane to appear on BBC Radio this week...

This article was published in 2003.

Duncan McFarlane will appear live on BBC Radio this week, as he appears on the Henry Ayrton Show. As well as being interviewed on the 'folk & roots' section of the Real Music Show, he is expected to play around five songs. The show, on BBC Radio Leeds, will air on Thursday 6th March between 9-10pm.
