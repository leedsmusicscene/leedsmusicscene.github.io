Timeless Music Project announce line up for Yorkshire Showcase at SXSW

This article was published in 2010.

Huddersfield Social Enterprise Timeless Music Project have today announced the line up for this year's all Yorkshire Showcase at SXSW, Texas.

SXSW is arguably the most important US music conference for breaking UK music and Timeless Music Project has secured sponsorship from NorthernNet in order to showcase the region's bands at the Yorkshire SXSW party at the prestigious British Music Embassy venue in Austin on Thursday 18th March.

The bands appearing at the Yorkshire SXSW party are Helmsley's One Night Only, Sheffield's Slow Club and 65daysofstatic, and Leeds bands Middleman and Pulled Apart by Horses.

Timeless Music Project will be supporting all Yorkshire bands at SXSW via their promotional compilation "Independent Sounds of Yorkshire & the Humber 2010" which will be handed out to music industry delegates attending SXSW.

For more details on the Yorkshire showcase at SXSW visit the Yorkshire Music website.

To help Leeds band playing at SXSW raise funds to travel to Austin, a couple of events have been set up. The Brudenell Social Club will be hosting Middleman and Kava Kava on March 4th, and Serious Sam Barrett will be appearing at the Duck & Drake on March 10th.
