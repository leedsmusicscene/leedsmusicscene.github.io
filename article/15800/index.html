Tickets for Leeds Festival 2012 on sale

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds 2012 tickets will go on sale tomorrow morning as part of a month-long pre-sale ending at 6pm on New Years Eve, Saturday, 31st December.

A limited batch of tickets will be available to Vodafone VIP members for 48 hours from Wednesday 30th November. The sale of Leeds tickets will then be opened to the general public at 9am on Friday morning.

During the pre-sale all tickets will be held at 2011 prices, which means paying £192.50 for a weekend ticket. You can also buy an Early Entry permit for £15 that will allow you to enter the campsite the day before regular ticket holders.

Earlier this month we reported how festival organisers were winning the battle against ticket touts, but you should remain vigilant and make sure you only buy your Leeds tickets from reputable authorised ticket agencies.

You can find a list of approved ticket shops on our Leeds Festival tickets page.

For the December pre-sale we recommend Ticketmaster and See Tickets.

The official line up for Leeds 2012 will be announced in the Spring next year to coincide with the release of the main batch of tickets when prices are expected to be increased.
