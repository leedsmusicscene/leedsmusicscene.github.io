The Luminaries

This is an archive of the band profile for The Luminaries.

The Luminaries hail from Keighley, near Leeds, West Yorkshire. They consist of Calum Scrimgeour, Jordan Brown, Max Kidd, Sam Stevenson and WIll Stevenson.
Having formed in early 2008, the band have gone from strength to strength. Drawing influence from The Smiths to The Maccabees and Tokyo Police Club to The Strokes, their passion for music is reflected in their songs. They have played at many venues across Yorkshire and London with bands such as The Maccabees, Late Of The Pier, The Kabeedies, Mark Morriss (ex-Bluetones) and many more!

Their EP is currently up on their Bandcamp (wearetheluminaries.bandcamp.com) as a free digital download!
