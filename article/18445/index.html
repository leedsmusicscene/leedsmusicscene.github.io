The Dukes of Bevington unveil video for new single 'Days & Nights'

This article was published in 2014.

Yorkshire trio The Dukes of Bevington have shared their new video for new single 'Days & Nights'.



Taking inspiration from Temper Trap, Muse, Red Hot Chilli Peppers and Two Door Cinema Club, The Dukes of Bevington are for listeners who enjoy a hefty dose of infectious indie-pop-rock blaring from their speakers.

Comprising of Scott Quinn on Lead Vocals and Bass, Christian Davies on guitar and James Locke on drums, the three-piece met at college in Leeds four years ago.

Since the days of cardboard choruses The Dukes of Bevington have self-released two EPs, which gained local attention and praise from BBC Radio Leeds.

The track is available now on pay-what-you-want basis.
