James and Primal Scream to co-headline at Roundhay Park in September

This article was published in 2016.

OnRoundhay Festival is a family, music and food festival taking place in Roundhay Park on 17th September 2016 and will feature a fun-filled program of entertainment for all ages.

Confirmed as co-headliners for the inaugural festival are multi-Platinum selling James, fresh from their triumphant set at Glastonbury, and Scottish rock legends Primal Scream, who've had another incredibly successful year with the release of their new Top 20 album 'Chaosmosis' and a string of critically acclaimed live performances.

Joining them will be London four-piece Wolf Alice, Iowa's Max Jury, and Leeds 7-piece The Haggis Horns who will bring a live funk extravaganza guaranteed to get the crowd grooving.

More bands will be announced during the summer for what is guaranteed to be memorable family experience, with the event bringing live entertainment back to the historic green space of Roundhay Park.

As well as the music, the John Lewis Food Village will make its OnRoundhay debut, and will be packed with dining experiences, demonstrations, celebrity chefs, pop up stalls and street food to whet any appetite. A feast of tastes and cuisines will be on hand all day to accompany the world-class line-up of music and entertainment.
