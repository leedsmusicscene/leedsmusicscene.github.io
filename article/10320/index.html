Description:
Angelfish Records are pleased to introduce the amazing voice of Leeds girl Leesa Mae.
Her hot new debut EP 'Love Eternity' is now available online on itunes and Amazon.
The tracks on 'Love - Eternity' feature an array of snazzy memorable pop hooks along with captivating, seductive vocals and harmonies.
Enriched with her hypnotic voice and rich melodic tunes, Leesa Mae's music is one not to miss.

Leesa Mae's original music is now frequently featured in the background of scenes in the ITV Soap - 'Coronation Street'

Biography:

Leeds based singer/songwriter Leesa Mae is no stranger to the stage, as she has been performing publicly since the age of three. Part of a family with a long line of musical talent, Leesa was encouraged to sing at a very young age and has gained skills in Piano, Violin and the Flute.
Her principle instrument has now become the guitar, which Leesa uses to successfully write a wide range of her own catchy, acoustic pop material gaining influences from musicians including KT Tunstall, Rene Olstead and Jason Mraz

Performing regularly at top Leeds and Manchester venues, Leesa is building up quite a fan base and can be found on Facebook, MySpace, YouTube & Leeds Music Scene.
