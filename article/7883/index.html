The Sugars to release "Monsters" on the Bad Sneakers Records label

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band The Sugars will release a new single on the Bad Sneakers record label on Monday, 12th February. Titled "Monsters", the forthcoming single will be available on limited edition red vinyl, CD and Digital Download formats. The B side to "Monsters" will be "Serenade".
