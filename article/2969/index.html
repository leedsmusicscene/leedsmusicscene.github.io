"Live Music Updates" set to return to 96.3 Radio Aire...

This article was published in 2004.

Good news in the regional commercial radio world! After a couple of years spent in the wilderness the popular local "Live Music Updates" are soon to be back on the airwaves via 96.3 Radio Aire.

If any Leeds band or artist would like their material to be considered for a mention and possible airplay, or simply want a gig promoting, or have any other useful info or news then send it to:

Andy Philpott
Live Music Update
41 Barleyhill Road
Garforth
Leeds
LS25 1DX.

More details on airtimes and dates will be announced shortly.
