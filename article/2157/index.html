Baby Food

This is an archive of the band profile for Baby Food.

A three-piece raunchy electro-punk stew, featuring Noah on beats, yelping and crooning, JP on none-more-fuzzed-up bass abuse and snake-hips stage moves, and the newest addition to the fold, Joe on synth-and-being-handsome duties. We document the crack trade, social ineptitude, sex, surrealism and the dramas of everyday life. We've been compared to Devo, The Fall, The Streets, Adult., and other disparate artists. We're fighting a war on blandness, fuelled by LOVE. Damn straight..
