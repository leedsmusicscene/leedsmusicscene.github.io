Blackout

This is an archive of the band profile for Blackout.

Blackout have been gigging around Yorkshire and beyond since 2005 playing pubs, clubs and biker rallies. The line-up consists of Gazz on Vocals, Mark on Guitar, Geezer on Bass and Tim on Drums. Blackout's set consists of classic cuts from legendary bands such as Led Zeppelin, Black Sabbath, Queen, Thin Lizzy, UFO, Ozzy Osbourne, Metallica and many more. The band are regulars at Leeds venues The New Roscoe and The Duck and Drake. Indeed the Blackout Halloween Fancy Dress Horrorfest has become a tradition at the Duck and Drake..
