Embrace to put out a full-length live DVD...

This article was published in 2005.

On November 7th, Embrace release their first full length concert DVD "A Glorious Day - Live In Leeds" through Eagle Vision.

Filmed at the Millennium Square gigs in Leeds city centre earlier this year it includes all the band's hits and most popular album tracks performed in front of an ecstatic sold out crowd. The initial stock of the DVD will be in a limited edition digipack that also includes a special three track audio CD from the concert and an exclusive poster.

Bonus features on the DVD include: Original videos for "Gravity", "Ashes", "Looking As You Are" and "A Glorious Day"; behind the scenes documentary; interview; and fan film.
