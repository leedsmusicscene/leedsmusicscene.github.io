Solus Locus and The Velvet Orchestra to release a split single

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds-based bands Solus Locus and The Velvet Orchestra will release a split single on the Salt The Wound Records label. The Velvet Orchestra tracks are "The Creator" and "The Children At The Future". The Solus Locus track is “We Can Go Home”.

The limited edition 7” white vinyl will be released on Monday, 16th June. The official launch for the single will be at Brudenell Social Club on the 13th June - copies of the single will be available to buy at the gig.

Salt The Wound Records are an independent record label based in Burley, Leeds.
