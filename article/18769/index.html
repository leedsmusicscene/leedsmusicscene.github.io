Debut EP from Deuce & Charger out 10th November

This article was published in 2014.

An incredible year on since their debut track 'Always Broke' first appeared online, Leeds-based writers/producers Deuce & Charger are set to release their highly anticipated debut EP on Monday 10 November.

Deuce & Charger EP1 perfectly captures the atmosphere of this amazing first year, highlighted by overwhelming support from BBC Introducing and tastemaker press, as well as Deuce & Charger's eye-catching, interactive video campaigns.

The lead track is the brand new 'Wild & Free', featuring soul legend Rev. Chunky and The Voices of the Day Gospel Choir. Blasting off with dramatic strings and riotous drum & bass beats, 'Wild & Free' is the revolutionary 60s counter-cultural spirit re-imagined for a 21st century audience.

Deuce & Charger EP1 also includes fan-favourites 'Always Broke' ft. Benn Moore (together with the awesome TYR remix - 125,000 plays and counting on SoundCloud alone) and both the full and jaw-dropping live versions of 'Stop The Sky From Falling' ft Bianca Gerald & Weezy Jefferson. Deuce & Charger EP1 encapsulates the Deuce & Charger sound: uplifting strings and punchy breakbeats blended with anthemic vocal hooks and powerful, relevant lyrics.

A year in the life of Deuce & Charger, Deuce & Charger EP1 showcases Deuce & Charger's search for the most exciting collaborators and emerging talents to create music described as "intense pop with...lyrical depth, soaring strings and a whole lot of soul" - Idolator
