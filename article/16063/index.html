Alvin Purple announce the release of their latest single

This article was published in 2012.

Alvin Purple release their new single "Huh Her" / "Please Please" on 23rd January 2012. The single was recorded with mixer/producer James Kenosha in his Bridlington studio.

It's available to download from bandcamp, initially for free.

The video to Huh Her was made with Leeds based filmmakers [B-Side].

The band are celebrating its release with a show at Fibbers, York on 3rd February.
