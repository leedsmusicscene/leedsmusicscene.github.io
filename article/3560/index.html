New Leeds label launch with a free album giveaway in March...

This article was published in 2005.

Friday 11th March will see the launch of a cutting edge, limited-edited FREE compilation album from new Leeds label "Dance To The Radio", showcasing some of Leeds' finest musical talent.

Featuring no less than 18 bands, the free CD - titled "Dance To The Radio: Leeds" - includes the likes of The Rebellion Threat Kills, The Somatics, ¡Forward, Russia!, Robochrist, The Scaramanga Six, Buen Chico and This Et Al.

A strictly limited 500 copies will be pressed and will only be available at the launch event.

Playing live to launch the CD at Woodhouse Liberal Club will be Duels, ¡Forward, Russia!, iLiKETRAiNS, The Lodger and The Old House. The CD is available FREE (subject to availability, on a first come, first-served basis) and the full tracklisting can be obtained from the label's website at www.dancetotheradio.com.

Further singles from This Et Al, The Lodger, Napoleon IIIrd and ¡Forward, Russia! are in the pipeline for the remainder of 2005. The next release on the label will be a split 7" single featuring This Et Al and ¡Forward, Russia! (tracks being "He Shoots Presidents" and "Nine" respectively) in April 2005.
