The Rosie Taylor Project announce new album with free download

This article was published in 2012.

In the lead-up to the release of The Rosie Taylor Project's new album, the band are offering their fans a free MP3.

You can download 'The Last Happy Writer' for free from bandcamp.com.

The track was recorded and produced by Richard Formby (Wild Beasts, Herman Dune) and conjures an aptly seasonal melancholy, showcasing a gorgeous warmth and breadth of talent.

'The Last Happy Writer' is taken from the band's forthcoming album - titled 'Twin Beds' - which will be released on 13th February 2012 through the Odd Box Records label. The album includes a guest vocal appearance from Wild Beasts' singer Tom Fleming.
