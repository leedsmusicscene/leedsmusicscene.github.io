Kaiser Chiefs to release "Oh My God" in Spring 2004...

This article was published in 2004.

Kaiser Chiefs have completed the mastering of their debut single - titled Oh My God - which will come out on the Drowned in Sound record label in Spring. A demo of the track was played on BBC's 6 Music channel and had Steve Lamacq write in his A&R column: "[Oh My God] builds into a jerky, lurching chorus which had Your Humble Scribe hooked after three plays".
