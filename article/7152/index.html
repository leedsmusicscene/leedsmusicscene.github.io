Leeds favourites Bodixa are back with a new single

This article was published in 2006.

After a Michael Eavis-endorsed set at Glastonbury 2004 and signing a publishing deal with Complete Music (Everything but the Girl, Roddy Frame, the Buzzcocks) Bodixa have spent time in both south east Ireland and their hometown, writing and recording. The results of these sessions are two EPs set for release in 2006 and a brand new album will following early 2007.

The first release is the “The Way Back Home” EP, available through the Fear Of Pop label on Monday, 11th September (available for pre-order now). The EP includes four songs: “Goodbye Winter”, “A Room”, “Sing Your Bones” and “Nothing to Show”.

Bodixa launch the EP on Saturday 19th August at Otley’s Courthouse Arts Centre.
