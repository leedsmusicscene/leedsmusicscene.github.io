The Pigeon Detectives in at number 1 in the charts...

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds' The Pigeon Detectives hit the top of the charts this week with their latest single “I Found Out” which was released on the local Dance To The Radio record label.

The band took the number 1 spot in the UK Indie Singles charts away from Babyshambles and also reached number 39 in the UK Top 40 main charts.

The Pigeon Detectives follow last week's successes of The Sunshine Underground (“Commercial Breakdown”) who reached number 2 in the UK Indie Singles chart and Yes Boss (“Tongues In Knots”) who had a new entry at number 23.
