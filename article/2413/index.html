New Leeds radio station Interplay Radio looking for unsigned bands...

This article was published in 2004.

Over Easter a new Leeds radio station called Interplay Radio will emerge, broadcasting on FM from the 2-8th April. The station are looking for unsigned bands of any genre, from R&B to goth to rock, to submit their material for airplay. Interested bands and musicians should contact Helen at Interplay on (0113) 2638556 or e-mail danny@interplaytheatre.org.
