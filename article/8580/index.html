WhoresWhoresWhores and Omerta to release a split 7" single in July

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds bands WhoresWhoresWhores and Omerta have revealed that their split 7" single will be available in July.

The single includes brand new tracks from both Leeds bands, released via Seduction Records, Godisdad Records, Childhood Sweetheart Records and This Ones For The Crew Records.

Each 7" is limited and individually hand screen-printed featuring artwork for Chris (Narcosis) and Geith (Art Of Burning Water).

In other news, WhoresWhoresWhores will be releasing another split CD, this time with Brighton’s With Scissors, who feature members of 80's Matchbox B-Line Disaster, and Glasgow’s Take A Worm For A Walk Week, who have recently toured with The Red Chord.

WhoresWhoresWhores have just completed a 10-date UK tour with With Scissors and will be writing over the next few months for the release of their second album. Titled 'Monkey Never Dies' the album is due out on Seduction Records at the end of the year.

Omerta are also writing for a new album and several splits. The band will shortly head out on a four date tour with the mighty Converge.
