J Walkers to release an album on the GLP record label

This article was published in 2007.

Sunday 9th September sees the release of a brand new album from a brand new band on Leeds label GLP (Groove Laboratory Productions), the J Walkers. Capri's lead singer Benson Walker got together with keyboard player Joe Tatton, who plays with Leeds Soul/Jazz legends The New Mastersounds. Both artists wanted to explore a side of their writing that they weren't able to express through their current bands. The result is the brilliant new album "All We Can Do" which will be launched at a special gig at The HiFi Club in Leeds on 9th September. The gig will kick off at around 9.30pm with special guest appearances from Jack Foster and popular Saxophonist 'Al' from Leeds band Soul Vaccination. Entry to the club is free.
