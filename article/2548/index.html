LSR looking for local bands...

This article was published in 2004.

LSR (Leeds Student Radio) is looking for local bands who would like to have some air time showcasing their material. The station say that there are "No criteria, just a will to be involved and an integrity to be exposed". Shows will be aired towards the end of April 2004.
