The Well to host Leeds United fans remembrance gig

This article was published in 2009.

April 5th 2010 will mark the 10th anniversary of the tragic death of Leeds United fans Chris Loftus and Kevin Speight, and family and friends wish to commemorate this significant milestone by raising money for the Candlelighters childrenâ€™s cancer charity in Leeds in their memory.

They will hold a fundraising event every month between September 2009 and April 2010, and one of these event is to be a music gig at The Well in Leeds on Saturday 21st November.

The bands performing the charity gig are Stoned Roses, The Hypes, White Comic and Inbetween. There is also a DJ set from Mark Alexander.

Doors open at 7pm.

More information can be found at www.leedsfansremembrance.com.
