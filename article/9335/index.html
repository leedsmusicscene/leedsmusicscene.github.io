The debut single from Heads We Dance will be released on April 28th

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds based pop group Heads We Dance will be releasing their eagerly anticipated debut single on April 28th! The single will 'Love In The Digital Age' b/w 'Work It Out' and will be coming out on hot new label Lost In Paris, home to Bolt Action Five and Adam Kesher.

This year Heads We Dance have been named by Popbitch as their 'new favourite band', were described as 'one to watch 2008' by This Is Fake DIY and have been a MySpace featured artist. They are also garnering a reputation as a band with a knack of spotting top remixing talent early; having had 'Love In The Digital Age' remixed by Tepr (Wall of Sound/Kitsune) and Frankmusik (just remixed Alphabeat/signed to Island) for a limited edition white label released late last year.

Heads We Dance are an exciting new band with feet planted firmly in both Pop and Electro camps. Both songs and remixes are available to hear now on their MySpace.
