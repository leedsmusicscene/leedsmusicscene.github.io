COMPETITION TIME WITH VITAMIN

This article was published in 2015.

'Brothers and Sisters' is a night of future-pop and good times hosted by Leeds based Vitamin and offers an open door to people looking for a unique atmosphere with exciting bands and DJs. It is aimed at bringing together a new kinship between music lovers, party goers and artists in Leeds.

The first show featuring Marsicans and Pik, went down a storm, packing out The Brudenell Games Room. The next line up, featuring Polo and Krrum will be held on Tuesday 10th of November. All the artists are hand-picked by Vitamin, with some of Leeds most promising and best new bands, guest DJs, cheap drinks, regular giveaways and free sweets.

In addition to hosting this fantastic night, the lovely boys of Vitamin, have given us some great prizes to give away. This is for their final gig of the residency this year, to be held on the 8th December, featuring Actor and Snowfox.

Winner will receive:
1 Varsity Jacket, 2 Tickets to 8th Dec Gig and a Signed EP

Runner up will receive:
2 Tickets to 8th Dec Gig and a Signed EP

All you have to do is tell us the name of the vegetables that appear on the cover artwork of the singles released by the band.
