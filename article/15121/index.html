The Music to release a DVD and Live CD of farewell gig

This article was published in 2011.

The Music have today announced that they will be releasing a special edition DVD and live CD recording of their last ever performance.

The Leeds-based band are to team up with instant recording specialists Concert Live to bring their fans this special DVD and CD package, which will be ready to pick up from the O2 Academy Leeds immediately after their farewell gig on Saturday 6th August.

The gig is now sold out - if you don't have a ticket, you can still purchase a copy of the DVD and CD, or just the DVD, by visiting the Concert Live website.
