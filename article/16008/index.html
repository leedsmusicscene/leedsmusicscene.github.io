Sound Of Sirens release 'Throw It Away' EP

This article was published in 2012.

Indie five piece Sound Of Sirens have released their 'Throw It Away' EP, which is available now for free download from bandcamp.com.

A limited run of physical CD copies is also available - for free - directly from the band at gigs.

The track listing is as follows:

1: Fly
2: The Seahorse Chorus
3: Never Die

Sound Of Sirens held an unofficial launch of the EP release at their recent BBC Introducing gig at the 360 Club at The Library on 23rd December.

The band are also making available a remastered album of their early material which was played live between 2009-2010. The 11-track album 'This Day in History' is also available for free download via bandcamp.com.
