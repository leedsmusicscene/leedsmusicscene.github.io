Eardrumspop label to release new single from Insect Guide

This article was published in 2010.

The new single from Leeds band Insect Guide is released this week.

The single is released via the Eardrumspop label who asked the band to join their singles club, a unique collaboration between the label, band, illustrator and A Negative Narrative.

The single includes a new track 'Bats', an acoustic version of the album track 'Hearts don't Break' and a cover of the Big Star song 'Nightime'.

The innovative website A Negative Narrative make interviews with bands, but instead of answering with words, the artists have to take a photograph for each question. The interview with Insect Guide can be found online here.

For their illustrator, the band were lucky enough to be paired with the Brazilian artist Daniela Hasse whose work can be found in the single cover art.

All this is packaged together into a digital download including PDF booklet. Go get it now from Eardrumspop.
