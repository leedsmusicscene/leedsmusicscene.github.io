Cloth Cat Spring Term: free music courses announced for Leeds' music enthusiasts

This article was published in 2007.

Cloth Cat will again be running a series of free music courses for people living in the disadvantaged areas of Leeds.

In association with the Workers’ Education Association, free courses on offer range from live performance through to the music business, music technology to engineering, DJing to song composition.

The Workers’ Educational Association is also running a beginner’s course on Music Journalism that is available for a fee of £4 per lesson.

All courses begin in the week of 15th January 2007 and are based at The Cardigan Centre, Primrose, Cardigan Arms and Wrangthorn Hall.  For more information on the available courses and to book your place, call 0113 2442773 or e-mail mike@clothcatleeds.org.uk.
