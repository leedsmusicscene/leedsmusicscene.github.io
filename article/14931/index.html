Fade Away

This is an archive of the band profile for Fade Away.

Michael Cross
Oliver Bronzewhaler
Steven Cross
Paul Raper
David Corbett

Fade Away are a melodic hardcore band influenced by bands such as Heart In Hand, Azriel and Touche Amore that were formed in 2010. After releasing a debut e.p at the end of the 2010 then adding a second guitarist to create a much bigger more dynamic sound the band took a break to write brand new material which will feature on a new 2 track download which will be available from their Facebook very soon.

With lots of gigs coming up playing alongside some of the most respected names in British hardcore, such as more than life and landscapes, 2011 is going to be a big year for Fade Away..
