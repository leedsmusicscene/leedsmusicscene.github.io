SXSX 2012 and Midem briefing event to take place at HiFi Club

This article was published in 2011.

Are your band hoping to showcase at SXSW or Midem next year? If so, then don't miss the Yorkshire briefing event on 1st September at the HiFi Club in Leeds.

Guest Speakers at the Yorkshire briefing include Una Johnston (SXSW), Javier Lopez (MIDEM), Phil Patterson (UKT&I Music Industry Specialist), and Pat Fulgoni (Timeless Music Project). Topics that can be discussed include:

- how to apply for international showcases
- more about the events and whether they are right for you
- how to get there
- how to make the most of your time at the events
- financial assistance and other support available
- SXSW film, music and interactive festivals
- meeting music industry trade associations

Registration is free. Send your name, address, company / artist name, role and your email address to Pat Fulgoni at pat [ at ] timelessmusicproject.co.uk.

For further info check www.musicyorkshire.com.
