Inspades announce new single

This article was published in 2014.

On 1st August 2014 Inspades will release their brand new single "Deadlines".

The new single marks a new era for the band with a further turn towards their more darker and heavier feel; mixing groove and bounce with a cutting edge and contemporary muscle.

Adjusting as a three piece has been a natural progression and since the release of their previous singe "D.N.R." last year, the band are not only lining up some follow up releases but have taken to writing a whole new set with an EP in sight.

Deadlines is a big stab, recorded and mixed by Joe and Sam Graves at Inner Sound Studios there is the obvious lend of production quality but this; almost debut, see's much more of the portent of things to come.

You can purchase the single from the band's online store.
