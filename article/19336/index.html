Those Delta Wolves release single 'Whispers'

This article was published in 2015.

Those Delta Wolves have announced the release of their highly anticipated single 'Whispers', available via digital download only from 1st November. However, 50 limited edition physical copies will be available for free with a ticket to the single launch on Halloween (31/10/15) at the 360 Club.

Alan Raw, who is a fan of the band, played the song exclusively for the first time during an interview on BBC Introducing in West Yorkshire on 24th October. This can still be heard on the BBC iPlayer if you want to check it out.

We sent one of our writers, Tomas Cummings, down to the practice rooms at Eiger Studios for a sneak preview. He says "... I must say, it is an absolute head banging banger!! Lulling you into a false sense of security with its down tempo intro, before repeatedly slamming your head into a rhythmic bounce, that leaves you feeling both exhausted and satisfied."

Tickets are available from Jumbo Records and Crash Records, or OTD.
