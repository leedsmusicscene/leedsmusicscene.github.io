Dead Disco announce new single details and launch party

This article was published in 2007.

Dead Disco have announced details of their new single, their first since signing to new label 679 Recordings / Warner Music. The track "You're Out" will be available to download from Monday, 28th May, although the physical release of the record will not be until later in the summer.

To celebrate the band will be playing a one-off single launch gig at The Tunnel Club, Mine Bar in Leeds University Union on Tuesday 29th May. The night will be horror themed, and special guests Buen Chico will also be playing.

The gig will be open to over 14s until 10pm, and tickets include free entry to the Tunnel Club aftershow party for over 18s.
