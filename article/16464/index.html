Many more bands added to the Beacons Festival 2012 line up

This article was published in 2012.

Beacons Festival are pleased to announce further additions to the line-up to the festival this summer, which includes a northern exclusive from Mercury prize nominated Wild Beasts.

JACUZZI BOYS
STOPMAKINGME
WEIRD DREAMS
BLEEDING KNEES CLUB
SOULJAZZ SOUNDSYSTEM
LUVJAM
TALL SHIPS
BLACKLISTERS

The bands join a line up that includes Wild Beats, Roots Manuva, Patrick Wolf, and many many more.
