Her Name is Calla to release a new album on 8th November

This article was published in 2010.

Leeds / Leicester six-piece Her Name Is Calla have announced the release of their new album, 'The Quiet Lamb', via the Denovali Records label.

Available to buy from 8th November 2010, 'The Quiet Lamb' is available on 2 X 12" Vinyl, CD, and special-edition CD formats.

The album follows the band's 2009 album 'The Heritage' [read review] and should also be available in Digital Download format from iTunes and other online stores.
