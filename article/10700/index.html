Lowlife UK to release their 6th studio album

This article was published in 2009.

Lowlife UK's new CD "The Hypnotised Never Lie" will be out in June 2009 on the Punks 4 Profit label. Recorded at Voltage Studios, Bradford in March and April by Tim Walker this is their 6th studio album and the best-recorded material to date.

Next year is their 15th anniversary which will see a live CD and DVD of collected footage to mark the occasion.
