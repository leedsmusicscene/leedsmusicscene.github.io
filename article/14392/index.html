Casual Shoes

This is an archive of the band profile for Casual Shoes.

Casual Shoes formed in Leeds in 2010. They are 4 like minded individuals striving to make music that they would listen to. Free from the shackles of trying to fit into a genre, theirs is a sound that is individual and yet familiar to all. Their classic four piece line up allows the band to explore songs made up of thundering drums, vibrant bass and dual attack lead. This gives Casual Shoes a sound that is one part jangle pop, one part indie rock and one part soaring terrace anthems.

Having recently completed their debut gig, they are now looking to build on a solid foundation and make some moves in their local music scene. Coming up on the horizon, the band will be playing more gigs to hone their live shows whilst writing new songs that will be recorded for their forthcoming debut LP.

So keep your eyes open in the local press and hopefully you will be able to catch them at a venue near you in the not too distant future!.
