Kaiser Chiefs to release AA side single in August...

This article was published in 2005.

Following on from last week's nomination for Mercury Music Prize 2005 for their album “Employment”, Leeds' Kaiser Chiefs will release a double A-side single on August 22nd.

The single will feature a new song – “Sink That Ship” - which was written whilst the band were on tour and recorded in June with Steve Harris, and the popular live track “I Predict A Riot” which was given a limited release in late 2004. The AA-release will feature a third, also brand new, song by the name of “Less Is More”.

Kaiser Chiefs play three headline shows in Leeds at the Town Hall on the 18th, 19th and 20th October.
