Fenton release the promo video to their debut single 'Killer'

This article was published in 2015.

Leeds three-piece Fenton have released the promo video to their debut single 'Killer'. Shot on location at Eiger Studios and other settings around Leeds, the video was produced and directed by Guglielmo Soreca. It stars actor Lindsay Stanger as the alluring siren whose charms prove irresistible to each member of Fenton. All the more impressive, the entire video was shot without any financial budget.

Indiemunity, in a recent review, said: "'Killer' is an excellent track by a very promising group. I absolutely love their sound and the classic influence they've sprinkled throughout a contemporary offering."



Fenton are taking part in the annual Futuresound competition 2015. Their heat takes place on Thursday 23rd July at The Wardrobe.

Fenton will also be playing at the Packhorse in Leeds on Friday 20th July.
