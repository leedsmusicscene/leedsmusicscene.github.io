Buffalo Bones

This is an archive of the band profile for Buffalo Bones.

"Marching through the afterlife rattling out Neolithic grunge on the carcass of a mastodon." A NEGATIVE NARRATIVE

"A loud, brash three piece who will give you the best possible headache. There are glam and metal elements, but in the vocal expression there are suggestions of a totally fresh thrash band fitting in next to Eagles of Death Metal and The Dead Weather." MUSIC BROKE MY BONES

"Whatever the situation, put this track on (Turn It On Again) and you will immediately be transported to some small, sweaty back room, where a raucous three-piece are doing their very best to shake your brains out with their bottom-heavy, distorted brash-rock." CROON.
