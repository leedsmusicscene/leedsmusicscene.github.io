Arizona Bay to launch new EP on November 22nd

This article was published in 2010.

Arizona Bay have announced that the launch party for their new self-titled EP will take place on Monday November 22nd at Carpe Diem in Leeds city centre.

A free entry event with support from Sheffield based [sub]Nova and a UK tour to follow, the band get things kicked off in style with an evening of live music in association with Lyrically Justified.

Arizona Bay will be playing tracks from the EP along with brand new material and previously unheard cover versions. Brand new band merchandise will be available, as well as drinks offers and plenty of opportunity to get your hands on a copy of the new EP.

The tracklisting for the EP is:

1. Footsteps
2. Letting Go
3. Nothing Left To Say But Goodbye
4. Second Best
5. Safe As You
6. Costain
7. White Noise
8. Last Song (acoustic)
