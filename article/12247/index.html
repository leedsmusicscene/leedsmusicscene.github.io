Charlie Bath

This is an archive of the band profile for Charlie Bath.

Charlie Bath is a 22 year old singer songwriter and producer originally from the south west, and is now studying in her 3rd and final year of a Bachelors degree in Music Production at Leeds College of Music.

Charlie has been writing and performing her music for over 6 years and have gigged in well established venues in Leeds, Liverpool, Manchester, Cardiff, Bristol and Swindon, both as a solo act and with her supporting band..
