Duels to release the first single to be taken from their new LP

This article was published in 2008.

Duels will release a new single (‘Regeneration’) on the 14th April. The single is the first release to be taken from the band’s current long-player ‘The Barbarians Move In’ – although only currently available as a Digital Download, the album will have its release proper on the 28th April.

Both single and album are released on This is Fake DIY Records.

Available on 7” vinyl, the single will come backed with ‘When The Sea Comes’.

Duels headline the Dance To The Radio (and Friends) all-dayer at the Cockpit in Leeds on March 21st. Tickets are still available from See Tickets, priced at £8.

You can watch the video for 'Regeneration' via the Duels YouTube Channel.
