Many more bands added to Live At Leeds 2012 event

This article was published in 2012.

Organisers of Live At Leeds 2012 have today revealed the second wave of bands that will be appearing as part of the day-long Saturday event on May 5th 2012.

Live At Leeds 2012 takes place across 11 stages at 9 venues around the city of Leeds and features hundreds of bands.

Those added today include THE ENEMY, GHOSTPOET, LIANNE LA HAVAS, SCROOBIUS PIP, SPECTOR, JAKWOB, DOG IS DEAD, CHEW LIPS, STOOSHE and JESS MILLS.
