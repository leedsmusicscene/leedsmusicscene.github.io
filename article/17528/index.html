Happy Daggers release 'Reverse /// Rewind' EP

This article was published in 2013.

Happy Daggers, have released their debut E.P. 'Reverse /// Rewind' via major digital stores.

Unleashing their unique blend of Indie-Rock and Disco-Soul, the four piece, hailing from Leeds, have produced something to tantalise music lovers who have roots in a wide range of genres. This rare blend of melody, drive, and energy culminates in something fresh that somehow manages to display the same thumping characteristics produced during one of the band's dynamic live shows.

It has been these live performances that have enabled Happy Daggers to develop from a fresh act, on the ever bustling Leeds scene, to an established act within the city. This journey has taken them from humble beginnings in 2011, to sell out support slots with The Milk, recent confirmation at the award winning city-wide festival Live at Leeds, and dates at various reputable venues around the country.

Happy Daggers are now preparing to return to the studio, in May, to record tracks for their next single. They plan to unveil this follow up release in the summer, alongside a selection of dates around the country.
