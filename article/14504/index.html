Icelandic Death Cloud

This is an archive of the band profile for Icelandic Death Cloud.

Icelandic Death Cloud was formed by Vicky Smith (Gentleman's Monster Club, Katie And The Questions) and Kenny Parsons in spring of 2010, as a punk outfit with touches of blues. To this end, blues guitar prodigy Tom Durant was quickly recruited, forming a solid alliance with Kenny on bass and creating the basis of the band's first track, "Offender"...

...only one piece now remained, namely the drummer. Several candidates expressed an interest in the role, but their general failure to turn up to things (or, in one case, not actually owning a drum kit) left our intrepid trio frustrated, until the Gods of Rock sent a saviour in the form of thunderous, no-bullshit sticksman Jake Morton. The band was now complete, and since then they have been squirrelled away in Rock and Roll Circus plotting their imminent rising, fuelled by beer, lizards, a general displeasure with Thatcher and songs about falling in love with some quite bad people, the end of the world, a plague of irritating velociraptors and Nick Clegg's tragic, tragic future..
