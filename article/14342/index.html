James Owen Fender booked for Bestival 2011

This article was published in 2011.

James Owen Fender has been booked for Bestival 2011 off the back of his latest release, 4-track E.P - WooF (out through Plumpton Presents Records Monday 14th February).

A video filmed in Paris for the E.Ps lead track Everyday is now online and can be seen at the following link: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1didZVbHM2U

Fender will be concluding his first UK tour with dates in London, Nottingham Newcastle, Glasgow and York.
