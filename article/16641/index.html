Pulled Apart By Horses announce new single and headline tour

This article was published in 2012.

Hard-living, hard-rocking Leeds quartet Pulled Apart By Horses are announcing their biggest UK tour to date in November 2012.

"We can't wait to get out on tour again," said guitarist James Brown. "It's a sweet run of dates across the UK and finishes with a long overdue homecoming show in Leeds. We can't wait! This is going to be the shit!"

The band will also be releasing a new single - 'Bromance Ain't Dead' - on July 23rd, an awesome slice of driving rock action taken from the 'Tough Love' album.

Aside from slaying festivals across Europe this summer - including their first Main Stage performance at Leeds Festival in August - you can see Pulled Apart by Horses at Leeds Metropolitan University on Friday 16th November.
