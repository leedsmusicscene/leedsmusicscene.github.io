Hadouken! to release a new EP in September

This article was published in 2009.

Hadouken! will return with a brand new EP on September 15th 2009.

Recorded in Holland with Noisia, the ‘M.A.D EP’ is proof of further progression for the quintet who formed in Leeds at university just three years ago.

The EP evolves the cutting edge dance-rock sound unveiled on their debut album, ‘Music For An Accelerated Culture’, by delivering tracks that are harder, louder, more rhythmic and tightly focused than anything they’ve attempted before.

The EP includes three remixes by Detboi, Phace and Plastician, as well as two brand new tracks ‘Something Very BAD’ and a dub of ‘Ugly’, a track that will appear in a different form on the forthcoming, as yet untitled second album.

Hadouken! will appear in Leeds on 30th September when they play at the Stylus venue at Leeds University.

Tickets for the 14+ show are available now for £12 via Leeds Gig Tickets.
