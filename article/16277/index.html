The End Frets

This is an archive of the band profile for The End Frets.

We are The End Frets! We play original alternative post punk style music and we're based in Leeds and Wakefield. We first started playing late 2011, and started writing original work right from the off. Above all else were good mates, having a laugh where we can and playing music as often as possible and just starting to hit the local areas with our new sound. Our influences are so varied from 70s punk to modern acoustic which has really helped to create an original sound..
