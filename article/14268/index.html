Hannah Trigwell - Debut Single announced

This article was published in 2011.

The DEBUT single from Leeds musician Hannah Trigwell will be the first of many releases in 2011 with material co written by the full band.

The HEADRUSH teaser first previewed on Trigwell's YouTube channel received 5,000 views in a week and is now highly anticipated by her worldwide fanbase.

The single will be released via Digital Download from April 11th with hard copies available from the single launch at The Cockpit on Saturday, April 16th, and will include an acoustic version of 'HEADRUSH'.

The single will be self released in association with Hear Me Roar Records, her managers label.
