Nikoli to give away their 2003 single "The Resigned EP" ...

This article was published in 2004.

Nikoli have made "The Resigned EP" (originally released in 2003), available in full, for download from the band's website. Tracks include "Resigned", "The People", "Sandstone" and "Let Down". A free CD version of the single is also available by sending an e-mail to nikoli@mail.com, providing your name and address.

The band are currently recording their debut album and will be playing a series of acoustic gigs around Leeds in the next two months.
