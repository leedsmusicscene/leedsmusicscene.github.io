Alex Rennie & The City Bohemians announces EP launch

This article was published in 2013.

Alex Rennie & The City Bohemians have announced the release of their debut EP, 'All At Sea'.

The EP, which was mixed and mastered by Rich Collins at Snug Recording Co, Derby, is released on 16th November 2013 and is available in two formats: pay-what-you-like digital download and CD with hand screen-printed gatefold sleeve.

Both formats are available from alexrennie.bandcamp.com.

Watch the video to track 'Sentimental' below:



Other musicians who appear on the EP are: Aron Kyne, keyboard player with Thabo & The Real Deal and regular on Leeds' Jazz scene; Matthew Mason, Derbyshire guitarist with Echoed In Earth, Barkley Smith and Renbot; and Elliott Rennie, Cello player and touring actor musician.

The band will be celebrating the release with a gig at Hukaz, Leeds on Thursday 21st November. The gig is free entry.
