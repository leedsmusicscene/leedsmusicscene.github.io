In September 2011, Leeds Indie Pop band, Blood Oranges, took the executive decision, after months of procrastination, to change their name, we are now...Shark Teeth.

After a successful 18 months of playing high profile shows with the likes of The Vaccines, Two Door Cinema Club, Summer Camp, Surfer Blood and many more, as well as releasing a debut single on 7' vinyl, the band have been back in the studio with Whiskas (Forward Russia), recording tracks for a brand new EP, expected later in 2011..
