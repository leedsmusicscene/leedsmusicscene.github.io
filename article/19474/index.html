Fie! Fie! Fie!

This is an archive of the band profile for Fie! Fie! Fie!.

Our 2015 album: 'Can You Hear This?', and single, with video; 'The Alternative', is available on iTunes, Amazon, et al.

Fie! Fie! Fie! have featured recently on BBC Radio, MADE TV, Radio Rock - The Blitz (Hollywood, USA), Shed Seven's Rick Witter - Disco Down show, plus many other shows and media publications, nationally and globally. We have many more upcoming radio and media spots, plus a rapidly growing gig and festival schedule.

We have performed sets, across the country, for and alongside some great artists such as; Dave Sharp (ex The Alarm), The Reminisce, Paul Heaton and Jacqui Abbott (Beautiful South), The Bondurants and Tom Hingley (ex-Inspiral Carpets), amongst others.

We currently have brand new material for release soon..
