Unsuitable Pets make available their debut single for free

This article was published in 2010.

Leeds four-piece Unsuitable Pets have announced the release of their debut single, Tambourine Girl, which is available to download for free from the band's bandcamp.com page.

The band will also be giving away free CDs at gigs.

The single was been recorded and produced by the band and includes two tracks: 1. Tambourine Girl and 2. Sausage Song.

Unsuitable Pets are currently recording an EP, which will be available soon.
