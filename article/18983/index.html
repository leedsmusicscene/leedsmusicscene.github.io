West Yorkshire comes out on top at Pure Rawk Awards 2015

This article was published in 2015.

Leeds and Huddersfield, and the West Yorkshire region as a whole, were well represented on Friday scooping several of the Pure Rawk Awards on offer.

The Scaramanga Six took home two awards - "Axe Hero Of The Year" and "Video Of The Year" (see the video below) - while Eureka Machines brought home four awards including "Band Of The Year".



After the event Eureka Machines posted, "Overjoyed to report huge Pure Rawk Awards success, bringing home four awards including 'Band Of The Year'. Huge thanks to all who voted. X".

In what turned out to be a fantastic night for Huddersfield, Mikey Shiraz (The Parish venue, and front man of Mr Shiraz) won the award for "Promoter Of The Year" and Tropical Contact won the award for "New Breed Of The Year".
