Wordy

This is an archive of the band profile for Wordy.

A band fully formed in early 2012. The five members all currently study at the University of Leeds and met at Devonshire Halls. Currently finishing a 3 song cover EP with many ideas in the pipeline for original material.

What started as a simple open mic endeavour, over time grew into what you now see. Five students with a love for music, an excess of time on their hands and a great sound when it all comes together making a decision to create a something a bit different. They love what they do and the chemistry within the band is born out of great friendship..
