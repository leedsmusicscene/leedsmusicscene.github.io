The Pigeon Detectives to headline the White Air Extreme Sports Festival

This article was published in 2008.

The Pigeon Detectives will round off their summer of playing outdoors by headlining at the White Air Extreme Sports Festival on Sunday 20th September on the Isle of Wight.

White Air has run for 11 years, but this is only the second year they have introduced a music stage to the event. Last year it attracted more than 500 competitors and 50,000 spectators.

The band have already performed throughout Europe over the summer in support of their new album 'Emergency' and have also announced a very special London show at Alexandra Palace in December.

'Everybody Wants Me' is the new single, released this week on Dance to the Radio.
