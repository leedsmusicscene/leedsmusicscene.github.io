Amy Claro

This is an archive of the band profile for Amy Claro.

Amy Claro is a singer, songwriter, pianist and electronic musician maker of beautiful and affecting alternative pop music with electronic beats and production.

Her song Sunshine Machine is available to download free for anyone signing up to the mailing list at www.amyclaro.com. Also available is her debut album of original songs, This Is Yours, a collection of memorable choruses, witty lines, interesting beats and production sometimes intricate, sometimes epic. Download or buy it on CD at www.amyclaro.com. CDs are also stocked by Inkwell on Potternewton Lane, Leeds.

Amy is currently writing and producing new material. Earlier this year she finished the instrumental electronica track Meltwater, available to listen to on SoundCloud.

Live, she has been compared to Tori Amos and described as 'a sultrier Kate Bush'. Has piano, will travel..
