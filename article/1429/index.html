four day Hombre to release April single through Crystal Songs...

This article was published in 2003.

four day Hombre have announced that they will be releasing a single through the Crystal Songs label at the end of April. their announcement continues:

"They're quite a small label but we like 'em. Any label who's stated A & R policy is that '...the label has no A & R policy except to release good music by good bands' is good by us..."

Crystal Songs is actually a new label founded by independent publicists Hall Or Nothing, who are responsbile for artists such as: The Bluetones, Elbow, Feeder, Hell is for Heroes, Ikara Colt, John Squire, Manic Street Preachers, Oasis, Super Furry Animals and Leeds' own Rhianna (to name but a few).

So although the label may be 'small', expect four day Hombre to receive maximum exposure for their Spring release.
