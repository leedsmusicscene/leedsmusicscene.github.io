The Robots to record a new EP...

This article was published in 2002.

And None Of Them Knew They Were Robots are scheduled to record their new EP this week, with the record set for release in January 2003 on Jealous Records. The EP will include the tracks Victory as a Drug, Fighting Dragons, Funeral of flowers and Push and Pull.

The Robots will tour the EP in February, before hitting the road again next Easter with This Ain't Vegas.
