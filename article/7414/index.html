The Haggis Horns

This is an archive of the band profile for The Haggis Horns.

The Haggis Horns are an eight piece live funk extravaganza combining heavy breakbeat funk, soul, hip-hop and afrobeat with the virtuosity of trained jazz musicians. The members have performed and recorded with many top artists including: The Cinematic Orchestra, Lou Donaldson, Nightmares on Wax, Roots Manuva, Odyssey, Martina Topley-Bird, Corinne Bailey Rae, Lily Allen, Amy Winehouse, Snowboy, Stuart Zender (Jamiraqui's bassist) and Mark Ronson..
