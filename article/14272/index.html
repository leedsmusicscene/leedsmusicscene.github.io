Rosie Doonan releases 'Lay Your Love' in March

This article was published in 2011.

Rosie Doonan has announced the release of her new single, 'Lay Your Love', which hits the shops on March 7th.

The single will be released via the Silvertop Records label, and is an upbeat tale of a torrid break-up which finds its roots in traditional British folk-pop with a sprinkling of Americana for good measure.

'Lay Your Love' follows Rosie's second solo album 'Pot Of Gold' and her November single 'Fall For Me'.

Rosie will be touring her new single around the UK with an appearance in Leeds scheduled for the Adelphi on 5th March.

On 19th March Rosie Doonan will be appearing at the Shaw Theatre in Leeds.
