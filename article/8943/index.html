Leeds label Littlest Mojo Music to release the debut UK single by The Aftermath

This article was published in 2007.

New Leeds-based indie label Littlest Mojo Music will release the debut UK single by The Aftermath, a band based in their hometown Ireland but originally formed in Leeds by Johnny and Mic Cronin.

The single, “All I Want is for You to be Happy”, will be released on 12th November 2007.

The Aftermath return to Yorkshire to play at Joseph's Well (November 10th) and at Harrogate's Blues Bar (November 14th).
