The Blueskins to release 'User Friendly' on June 9th...

This article was published in 2003.

The Blueskins release their debut single User Friendly on 9th June.

They cut the EP with Richard Formby (formerly of Spacemen 3) at the controls. The result is a 120-second voodoo boogie with a twist of harp. B-side Give Me Your Hand is a stomping crowd-rouser, while Blue Sneakers sounds like a lost rock’n’roll gem but was freshly minted in February.
