Bands to battle it out to support Heart Research UK

This article was published in 2011.

Bands from Leeds, Pontefract, Bradford, Lincoln, Rugby, London and Brighton will come together in Leeds over the next few months to raise money for Heart Research UK and to be named as the Battle Of The Bands 2011 Champion.

The competition features a wide variety of genres including pop, dance, dub-step, metal, rock and punk.

Heats are taking place at the Royal Park Cellars in Leeds on 4th April, 11th April, 2nd May, and 16th May. The Grand Final will take place on 30th May,

Each show will open at 8pm and features 4 bands.
