Feeds EP launch night on Love Leeds Radio

This article was published in 2013.

Love Leeds Radio exclusive as Feeds are all set to launch their latest EP "Mind, Soul & Me' on Thursday 5th December.

To celebrate this they'll be taking over Love Leeds-Radio for the evening, playing the EP in full as well as some of their favourite songs.

Join Feeds as they Hijack the Love Leeds Radio studios on Thursday 5th December at 730PM
