Malibu Stacey release their debut single on October 11th...

This article was published in 2004.

Malibu Stacey release their debut single "Invasion" on Monday 11th October on the Townsend Records (Universal) label. The enhanced CD single also includes demo versions of favourites "Grade A Fellini" and "Rockstar", as well as the full length video to Invasion (and more).

You can also catch the video on the Scuzz music channel on Sky, as part of their "New Skids on the Block" feature

The band headline Manchester's Dry Bar on Sunday 19th September (on stage at 10pm) during the annual In The City event.
