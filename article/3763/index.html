LaRusso to release "Can't Let It Go" single...

This article was published in 2005.

Angst-pop-rock purveyors LaRusso have announced the forthcoming release of their new 7" single "Can't Let It Go". The track, which was self-recorded and self-produced in the band's personal recording studio, will be released on new Bradford indie label YeahMan in early May. An official launch date will be confirmed within the next week.

The b-side will feature live favourite "Seven Days".

You can catch LaRusso live this week at the Transmission Weekender (Joseph's Well, Sunday 17th April).
