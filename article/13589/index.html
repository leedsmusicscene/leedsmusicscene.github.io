Arthur Rigby & The Baskervylles release 'White Houses'

This article was published in 2010.

Arthur Rigby & The Baskervylles have released their new single, 'White Houses', on Monday 11th October.

The song was produced by Whiskas (Forward Russia, Dance to the Radio) and features the live-set opener, 'Heroes', as the B-side.

The song is now available on iTunes and through the band's page at bandcamp.com.

Arthur Rigby & The Baskervylles are a 9-piece orchestral pop band, based in Leeds. The sound the band creates has been likened to that of The Divine Comedy, Guillemots and Elbow.

Arthur Rigby will play The Library on the 29th October 2010.
