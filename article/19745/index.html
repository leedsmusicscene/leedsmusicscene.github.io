The Scaramanga Six release new single 'As We Take The Stage / Owned'

This article was published in 2017.

The Scaramanga Six have announced the release of their new single 'As We Take The Stage / Owned'.

Over the past 14 months, the irrepressible group have been back to Lord Alan Smyth's studio in Sheffield galvanising a new arsenal of material that will come to form their 9th studio album. So much has been accrued of sufficient quality that they have finally taken the inevitable step of planning a double-album, 'Chronica', for release in the summer of 2017.

Taken from the new album, the new single release will be entirely digital on a pay what you want basis from bandcamp.com. Listen below.
