Party Station

This is an archive of the band profile for Party Station.

Party Station were formed in 2002 by alumni of Leeds College of Music. The band has been working across Yorkshire on weddings, University Balls, parties and corporate events since then. In this time the band have performed at more than 300 successful events. Party Station are managed by G-Sounds, a Leeds based social enterprise who work in conjunction with Leeds College of Music Alumni staff to provide sustainable employment for some of the regions top young musicians.
