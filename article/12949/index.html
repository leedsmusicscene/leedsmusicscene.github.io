Festival Republic stage line up announced for Leeds Festival 2010

This article was published in 2010.

The Festival Republic Stage line up was announced by Leeds Festival 2010 organisers today and alongside the headliners will be a trio of local bands.

Yorkshire bands The Sunshine Underground, Pulled Apart by Horses and One Night Only will appear on the stage that always showcases the headliners of tomorrow and features the hottest up and coming names alongside more established artists.

Headlining at Leeds 2010 will be Ash (Friday), British Sea Power (Saturday) and Marina & the Diamonds (Sunday).

Supporting Ash on the Friday are Atari Teenage Riot, The Black Angels, Fight Like Apes, Giggs, Darwin Deez, Chapel Club, Villagers, Alberta Cross, I Blame Coco, Freelance Whales, Chief, Yuck and The Crookes.

The full Saturday line up is British Sea Power, Caribou, One Night Only, Minus The Bear, Tame Impala, The Like, Free Energy, Fools Gold, General Fiasco, Sound of Guns, Foxy Shazam, Goldhawks and Kassidy.

Finally, the Sunday sees Marina & the Diamonds along with a supporting cast of The Sunshine Underground, Blood Red Shoes, Adam Green, Warpaint, Avi Buffalo, Egyptian Hip Hop, Pulled Apart by Horses, Summer Camp, Funeral Party, Harlem, The Cheek and Gaggle.
