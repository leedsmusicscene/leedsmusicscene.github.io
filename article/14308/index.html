First bands announced for Long Division festival

This article was published in 2011.

The first set of bands were announced today for Long Division, a three-day multi-venue music festival taking place in Wakefield in June 2011.

Long Division is organised by indie fanzine Rhubarb Bomb and Wakefield music venue The Hop.

The main event will take place on Saturday 11th June, when tickets can be exchanged for wristbands allowing entry into all participating venues. The bands announced for the Saturday so far include:

The Wedding Present
Los Campesinos!
Emmy The Great
Napoleon IIIrd
Middleman
Skint & Demoralised
The Lovely Eggs
Blood Oranges
Runaround Kids
The Spills
Piskie Sits
