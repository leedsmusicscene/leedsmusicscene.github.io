Ali Whitton and the Broke Record Players complete their debut album

This article was published in 2007.

Ali Whitton and the Broke Record Players have completed their debut album entitled "A Failed Attempt at Something Worth Saying". The album was recorded in London at the end of last year with Fraser Smith producing. It is hoped that the 14 track record will be available towards the end of the Spring.

The band are currently discussing their options and will be talking to labels with a view to a prompt release.

Four of the new songs are available to listen to through the band's website.
