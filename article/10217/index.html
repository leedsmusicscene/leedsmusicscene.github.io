Micky P Kerr to release 'Credit Crunch Christmas' on Monday

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds musician Micky P Kerr will release his new single, ‘Credit Crunch Christmas’, on Monday, 15th December through the Modern English Records label. The single is available on all major online stores (iTunes, 7digital, etc).

You can watch the video at YouTube and watch Micky P Kerr live at the Brudenell Social Club on 19th December.
