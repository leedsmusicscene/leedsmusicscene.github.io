Fulc begin recording a follow up to "Biting Insomnia"

This article was published in 2004.

Leeds based Fulc return to the studio in October to record the follow up to last year's album "Biting Insomnia". The band are booked into the Chapel Studios in South Thoresby, where The Darkness, Machine Head and Funeral for a Friend have been recent clients, to spend two weeks recording a mini album scheduled for release in February 2005 on Stunted Records (via Shellshock Distribution).

Following recording Fulc will be undertaking a series of UK dates from mid October to end of November to road test the new material. Full details will be announced once the schedule has been finalised.

Fulc are booked to play a couple of shows, an In The City fringe event and Royal Park Cellars on Friday 24th September.

In other news it has been confirmed that following growing interest in Europe, Fulc's debut album "Biting Insomnia" will have an October release in Germany and Holland via Rough Trade Germany.
