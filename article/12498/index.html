Leeds Festival 2010 tickets to go on sale on Monday 29th March

This article was published in 2010.

Leeds Festival organisers have today announced that the tickets for Leeds 2010 will officially go on sale on Monday, 29th March at 6.45pm.

Leeds 2010 will take place at Bramham Park over the August Bank Holiday (27th to 29th August inclusive) and tickets for the 3-day music extravaganza are expected to be priced at around Â£180 plus booking fee.

As has been the norm in recent years, the line up for Leeds Festival is expected to be revealed at the same time with Blink 182 currently leading the rumours for a headline slot.

Other bands rumoured for Leeds 2010 are Arcade Fire, My Chemical Romance, Green Day, and Yorkshire band The Cribs.

You can keep up to date with the latest Leeds Festival line up news on the Leeds Festival 2010 website and all the official ticket links can be found on the Leeds Tickets page.

Organisers recommend that you only buy from official ticket websites with our recommendation being to buy via Ticketmaster or See Tickets.
