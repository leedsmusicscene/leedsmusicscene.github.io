The Cribs continue their musical blitzkrieg with a new single on 22nd August...

This article was published in 2005.

On Monday 22nd August, The Cribs release the third single – titled “Martell” (Wichita) – to be taken from their 2005 album “The New Fellas”. With two top 30 hits already this year, this new single again epitomises the trio's ability to knock out some killer tunes!

Available on 7” and CD formats you can pre-order from HMV.

The Cribs return from touring the USA with shows at this year's Leeds and Reading Festivals, before heading out on tour again with Kaiser Chiefs as part of the NME Rock'n'Roll Riot Tour, playing Leeds Town Hall on 18th, 19th and 20th October.
