Nightmares On Wax to perform intimate DJ set at Jumbo Records this weekend

This article was published in 2013.

Jumbo Records are delighted to welcome Nightmares On Wax in store this Saturday, 9th November, for a very special intimate DJ set.

George Eveyln is the creative force behind Nightmares on Wax and responsible for some of the defining moments within dance music of the 90's. He is also credited as a founding member of trip-hop and tacit instigator in the explosion of the chill-out genre, following the release of the seminal Smokers Delight in 1995, a genre that is still going strong today.

George was also responsible (alongside Richard Watson - 360 club) for bringing underground artists such as Afrika Bambaataa, DJ Shadow, Cold Cut, Kid Koala and Roni Size to the fore, through the legendary Leeds club nights Funky Mule & Headz Club which began in '92.

So come and spend your Saturday afternoon soaking up some lovely vibes with a real legend. We can't think of many better ways to spend your day, so if you don't want to miss out, get yourself there! DJ set starts at 1pm.

Available in store on the day, will be the Nightmares On Wax back catalogue at a very reasonable price and the new album will also be on offer. Following the DJ set George Eveyln will be available for signing those all important purchases.
