The Sunday Postmen

This is an archive of the band profile for The Sunday Postmen.

Tom Lillywhite - Lead Vocals
Cullen Spurr - Guitar
Edward Borrett - Guitar
Daniel Tait - Bass Guitar
Luke Furlong - Drums

We were on a bus one day joking about how we should start a band called "The Sunday Postmen". At one of the stops a woman got up from behind us, on her way off the bus she remarked that it was a good idea and that she could be our first fan so we decided that we'd make it a reality - a few weeks later we released "Cup Of Misery" on YouTube.

The name comes from the line "No post on Sundays!" which is said by Uncle Vernon in Harry Potter and the Philosophers Stone..
