Beacons Festival announces first wave of acts

This article was published in 2013.



The Mercury Music nominated Django Django lead the way as one of many highlights as Beacons Festival yesterday revealed the first 25 acts that will be performing at the festival in August.

The cutting-edge three-day, four-night music and arts festival will once again be taking place in the picturesque setting of the Funkirk Estate in Skipton, Yorkshire between Friday 16th and Sunday 18th August 2013.
