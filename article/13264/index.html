Talk To Angels release their new single 'Enemies' in October

This article was published in 2010.

Bradford band Talk To Angels have announced the release of their new single. The single will be entitled 'Enemies' and will be available in October via the Warp Speed Carousel label.

The band, who include Embrace keyboard player Mickey Dale, will tour the single in September. They also play a live set on Ian Wright's Talksport Radio show in October.
