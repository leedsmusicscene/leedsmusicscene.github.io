Last Night's TV to release new a double A-side single on Monday...

This article was published in 2004.

Last Night's TV will relese a new double A-side single on Monday, 1st November, consisting of "Holidaymaker" and "The First Days Of Winter". The former got its debut outing at the band's gig last month; the latter got its one and only public airing four and a half years ago, but the recording is brand new.

The band have also completed work on the new version of their "Too Much In Doubt" album, which now comes as a 2-disc set with bonus CD-rom containing the video for "From The Top Of The Watchtower".

Both of these releases are available from the band's website.
