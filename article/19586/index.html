Deuce & Charger release new single 'Who Can You Trust?' on 22nd July

This article was published in 2016.

Following extensive press and radio support for recent singles 'We Can Beat The Night' and 'Start A Fire', Deuce & Charger emerge from the shadows with the paranoid double-punch of 'Who Can You Trust?' / 'Equilibrium', released on In Charge Recordings on 22 July.

'Who Can You Trust?' evokes a schizophrenic future world; spectral vocals glide over an ominous soundscape that builds to a dark, dramatic climax. 'Equilibrium' intensifies the warped nocturnal mood of the release by setting dystopian vocal commands against menacing atmospherics and speaker shredding bass. To round-off the release, new Viper Recordings signing Dossa & Locuzzed take 'Who Can You Trust?' from the half-light to the sunlight with a massive synth soaked remix.

Deuce & Charger have been breaking on the dance underground since 2014 with support from tastemaker blogs, channels and radio stations including Less Than 3, Skank & Bass, Magnetic Magazine, Drum & Bass HQ, YourEDM.com, The Noise Engine and Bassport FM. They have been playlisted on 1Xtra, played on Radio 1 and Kiss Fresh, featured in Mixmag and DJ Mag, and remixed by Chords (RAM Records) and Kronology (Technique Recordings).
