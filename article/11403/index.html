The Meerkats

This is an archive of the band profile for The Meerkats.

They are:
Howard - Vocals, piano and guitar. Amazing vocal range, anything from Joe Cocker to the Scissor Sisters, backed up by quite superb piano playing. (Look out Jools!)

Tony - Lead guitar. Fine player in the style Eric Clapton meets Gary Moore (round at Carlos Santana's house!) Faithfully reproduces those well known solos from some of the best players around.

Wedding: 2 x 45 minute sets + 30 minute encore. £300 + expenses.
Wedding: 2 x 45 minute sets + 30 minute encore + all incidental music (you choose) + 1 hour Disco at the end of the evening. £400 + expenses.

Party: From £200, depends on numbers and location.
Pubs: Always happy to play an evening of fine Blues/Rock and Pop Classics, finished off with a bit of cheese where appropriate.

They always enjoy the night, and so will you!.
