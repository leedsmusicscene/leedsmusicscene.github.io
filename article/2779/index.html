The Music to release a track from their forthcoming album...

This article was published in 2004.

The Music have announced details of the release of the first new material from their, as yet, untitled second album.

Welcome To The North will be made available to download on Monday 19th July 2004 via the band’s website.

Recorded with producer Brendan O’Brien (Stone Temple Pilots, Korn, Soundgarden, Pearl Jam) in Atlanta in the early part of 2004, the song is one of 12 tracks from the album due out in September. The track was previewed earlier this year at the band's sold out NME Awards headline show at London’s Astoria.

The album will be preceded by a single, Freedom Fighters in September.
