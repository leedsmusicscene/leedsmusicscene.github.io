'Done In Secret' is the brand new single from The Pigeon Detectives

This article was published in 2011.

The first single to be taken from the forthcoming The Pigeon Detectives album, 'Up, Guards And At 'Em!', will be the track 'Done In Secret'.

'Done In Secret' will be released through Dance to the Radio records on Monday, 28th March and is the band's first single since 2008. It was recorded in New York at Brooklyn's Recording Company with Justin Gerrish.

'Done In Secret' is available on limited edited 7" vinyl, CD and Digital Download formats.
