3milehigh

This is an archive of the band profile for 3milehigh.

Rob Karl - vocals
Will Grinder - guitar
Wozza - drums
Tom Hall - bass

"This is a rock band with a difference, I have no doubt that these guys will be big" - Absolute Leeds

"This is a band we will definitely see on CD:UK. If you like Feeder, Terrovision and Rasmus you will love 3milehigh" - Sandman Magazine

Not satisfied with selling out the album launch show at the Borderline London, or rocking out a capacity audience at the Camel Rock Festival (Isle of Scilly) and at the ITC Music Conference in Manchester, 3MH are now set to play live on BBC TV and BBC Raw Talent radio in the upcoming months.

Their debut album "CARDBOARD WORLD" has received rave reviews as far a field as Canada and Australia. The all-Leeds band signed to the Independent record label Global Music Development in January of this year and have since not looked back.

Led by the passionate vocalist Rob Karl with Will Grinder on guitar,Tom Hall on bass and "Woz" on drums, this four piece rock band are definitely one to look out for ....
