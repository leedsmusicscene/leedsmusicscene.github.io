Leaders Of Men!

This is an archive of the band profile for Leaders Of Men!.

Leaders of Men! are a four piece postpunk dave (dance/rave) band from Leeds who focus on live performance rather than studio performances. They take influences from bands so varied as Joy Division and Bloc Party and sound like a melodic mix between Joy Division and The Horrors.

The messages in their songs are usually political and relative to communism, anti-monarchism and revolution. They Consist of: Joe North (Lead Vocals, Lead Guitar, Keyboards), Dominic Lehany (Rhythm Guitar), James Hunter (Bass Guitar) and Harry 'Dobby' Dobson (Drums/Percussion)..
