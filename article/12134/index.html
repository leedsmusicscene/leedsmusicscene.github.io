Ryan Spendlove

This is an archive of the band profile for Ryan Spendlove.

Ryan Spendlove was born ind raised in a small ex-mining village in West Yorkshire called Gawthorpe. He started playing guitar at 18 and helped to start a 4 piece band called The Blueskins who managed a tour in Europe and America before they split around 2005. they played a few major festivals(Leeds, Reading, South By South West) one of their songs "change my mind" was used in a Lynx deodorant TV commercial and in the remake of the cult classic series The Dukes of Hazzard. Ryan is now playing solo and is gaining popularity in the Blues/Accoustic scene..
