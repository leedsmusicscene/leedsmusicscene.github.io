Mr Beasley

This is an archive of the band profile for Mr Beasley.

Bobby (from Hull) and Sarah (Leeds) are the electro act, Mr Beasley. They met when Bobby stencilled a smiley face all around town, catching the eye of Sarah, who decided to track down the artist and make music with him.

Their debut single 'Neon' was named single of the week on Radio 1's One World and made it on to the playlist of DJs such as Evil Nine and Adam Freeland, at clubs like Fabric Live, Bugged Out, Ministry of Sound, and Sonar Festival.

Their next single, Right As Rain, is released on the 10th November on Shoes Don't Fit, a new label collaboration between Mr Beasley and their management company, Bad Sneakers (Wild Beasts/ The Sugars/ The Old Romantic Killer Band), and is taken from their forthcoming album..
