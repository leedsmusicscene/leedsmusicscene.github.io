Rhythmic Movement Disorder

This is an archive of the band profile for Rhythmic Movement Disorder.

Vocals: Rob
Guitar: Maff
Guitar: Elliott
Bass: Sly
Drums: Chris

We formed about a year and a half ago, we hail from the mean streets of Wakey and Ponte, have played quite a few gigs around the wakefield and Pontefract area, also recently played the Cockpit with Kiss The Dawn, Mishkin, Shot by Both Sides and Liquid Sky. We're a five piece Stomp Metal band, incorporating influeneces from Hatebreed, Pantera, Crowbar, Black Label Society and all things heavy..
