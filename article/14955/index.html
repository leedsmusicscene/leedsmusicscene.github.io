The Pigeon Detectives announce new single

This article was published in 2011.

Local band The Pigeon Detectives have this week announced the release of 'Lost', the second single to be taken from their third album 'Up, Guards And At 'Em!'.

The single was recorded with producer Justin Gerrish in New York, and you can watch the video for 'Lost' below:



Already established as a firm live favourite, 'Lost' is released ahead of the band's appearance on the main stage at Reading and Leeds Festivals this August, after which the band head out on the road again this autumn.
