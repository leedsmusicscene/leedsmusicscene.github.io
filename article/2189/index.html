Pierpoint

This is an archive of the band profile for Pierpoint.

Daniel Louch - vocals/guitar
Matthew Troth - lead guitar/backing vocals
Richard Troth - bass guitar/backing vocals
Kieran Troth - drums

Pierpoint are indie/rock fine-tuned and unmistakebly West Yorkshire.
A mix of The Cribs,Kings of Leon,Kasabian and any other band really worth listening to.
Go see them now while you can still get to the front!
Check profile for upcoming gigs.
Also on myspace and facebook.

"Pierpoint-named after Albert,the famed hangman of Bradford-are a tight collective of would be post-punk/new wave guitar heroes.
They have a decent following already and the dedication they obviously have used to file jagged metal edges into sharp songs is impressive.
Really rather interesting!"
-Review from dalliance.co.uk of Granadaland gig at The Love Apple,Feb '08..
