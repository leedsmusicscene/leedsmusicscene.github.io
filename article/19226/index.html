Allusondrugs announce new single

This article was published in 2015.

Allusondrugs have announced the release of a limited edition 7'' purple vinyl for the 'Too Pure Singles Club' via Beggars Banquet Records in September.

Just head over to www.toopure.com to find out more.

The band have also put out a video and re-recording of the previous 2014 E.P version of 'Sunset Yellow' which you can listen to below. This will be the band's next single.
