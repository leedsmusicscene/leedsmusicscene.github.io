Glory Never Fades

This is an archive of the band profile for Glory Never Fades.

Punk Rock arrived in Leeds thirty years ago, and with it came John Keenan's famous F Club.

The F Club spawned many local punk bands, amongst them were The Expelaires, Tragic Minds and Music for Pleasure

Thirty years on, three original members of those well known bands are back together. They are Sean Walker (Expelaires), Joe Lewis (Tragic Minds) and Alan Peace (Tragic Minds & Music for Pleasure). Along with two other well known local musicians, Rick Smith and Anna Gaches - they are - Glory Never Fades.
