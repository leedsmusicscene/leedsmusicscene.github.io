Pulled Apart by Horses release 'Yeah Buddy'

This article was published in 2010.

Pulled Apart by Horses have released their latest single, 'Yeah Buddy'.

'Yeah Buddy' is out today and comes backed with a brand new B-side 'Soft Handed Punk'. You can buy the single on 7" single and Digital Download formats.

To coincide with the release, Pulled Apart by Horses will be playing a special free show in Leeds on Friday 3rd December with Vice and Dr Martens. The event takes place at the new VOX Warehouse and also includes Fucked Up, Kong and That Fucking Tank.
