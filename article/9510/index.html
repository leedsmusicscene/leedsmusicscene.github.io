The Manhattan Love Suicides to release 27-track album in June

This article was published in 2008.

The Manhattan Love Suicides will release their CD album ‘Burnt Out Landscapes’ in early June 2008 through the Leeds-based Squirrel Records label.

The mammoth 27-track album includes all of the band's singles to date, including all four tracks from the band’s latest EP (‘Clusterfuck EP', March 2008). It will also include radio session tracks and four exclusive brand new recordings.

Catch The Manhattan Love Suicides live in Leeds when they play at Oporto on 26th May with Yucatan and Jonjo Feather.
