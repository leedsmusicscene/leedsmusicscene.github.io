Luna Kings

This is an archive of the band profile for Luna Kings.

Luna Kings are a the best kept secret in Leeds. With their atmospheric guitar hooks, punching bass lines, pulsating rhythmic beats and anthemic melodies, Luna Kings are a band you simply cannot ignore.

Luna Kings have quickly assembled their distinctive yet infectious sound. Their songs connect and resonate with society telling tales of love, lust and regret against the backdrop of powerful and emotive music.

Dreamy, spacious rhythmic sounds with a sudden sense of urgency, punk aggression and intensity, this band will soon be reaching new heights in popularity and acclamation..
