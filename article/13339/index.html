Leeds Festival Preview: who to watch at Leeds 2010

This article was published in 2010.

Are you the indecisive type? It's ok - you don't have to make up your mind right away. For now just accept that maybe you're the type of person who is confident enough to take advice. Someone not so stubborn that they would ignore a golden opportunity staring them in the face. Like right now for instance...

Webster's Dictionary defines Leeds Festival 2010 as "a rag-tag collection of ass-kicking rock and rollers assembled for your listening pleasure" and we wouldn't want to argue with them, what with their extensive vocabulary an all.

So how can we really help you? Here's how. First we asked for assistance from a host of local musical luminaries. Some of them answered. Below each of their set of five recommendations ("Five To Follow") are listed in time and date order together with links showing where you can find out more information on every artist. Secondly we've tallied up their votes towards a not at all arbitrary (NAAA) top 9 selection.

You've got to ask yourself one question. Do I feel lucky? If not, then maybe it's best to take some advice from the professionals.

Please leave your comments at the end and make your own recommendations for who to see at Leeds 2010.
