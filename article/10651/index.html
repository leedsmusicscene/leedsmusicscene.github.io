Leeds label Tip Toe Records to release a limited edition compilation album

This article was published in 2009.

Leeds indie label Tip Toe Records are to release a very limited-edition compilation album at the end of May 2009. Released on 27th May, the CD will be restricted to just 200 copies, all of which will come with brown cardboard sleeves.

The compilation featuring the following bands, several of which come from Leeds: The Old Romantic Killer Band, The Bear Driver, Out of the Afternoon, Essay Like Nephew, peasman, King Alexander, Yonderboy, Just Handshakes (We're British), Red Trees, The Tailors, Fists, Travels by Telephones, Swimming, Crayon, Ten and Love Ends Disaster.
