Harmacy

This is an archive of the band profile for Harmacy.

Harmacy were drawn together in late 2004 through a passion for lo-fi rock n roll and over kitchen sink tales of love and loss. Girls having dates with barstools and searches for love in cyberspace formed the basis of their alt.pop sensibilities. With a sound that is both off the wall and precise at the same time, they set out to write skewed pop songs and to deliver them in there own laid back slacker rock style.

We're releasing a ltd edition 7" single 'Carla's Song/Dead Letter Day'on September 21st 2006 through MonoTones records, which is part of the new Mono fanzine group.
