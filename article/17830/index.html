Marsicans - MJ

This is a review of "MJ" recorded by Marsicans. The review was written by Amy Walker in 2013.

Cool, beachy, indie surf-pop. No I'm not talking about The Drums, this is even better.

From sun-kissed guitar riffs and refreshing vocals to the infectious drum beat - MJ by Marsicans is the latest drop in their talent-ridden ocean. And forget about surfing, you'll wanna go scuba diving instead. The Leeds-based quartet have even made this sparkling little number available on a free download to anyone who signs up to their mailing list [sign up here]. Now you're just spoiling us!
