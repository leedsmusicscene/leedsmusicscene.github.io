Beacons Festival add British Sea Power and Joan As Police Woman to 2014 lineup

This article was published in 2014.

Organisers of Beacons Festival have tonight confirmed the additional of 29 new acts for 2014, including special performances by British Sea Power and Joan As Police Woman.

Opening the main stage, British Sea Power will play an exclusive live soundtrack to the award winning film, "From The Sea To The Land Beyond".

Joan As Police Woman, following her recent sold out UK tour, is another big name added to the Yorkshire festival.
