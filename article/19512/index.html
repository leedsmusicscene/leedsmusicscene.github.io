Edgar Duke

This is an archive of the band profile for Edgar Duke.

A well-oiled three-piece who never cease to bring the funk whenever they play. They have a sound that fluctuates between soft and heavy. In the heavy moments you can hear the likes of Queen Of The Stone Age, Drenge and The Amazing Snakeheads. The softer moments are reminiscent of The Velvet Underground, Mac Demarco, and Foxygen.

Their over-all genre being Psychedelic-funk rock and their live presence is full of energy and charisma..
