The Cribs to play free show at HMV in Leeds

This article was published in 2009.

To celebrate the launch of the band's latest single 'Cheat On Me', The Cribs will be appearing at HMV Leeds today at noon.

Wristbands for the event, which will feature a live show and a signing session, will be given out free on a first-come first-served basis from 11am.

The band have continued to deny rumours that they will be making a 'secret' appearance at the Leeds Festival this weekend. Rumours of a show have continued to frequent the internet, especially as the band play Reading HMV tomorrow.

The single is taken from the band's fourth album, 'Ignore The Ignorant', which is to be released on 7th September 2009 through the Wichita Recordings label.

The Cribs will play Leeds O2 Academy on 4th October as part of the UK tour this October. Tickets are currently all sold out - visit Leeds Gig Tickets for the latest ticket information.
