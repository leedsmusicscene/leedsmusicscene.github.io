Wireless Festival 2006: tickets available now...

This article was published in 2006.

The line up for the Wireless Festival 2006 was announced this week, with the event taking place across two days at Harewood House in Leeds as well as its original home at Hyde Park in London, and tickets are now available.

The event takes place on Saturday 24th June and Sunday 25th June and the bands playing include Massive Attack, Goldfrapp, Pharrell and DJ Shadow (Saturday) as well as The Who, Super Furry Animals and The Flaming Lips (Sunday).

Tickets for the festival went on sale today priced £32.50 (Saturday) and £37.50 (Sunday). For more details, and to purchase tickets visit www.wirelessfestival.co.uk.
