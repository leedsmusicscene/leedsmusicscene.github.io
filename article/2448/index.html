Kenosha to release double A-side...

This article was published in 2004.

Kenosha release a double A-side single (containing songs One Percenter and Stop Sign) on Bass Lake Recordings on Monday 8th March, available at all of Leeds' music shops and via the band's website.

In other news, two tracks taken from a recent Kenosha demo have been loaded to the BBC OneMusic website. The tracks include One Percenter and Get Good, Get Dead, and both are now available to download, rate and review by clicking here.

Finally a recording of The Snow, again taken from a recent demo, will feature on the third Public Service Broadcast Compilation from Smalltown America Recordings. The CD will also feature tracks from a host of other unsigned, but equally killer bands from near and far. Available from www.smalltownamerica.co.uk and also at Jetplane Landing gigs (8th May @ Cockpit) from 12th April. Run by Jetplane Landing, Smalltown America is an independent record label run on the principal that "good music needs to be heard - that's the bottom line". For more information visit the website.

Kenosha's next confirmed gig is at the Fenton on 26th March.
