jamiesaysmile New EP 'Grand Authentic Stage' Released

This article was published in 2013.

The new 5 track EP by jamiesaysmile was self-released on 1st July titled 'Grand Authentic Stage'.

It's all the work of jamiesaysmile (Dan Hayes) and includes 3 layered, full band sound tracks and 2 intimate acoustic versions.

First review from Joe Lepper over at Neon Filler (Joe gave it a very encouraging 7/10 too!): http://www.neonfiller.com/wordpress/?p=8905
