Violet Shrink

This is an archive of the band profile for Violet Shrink.

Amy Boothman, Pete Wheeler, Gillan Cash and Allan Jones are collectively known as Violet Shrink.

Bouncing onto the music scene last year with a slightly different line up, Violet Shrink have gone from strength to strength perfecting their catchy songs and refining their sound.

Their music has been described as;

"An explosion of emotion and passion fused with a heady mix of pop/rock and a sprinkling of angst. The underlying message is one of hope and escape. And the need to totally rock out in order to stay sane!"¬ù

Now with gigs lined up at a variety of venues and freebie CDs to give away, Violet Shrink are ready to spread their infectious melodies throughout the North in 2009 and beyond...
