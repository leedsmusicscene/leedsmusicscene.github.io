Headliners announced for O2 Wireless Festival 2007 in Leeds

This article was published in 2007.

The headliners for the O2 Wireless Leeds 2007 festival have been revealed today, and as previously announced it will be the Leeds five-piece Kaiser Chiefs that will be the main attraction on the Saturday night (16th June). Support will come from Editors, The Cribs, The Rakes, and The Twang.

Other headliners for the three-day event at Leeds' Harewood House will be The White Stripes and Queens Of The Stone Age (Friday, 15th June) and Daft Punk and CSS (Sunday, 17th June).

Tickets go on sale on Friday, 16th March at 9am, priced individually at £40 per day. Or you can attend two days for £75, or all three days for £105.
