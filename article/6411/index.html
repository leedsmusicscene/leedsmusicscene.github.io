The Pigeon Detectives to release their debut single on March 13th

This article was published in 2006.

Following on from the “What We All Want” compilation album (released 27th February), Leeds-based record label Dance To The Radio have announced that the next single to be released on the label will be by The Pigeon Detectives. Available as a limited 7” single (500 copies only), “I'm Not Sorry” comes backed with “I'm Always Right” and will be released on Monday, 13th March.

With regular airplay at Radio One and XFM, The Pigeon Detectives' single is likely to sell out fast and pre-orders are being taken now from the Dance To The Radio website.

The Pigeon Detectives, who supported Editors and The Rifles last year, and Battle this, play a single launch party at the Cockpit on Friday, 17th March as part of a UK tour.
