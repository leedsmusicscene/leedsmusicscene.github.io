Under Darkened Skies

This is an archive of the band profile for Under Darkened Skies.

When you mix metal and hardcore, you get metal-core! When you mix symphonies and metal, you get symphonic metal! But what do you get when you mix metal, hardcore, thrash, death-metal, black-metal and screamo? You get Under Darkened Skies muthaâ€™fucker!

Under Darkened Skies consists of 5 individuals who have come together from the ashes of failed bands to create a more explosive, original and downright fucking awesome sound ever heard before. Combining lots of different styles into one you simply cannot categorise Under Darkened Skies. Despite their young age Under Darkened Skies are a force to be reckoned with. Their songs show a musical maturity way beyond their years. Under Darkened Skies aim is to play aggressive music but still with emotion built into, not just aggressive music to make you feel angry, but aggressive music with meaning behind it. They accomplish this with flying colours.

2006 will see Under Darkened Skies starting to establish themselves in the UK underground metal scene. Recordings will be up shortly and more gigs will follow. Also the band will start to write songs for an EP which will hopefully be out by the end of the year.
