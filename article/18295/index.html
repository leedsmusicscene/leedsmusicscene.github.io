Ragdoll

This is an archive of the band profile for Ragdoll.

Ragdoll formed in September 2013 when 3 people with slightly different tastes in music starting rehearsing with an aim to mix a lot of their favourite music together and not fall under the name of one genre.

We released our d√©but demo on the 24th December 2013 which featured the first two songs we penned - Subterfuge & You & I. The demo was very well received and inspired us to create more music heading into 2014.

We are now equipped with a full set and we are currently looking to gig anywhere and everywhere.

We would describe our music as a crossover between Rage Against the Machine and Refused with our own personal touch thrown in there.
