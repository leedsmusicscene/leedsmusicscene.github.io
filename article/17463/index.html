The Soul Circle Gang celebrate new single 'Fresh Fruit', out 26th April, with launch at Smokestack

This article was published in 2013.

The Soul Circle Gang release their brand new single 'Fresh Fruit' at the Smokestack on 26th April. Make sure you're there for what's sure to be a great night and be the first to own a copy!! Doors at 20:00. Band on at 21:00

The Single 'Fresh Fruit' an up-beat Pop/Reggae tune was recorded at Greenmount Studio's and comes complete with B-Side 'Umbrella of Life' a twisting and turning Jazz/Funk piece.
