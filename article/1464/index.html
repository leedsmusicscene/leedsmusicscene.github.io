Fulc announce the release of their debut album Biting Insomnia.

This article was published in 2003.

Fulc have announced that the release of their debut album Biting Insomnia will be on Monday 19th May 2003. Their blistering grunge, punk, metal has been described as:

"Beyond categorisation unless the word you are looking for is remarkable" - Dom Gourlay March 2003.
"One of the most vibrantly inventive young bands in the UK today" - Kerrang Oct 2002.
"Gobsmacked ! the best band I have seen in 5yrs" - Atomic Duster Sept 2002.

Recorded at Sawmills Studios in Cornwall by Mark Thomas (engineer on all the Muse recordings) the album builds upon the bands previous release of September 2002 The Fallen EP which was received to great critical acclaim. The twelve track CD includes re-recordings of Dolly and Drawn Out (two tracks from the bands original demo) and an undisclosed secret track.

The band has already embarked upon an extensive touring schedule to promote the album, further details of this and the tracklisting for the album are available via the band's website.
