Vib Gyor selected for XFM Unsigned show on 13th July...

This article was published in 2004.

Vib Gyor have been selected as one of this week's unsigned bands as part of XFM's radio show "X-posure" presented by Claire Sturgess. The band will have their track "You Wear Me Out" played on air on Tuesday evening (13th July) between 9pm and 11pm, and an online voting system will reveal the week's audience favourite.

For more details, or to vote for Vib Gyor please visit XFM.co.uk here.
