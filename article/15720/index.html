Lifescreen's new EP 'The Lockup Sessions' is out now

This article was published in 2011.

The new EP 'The Lockup Sessions' is out now.

Produced by Mark Burrows
Mastered by Andy Richards

You can listen to it on: 92.4 FM/95.3 FM and DAB

Next gig: Saturday 3rd December at Carpe Diem, Leeds.
Bands from 8pm, free entry.
