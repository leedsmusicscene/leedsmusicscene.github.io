Ashley Hicklin hits Number 1 in Belgian charts

This article was published in 2010.

Since singing to EMI Publishing, Ashley Hicklin has co-written several hit songs across Europe for other artists, including a No.1 single in Sweden and 2 x Gold album at No.3 in Sweden: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MwIjw0mhOYA

More recently, Ashley's co-written song "Me And My Guitar" has been chosen as Belgium's entrance song to Eurovision 2010. It will be performed by Tom Dice:
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uqZdOc6E4m8&feature=related

The song has already charted at No. 1 on the iTunes chart 48 hours after release and is set to reach No. 1 in the physical sales chart later this week.

In other news, Ashley Hicklin's debut single as an artist in his own right is to be released on Ferryhouse / Warner Music in Spring 2010.
