Feeds release new single 'Indecision' for FutureSound Competition

This article was published in 2014.

To celebrate being picked for the FutureSound Competition, the winners of which play Leeds/Reading Festival, Feeds have recorded a new single 'Indecision' due for release on FRIDAY 11TH JULY. The song will be FREE for anybody who buys tickets (Â£4) to Feeds' heat, also on FRIDAY 11TH JULY at The Wardrobe, Leeds.



Continuing their relationship with James Mottershead (British Sea Power, Marmozets) the single crafts a unique sound, an eclectic mix of old school prog and modern alternative pop. From its weighty guitar riffs to the soaring chorus, 'Indeicion' is a showcase of Feeds' sound, giving you a glimpse of the energy they're acclaimed for live.

Their previous singles 'Beware Believe' and 'Shallow Eyes' gained praise from the likes of BBC Introducing's Alan Raw and global star Nitin Sawhney. It also got them support slots with the likes of The Hoosiers and NME favourites Circa Waves.
