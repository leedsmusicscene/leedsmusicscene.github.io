Middleman to release a new single on August 30th

This article was published in 2010.

August 30th sees the infectious new single by Leeds-based Middleman, titled 'It's Not Over Yet'. The single is available as a Digital Download and is released via the Blip Records / Universal label.

'It's Not Over Yet' follows the band's previous singles, 'Blah Blah Blah' and 'Good To Be Back', which reached 14 and 10 in the UK Indie Chart respectively.

Middleman have appeared at Leeds Festival, both the Festival Republic Stage and the BBC Introducing Stage, and earlier this year they wowed the crowds at South By South West 2010 in Texas. They are currently finishing their self-produced debut album, which is set for release in early 2011.

You can listen to "It's Not Over Yet" on Middleman's Facebook page.
