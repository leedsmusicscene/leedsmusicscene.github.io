Local young guns Kill The Silence set for sell-out show

This article was published in 2016.

Kill The Silence, a Post-Hardcore band from Leeds, West Yorkshire, have announced a special Christmas headline show at Santiago Bar in Leeds City Centre, following a monstrous return at the 360 Club this month, complete with new music, and new faces.

Consisting of long-time members: Jason Walsh (Lead Vocals, Rhythm Guitar) and Sam Rickerby (Lead Guitar). Kill The Silence are joined by two promising new faces in the form of Joe Del-Grande (Drums) and Chris Beal (Bass). After an overwhelmingly successful return performance on Friday 25th November, the band announced the Christmas special the day afterwards and witnessed a flood of fans, new and old, rushing to online stores and guaranteeing their attendance.

A statement from the band, outlines that they realise, around the Christmas period, a lot of people cant afford tickets to see their favourite bands. "In a time where we are rushing around buying gifts for our loved ones, a lot of people don't have time or money to go out and have fun in the run up to Christmas. We decided to give the fans a gift of our own in the form of this show, the tickets are cheap, its local and we just wanted to give people a break, for them to be able to let their hair down for one night in an otherwise stressful few weeks" explained frontman Jason Walsh.

The band will be joined by support acts; They Say Fall, Accident/Happy and Curses & Cures on Tuesday 20th December for an intimate show at capacity of 100, leaving the tickets in high demand. With a good portion of tickets already allocated, it will not be long until Kill The Silence's triumphant return will fly the 'Sold Out' banner.
