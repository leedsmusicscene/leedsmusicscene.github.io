Jack Afro to release a new 5-track EP

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds band Jack Afro has announced the completion of their new 5-track EP which will be released from Monday, February 11th. The tracklisting includes the songs ‘Regular Guy’, ‘Time Wasting’, ‘Circles’, ‘Arguing Is Like Riding A Bike (You Never Forget)’ and ‘Self Pity For The Young’.

The band has also been chosen by The British Council to represent The Visit Britain Rocks Campaign 2008. The Visit Britain Rocks Campaign will feature a compilation CD that will be distributed around every major University in America.
