The Pigeon Detectives to launch album at Leeds in-stores

This article was published in 2007.

The Pigeon Detectives will be playing an exclusive "Wait For Me" album launch in store at HMV Leeds on Tuesday 29th May at 6pm, one day after its release on the Leeds-based Dance To The Radio record label. They will also stay in store to sign copies for fans afterwards.

Free wristbands for entry can be picked up from the store from 9am on the day, limited to one pair per customer and there is obviously a limited amount available!

Crash Records on Headrow will be opening at midnight on Sunday 27th for anyone eager to get their hands on the very first copies of album - some of which will be signed!

The single "I'm Not Sorry' is out now on digital download, and hits shops on Monday 21st May on CD and 7" vinyl.

The Pigeon Detectives play Leeds Town Hall as part of the Live At Leeds festivities. Saturday is now sold out, however tickets are still available for Sunday, 27th May - check See Tickets or Leeds Gig Tickets to buy a pair.
