The Sunshine Underground to play at Bingley Music Live 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Organisers of the Bingley Music Live festival have today announced that Leeds favourites The Sunshine Underground will be playing live at the event, in what is billed as their only Yorkshire festival appearance of the summer.

Bingley Music Live takes place over the weekend of 2nd-4th September 2011.
