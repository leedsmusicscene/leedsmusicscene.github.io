I Like Trains reveal Mnemosyne Video

This article was published in 2012.

I Like Trains have revealed the video and artwork for Mnemosyne, the first single taken from critically acclaimed third album, The Shallows.

Mnemosyne is part of a trilogy of videos reflecting how technology has taken over peoples lives, showing the emergence, the boom and predicting the bust of modern technologies. In this video we see an 80s child discovering technology, now in our everyday lives.
