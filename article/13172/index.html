Rolla The Dice

This is an archive of the band profile for Rolla The Dice.

Formed in 2006, Rolla The Dice (formally known as Epic) have clocked up a number of gigs over the years and have stunned onlookers with their impressive "amps-up-to-11 no holds barred" live shows.

Originating from the small market town of Bedale (the gateway to the Yorkshire dales) but now residing in and around Leeds & York, Donna Petrie (vocals), Ben Fessey (guitar), Marcus Clarke (bass) all met at primary school and formed through a mutual love of AC/DC, Rage Against The Machine and Led Zeppelin. John Bonham inspired John Maughan (ex-Whitefire) joined the band on drums in late 2009 to complete the current line-up.

Rolla The Dice went into a shed on a farm in the middle of no-where to write new material and record in Leeds at The Soundmill in the early months of 2010.

With an EP with 3 new songs ready for release Rolla The Dice hope to bring their raw power to a town near you very soon..
