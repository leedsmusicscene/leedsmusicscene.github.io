Festival Republic Stage line up at Leeds Festival 2011 is revealed

This article was published in 2011.

The line up for the Festival Republic Stage at Leeds 2011 was announced live on air at 7pm this evening on the Zane Lowe show on BBC Radio One.

The headliners for the Festival Republic Stage were announced in March and are Peter Doherty (Friday), The Horrors (Saturday) and The Midnight Beast (Sunday).

Bands announced this evening included Rival Schools, Digitalism, Brother, Tom Vek, So I Watched You From Afar, The Twilight Singers and many more. The full line up is now:
