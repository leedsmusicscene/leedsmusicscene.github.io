Pete Briley

This is an archive of the band profile for Pete Briley.

pete briley was raised for the first eighteen years of life in maidstone, kent. midst the angry young people in cheap sports wear and the middle class suits that walk around the small, insignificant town during the day, he sought to find a sound.
as the first eighteen years drew to a close, pete decided to re-locate to the north of the country and live in the fair city of leeds, west yorkshire. between the four walls and ceiling, pete began to write music as he had in that far away town at the bottom of the country.

at the dawn of pete's fourth year living in the north, he continues to explore expressive possibilities within music as well as keeping an eye on the space between the dark, dingy and dirty and the centred, clean and clear.

peace and love.
