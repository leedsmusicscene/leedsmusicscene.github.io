Embrace to release "Ashes" on November 15th...

This article was published in 2004.

Embrace follow up their top 10 single "Gravity" with a new single scheduled for release on November 15th.

"Ashes", the lead track from their chart topping album "Out Of Nothing", is hailed by critics as one of the highlights from the album. Driving and anthemic with a soaring chorus, it's an exhilarating start to the album and a standout track in the band's live set.

In other news, Embrace continue their series of guerrilla gigs, having recently jetted competition winners for a one off gig in Majorca. Look out for a late October appearance; "We're going to get a whole lot more ambitious on the next one", said Danny. Although what can be more ambitious is yet to be seen!

Embrace will also be making a live appearance on Tim Lovejoy & The Allstars (Sky One) on Wednesday from 10pm.
