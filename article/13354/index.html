Leeds Festival 2011 tickets go on sale today

This article was published in 2010.

Did you enjoy Leeds 2010?

You may only have just taken your boots off from arriving home after an enjoyable Leeds Festival 2010 watching Axl Rose, Arcade Fire and Blink 182, yet festival organisers have revealed that a small number of Weekend and Early Entry tickets for the event in 2011 will be going on sale this morning at 9am.

The tickets for Leeds 2011 will be on sale from Tuesday 31st August at 9am and the prices for these advance tickets are being held at 2010 levels (Â£180 plus fees).

You can buy your Leeds 2011 tickets from See Tickets or Ticketmaster.

Tickets will be on sale for a limited time. Last year they were taken off sale after a few weeks, so be quick if you want your ticket for Leeds Festival 2011.
