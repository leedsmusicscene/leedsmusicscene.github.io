Hoover Dams to release a new single 'Go!' in January

This article was published in 2009.

Hoover Dams will release their new single, titled 'Go!', on Monday 11th January 2010 through the Belle Records label.

The CD comes backed with a DubClub 93 Mix of 'Go!' along with an alternative version of 'Blood On The Collar', and the video for the single is now available to watch below:



You can pre-order the single now through the band's official website. Alternatively, the single is available through iTunes from March 2010.

Hoover Dams will appear live at the Primrose in Leeds on Friday, 8th January 2010.
