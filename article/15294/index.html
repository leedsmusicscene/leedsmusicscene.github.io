The Gonzo's announce the release of their debut album

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds band The Gonzo's have announced the release of their debut album, 'Welcome To Our World', which will be available at the album launch at The Cockpit in Leeds on Friday 16th September.

The album is 10 tracks of fast paced pop rock from the young four-piece that came together last year in a music lesson at Boston Spa School. The band recently won the first Leeds AMP Awards at the O2 Academy.

The album will be available to download via Mind Jack Records from all the usual online sources or direct from the band at any of their gigs.
