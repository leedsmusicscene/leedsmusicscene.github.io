The Lost 45s UK to release their debut album in January 2006...

This article was published in 2005.

Leeds combo, The Lost 45s UK, are about to hit the UK with their debut album, "What Time Do You Call This?", on Biff Bang Pow Records (Cat No. BBPCD013).

Scheduled for release in January 2006 the album contains all of their live favourites such as "Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday", "Billy", and "Russian Roulette" as well as their previous singles, "Ivy" and "The Man That Time Forgot".

The Lost 45s UK will also feature on the third installment of Biff Bang Pow's Compilation CD "SHIMMY" out in January/February '06.
