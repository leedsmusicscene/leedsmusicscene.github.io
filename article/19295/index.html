Vitamin present 'Brothers and Sisters'

This article was published in 2015.

'Brothers and Sisters' is a night of future-pop and good times hosted by Leeds based Vitamin and offers an open door to people looking for a unique atmosphere with exciting bands and DJs. It is aimed at bringing together a new kinship between music lovers, party goers and artists in Leeds.

The residency will be held on the second Tuesday of the month in the Brudenell Games Room, featuring line ups hand-picked by Vitamin, with some of Leeds most promising and best new bands, guest DJs, cheap drinks, regular giveaways and free sweets.

Vitamin formed whilst at college together, dropping out to focus on the band full-time when they signed to National Anthem Records, who are responsible for launching the careers of Haim and Chvrches.

The band's first EP 'Giving it Up' has been featured by NME, Q Magazine, played on XFM and Radio One by Huw Stephens, resulting in the band appearing twice at Leeds Festival.

Tickets for 'Brothers and Sisters' are on sale now, or free with the band's EP vinyl when purchased at Jumbo Records.

'Brothers and Sisters' Residency Dates:

13th October: Vitamin + Marsicans + Pik
10th November: Vitamin + Polo + Krrum
8th December: Vitamin + Actor + Snowfox
