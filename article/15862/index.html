Round Window release Debut EP "Pedestals"

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds based noiseniks Round Window release their debut EP "Pedestals" ahead of their debut gig at the Fox & Newt, Leeds on 16/12/11 with their good friends The Asa Hawks.

To mark this milestone event, the band have decided to launch the EP as a free, yes FREE download for a limited period of 1 week in the run up to the gig. This offer is available on the band's Soundcloud page as well as on MySpace and Reverbnation (via Facebook)

The EP itself shows the band plying their trademark big riffs, catchy melodies, sweet harmonies and tight rythmns and leaves you feeling like you've been hit with a sledgehammer and tickled with a feather at the same time!

A limited supply of CD's will be available at the gig on the 16th, again as a freebie, after all it is the season of goodwill!

Track listing:

Pedestals
Floating Boy
Lucky You
Through the Backdoor
