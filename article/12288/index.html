The Horn The Hunt

This is an archive of the band profile for The Horn The Hunt.

The Horn The Hunt is Clare Carter and Joseph Osborne. They are a two piece, pop band. They began writing and recording songs in a flat in Sandnes, Norway in early 2008. Then they made some more in a room carved into the side of a canyon in the middle of Spain, and then some more in an old school in Dordrecht, Holland. The result of this work was an album, which was completed in a bedroom studio in Leeds, England, where they are now based. The album was released independently in August of 2009. Since the release of the album, they have been playing this music live, featuring electronic and live instruments, costumes and visuals..
