Kaiser Chiefs launch new video for 'Meanwhile Up In Heaven'

This article was published in 2014.

Kaiser Chiefs have launched the video for their new single, 'Meanwhile Up In Heaven', which is the second to be taken from their album 'Education, Education, Education & War'.

The latest single follows from the album's lead single 'Coming Home' and you can watch it below.



Kaiser Chiefs will play live in Leeds at the First Direct Arena in February 2015 for what will be one of only two arena shows next year. Bassist Simon Rix said, "It's a great time for the Leeds music scene, there's a lot going on right now. We're so happy to be able to play the new Arena again, nothing can match the support we get from the Yorkshire crowds".
