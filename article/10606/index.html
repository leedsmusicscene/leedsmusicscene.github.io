Worriedaboutsatan to celebrate their new album 'Arrivals' with a double header event

This article was published in 2009.

Worriedaboutsatan release their new album ‘Arrivals’ on May 25th via the Gizeh label, and to celebrate the label and band are putting on a special double-header show in Leeds.

It starts at the Packhorse where Worriedaboutsatan will play a live set with full visuals. Support comes from Fuzzy Lights and Lupercal. The band will then appear at Dare Cafe in Headingly where they will play a part live / part DJ set at 1am.

The event is being put together in conjunction with Krucht and you can buy single tickets or a double ticket which gets you into to both shows for a reduced rate. The Packhorse gig costs £5 and starts at 8pm, will the Dare Cafe show costs £4 and opens up at 11pm. A double ticket gaining access to both shows will cost £7. Tickets are available from www.gizehrecords.bigcartel.com.
