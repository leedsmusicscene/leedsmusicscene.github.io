Royal Park Rent Party

This is an archive of the band profile for Royal Park Rent Party.

The Royal Park Rent Party are a young swing band based in Leeds. Reminiscent of Louis Armstrong's groups of the 30s , the band have been making an impact on the Leeds music scene from the word go. The band are led by jazz violinist Matt Holborn and piano man James Browne, playing a broad mix of Swing, New Orleans Jazz and Blues with a fresh new approach. This band is guaranteed to have the whole room swinging and shouting for more.

Sean Hollis-trumpet
Matt Holborn-violin
Rose Perry-tenor sax
James Browne-piano
Tom Wheatley-Bass
Matt Davies-drums
Nik Rutherford/Ben Danzig-guitar.
