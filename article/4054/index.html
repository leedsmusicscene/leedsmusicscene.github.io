Electric Street Police - Untitled

This is a review of "Untitled" recorded by Electric Street Police. The review was written by Simon Glacken in 2005.

For those eagerly anticipating the next Boards of Canada album here’s a little something to fill the void. The sort of well-crafted ambiance that isn’t from this world and was most likely created by one of those inter-dimensional lizard people; well that’s what David Icke might claim anyway.

“Placebo” is the only track that might tempt you onto the dance floor with the sort of beat that wouldn’t sound out of place on Daft Punk’s album “Homework”. The rest of the EP is an out of this world, electro, sample filled adventure that can only be truly enjoyed with the lights turned down low and your mind switched to the open setting. After a couple of listens you’ll have the sudden urge to purchase a couple of keyboards and start making your own superb futuristic soundtracks.
