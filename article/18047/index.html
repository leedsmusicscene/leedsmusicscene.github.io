The Stolen Mondeos

This is an archive of the band profile for The Stolen Mondeos.

STEVEN LIGHTFOOT........vocals
RYAN PALFREEMAN.........rhythm guitar
SCOTT MAHONEY...........lead guitar
ADAM HAWKE.................drums
JOE YOUNGMAN..........bass

After spending a year building a following packing out local pubs and clubs TSM were asked to headline the Rodley Beer Festival playing to and blowing away 400+ people

The success of this gig then spawned several bookings around Yorkshire and then more importantly signing a contract to become the resident band at The Stone Roses Bar, Leeds city centre..
