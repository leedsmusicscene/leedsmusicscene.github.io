Leeds bands to feature on 'White Noise 2' charity compilation

This article was published in 2014.

In 2010, Leeds bands came together for charity to release the 'White Noise' compilation CD.

This year, they're back ... and it's bigger and better.

Released through the Leeds Mayhem label, 'White Noise 2' will be available from 1st July 2014 in Crash Records and Jumbo Records in Leeds, and in Wha Wha Records in Wakefield. It will also be possible to purchase the CD via the Leeds Mayhem website.
