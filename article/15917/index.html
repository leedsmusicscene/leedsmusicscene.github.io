McCarthy Vigil are five young cats in heat, playing intense jams. Based in South Yorkshire, they live, breathe, eat and fuck music. Acoustic guitar never sounded so dangerous with its gang of hard-arse electric sycophants and gut-busting drums. They put their finger on the button, and eat the button. They turn it up. Fuck 11, they go all the way up to 27. And then die..
