More acts announced for Bingley Music Live 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Having recently confirmed the first four acts for Bingley Music Live 2011, Bradford Council have announced the addition of Chase and Status and Wretch 32 to the line up.

They will join Fun Lovin' Criminals, Maximo Park, Feeder and Eliza Doolittle a the festival, which takes place between the 2nd and 4th September 2011 in Myrtle Park, Bingley.
