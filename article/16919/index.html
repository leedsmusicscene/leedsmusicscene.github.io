Leeds Festival 2013 tickets on sale

This article was published in 2012.

With the sound of event closers Kasabian still reverberating around Bramham Park, and with the clothes still muddy, organisers Festival Republic have announced that pre-sale tickets for Leeds 2013 will be on sale from 9am on Thursday 30th August.

The presale will feature a limited number of Leeds 2013 weekend tickets available for a short time held at 2012 prices, meaning fans can begin the countdown to another amazing event, safe in the knowledge their place is secured.

Early Entry tickets will also be available. These are often in short supply so it would be a good idea to buy yours now while the opportunity exists.

You should only buy from the official ticket agencies Ticketmaster and See Tickets.

As the Official Communications Partner of Reading & Leeds, Vodafone is offering VIP customers the chance to buy presale tickets for Reading & Leeds 2013 up to 48 hours before they go on general sale. Obtain a free Vodafone sim and then register at Vodafone VIP for your chance to buy tickets from 9am on Tuesday 28th August.

All pre-sale weekend tickets are £197.50 plus booking fees. Early Entry passes are priced at £15 and will provide access to the campsite on Wednesday 21st August 2013.
