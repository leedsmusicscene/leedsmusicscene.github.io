Post War Glamour Girls

This is an archive of the band profile for Post War Glamour Girls.

Post War Glamour Girls are four screaming charmers formed in late 2010. Heavy hitting and dry witted, they create songs which swell up and boil over into brooding, thumping masterpieces reminiscent of Tom Waits, Grinderman and 'Moon and Antarctica-era' Modest Mouse.

Wearing their hearts firmly on their sleeves, PWGG live shows are a gripping affair. The sets are laced with reckless abundance as songs tinker into a clatter and clatter into a roar before collapsing in a proud heap on top of the audience.

With their first single 'Ode To Harry Dean' due to drop after summer, Post War Glamour Girls are picking up pace fast and are most certainly one to watch..
