Laboratory Noise release their debut album on 21st June

This article was published in 2010.

Laboratory Noise release their debut album, 'When Sound Generates Light', on Monday 21st June 2010 through their own Recurring Accident label.

Taking influences from psychedelia, shoegaze, electronica and post-rock, they create multi-layered effect driven soundscapes, underpinned by strong melodies.

The 13 track album was recorded & mixed at their own studio in Bradford over a period of two years...and mastered by Tom Morris (Her Name is Calla).

A free download only single, 'She Dies Screaming', will be available on Bandcamp and LMS from 19th June.
