Volcanoes dwell in the musical ether between Leeds and Sheffield, wiggling their toes into both scenes and sprinkling in some fairy dust of their own. With no particular style or apparent purpose, the band explores the musical cosmos freely, combining both music and words to form what can only be described as.....songs.

Volcanoes have been dormant for 3 years
