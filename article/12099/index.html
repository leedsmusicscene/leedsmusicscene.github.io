Ashley Hicklin signs record deal with Ferryhouse / Warner Music

This article was published in 2010.

Ashley Hicklin has signed a three-album record deal with Ferryhouse / Warner music.

The singer-songwriter, originally from Leeds via Scarborough, is due to release his debut record in the summer of 2010 with tour dates to be announced.
