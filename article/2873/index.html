And Then The Amber Slips

This is an archive of the band profile for And Then The Amber Slips.

And Then The Amber Slips are a political progressive rock band, fusing melody, noise, and gliding seductive vocals. The band are rapidly becoming known for their edgy, heavy live shows and outspoken views. They recorded two songs very early on which were produced by them (Black Star and Fuga). A new EP has since been recorded..
