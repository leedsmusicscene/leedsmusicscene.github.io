Slam Dunk festival announce first bands for 2013

This article was published in 2012.

Slam Dunk 2013 will be headlined by All Time Low organisers revealed earlier today.

The festival, now in its 8th year, will expand into a full 3-day touring event with a new site in Wolverhampton to go with the current sites in Leeds and Hatfield. All sites will have the same line up across all 7 stages.

The Leeds date, known as "Slam Dunk Festival North", will take place on Saturday 25th May at Leeds University.

Headliners of Slam Dunk 2013 are All Time Low. Other bands announced today were Pierce The Veil, Streetlight Manifesto, Woe Is Me, The Early November, The Wonder Years, Fireworks, Four Year Strong, The Story So Far, Senses Fail, The Skints and Polar Bear Club. Many more bands will be announced over the coming weeks.

Slam Dunk Festival tickets go on sale on Friday 7th December at 10am via Leeds Gig Tickets. All shows are 14+ and after shows are 18+.
