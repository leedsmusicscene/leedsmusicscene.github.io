The Witch Hunt giveaway track from debut EP

This article was published in 2014.

Leeds based duo The Witch Hunt are streaming a new track from their debut EP 'Little Book of Hate'. It's called 'Wide and Laughing' and you can listen to it below:



You can also grab it for free from the band's Facebook page.

The debut EP is available now through Dead Young Records, and is a collection of 5 songs showcasing an incredibly promising range and depth, with dark, twisted melodies and haunting vocals. The tracklisting for the EP is:

1. Army Man
2. One Big Bite
3. Wide and Laughing
4. Can You Believe it?
5. Little Book of Hate
