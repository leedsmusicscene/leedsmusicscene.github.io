Stan SB

This is an archive of the band profile for Stan SB.

Stan SB (Stanley Stanley Stevenson Byrne) is a young Pop/Drum and Bass/Dubstep/Breakbeat singer songwriter with a completely new look and what can be explored through the dance music medium. with over half a million views across Youtube and multi national fanbase this music is ready to be experienced live.

Equipped with an unstoppable drummer (Stephen Heselton) a god-like guitarist (Timothy Reid, described by Metal Hammer magazine as "second to no f**ker") and an arsenal of beautiful dirty synths, the army of three is ready to assault a venue near you.

With huge drums, brutal bass sounds, epic synth-lines and infectious vocals hooks this is not something you want to miss out on..
