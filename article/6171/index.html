Evil Eye

This is an archive of the band profile for Evil Eye.

We're called Evil Eye, we play heavy rock influenced by Motorhead, Fu Manchu, CKY and Kyuss. Evil Eye have been together for 2 years and have been bothering the Huddersfield music scene for a while. A lack of heavy rock bands in the area forced the band to seek a fanbase elsewhere. Upon hearing that Leeds plays host to heavier bands, Evil Eye decided to make an attempt at their music scene instead..
