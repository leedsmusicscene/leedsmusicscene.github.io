Interruptor Jones

This is an archive of the band profile for Interruptor Jones.

Interruptor Jones used to be a duo. But Bruce was too scared to play in front of people and promptly quit the band after one unremarkable gig at a birthday party held in a Working Men's Club. So the one remaining member, Antony, took it upon himself to carry on on his own. Specialising in acoustic whining of all styles and covering such already covered topics as heartbreak, death, regret and not wanting to grow up. He has been compared to such acoustic luminaries as Bright Eyes and Dashboard Confessional and his music was recently described by the Selby Times as "heartfelt melodic material"... but they say that about everyone these days. .
