Live At Leeds 2014 reveal full Saturday schedule

This article was published in 2014.

Live At Leeds 2014 organisers have today revealed the schedule for this coming May's highly anticipated event.

Over 20 official venues will welcome 15,000 people to Leeds over the weekend to watch over 200 artists and bands, covering a mixture of genres. This year features acts as diverse as Albert Hammond Jr, Chart Toppers Clean Bandit, BBC Sound nominee George Ezra and The Voice's Beth McCarthy who complement emerging and underground talent such as Wolf Alice, Youth Man and Saint Raymond.

Add the special show celebrating Dance To The Radio at Leeds Town Hall with a one-off show from ¡Forward, Russia! and I Like Trains performing a 10 year Anniversary concert, 2014 is set to be the biggest to date and one of the strongest line-ups yet.

New venues for 2014 include Leeds Town Hall, Leeds College of Music, HiFi Club, Oporto and Belgrave Music Hall.

And this year Live At Leeds have another media partner in the NME, who will also host a stage at The Cockpit.

The full Saturday schedule is now live on the official website.

The main event for Live At Leeds 2014 will take place on Saturday 3rd May.

Tickets are still available for just £25 via Yorkshire Gig Guide.

Tickets can be exchanged for a wristband on the day - this year the wristband exchange will be hosted by the First Direct Arena.
