Heads We Dance to appear live on Sky TV

This article was published in 2008.

Heads We Dance will appear live on Channel M's CCS show on Tuesday 12th February at 10pm. The band will play three songs and have a short interview. You can watch the show on Sky (channel 203) and Virgin Media (channel 878).

The band play at the Bad Sneakers event at the Faversham on Saturday 16th February.
