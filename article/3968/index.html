The Cribs release the album "The New Fellas" on 20th June...

This article was published in 2005.

The Cribs new album "The New Fellas" is released on Monday 20th June and formats include a special edition with a bonus 12-track live album recorded at the band's show in Leeds last month. The single "Mirror Kissers", taken from the album, is out now.

You can pre-order the special edition album from HMV and from Amazon.

The band play a few in-store shows before a number of summer festival dates, including T In The Park in July.
