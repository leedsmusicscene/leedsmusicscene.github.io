Kaiser Chiefs to release their third album in October

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds band Kaiser Chiefs have this week revealed that their third studio album will be 'Off With Their Heads'. The album will be released worldwide on Monday 13th October 2008.

The 11-track album was produced by Mark Ronson and Eliot James, and mixed by Andy Wallace (with a few tracks mixed by Cenzo Townshend).

In other news, Kaiser Chiefs have been shortlisted for Best Live Act in the Vodafone Live Music Awards for the third year running.
