Being A Tiger release 'X Predates Y' on 21st November

This article was published in 2016.

We are a strange band. We disappear, go underground, kick about talking to each other about "doing something", get motivated, get angry, get it down and then spend ages getting it out there.

Finally the track "X Predates Y" is set for release. The track will be released via ShoveItUpYourCult Records (an exciting new record label home to Adore//Repel, Johny Gibson, Grown Apart, Saint Cole, Robert Sharp and others) with a B-Side song called "The Accident".

You can find it on spotify, iTunes and bandcamp on 21st November.

You can also find it on ShoveItUpYourCult Records' Youtube channel as it has a video. The band hosted some incredible talent and recorded doing the only thing we know how to do... playing live.
