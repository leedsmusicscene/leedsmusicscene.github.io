Calling all Leeds musicians, bands and filmmakers... 20/20/02Vision 2007 revealed.

This article was published in 2007.

20 Bands, 20 Filmmakers, 2 Weeks: details of the third annual 20/20/02Vision opportunity have this week been revealed and organisers of the 20/20/02Vision 2007 event are now looking for Leeds bands and filmmakers to take part in the exciting film and music festival, which educates and celebrates the raw talent within the UK film and music sectors.

If you are a Leeds music producer, band or solo artist in any field of music looking to expose your work through film, then make sure you apply now.

The central element of 20/20/02Vision is a music video competition in which 20 filmmakers and 20 musicians are faced with the challenge to work together to produce 20 music videos in the short space of 2 weeks, under the supervision and mentoring from industry professionals.

20/20/02Vision offers all the artists involved the opportunity to showcase their work at events in and around Leeds in venues such as The Faversham, Corn Exchange and VUE Cinema. Some of the prizes on offer are: screening your work on the main stage at Leeds Carling Festival with VIP weekend passes; performing opportunities at the Moor Festival; and recording studio time.

Delivered in partnership with Mean Fiddler, Leeds Metropolitan University and Channel 4 Ideas Factory, and supported by Screen Yorkshire, BBC Big Screen, Host Media Centre, The Faversham, and Pavillion, 20/20/02Vision is now accepting applications.

Visit www.20202.co.uk or email sophia@20202.co.uk to receive more information or to request an application form.
