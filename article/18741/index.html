Marmozets release debut album and announce new single

This article was published in 2014.

Marmozets have this week released their debut album, 'The Weird and Wonderful Marmozets', through Roadrunner Records.

The album was produced by Larry Hibbitt (Hundred Reasons) and emphasises their growing skill in developing a broad range of styles, including the punk rock assault of 'Cover Up', and 'Born Young And Free', the brooding dynamic contrasts which feature on 'Captivate You', the visceral aggression of 'Why Do You Hate Me?' and the soaring progressive epic 'Cry'. In essence the album captures the riotous energy of their live show with complex melodies, compelling vocals and massive riffs.

The next single to be taken from the album will be 'Born Young and Free' which is available from October 6th.

Marmozets recently hit Radio 1 Rocks alongside Lower Than Atlantis, Mallory Knox and Lonely The Brave, as well as playing festival dates at Download, Leeds, Reading, and Glastonbury, along with the legendary Vans Warped Tour.
