Tug Ether

This is an archive of the band profile for Tug Ether.

Thank you for selecting tug Ether; we are a four-piece alternative rock band from Leeds, UK. We are influenced from a wide selection of music with each band member into completely different styles of music. From indie to rock to metal to funk. This equation doesn't always work but we have managed to blend our styles and tastes together to form a completely original sound, from chunky guitar riffs to soft melodic beats, funky bass lines with plenty of slap and an extraordinary vocal range we boast a raw, crazy and unique sound to which - we think works in perfect harmony, delivering exciting results..
