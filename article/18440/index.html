White Miles announce Leeds date supporting Courtney Love

This article was published in 2014.

White Miles, Fuzzy blues, stoner rock duo have also been announced as the main support to Courtney Love when she tours the UK, including Leeds on 16th May at 02 Academy.

The duo, Medina (guitar/vocals) and Lofi (drums/vocals) met in 2005 and decided to crash the music scene back in 2011. After releasing 4 live-videos, they quit their day jobs to live their dream. Infamous for their crazy rehearsal room parties, people say they live in their own world, but the truth is they make the world their own.

To coincide with the tour, they will be release their debut album "job: genius, diagnose: madness" on 12th May, which can only be described as a cruise through the deep and dark Atlantic ocean, mixed with soul-saving flights across the universe.
