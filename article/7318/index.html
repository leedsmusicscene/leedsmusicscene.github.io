Shut Your Eyes And You'll Burst Into Flames to release "Drop The Decade" on the DTTR label

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds quintet Shut Your Eyes And You’ll Burst Into Flames have revealed the details of their next single.

Scheduled for release on the Dance To The Radio record label, “Drop The Decade” will hit the shops on Monday, 16th October.

The single comes backed with “I Will Meditate And Then I Will Destroy You“ and will be available on limited edition 7" and digital download formats.

Shut Your Eyes And You’ll Burst Into Flames support The Sunshine Underground at York‘s Barfly venue on October 7th.
