Shattered Remains

This is an archive of the band profile for Shattered Remains.

Shattered Remains is a five piece metal band from the heart of West Yorkshire. We have a selection of original material and a few covers. Our style is a unique variety and covers a range of different genres... whatever we are, we like it!

Guitar - Joe Healy
Drums, Backing Vocals - Jordan Mcnamara
Vocals - Jordan Flynn
Guitar, Backing vocals - Rob Hemingway
Bass - Brano Rhodes.
