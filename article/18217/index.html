Vitasound Projects, The

This is an archive of the band profile for Vitasound Projects, The.

Ade Mincher - Guitars, Bass, Programming, Video editing.
Simon Blakelock - Drums, Video Editing
Adam Calvert - Guitars
Tom Allen - Guitars
Jamie Silver - Bass

In 2006 in-between playing and recording People in Jars, White Boys and Year of the Man the guitarist Ade Mincher started writing an album using 'An American Werewolf in London" as inspiration.

Originally influenced by Jeff Waynes War of the Worlds, a 45min syncopated concept album has been written over 6 years alongside an edited version of 'An American Werewolf in London'. Stupid experiment and challenging. The next part of this story is preparing to play the show live. Keep your eyes open for gigs 2015/6.
