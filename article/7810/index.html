Sidecar

This is an archive of the band profile for Sidecar.

Sidecar are a five piece rock 'n' roll band from Leeds.

The band is built on a collection of old friendships and the overwhelming desire to rock. With crunching guitars, spiky riffs, funky bass lines, soaring vocals and a drummer who likes to bash the sh*t out of his kit on a regular basis, Sidecar just wanna have a rocking good time....
