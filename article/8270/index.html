Kaiser Chiefs to release "Everything Is Average Nowadays" on May 21st

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds five-piece Kaiser Chiefs have announced the release of their next single, and the second to be taken from their chart-topping 2007 album “Yours Truly, Angry Mob”.

"Everything Is Average Nowadays" will be released on Monday, 21st May, with B-sides varying depending upon format. The 7” single will come with “I Like To Fight", and the CD copy will be backed with "Out Of My Depth". Both B-sides were recorded with Stephen Street at Hook End Manor in the same sessions as the album.

Meanwhile, tickets remain for the band's headline appearance at the O2 Wireless Festival in Leeds (Harewood House) on Saturday 16th June. Buy your ticket now from See Tickets.
