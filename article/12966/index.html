Knee Deep in the Dead

This is an archive of the band profile for Knee Deep in the Dead.

We are Knee Deep in the Dead, from Leeds. Formed in May 2009, we've played all round the UK and have shared stages with some awesome bands including While She Sleeps, Viatrophy, Chapters, Surfaces, Heart of a Coward and Maths. We've just released our self titled 5 track EP, which is available from us at shows and on this page for free download.

We're currently booking shows all round the UK and a UK tour in September..
