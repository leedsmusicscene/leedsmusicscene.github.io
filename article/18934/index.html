Full StrangeForms line-up announced

This article was published in 2015.

Bad Owl Presents... have announced the full line-up for this year's StrangeForms Festival. For fans of those who have an interest in anything post-rock, post-metal, math-rock and leftfield, the event will be back at Wharf Chambers over the Easter Bank Holiday (Saturday 4th and Sunday 5th of April) and will feature sixteen bands over the two days along with veggie/vegan food stalls, a record stall from Sound It Out Records and Echoes & Dust DJs.
