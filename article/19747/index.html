Maximo Park announced for special Live At Leeds 2017 closing show

This article was published in 2017.

Live at Leeds will be closing the festival this year at Leeds Academy with a special bill headlined by Maximo Park on Sunday 30th April 2017.

Included on the bill are other indie favourites Little Comets and Spector, as well as emerging indie-pop band High Tyde (recently toured with The Hunna) and Leeds guitar band The Indigo Project.

Organisers are offering a special reduced combination ticket for the main festival and the finale. You will need to bring your Live at Leeds wristband to the finale to gain entry on the reduced price.

Earlier this month, organisers announced 70 bands for Live At Leeds 2017 including Rag'N'Bone Man, Nothing But Thieves, DMA's, Gabrielle Aplin, The Hunna and Temples.

Live At Leeds 2017 tickets are on sale now via Leeds Gig Tickets priced at £32.50. (combo tickets are priced at £42.50).
