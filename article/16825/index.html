IT'S ABOUT THE SONG...

It's been over 15 years since Jean-Robin Farthing and Gareth Clegg first stepped into a studio together. Many projects and bands have revolved around the power core of musicianship and songwriting talent of this partnership. From Rock-Blues-Jazz Fusion to Alternative, Grunge, Metal and Hip Hop - these guys have done it all.

Always creative and never separable, they decided to embark on an acoustic project mid 2011 in which the core became the whole and Theory Of Form was born.

Currently in the process of unleashing an arsenal of well crafted, thought provoking songs Theory of Form will take you on a journey that knows no boundries..
