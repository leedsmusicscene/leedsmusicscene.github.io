NARCS album 'Two Birds, One Stone Later' is available now

This article was published in 2013.

NARCS debut album 'Two Birds, One Stone Later' will be released on CD on September 2nd. You can pre-order it now here: cluerecords.bandcamp.com.

If you pre-order the album you get a full immediate mp3 download and you will also get the CD before anybody else. The first 20 copies of the album will be accompanied by a lyric sheet, created by the band.

To celebrate the launch of the album the band are playing at The Packhorse on August 31st with Allusondrugs and Insatellites in support. The place is going to be fully kitted out to look like Willy Wonka's recurring nightmare and every attendee will receive a NARCSY bag which will contain several weird & wonderful delights.
