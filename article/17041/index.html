Counts of the Netherworld

This is an archive of the band profile for Counts of the Netherworld.

"Inspired Psychedelic Oddness" - Tom Robinson, BBC 6 Music

Formed early 2008 in Bradford, UK. Counts of the Netherworld write songs about love, sex and death... space, beauty and bananas... heartache, cynicism and the daily grind. A lo-fi tour-de-force of strange alternative jingles and out-there space-rock.
