iLiKETRAiNS to release their debut single on the Beggars Banquet record label

This article was published in 2007.

iLiKETRAiNS have today announced that their debut single on the Beggars Banquet label will be released on 26th March.

Available on limited edition 10” vinyl, CD and Digital Download formats ‘Spencer Perceval’ is the first single to be taken from the band’s debut album (set for a release later in the year) and is backed by ‘I am Murdered‘.

To coincide with the release, iLiKETRAiNS will play a special launch gig at the Cockpit on 28th March, with support from Troubles and AnteAter. Tickets are still available (buy from See Tickets).

In other news, iLiKETRAiNS have been added to the SXSW 2007 line-up, playing mid-March shows in Austin, Texas. To coincide with their visit the band's mini-album "Progress Reform" will be released in the USA on 23rd April, again through the Beggars Banquet label.
