Eclectic

This is an archive of the band profile for Eclectic.

Eclectic: Live, is a Soulful, genre bending band based in Leeds, the project is based on music written and produced by electronic producer Eclectic.

Eclectic is currently working on a third album and first full live album, as well as collaborating with artists Mahon and Mako.

The music and band have been creating a buzz on the Internet, as well as the North, and London..
