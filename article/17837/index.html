To Kill A King announce new single and Bastille tour support

This article was published in 2013.

To Kill A King have been announced as one of the main supports on Bastille's UK tour this October. The two bands previously toured together in March, where they often joined each other on stage.

The Bastille support tour will precede To Kill A King's biggest ever headline tour, which includes a show at Brudenell Social Club on October 22nd (buy tickets).

The captivating five-piece are set to re-release their acclaimed album 'Cannibals With Cutlery' as a deluxe edition with four extra tracks, on October 7th through their new label Xtra Mile Recordings. First single 'Rays' will also available as a digital download from October 7th.

To Kill A King are currently offering the sublime 'Bones' - the first track they ever recorded - as a free download.



As well as the October date in Leeds, To Kill A King will be appearing at Leeds Festival on Sunday 26th August.
