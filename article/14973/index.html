Six local bands to battle for a place at Bingley Music Live

This article was published in 2011.

Last month Bradford Theatres launched a search for local music talent to appear the Bingley Music Live festival in September.

Over 60 bands entered the competition, 18 bands were selected to take part in the heats, and they're now down to the final 6.

Those bands are:

Ti Amo
Tiny Giants
Kath and the Mighty Menace
Remember the Hurricanes
State Of Error
Kingheadlock

There are two places up for grabs on the line up in September, with the winning bands set to appear in front of a crowd of over 15,000 people.

The final is tonight, Friday 8th July, at St George's Hall in Bradford.
