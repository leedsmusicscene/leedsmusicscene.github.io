More top acts for Bingley Music Live 2014

This article was published in 2014.

Organisers of the Bingley Music Live festival have this week announced the addition of more acts to the line up for 2014.

The additions will join the previously announced Pet Shop Boys and Example.

Additions this week included:

JESS GLYNNE / NAUGHTY BOY / GORGON CITY / MNEK / THE SELECTER / BIPOLAR SUNSHINE / CHLOE HOWL / CATFISH & THE BOTTLEMEN / JOHN LENNON MCCULLAGH / THE SOUTH / THE WYTCHES / ELIZA & THE BEAR / JOEL COMPASS / NIGHT ENGINE / CHAMPS / AVEC SANS / DC / PRIDES / WE ARE CATCHERS / NATASHA NORTH / KYLA LA GRANGE / EMMA GARRETT / YOUNG KATO / THE RAMONA FLOWERS / YOUNG KATO / BIRD TO BEAST / RENEGADE BRASS BAND / THE SHERLOCKS.

Bingley Music Live takes place in Myrtle Park across the weekend of August 29th - 31st.
