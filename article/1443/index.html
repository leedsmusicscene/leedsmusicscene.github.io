Wrath Records announce Super Sevens singles club...

This article was published in 2003.

WRATH SUPER SEVENS - the aural equivalent of Reader's Digest, this singles club subscription will bring you a veritable smorgasbord of indie/rock/garage/punk mayhem in six highly collectable split 7-inch releases featuring the might of the Wrath roster plus some rather distinguished guests.

First up is a double-bill of split 7-inches, scheduled for release on 14th April 2003. One features lunatic frenchcore punkers les Flames! with Vive les Flames! back to back with Little Japanese Toy's frantic squelchfest Fingermouse. It's counterpart split 7" will feature the 'feelgood band of the year' Being 747 with the sinister Music Of The Clones being backed by Leeds' delightful debutantes sammyUSA and their sublime track Fine.

The rest of the year's releases will consist of exclusive tracks from Beachbuggy, Farming Incident, Galitza, Stuffy & The Fuses, The Scaramanga Six plus several more.

Each record will have a limited pressing of 400 and will have cover art based on a generic B&W disco bag. The labels on the single will be a combination of two colours and each release will have it's own single label colour scheme - the end result should be a highly collectable set of singles.

Subscription fee: £18 (consists of six 7-inch singles, P+P, special offer vouchers for other Wrath releases, plus an exclusive CD compilation at the end of the year).

All you have to do to subscribe is to send a cheque payable to 'Wrath Records' for £18 with your name, address & email to: Wrath Records, The Cardigan Centre, 145-149 Cardigan Road, Leeds, UK, LS6 1LJ. For more details visit www.wrathrecords.co.uk
