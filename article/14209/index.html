The Finnlys are all set to release their debut single in March

This article was published in 2011.

Following on from the release of two successful EPs, The Finnlys begin 2011 with the release of their debut single 'For Love Nor Money', a sweeping and ambitious song of reawakened love.

'For Love Nor Money' will be released through the band's Rudiment Recordings label on 14th March 2011 and will be available from bandcamp.com on a pay-what-you-want basis.

You can also buy the single from iTunes and other download sites.

To celebrate the launch of the single the band will be playing on 5th March in Morley, as part of a mini Northern tour.
