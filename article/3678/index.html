She's Not Dead to release their debut album...

This article was published in 2005.

With a CD launch at the HMV Showcase at Joseph's Well and a music video shoot shortly after, She's Not Dead are to release their debut album - "Together We Are Strangers" - on 1st April. The 10-track album which has taken over a year to make, will be available from HMV Leeds (City Centre) from 1st April, and other independent music stores from 4th April.
