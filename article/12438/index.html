The Lodger announce details of new single and album

This article was published in 2010.

The Lodger are set to release their third album "Flashbacks" on Monday 26th April 2010 on CD/DL through This is Fake DIY Records (UK) and Slumberland Records (USA). Slumberland will also release the album on 12" vinyl. The artwork was designed by recent NME award nominee Nick Scott (The Cribs).

This will be preceded by a DL-only single "Have A Little Faith In People" which will be available with two exclusive non-album B-side tracks "The Honest Song" and "The Best I Ever Had" on Monday 5th April.

The album was recorded and produced by Richard Formby (Wild Beasts) in Autumn last year and the band have been augmented in the studio by horn and string sections and backing vocalists. The full line-up will perform a one-off free show at Nation of Shopkeepers, Leeds on Thursday 22nd April with support from Just Handshakes (We're British).
