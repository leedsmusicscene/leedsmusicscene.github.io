Line up announced for British Wildlife Festival in March 2012

This article was published in 2011.

In the early hours of this morning, we announced via our advent calendar the first wave of artists playing the upcoming British Wildlife Festival.

The next British Wildlife Festival takes place across three days in March 2012, with the main event being held jointly at Brudenell Social Club and its neighbour Royal Park on Saturday 3rd March.

An initial batch of British Wildlife Weekend tickets are currently on sale for just £11 and can be bought via the Leeds Gig Tickets website.

These early bird tickets will only be available until the end of December.
