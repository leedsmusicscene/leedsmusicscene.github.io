Leeds Music Scene release online Leeds city music venue map

This article was published in 2010.

Here at Leeds Music Scene we've been busy plotting the location of Leeds music venues - which is no mean feat given we have 155 venues in our Leeds music venues section.

The result has been the production of a street map overlayed with Leeds live music venues along with the integration of all upcoming gigs from Leeds Gig Guide.

Try out our Leeds gig guide map here and let us know what you think.

If we're missing a venue please let us know. And if we're missing a gig from the listings, please add your gig here.
