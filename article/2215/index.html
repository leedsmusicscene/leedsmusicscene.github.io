The Echo

This is an archive of the band profile for The Echo.

Andrew Barton - Vocals, Guitars
Sam Woodings - Piano, Acoustic Guitar, Harmonica
Chris Challenger - Bass, Acoustic Guitar
John Wright - Drums, Vocals, Percussion
Andy Challenger - Guitars, Vocals

Since 2000 The Echo bubbled and finally burst all over themselves and their scene with Barton and Woodings in Sheffield. The two have collaborated and performed together with intelligent music influenced by the likes of John Renbourn, Pentangle and Rory McLeoud.

In 2003 the two joined forces with Chris Challenger and John Wright and The Echo was born in Leeds. However, was something missing?

It seems so, for Andy Challenger joined their crusade for original sounds to add a Latino under carriage and not just with his looks. You should see his... . moves.

The Echo are maturing together as a band and it shows in each performance.

Come and see for yourselves..
