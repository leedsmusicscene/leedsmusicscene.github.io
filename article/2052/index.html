The Dharma

This is an archive of the band profile for The Dharma.

The Dharma are an indie electro band from Leeds. UK. Influenced by bands ranging from The Beatles to Franz Ferdinand. Formed 6 years ago the band is comprised of Paul Houlihan (Guitar+Vocals), JB John Butler (Bass+Vocals), Jon Thornton (Drums+Vocals). A Leeds based band currently working as an un-signed act they are well known for their energetic and outstanding live performances.

Throwing everything at putting on a memorable show, the Dharma have a built a solid reputation for delivering impressively energetic live shows. Their sound can only be described as powerful, whilst retaining the key components to satisfy everyone's dancing needs. Following on the back of support tours with bands the like Cage The Elephant, The Sunshine Underground and The Maccabees they are wasting no time dropping their explosive new EP I Dance Like This out on the market..
