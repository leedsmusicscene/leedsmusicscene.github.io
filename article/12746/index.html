Glissando to release a double A-side single on June 7th 2010

This article was published in 2010.

Glissando have announced the release of a new double A-side single on June 7th via the Gizeh record label. The duo will release two brand new songs entitled 'The Long Lost' and 'Of Silence'.

The single is to be released as both a Digital Download and a limited edition handmade CDr.

Only 150 copies of the CDr will be available and pre-orders will go on sale on May 3rd through the Gizeh Records online store.

There will be 40 copies available via the store and all pre-orders will receive an instant download code to enable fans of the band to obtain the songs and artwork before the release date.

The other 110 will go via distribution to a few select outlets and Glissando will be taking a handful with them on the road as they play a handful of special UK shows in June, where they will be appearing as a duo for the first time in two years.

Before the duo tour hits the road they will be doing two shows at the end of May with their Fleeting Glimpse Ensemble. The band will open up for Johann Johannsson in Leeds on May 29th at the Howard Assembly Room (Leeds Grand Theatre).

The band return to Leeds on June 5th when they play at Left Bank as part of the Forest of Sound all-dayer.
