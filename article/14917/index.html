Transgressive Records to showcase at Leeds Festival 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Transgressive Records will once again curate part of the entertainment at Leeds Festival.

Presenting its five year anniversary party at Leeds Festival, Transgressive will take over the Alternative Stage on Saturday night with special guests Johnny Flynn, who arrives with his band The Sussex Wit, Dry The River, and Pete & The Pirates.

In other news, those with weekend camping tickets can experience a new collaboration from Future Cinema and Mercury & Brit Award nominees Guillemots, an event that promises an immersive fusion of cinema and sound while the band play live to very special secret film. This takes place on the Alternative Stage on Thursday evening.
