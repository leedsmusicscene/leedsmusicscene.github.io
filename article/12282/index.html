Corinne Bailey Rae to release 'Paris Nights / New York Mornings' on March 29th

This article was published in 2010.

This week it was announced that Good Groove / Virgin Records will release 'Paris Nights / New York Mornings' as a single on March 29th.

The track is taken from Corinne Bailey Rae's hugely acclaimed new album 'The Sea' and has been described as a "60s influenced pop song in the manner of Belle & Sebastian" by Q and a "fizzing pop gem" by the Observer Music Monthly.

Corinne's album 'The Sea' was released last week in the US and has smashed straight into the Billboard chart at no.7. Released this week in the UK it's currently sat at no. 4 on the midweeks, the highest new entry.

Corinne Bailey Rae will play her first major concert of 2010 at the 02 Shepherds Bush Empire on February 24th and has just been added to the Glastonbury Pyramid Stage line up. She last played at Glastonbury as headliner of the JazzWorld stage in 2007.
