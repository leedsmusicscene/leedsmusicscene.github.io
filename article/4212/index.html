LSRfm.com looking for local bands for airplay and new live music event "New Slang" ...

This article was published in 2005.

LSRfm.com, the best alternative music station in Leeds, will host a new live music night "New Slang" at the Old Bar (Leeds University Student Union) from 8th October. The Saturday night show will feature the best in local (and national) up and coming unsigned indie talent with free entry.

If you are interested in submitting your demo for possible airplay (and for playing at the venue) contact:
