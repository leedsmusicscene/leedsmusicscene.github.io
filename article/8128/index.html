Royal Vendetta

This is an archive of the band profile for Royal Vendetta.

Jonathan Banks - Vocals
Tom Flanagan - Guitar
James Gierula - Bass
Richard Wales - Drums

The band have been together since mid 2006, and within this time have already amassed a huge following. Their very first gig sold out, and the hype surrounding the band is now escalating to greater heights.

The music is inspired by many modern bands including Kasabian, The Cooper Temple Clause and DJ Shadow however it is with bands such as The Doors, Pink Floyd and Led Zep where the heart of the music originates.

Individually the members of the band have played many venues nationwide including the Carling Leeds festival, Rock City (Nottingham), Borderline, The Garage, Hope and Anchor (London), Roadhouse (Manchester),the Cavern Club (Liverpool) to mention a few and the experience gained is evident in the live show.

Royal Vendetta respects other bands and their styles of musical trends, but they have a belief in their own creative ability and genre. The confidence and the want to do more has grown with sell out gigs and audiences travelling from other cities to see them..
