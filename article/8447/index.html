i concur to release their debut single "Whatever it's going to be"

This article was published in 2007.

i concur’s self-released debut EP “Whatever it’s going to be” will be available from Monday, May 28th. Copies of the EP are available for pre-order from the band’s website or myspace at £4, or alternatively you can download MP3s from Tunetribe at 79p per track.

To celebrate the release of the EP the band has organised a launch night at The Brudenell Social Club on Thursday 31st May featuring i concur, The Pattern Theory, Rotary Ten, Mickey Charbagz, Pete Wright and Vessels DJs.

Entry is £3 and the first 50 sales of the i concur EP will be £2 on the night.
