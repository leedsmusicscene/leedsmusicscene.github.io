B.R.M.C. gig stopped due to safety concerns at Leeds Town Hall...

This article was published in 2003.

Black Rebel Motorcycle Club's gig at Leeds Town Hall was abandoned mid-set last night after fears that the floor might collapse under the 900-strong mass of music fans. Some sort of clever technology monitors the floor's movement and it was this that alerted officials. Safety concerns prompted an evacuation of the building and the gig was later abandoned.
