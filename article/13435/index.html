The Scandal

This is an archive of the band profile for The Scandal.

The Scandal is a three piece Funk-Rock band from Leeds featuring George Fayle on guitar/lead vocals, Conor McDonald on bass/vocals and Michael McManus (Manni) on Drums/vocals. The Scandal's live sound and energy persuades fans of other genres to be invited to a new experience of what has been described as "raw" music. Their live experience over the five years they have been together has varied from small pubs such as The Primrose, to prestigious venues such as The Wardrobe, The Cockpit and The Brudenell Social, each allowing them to build their sound and fan base..
