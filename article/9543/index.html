Wax:On Live 2008 cancelled

This article was published in 2008.

Wax:On Live organisers have announced that the event, due to be staged at Lotherton Hall in Leeds on May 31st, has been cancelled.

Organisers said, “We send out most sincere apologies to all of you that have bought tickets. We threw everything we had at the event to try and make it go ahead and we’re really disappointed we couldn’t see it through”.

Ticket holders are advised to contact their ticket agent to obtain a full refund.
