The Bilderberg Group release single and take to the radio airwaves ...

This article was published in 2004.

The Bilderberg Group release a new single - This City! - on Monday, 15th November, available from the band's website and Leeds record shops. As well as the title track, the CD includes live favourite "Live Music" and "Working Weak".

The launch party for the single is being held at The Vine on Wednesday, 17th November.

The group take to West Yorkshire’s airwaves when they are scheduled to play live on Leeds Student Radio (106.6FM) on Wednesday 3rd November (between 4–6pm).

The Bilderberg Group are also booked in to play the HMV Showcase at the start of December (2nd), whilst their next gig is at the Primrose on 30th October.
