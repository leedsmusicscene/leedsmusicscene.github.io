Draw announced for Live At Leeds football tournament

This article was published in 2011.

The draw for the inaugural Live At Leeds Football Tournament was made on Tuesday 12th April during the soundcheck of The Pigeon Detectives show at Leeds Metropolitan University.

The draw was hosted by Whiskas, with guests The Sunshine Underground and The Pigeon Detectives selecting the bands from a hat.

You can watch the draw "live" in the video below. The video also includes post-draw interviews and reactions.



The tournament will take place on May 2nd at Goals (Kirkstall Road), with matches kicking off at 2pm.
