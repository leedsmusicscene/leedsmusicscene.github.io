Unity Day 2004 stage line ups announced...

This article was published in 2004.

The annual community festival that is Unity Day takes place on Woodhouse Moor (Hyde Park) on Saturday 7th August and the official stage line ups have been announced.

It should be remembered that the event features more than just live music and is always a fun family day out. For full listings and times, and for details of the many non-music events please visit the official website @ www.unityday.com.

Those live music listings include:-

The acts on the Main Stage (noon till 11pm) are: Foxwood Steel Bandits, The Smokestacks, The Bilderberg Group, Samsa, Natlie and Friends, Street Dreams, A Day Left, This Et Al, The Dead Pets, Greenbeats and Deckshufflers.

Cops & Robbers (6 till 11pm) will present: Polaris, Mz Sojourn, Brown Owl, That Fucking Tank, The Seven Inches, The Holy Terror, and Like a kind of Matador.

Global Rhythms (6 till midnight) present Leeds' finest in world, jazz and acoustic music: Sawa Teen, Honeydrum, Anetolio, Kosala, The Grand Wazoo, Feluka and Jon Gomm.

The Primrose (noon till midnight) tent will showcase: Liquidhead, Just like Moses, Grass Hopper, Melody Maker, Cardboard Cowboy, Under the Covers, Yellow Stripe Nine, Tunji, Rockadile, New Minds Eye, The Electric Co., 10,000 Things and Mick Artistic.

More acts are expected in the Bricklayers / Chemic tent.
