The Kiara Elles to release a new EP on the Vandal record label

This article was published in 2009.

The Kiara Elles have announced the release of their new EP on the Vandal record label (Choque Hosein) on Monday, October 12th.

Titled 'Odio', the four-track EP is initially available as a limited edition 7 inch vinyl. Each record will come in a hand-made sleeve made from 3 different types of textured wall paper, and will be hand stamped and signed by the band.

The tracklisting for the EP is: ‘Odio’, ‘Night Terrors’, ‘Supergroupie’ and ‘Rust’.

The vinyl version will be available to buy from Jumbo Records and Norman Records in Leeds, with a Digital Download version available on iTunes from 26th October.

The band have arranged a special launch party at The Library on October 10th as part of Night Of Vandalism 2. Support comes from Damaged Goods latest signing Nat Johnson & The Figureheads along with and acoustic set from A Lot Like Eskimos.

The Kiara Elles will release their debut EP in Spring 2010.
