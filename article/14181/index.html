Izzy Thomas

This is an archive of the band profile for Izzy Thomas.

Biography

"Keep selling your soul, to make your gold, keep singing those words like their your own, but I'll never sell my soul" - This statement summarises everything Izzy stands for as a young, female UK artist, driven on making music with a message a come back, on today's scene of popular music.

Izzy flaunts the sounds of old school blues, interpreted with a current electro/pop twist, effortlessly blended by powerhouse, yet soulful vocals.

Her debut six track EP "Sell Your Soul" has just been released, and has gained interviews/radio plays with BBC (Asian Network & West Yorkshire), and a support slot with Stooshe this year.

Following the recognition from the BBC, EP title track "Sell Your Soul" has been secured as sound track for upcoming film/documentary "God Save the Queen", set to premiere on television/online in early 2015.
