Summer comes early for Leeds music company...

This article was published in 2004.

Leeds-based music production company Joe Williams Music has teamed up with distributors AKM Music to release their new CD album Summer Party. The album is the latest in AKM’s series of royalty-free music CDs for use in TV, video, film and multimedia projects. It will be launched at the Video Forum 2004 at Wembley Exhibition Centre from 10 - 12 February.

Composer and producer Joe Williams said: "The idea behind this CD was to produce a collection of uptempo, summery tunes, the type of music you might expect to hear at a beach party in Brazil or the Caribbean. We’re very pleased with the finished product and it’s been great to work with AKM who are well established in the field of royalty-free music."
