Twelve Angry Men

This is an archive of the band profile for Twelve Angry Men.

A Leeds based acoustic alt. folk group sporting mandolins, violins, delicate harmonies and damaged goods. Noted for their sharp and honest lyrics, heartfelt performances and songwriting that tips it's felt hat towards the likes of Jackson Browne, Ben Folds and Okkervil River, the band take their intoxicating brand of acerbic wit and wonderment all around Yorkshire and beyond in their various forms. Last years live session on BBC Humberside and appearances at the Harrogate Theatre, The Adelphi in Hull, and Manchesters B Lounge have helped push this group into the light, and upcoming dates across Leeds and at the Strummercamp Festival in Manchester in summer will see them lurch even further into centre stage..
