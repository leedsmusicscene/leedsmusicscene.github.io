The Atlantic Mirrors

This is an archive of the band profile for The Atlantic Mirrors.

The band's influences draw on rock, pop, electro and dance, with a sound that is equal parts Chvrches and My Bloody Valentine.

Smoky female vocals rest upon a soundscape of punchy synth riffs, swirling guitar effects, crunching bass, distorted loops and powerfully precise drums.

The band's line up comprises Claire Bull (vocals), Emma Casey (Keys), Chris Parkinson (guitars, vocals), Graham Lees (bass & synth) and Lee Talbot (drums & programming).

The Atlantic Mirrors release their debut album in summer 2014, with UK tour dates to follow..
