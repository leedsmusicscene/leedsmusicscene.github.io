Heads We Dance contribute to new Rewind2 Project

This article was published in 2009.

Heads We Dance have contributed a cover of Kraftwerk's 'Computer Love' to the Rewind2 Project. Organised by the Buffet Libre DJs, the Rewind2 Project features loads of exciting new artists covering their favourite tracks from the 70s, 80s and 90s. The last Rewind Project received more than a million downloads.

Joining Heads We Dance for the Rewind2 Project are artists such as Au Revoir Simone, Bonde Do Role, Little Boots, Sidechains, Miami Horror, Justin Faust, We Have Band and Freezepop.

All the songs are available as free downloads from www.buffetlibredjs.net.
