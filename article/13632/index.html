Constellations Festival line up and stage times confirmed

This article was published in 2010.

Futuresound have confirmed the full line-up of bands and stage times for the inaugural Constellations Festival, which takes place across three venues/stage at the Leeds University on 14th November 2010.

As part of this week's announcement, the organisers have also confirmed three more additions to the already incredible festival line up: Sleigh Bells, I Like Trains and Sky Larkin.

Constellations is a brand new music and arts festival taking place against the backdrop of one of the UK's most creative cities. The full line-up and stage times are shown below.

Tickets for the Constellations Festival can be bought for £22.50 through See Tickets or via the Leeds Gig Tickets website.

On November 13th, promoters Room 237 will host a Constellations Pre-Party at Brudenell Social Club which features BEAK> (Geoff Barrow from Portishead's new band) and Ade Vigoda. If you have bought a Constellations ticket already you can get a pre-party ticket for £5 otherwise this is a £10 stand alone show (buy tickets).
