Aidan Marriott

This is an archive of the band profile for Aidan Marriott.

A sparodic performer on the Leeds music scene, Aidan's mixture of traditional songs, contemporary covers and self-penned material perfectly suit a half drunken haze on a weekday evening. Heavily influenced by Bob Dylan, Woody Guthrie and modern songwriters such as Josh Ritter, his periodic performances tend to go down well with whoever happens to be in the room at the time..
