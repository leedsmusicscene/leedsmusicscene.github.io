Leeds University BandSoc set up home at Royal Park Cellars...

This article was published in 2004.

The promotion team behind live music events at Royal Park Cellars has reached agreement with Leeds University Union's "BandSoc" to make it their "home venue". Society members will get reduced admission to most gigs and there will be special BandSoc shows throughout the academic year.

The announcement marks the re-opening of the venue from its traditional summer recess, and the venue will emerge with a big re-opening programme aimed at drawing as many new (and returning) students as possible into the Leeds music scene, as well as making Leeds' resident gig-goers know that the Royal Park Cellars is well and truly open for business.

The venue reopens with nightly gigs from September 24th.

More information can be found at www.royalparkcellars.co.uk.
