D-Rail to release an album in June...

This article was published in 2005.

Leeds Six residents D-Rail are currently hiding in a top secret location in Sheffield recording the as yet untitled follow up to their "Soundtrack To A Heartbeat" EP. The album is set for a June release via Calculated Risk Products.

D-Rail will tour with Prosthetic artists Byzantine in May, including a show at Leeds' Joseph's Well on 24th May.
