Kaiser Chiefs - live on BBC 6 Music ...

This article was published in 2004.

Kaiser Chiefs will be live on BBC 6 Music on Sunday (19th September) as guests of Steve Lamacq in his "New Favourite Band" feature. On air between 2pm and 5pm, Nick and Ricky from the band will be interviewed and you'll also be able to hear the new single "I Predict A Riot" (B-Unique) and a new B-side.

Produced by Stephen Street, "I Predict A Riot" is released on October 18th and comes b/w "Wrecking Ball" and "Take My Temperature".

The video for the single is currently doing the rounds on MTV2 and if you live in London you can catch the band live on 22nd September as part of the first ever Club NME show (a co-headline with The Others).
