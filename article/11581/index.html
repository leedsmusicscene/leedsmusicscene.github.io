These Monsters to release their debut album in March 2010

This article was published in 2009.

These Monsters will release their debut album – ‘Call Me Dragon’ – in March 2010 through the Brew and Function record labels. Available on 1st March, the album will be available on CD, LP and Digital Download formats.

Over the past three years These Monsters have played an integral role in the Leeds experimental rock scene, working closely with bands such as Grammatics, Pulled Apart by Horses, Humanfly, That Fucking Tank, Chickenhawk and Castrovalva. ‘Call Me Dragon’ demonstrates the freaked out, riff driven prog-force that the four-piece have become.

These Monsters will play live at the Cockpit in Leeds on Saturday, 13th February 2010, with tickets available now from the Cockpit gig tickets site.
