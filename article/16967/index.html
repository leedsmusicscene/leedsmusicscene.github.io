Galaxians announce UK tour and split 12" single on Stargaze Records

This article was published in 2012.

Galaxians will embark on their first UK tour on October 24th, taking in shows in Sheffield, Leeds, Manchester, Nottingham and London.

Their touring partners on the trip will be Nottingham's Cantaloupe (Hello Thor // soundcloud.com/hellocantaloupe).

The tour will coincide with the release of Galaxians first vinyl release, a split white label 12" with Leeds band Runners, to be released by new Leeds label Stargaze. The record will be part of a very limited edition first press of 100 and is expected to disappear as quickly as it appears!

There will be an official release party post-tour at an intimate Leeds venue, with special guest DJs.

Galaxians are also pleased to announce that their first batch of studio recordings are now available to buy on Bandcamp, along with a free track download from the same session. Visit galaxians.bandcamp.com.

The tour dates in full:

24.10 - SHEFFIELD Green Room
25.10 - LEEDS Wharf Chambers
26.10 - MANCHESTER Fuel
27.10 - NOTTINGHAM 1 Thoresby St
28.10 - LONDON Old Blue Last
