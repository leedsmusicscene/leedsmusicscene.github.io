Live at Leeds 2017 announce over 70 additions

This article was published in 2017.

Live at Leeds have announced over 70 additions to the bill for the city-wide festival in Leeds on 29th April 2017.

Names announced today include Rag'N'Bone Man, Nothing But Thieves, DMA's, Gabrielle Aplin, The Hunna and Temples, who join Slaves, Wild Beasts and White Lies who were announced as the first acts for Live at Leeds 2017.
