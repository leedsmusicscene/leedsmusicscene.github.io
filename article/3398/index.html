Kaiser Chiefs to re-release their debut single "Oh My God" in February 2005...

This article was published in 2004.

Kaiser Chiefs' next single will be a re-release of their debut "Oh My God", this time re-recorded by Stephen Street. The single will be released on 21st February 2005 on CD and signed 7" formats.

In other news, the band play the Cockpit on Monday 13th December (sold out), and again in January as part of the 2005 NME Awards Tour with The Killers, The Futureheads and Bloc Party.

Finally, the video for the band's previous single "I Predict A Riot" is available to watch online now via their website.
