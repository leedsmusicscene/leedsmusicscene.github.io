Glissando release a live album as a free download

This article was published in 2010.

On December 20th 2008 Glissando performed a rare show as a duo at the wonderful setting of the Union Chapel in London and the band have recently offered the entire 30 minute set as a free download.

The band are currently working on their new record, the follow-up to the highly acclaimed 'With Our Arms Wide Open We March Towards the Burning Sea' which was released on the Gizeh Records label in June 2008.

Download the Union Chapel show here.

A video for the song 'Floods' was also recorded at the show which you can see here.

Glissando have a few select live dates coming up and a special tour which will be announced very soon. You can catch the band live in Leeds on March 18th (Brudenell Social Club) and on May 29th (Howard Assembly Room/Grand Theatre).
