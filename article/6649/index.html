Line up and ticket details for Moor Music Festival 2006 revealed

This article was published in 2006.

With yet more spectacular sounds promised and a fantastic mix of rock, blues, folk, bluegrass and acoustic music the Moor Music Festival returns for another year to showcase the best bands and artists the area has to offer. Taking place over two days in July (21st-22nd) Moor Music 2006 features a Leeds Live Stage, the Homespun Tent, the Acoustic Tent, film and visuals and more!

The Leeds Live Stage line up currently includes Rick Witter and The Dukes, Stateless, 10,000 Things, Solo, Vatican Jet, iLiKETRAiNS, The Rivers, The Research, Jon Gomm, Samsa, Hung Honeys, Dog City, Idle Jack and the Big Sleep and Mishkin.

The line up for the Homespun Tent features Crazy Penis, The Nextmen, Jamie Finlay, Monkey Business, Kava Kava, Middleman, Mad Science Project, Iration Steppas, Tony Global, Michael Morph, Jon Wood, Badly Dressed Boy, Ezy Ryder, girlnextdoor, Jon One, and Spencer.

The Acoustic Tent lineup is listed as Hayley Gaftarnick, David Broad, Ben Broad, Ben Pike, L-Mo, Giulliano Modarelli, Troy Faid, Chris MacArthur and Jordan Senior.

Moor Music Festival 2006 takes place at High House Farm (Addingham Moorside) and tickets cost £50 for the weekend. Day Tickets are priced at £30 per day. Camping and Car Parking are included, with ticket gates opening at 7pm on Thursday 20th July.
