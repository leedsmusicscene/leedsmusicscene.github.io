The Breech in free CD giveaway...

This article was published in 2004.

The Breech are giving away free CDs of their music and to obtain a copy email your name and address to thebreech@hotmail.com. The CD includes 3 tracks from their forthcoming debut album, due to be released next year; "Chasing The Sun", "Sad But A Beautiful Age" and "Getting Away With It". The first single from this album will be released at the start of 2005 and The Breech are currently filming the video for it.

To coincide with the CD, the band are currently on a mini tour with shows in Liverpool, London and Leeds (13th November at The Vine) amongst many others.
