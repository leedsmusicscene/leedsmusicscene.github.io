Elephants On Acid release their second album on May 3rd 2010

This article was published in 2010.

Elephants On Acid will release their second full length album titled 'Good Time Music' on Monday 3rd May.

After more than two years recording, releasing and rocking - four EPs and the full length debut 'From Dusk Till Dawn' - Elephants On Acid are playing tighter than ever before.

Their second album brings out a darker, emotional side to the psychedelic group, yet retains the raucous party spirit of their influences.

The 9-track album will be available as a Digital Download via iTunes or on CD from the band's MySpace.
