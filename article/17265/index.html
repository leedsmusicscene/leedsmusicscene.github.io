Figurehead

This is an archive of the band profile for Figurehead.

We are a Leeds based 5 piece Alternative/Indie Rock band who started in summer 2011. Our influences include a variety of bands such as Joy Division, Radiohead, Wild Beasts and White Lies. We've gone on to play gigs across Yorkshire including gaining a slot to play at CultureShock festival the following summer as well as regular plays of our single Comfortable Prison on local radio. Recently we've been working on our debut EP to release before summer 2013. We're looking to play more gigs, expand our range of material and work towards a full length album.

Members:
Mason Boycott-Owen - Lead Vocals and Synths
Luca Rizzello - Guitar and Vocals
John Lamontagne - Guitar
Jacob Beckwith - Bass
Tom Hewitt - Drums
