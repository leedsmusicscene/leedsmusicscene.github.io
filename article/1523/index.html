Stateless and Fulc selected for OneMusic Playlist...

This article was published in 2003.

Leeds' Stateless and Fulc have been selected by Radio One for their OneMusic Unsigned Playlist, two of only six bands selected nationwide. They follow fellow Leeds band four day Hombre whose single, originally played through the first Unsigned Playlist earlier this year, is out on Monday through Crystal Songs.

The first tracks will be played during Colin & Edith's show on Saturday (10am-1pm).
