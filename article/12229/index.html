Hadouken! to do Leeds HMV in-store to coincide with album release

This article was published in 2010.

Five-piece Hadouken! will be appearing in store at Leeds HMV Headrow tomorrow (Tuesday, 26th January) to coincide with the release of their new album 'For The Masses'.

The session will be free entry and will be an opportunity for fans to watch Hadouken! play live, meet the band and have their albums, t-shirts, and posters signed.

The event starts at 5pm but as there are no tickets you need to get there early.

'For The Masses' is Hadouken's second album and was released today.

The tracklisting for the album is:

1. Rebirth
2. Turn The Lights Out
3. M.A.D
4. Evil
5. House Is Falling
6. Mic Check
7. Ugly
8. Bombshock
9. Play The Night
10. Lost

Hadouken! will head out on a headline tour throughout February and March. They will appear live in Leeds at the Stylus venue on Wednesday 17th February - tickets are still on sale from See Tickets.
