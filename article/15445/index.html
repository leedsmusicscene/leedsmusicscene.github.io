The Scaramanga Six announce immediate release of 'Trouble'

This article was published in 2011.

One of UK's most prolific and consistent underground rock bands, The Scaramanga Six have this week announced the immediate release of a new single.

Taken from their current album, 'Cursed', the band chose 'Trouble' as it had been a staple of their furious live set for some time. The song is a 4-minute rock & roll rampage that incorporates a full brass-section into the band's already dense sound.

The release is backed by a version of the Cardiacs classic song, 'Everything Is Easy', which was recently included on the very limited edition loyal companion album to 'Leader of the Starry Skies: A Tribute to Tim Smith'.

The single is available right now as a 'pay what you like' download via bandcamp.com
