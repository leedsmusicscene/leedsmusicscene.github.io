Live At Leeds 2013 line up revealed

This article was published in 2013.

The initial line up for Live At Leeds 2013 was revealed this evening and you can watch our festival video playlist below.



Shortlisted for 'Best Metropolitan Festival' for the second year running, and now into its seventh year, Live At Leeds will take over the city centre on Saturday 4th May.

The event will host over 100 bands across 12 stages and the line-up features two Brit nominees and five of the BBC Sound of 2013 shortlist.

Highlights from the initial announcement include The Pigeon Detectives, Rudimental, Everything Everything, Darwin Deez. King Krule, Tribes, The Staves, AlunaGeorge, Peace, Laura Mvula, The 1975, and Swim Deep amongst many more.

Tickets for the Saturday multi-venue event can be bought now via Leeds Gig Tickets.
