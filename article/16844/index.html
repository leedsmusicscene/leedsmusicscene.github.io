Corona Bipolar

This is an archive of the band profile for Corona Bipolar.

Corona Bipolar are a band who give a show you will never forget featuring lights, smoke machines and strobe lights.

They play Ambient/Dub music decribed as Tao Dub (goes with the flow music) inspired by people such as Jah Wobble and The Orb.

The band are Ash on electric drums, Darren on bass and vocals and Simon on synthesizers.

Their shows are always different but always great..
