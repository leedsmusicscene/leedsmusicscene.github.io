New Year single release for The Music...

This article was published in 2002.

On February 17th, The Music will release The Truth Is No Words as the final single to be taken from their gold selling UK debut album.

The album will receive its US and Canadian release on Capitol Records on February 25th next year; the US version of the album will be enhanced with the Take The Long Road And Walk It video and live footage shot during the band's recent North American tour.

Additionally, The Music's performance at this year's Fuji Rock Festival in Japan is to be featured on Channel 4 in the early hours of this Saturday morning - 00:30am.

Finally, rumour has it that a DVD release will be available in the UK sometime around late-Spring 2003.
