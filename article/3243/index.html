Kaiser Chiefs to appear live on Soccer AM...

This article was published in 2004.

Kaiser Chiefs members Ricky, Simon and Whitey will appear on Soccer AM (Sky Sports 1) on Saturday 30th October to coincide with the release of their single "I Predict A Riot" which has been scheduled to now hit the shops the following Monday (1st Nov).

In what is a busy week for the Leeds boys, they will be live guests on London's XFM Music Response Show between 7-9pm on Tuesday 2nd November, whilst Ricky and Nick will feature with Zane Lowe on Gonzo's big brown couch (MTV2 9pm) the same evening.

In other Kaiser Chiefs news, the video to "I Predict A Riot" is currently playlisted on MTV2 and their debut single "Oh My God" is now officially sold out.
