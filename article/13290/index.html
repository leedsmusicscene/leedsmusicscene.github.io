Line up for Brainwash Festival V announced

This article was published in 2010.

The line up for Brainwash Festival V has been announced this week, with around 50 bands scheduled to appear on stages at both the Brudenell Social Club and Royal Park Cellars venues between 22nd and 24th October 2010.

On Friday 22nd October the festival will play host to Dosh; Trio VD; Juffage; Nitkowski; Cowtown; Two Minute Noodles; Nedry; and Nope.

Saturday at Brainwash Festival V will feature Shining; Chickenhawk; The Plight; Yndi Halda; 65daysofstatic (DJ); Glissando; Howard James Kenny; Invisible Cities; Mojo Fury; Loose Talk Costs Lives; Paul Thomas Saunders; Himself; Wot Gorilla?; Sam Airey; Holy State; Youves; The Bacchae; Diascorium; Wooderson; Yanni Montoya; Canaya; Sketches; and Mimas.

Appearing on Sunday will be Melt Banana; Tweak Bird; Gallops; Castrovalva; Blacklisters; Fran Rodgers; Milk White White Teeth; Humanfly; Dolphins; Traitors; Yonderboy; and Master & the Mule.

More bands are still to be revealed.
