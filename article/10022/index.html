The Research to release a new album in October 2008

This article was published in 2008.

The Research have announced the release of their new album, ‘The Old Terminal’ on Monday 27th October through This Is Fake DIY Records. The album will be preceded by a new single, ‘I Think She’s The One I Love’ on Monday 20th October.

The Wakefield trio released their debut album, ‘Breaking Up’, through EMI in 2006, and in 2007 they returned to their hometown to record the follow-up with Jamie Lockhart.

The Research play at Faversham in Leeds on Sunday 12th October and Escobar in Wakefield on Friday 31st October.
