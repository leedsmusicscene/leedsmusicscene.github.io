Stateless to release a new EP in September

This article was published in 2011.

Stateless have this week announced the release of a new EP on 19th September. The 'I'm On Fire' EP will be the third EP to be taken from their 'Matilda' album and is set for release on the Ninja Tunes label.

The EP comes with electronic mixes, courtesy of Blue Daisy and Slugabed; a new track, 'Blue Fire', featuring the ghostly vocals of Amenta; and a beautifully restrained string quartet reworking of the band's classic 'Bloodstream' by the ultra talented Daniel Keane.
