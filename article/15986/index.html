Alligator Gumbo

This is an archive of the band profile for Alligator Gumbo.

Jon Scully - Trumpet,
Greg Surmacz - Clarinet,
Matt Tiffany - Violin,
Chris Lloyd - Accordion,
Mike Simpson - Guitar,
James Paylor - Bass

Alligator Gumbo are a 6 piece swing band from Leeds. Their repertoire is built up of songs made famous by the likes of Louis Armstrong, Ella Fitzgerald, Jelly Roll Morton, and Billie Holiday, played in the traditional New Orleans style, with it's fast paced melodies and improvisations over foot-stomping rhythms..
