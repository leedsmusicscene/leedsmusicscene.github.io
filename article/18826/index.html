Dancing Years announce new single

This article was published in 2014.

Dancing Years have announced the release of their new single 'We Danced Last Night' on 1st December 2014.

Produced by James Kenosha (Rhodes, Dry the River, Pulled Apart by Horses), the new single will be released digitally and on 7" vinyl through Leeds based independent label Hide & Seek.



The band, who have also previously supported George Ezra, Dry the River and Benjamin Francis Leftwich will play a headline show at Brudenell Social Club on December 16th.
