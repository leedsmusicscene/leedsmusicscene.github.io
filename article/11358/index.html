The Plight release a new album on Monday

This article was published in 2009.

Leeds band The Plight will release their new album – titled ‘Winds Of Osiris‘ – on Monday 26th October.

The album follows on from the band’s 2007 debut mini-album ‘Black Summer’ and reveals a much more mature and technical side to the band, who have become notorious for their intense live shows and tour antics. The Plight have toured with Cancer Bats and Converge and made an appearance on the Lock Up Stage at the Leeds Festival 2009.

Leeds Music Scene reviewed the new album earlier this month and scored it 7/10 – read the review.
