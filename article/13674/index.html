The Scaramanga Six release a free 16-track compilation download

This article was published in 2010.

The Scaramanga Six have announced the release of a compilation album, titled 'An Introduction To The Scaramanga Six 1995-2010'.

The compilation includes 16 tracks released by the band on single or album format over the last 15 years.

The Scaramanga Six are widely recognised as "one of the most creative and prolific underground rock bands of the decade" and you can download the compilation for free now from their bandcamp.com page.

The Scaramanga Six will be releasing their sixth studio album 'Cursed' in early 2011.
