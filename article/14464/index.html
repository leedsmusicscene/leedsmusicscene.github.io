When Midnight Dawns

This is an archive of the band profile for When Midnight Dawns.

When Midnight Dawns are a melodic metal band from Leeds, taking influence from such bands as Megadeth, Children Of Bodom and Kreator. Formed in 2008, the band went through numerous line-up changes, eventually settling with Chris and Tom on guitar with Chris doubling on vocals, Joe on bass and Rob on drums. Each member has an eclectic taste in music, bringing an individual touch to the band's direction. The latter half of 2010 has seen the band start recording their first EP, with a perspective release for mid 2011. To be titled "Final Escape" and with a launch party to be planned it promises to be an exciting and forward moving year for the band..
