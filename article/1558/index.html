Hood to release split 7" with Themselves in New Year...

This article was published in 2003.

Hood release their first ever picture disc in the new year on Rocket Racer. There is a brand new Hood track as well as an 'interpretation' of same track by US West Coast skewed hip hop men Themselves. Check www.rocketracer.us for updates.
