Stag & Dagger Festival to come to Leeds in May 2009

This article was published in 2009.

Stag & Dagger Festival, the first one-ticket-gives-access-to-all music event to launch across London’s East End in May 2008, has announced its return in May. The 2009 Festival takes place across three UK cities (Leeds, Glasgow, London) on 21st-23rd May, with the Leeds event taking place on 22nd May.

After the successful debut in Shoreditch in 2008 which features over 120 acts and attracted over 4,500 music fans, Stag & Dagger will come to Leeds with a bigger line up, more bands and more DJs.

The local venues involved in the festival are Faversham, TJ's Woodhouse Club, The Library, Packhorse and Brudenell Social Club.

Tickets are available now for just £11 and can be bought from See Tickets.

More information, including line up, can be found at the Stag & Dagger website.
