Leeds music company sign web deal...

This article was published in 2004.

Leeds-based music production company Joe Williams Music has signed a deal with Chris Worth Productions of Louth, and will be providing content for their website, which offers downloadable royalty-free music for multimedia producers.

Company founder Joe Williams said: “Nowadays production companies often need music for their projects quickly and cheaply, and online libraries are a great way of providing that. We’ve been expanding our activities in this area significantly over the last couple of years, and we’re delighted to be working with Chris and his team.”

Joe Williams Music specialise in creating custom music for media and commercial projects.
