Metalcore bruisers Second Chapter set to release their debut EP

This article was published in 2012.

Huddersfield metalcore bruisers Second Chapter have announced the release of their debut EP on Saturday 14th January. Titled 'When the Clock Runs Out' the EP was recorded and self produced in the heart of Leeds.

Second Chapter are all about tasty riffs, meaty breakdowns, with plenty of two step and side to side beats to keep the crowd moving: crowd participation is a must and their launch gig at the Black Bull in Dewsbury on Saturday will be the ideal opportunity for fans to get involved.

With support slots for rising stars such as The Ocean Between Us, and signed bands from across the globe such as In League from Australia and the mighty Silent Screams from Coventry, Second Chapter are certainly climbing the ladder on the way to success and doing Yorkshire proud.

You can listen to the EP on the band's facebook page and it will be available to buy at the EP release show and every show thereafter.
