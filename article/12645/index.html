The Lodger release a new single on 5th April

This article was published in 2010.

The Lodger will release a new single - 'Have A Little Faith In People' - on 5th April via the This Is Fake DIY Records label.

'Have A Little Faith In People' is the first single taken from the band's forthcoming third album, 'Flashbacks', which follows on 26th April.

The single comes backed with two brand new B-sides - 'The Honest Song' and 'The Best I Ever Had' - which were both recorded by frontman Ben Siddall at home.

The album (and the single's A-side) were recorded with producer Richard Formby (Wild Beasts, Spacemen 3).

The Lodger head out on a mini-tour at the end of April and will play at Leeds Nation of Shopkeepers venue on 22nd April.
