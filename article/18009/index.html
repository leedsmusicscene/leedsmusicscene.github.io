Carnabells Christmas Party at The Cockpit, 20th December, in aid of Cancer Research

This article was published in 2013.

On the 20th December the Carnabells will be headlining The Cockpit for the 'Carnabells Christmas Show!'

By their own admission, they have had 'an ace year so far'. They have played many gigs, notched up a few festival appearances and released The Ramshackle Rattle EP. So what better way to top the year off, than with a big Christmas party at The Cockpit!

Joined by their friends Icarus and The Concetines, it is set to be a great night. All the ticket money and proceeds from the night will be donated to Cancer Research.
