Yonderboy and Just Handshakes (We're British) to release a split 7" single

This article was published in 2009.

Tip Toe Records’ third release will be a split 7” inch single from Leeds bands Yonderboy and Just Handshakes (We’re British). Available from all good independent record shops, the Single will be released on the 28th September.

The Yonderboy track is ‘Vast Ocean’ while the Just Handshakes (We’re British) track is ‘Brass Nuckles’.

The official launch night takes place at the Faversham in Leeds on the 26th September, where the single will be available to buy.

You can also pre–order the single from the Tip Toe Records website from the 21st September.
