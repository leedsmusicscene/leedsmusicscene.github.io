The second 12" from the DTTR 4x12" series will be released in July

This article was published in 2009.

Continuing their 4x12” series, Leeds label Dance To The Radio have today announced details of the second of their series of four limited-edition 12” singles for 2009.

4x12” Volume 2 – to be released on 27th July 2009 - features Suckers, Holy State, Das Racist and The Bear Driver.

The tracklisting is: 1. Suckers: It Gets Your Body Movin’, 2. Holy State: Jagged Lines, 3. Das Racist: Rainbow in the Dark, and 4. The Bear Driver: Mind Attack

The release will be limited to just 500 copies so get your order is now at www.dancetotheradio.com.
