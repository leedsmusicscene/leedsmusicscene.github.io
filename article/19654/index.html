OtherPeoplesLives announce release of 'Relentless Modern Living'

This article was published in 2016.

OtherPeoplesLives have announced the release of 'Relentless Modern Living' on October 28th.

The Leeds quintet have grown out of multi-fold roots in post-rock, shoegaze and indie, now sprawling a wide range of sonic and cultural influences. They are a deeply formidable proposition in live settings, creating atmospheres that draw attention in rooms and church halls alike.

The band's previous releases, all largely self-produced, have received airplay on BBC Introducing and XFM, among others.

OtherPeoplesLives will launch their single with a gig at Oporto on 13th November.
