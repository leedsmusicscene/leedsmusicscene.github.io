Round Window release new EP "Phase 3" on 10th May

This article was published in 2014.

Round Window are set to release their 3rd studio EP "Phase 3" on 10th May.

2013 was a year of change. Mainly a shuffle in personnel as the band parted ways with both a drummer and a guitarist. John and Simon welcomed new drummer Si Ward to the fold and decided to remain slimmed down to a 3 piece . This also brought a change in direction. Still the same Round Window sound but with more edge, purpose and energy. This is what Phase 3 is all about. From start to finish this finely crafted EP delivers four tracks from the new Round Window in an aural assault that is arguably the band's best works so far.

As ever this is a truly independent venture and will be available to download FREE from the band's bandcamp page or stream from their website. There will also be a limited stock of physical CDs available at gigs or through the band direct.
