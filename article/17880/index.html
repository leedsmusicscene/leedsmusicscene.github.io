Kascarade bring forward their 2nd EP release to coincide with Cockpit gig

This article was published in 2013.

It's been a busy few months for Kascarade and we've had our fair share of good news, given how happy we're feeling, we thought we'd spread the joy and accelerate the release of our new material.

With this in mind, we are ordering a limited run of a new EP, "Yours Sincerely" which will feature 3 songs, 2 unreleased to date, mixed by Steve Fenton then mastered at Air Studios in London. We have just this evening had the cover design done by P13 and the CD will be going to duplication in time for the 7th September Cockpit gig in Leeds supporting Audio Republic.

The track list is:
1. NORTHERN REIGN
2. NOTEBOOK
3. HIGH SOCIETY

Please note, this is a fairly early gig with doors opening at 7pm and the Kascarade set running from 7:30pm.
