Thelonious release a new CD...

This article was published in 2005.

Thelonious have announced the release of a new CD featuring live favourites "Roots", "Break The Chain" and an exclusive Goldphinger remix of "Rhyme & Reason".

"Roots" was recorded by Bruce Wood who works on the Nightmares On Wax and LSK recordings, "Break The Chain" was produced by Pete Shand of The New Mastersounds, and "Rhyme & Reason" was remixed by Dan Goldman of Morcheeba fame.

The CD is available from gigs, and from Knock On Wood and Borders. You can also purchase via mail order.
