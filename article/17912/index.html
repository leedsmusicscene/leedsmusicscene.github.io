Symphony Of Sirens

This is an archive of the band profile for Symphony Of Sirens.

Symphony of Sirens create powerful heartfelt tracks with a classically inspired synth edge, live drums and bass guitar

Born in Jan 2013 from Halifax West Yorkshire. Symphony Of Sirens hit the ground running with a string of headline slots across the North Of England, including The Roadhouse and O2 Academies both Manchester and Liverpool. Gaining momentum from these shows the band have added live projections to each of their live set songs creating a blistering audio/visual experience.

The band recorded a Double A Side single in April 2013 at Loom Studios Birstall. This release features the songs Pretenders To The Throne and Stop Pause and Rewind. These songs secured the band a slot on BBC Introducing West Yorkshire in August 2013.
