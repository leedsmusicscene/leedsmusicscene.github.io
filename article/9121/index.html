Benjamin Wetherill to release a single in February on the On The Bone Records label

This article was published in 2008.

The next single release from On The Bone Records will be OTB007 'The Derby Ram' by Leeds arch-folk super star Benjamin Wetherill. The 7" Vinyl single will be released across the UK and from all major MP3 download sites from February 18th. A self made music video is also being produced by Ben to promote the single.

You can hear an exclusive clip of the amazing A-side track now at www.myspace.com/leedsonthebone

The A-side 'The Derby Ram' sees Benjamin using all manor of instruments to provide a unique and enchanting take on a traditional folk ditty with the accompanying and popular live favourite 'I Would Love To' on the B-side.

A launch party will take place in Leeds on February 9th.
