I Like Trains release 'Sirens' in July

This article was published in 2011.

I Like Trains have this week announced the release of a new single, 'Sirens', and details of a one-off show at the Brudenell Social Club in Leeds.

Released on 11th July 'Sirens' will be available on 10" vinyl, and will include the new song 'Flood' and the previously digital only release of 'A Father's Son' and 'A Kingdom You Deserve'. A limited edition version of the 10" includes a special screen print of the artwork by TwoDucksDisco.

A digital package will also be available and will include two remixes of 'Sirens'.
