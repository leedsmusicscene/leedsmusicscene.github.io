Black Moth give away album track for free download

This article was published in 2012.

The debut album from Leeds band Black Moth will be released on Monday, 7th May through London label New Heavy Sounds.

From that album comes the track 'Chicken Shit', which you can listen to below or follow the link to soundcloud where the band are giving it away as a free download.

Chicken Shit by blackmoth

'Chicken Shit' was produced by Jim Sclavunos (Grinderman/Bad Seeds). From its super sludge chord beginning, through the break-neck thrash-punk squall, to the mordant laughter at its end, the song is 2 minutes 50 seconds of no compromise, a full on menacing wall of heavy, pounding riff and throb, shot through with the strychnine howl of Harriet Bevan's vocals.
