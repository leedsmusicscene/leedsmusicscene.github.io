Seraphic

This is an archive of the band profile for Seraphic.

Sash (vocals)
Craig (guitar)
Louise (guitar and vocals)
Helen (bass)
Claire (drums)

Seraphic are a pop-rock / indie band based in West Yorkshire. They are fronted by a female singer and female bassist/singer and play original material with powerful lyrics and guitar driven music. Their influences are wide ranging but include the likes of Alanis Morrissette, Skin, U2, Rolling Stones, Radiohead, Lush, Evanescence, Blondie.

The band have just released their debut album and are touring the UK in the summer..
