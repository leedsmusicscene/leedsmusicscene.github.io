Lifescreen's new album 'Connexions' is out now

This article was published in 2010.

The new album from Lifescreen is out now. Titled 'Connextions' the album was recorded, mixed and produced by Mark Burrows and was mastered by Andy Richards. The album photography and artwork is by Olli Wilson at Atikseven.

You can buy the new album now as a Digital Download at Tune Tribe or at Indiestore.

The album will be available soon through iTunes, Napster, Amazon, Rhapsody and eMusic and will also be available to buy at the band's forthcoming show at The Cockpit, Leeds on Monday 1st February for the bargain price of Â£4!

You can listen to five of the tracks now as they are available for streaming on the band's Myspace, Facebook, Reverbnation, Last FM and Purevolume pages.
