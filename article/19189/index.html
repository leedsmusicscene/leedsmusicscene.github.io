Fizzy Blood release their debut EP 'FEAST'

This article was published in 2015.

Leeds quintet Fizzy Blood have released their long-awaited debut EP 'FEAST'.



You can catch the band live on a run of upcoming headline UK tour dates and at Tramlines Festival in Sheffield.

Fizzy Blood will be taking part in the annual Futuresound 2015 competition - their hear takes place on 16th July at The Wardrobe. The band will also be playing live at Santiago in Leeds on 23rd July.
