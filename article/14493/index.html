Fay and Latta

This is an archive of the band profile for Fay and Latta.

A contemporary electro-acoustic duo providing cool, relaxed acoustic music in a live, intimate setting.

Their self-released debut album 'The View From Here' was released in 2011 and followed by their second studio album 'Beauty In Chaos' in early 2013. The latter featuring ELO violinist Mik Kaminski on his legendary blue violin.

The duo has had their music successfully placed on mainstream US television and within independent movies.

Their recorded music has also featured on compilation albums, notably the Children In Crisis charity album. The duo was also approached by legendary music producer John Fryer to remix the Hugh Cornwell song 'Blacksmoke' for the Danger Global Warming project. The track was released under the name of Kromosome Six.

Tom Fay and Anneka Latta also wrote and performed in the original heavy rock band Exoterik, releasing two albums and touring with the likes of; Lacuna Coil, Blue October, Stuck Mojo, Wednesday 13, Epica and many other established artists.

Fay & Latta also reached the semi-finals of YouBloom Song Contest in 2011 with their song Nothing..
