David Thomas Broughton releases "The Complete Guide to Insufficiency" ...

This article was published in 2004.

The debut release by David Thomas Broughton will be "The Complete Guide to Insufficiency", a five track 40 minute CD on the Leeds-based Birdwar Records label that was recorded in one complete take in Wrangthorn Church hall, Leeds.

Broughton, a fixture on the Leeds music scene for a few years playing his own unique blend of off-kilter folk, has been described by The Guardian as the creator of "layer upon layer of musical quality" and LMS said, "there really are no comparisons".

The CD will be available in all good record shops from February 1st; however, it can currently be bought direct from Birdwar Records via their website at www.birdwar.com.
