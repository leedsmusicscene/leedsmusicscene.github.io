High & Lonesome Festival 2015 artists announced

This article was published in 2015.

The first band announcement for High & Lonesome Festival 2015 was made this week and tickets are now on sale.

The first headliner to be announced this year is the incomparable Josh T. Pearson, whose stunning 2011 masterwork 'Last Of The Country Gentlemen' was ranked as one of Uncut Magazines 50 Greatest Singer-Songwriter albums of all time and made almost every notable end of year list.

Also joining the bill is Slowdive frontman, Mojave 3 songwriter and recent Sun Kil Moon guitarist Neil Halstead; one of modern song writing's brightest talents Tom Williams; and Lewis & Leigh, a Nashville and Wales born duo who are destined for big things.

The full list of artists includes:

- JOSH T. PEARSON
- NEIL HALSTEAD (SLOWDIVE)
- TOM WILLIAMS
- LEWIS & LEIGH
- HARRY MUNDY & THE MULES
- COTTONWOOLF
- FITZWALLACE
- OLIVIA NAIO
- SIMEON WALKER

High & Lonesome Festival 2015 will take place on Saturday 14th November at Brudenell Social Club and the Left Bank Church.

There will be many more announcements to come, including a second headliner, and the full art and film program.
