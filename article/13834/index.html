The Wind-up Birds to release a new single through Sturdy Records

This article was published in 2010.

The Wind-up Birds have this week announced the release of a new single, 'Courage, For Tomorrow Will Be Worse', available through Sturdy Records from Monday 6th December.

'Courage Now, For Tomorrow Will Be Worse' is a 4-track EP, following up The Wind-Up Birds' their debut single 'Tyre Fire' / 'There Won't Always Be an England' from earlier this year.

The single will be available on CD, limited 7" (200 only, free digital download with each copy) and Digital Download formats.

The band will celebrate the single with a launch gig at Baby Jupiter on Saturday 4th December. They will also be appearing live at the Cockpit Room 3 on Friday 10th December.
