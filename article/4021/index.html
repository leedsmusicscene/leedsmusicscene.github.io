Ali Whitton's debut album to be released on 18th July...

This article was published in 2005.

Ali Whitton and his band release their debut album "Kisses/Curses" on Monday 18th July through their label AMW Music. The album is in the form of two separate EPs that reflect the lyrical themes of the songs. These EPs will be sold at selected independent record stores around the country, as well as being available at gigs and also on the band's website (where you can pre-order them now).

Ali Whitton and his band play at the Cockpit on Monday 11th July (as part of Futuresound 2005). There is also a UK tour booked for early August to promote the album.
