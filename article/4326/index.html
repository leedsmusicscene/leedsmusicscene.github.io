InFlight Movie

This is an archive of the band profile for InFlight Movie.

Born from the ashes of failed electronica project "File Under Orange" InFlight Movie had a vision of creating something very different, by not limiting themselves to convention or formula. Having been together for little over 6 months they set about making a name for themselves by touring the country in their little yellow van! Shows in Shrewsbury, Wolverhampton, Exeter and Leeds already created a buzz among people who saw them play. At every show the band sold out of CDs and started to build a loyal fan base. IFM's blend of acoustic instruments, ambient piano, samples and various synths conveys a real ambiant haunting sound. The band have been compared to many artists so far, such as Coldplay, Interpol and Tetra Splendour, the best thing to do however is just to give us a listen and make up your own mind..
