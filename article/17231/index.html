The Do's release debut EP "Slide" on Philophobia Music

This article was published in 2013.

The Do's are a duo from Wakefield, West Yorkshire.

Elliot Oldroyd (Guitar vocals)
Matthew Oleary (Drums,Vocals)

The band's debut EP "Slide" has just been released through the independent record label Philophobia Music. It was recorded at Greenmount Studios in Leeds by Jamie Lockhart (Mi Mye) and Rob Slater (The Spills) and is available in Jumbo Records, Crash Records and through the Philophobia Music bandcamp page.
