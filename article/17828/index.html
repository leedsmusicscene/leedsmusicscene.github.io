Esper Scout release new video for single 'Assumpta Tang'

This article was published in 2013.

Filmed in Ancoats, Manchester with members of the WeAreWULF Collective Esper Scout released their new video 'Assumpta Tang' this Monday ahead of the single's release on Monday 19th August.

You can get the single via www.esperscout.com and esperscout.bandcamp.com.



Their debut mini-album 'Poet Curses' is due in Autumn through Bomb The Twist and is co-produced by the band and previous collaborator Paul Crompton.
