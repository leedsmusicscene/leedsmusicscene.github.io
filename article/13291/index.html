The Lullaby Junkies

This is an archive of the band profile for The Lullaby Junkies.

Doncaster's friendliest outcasts join forces to create passionately cheeky, toe-tapping, thought-provoking music. Honest, observational lyrics, combined with psycho sync drumming to kick out maximum energy with minimum effort. All fuelled by their paranoia of chav culture and a combined disdain for wideboy Oasis fans. The Lullaby Junkies do much more than just line their litter tray with your indie schmindie, they create an atmosphere to bring townies to their knees and steal your mother's heart..
