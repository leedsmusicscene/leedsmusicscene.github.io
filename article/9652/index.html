The Pigeon Detectives release 'Everybody Wants Me' on 28th July

This article was published in 2008.

Leeds band The Pigeon Detectives have this week announced the release of a new single, 'Everybody Wants Me'. Released on 28th July 2008 via the local independent label, Dance To The Radio, the track is taken from their second album 'Emergency'. It is available on 7", CD and Digital Download formats.

The single comes backed with two brand-new tracks: 'Get Up, Get Out' and 'Let's Stay Out Tonight', which were both recorded during the album sessions with Steven Street.

The Pigeon Detectives have just completed their UK tour, which kicked off in spectacular style with two thrilling live performances at Millennium Square in Leeds. They go on now to perform at a number of UK and European festivals, including the opening night of Ibiza Rocks, T In The Park and V Festival.
