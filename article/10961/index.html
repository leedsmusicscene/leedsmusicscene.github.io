More bands added to Bingley Music Live 2009 line up

This article was published in 2009.

After confirming headliners Editors and Doves last week, Bingley Music Live has today added The Dykeenies, The Moons, Howling Bells and White Belt Yellow Tag to the festival bill.

Taking place on the 4th, 5th and 6th of September, the festival looks set to be one of the hidden highlights of the festival calendar with Calvin Harris, The Zutons, The Sunshine Underground, The Futureheads and VV Brown also already confirmed across the weekend.

The festival coincides with big new releases from Editors and Calvin Harris and is sure to see the new acts build on this year's festival successes and open their own UK Tours with extra special sets.

Friday 4th September is free to attend. Prices for Saturday and Sunday start at just £15 for a day ticket or £25 for both days.

Weekend tickets are available now from See Tickets. Single day tickets go on sale from 31st July.
