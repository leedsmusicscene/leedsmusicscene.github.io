Lock Up Stage line up announced for Leeds Festival 2006

This article was published in 2006.

Mean Fiddler have today announced the line up for the Lock Up Stage at the Carling Weekend: Leeds Festival, and as previously announced for the first year ever the stage will feature two full days of ska, punk and emo.

The headliners will be Sick Of It All / Thursday (Saturday) and Reel Big Fish / Anti Flag (Sunday). Other bands to play the stage include Leeds zombie-core band Send More Paramedics who feature in their hometown on the Saturday.

Saturday’s line up is completed by Hundred Reasons, Bouncing Souls, Rise Against, Boysetsfire, Bleeding Through and Lightyear.

Sunday’s Lock Up Stage will also include Millencolin, Alexisonfire, Hawthorne Heights, Against Me!, Capdown, The Bled, Paramore, Adequate Seven and Captain Everything!

With Weekend Tickets for Leeds Festival 2006 now sold out, just a limited number of Day Tickets remain. Priced at £60 (plus booking fee) you can call 0870 060 3775 for tickets, or visit one of the Leeds outlets with day tickets remaining (HMV City Centre and HMV White Rose Centre).
