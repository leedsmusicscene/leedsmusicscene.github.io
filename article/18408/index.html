Palma Violets confirmed as special guests for Live At Leeds 2014

This article was published in 2014.

With Live At Leeds Festival 2014 only a matter of days away, organisers today followed the addition of Frank Turner to the line up with the announcement that Palma Violets would be special guests at the event on Saturday 3rd May.

As well as Frank Turner, the London four piece find themselves slotting into a line-up that also includes ¡Forward, Russia!, Albert Hammond Jr, Bipolar Sunshine, Blood Red Shoes, Catfish and the Bottlemen, Chloe Howl, Circa Waves, Clean Bandit, Courntey Barnett, Drenge, Ella Eyre, Fuck Buttons, Royal Blood and The Hold Steady (amongst over a hundred more bands across many venues and stages).

Also revealed this evening was that Nina Nesbitt will be performing an extra special Live At Leeds Takeover at the Dr Martens store at 6:30pm. With debut album 'Peroxide' proving a roaring success this is a chance to catch an up close glimpse of one of the UK's hottest stars.

Tickets for Live At Leeds 2014 are still available for just £25 via Yorkshire Gig Guide. Tickets are exchanged for a wristband on the day and will permit access to any of the many participating venues across Leeds.
