These Monsters return with new album

This article was published in 2013.

Having been relatively quiet since the release of 2010's 'Call Me Dragon', These Monsters are now finally set to return with new album 'Heroic Dose'.

The album is released on 13th May via London label Function Records (Maybeshewill, Shield Your Eyes).
