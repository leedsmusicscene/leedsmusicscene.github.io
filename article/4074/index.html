Paul Clarke

This is an archive of the band profile for Paul Clarke.

Roundhay Pub promoter and a past music student Paul Clarke is currently a solo artist.

Paul has been involved in music for 11 years now and hope to break through as an artist onto the live music scene. He has played at a few venues in Leeds such as New Roscoe, Packhorse and Irish Centre, but mostly he plays live in the Wakefield area and Castleford.

Paul's music has mostly indie/rock influences such as Jeff Buckley, Ash, Feeder and Damien Rice, and most of his songs are originals..
