No Change to release his debut LP 'Land Of The Lost'

This article was published in 2010.

From the streets of Leeds, No Change is an emcee who has been working hard for years to produce a back catalogue of over 200+ studio tracks.

He has now decided to compile his first LP 'Land Of The Lost' with tracks featuring Aiwan (Afro Physics), label mate El-D and production from Mike D (Subterrania).

The LP is to be released for free via Crackhouse Recordings, a Leeds based record label and event promoters established in 2000 and one of the largest distributors of UK hip-hop and urban music in the north.

No Change has been a strong live performer in recent years, regularly tearing up open mics and events. He is set to support Gift Of Gab (Blackalicious) at Leeds University on 9th March.
