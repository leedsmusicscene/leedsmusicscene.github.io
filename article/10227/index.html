The Cribs release 'Live At the Brudenell Social Club' triple DVD set

This article was published in 2008.

December 15th sees the release of the highly anticipated, limited edition triple DVD set from The Cribs.

Filmed across three nights last Christmas at Brudenell Social Club, the DVD set features live renditions of every The Cribs song ever recorded (some of which have not ever been released).

The DVDs also include behind the scenes footage and interviews with Johnny Marr and Alex Kapranos amongst others.
