Leeds Festival 2011 stage times announced

This article was published in 2011.

With less than one week remaining before the event kicks off at Bramham Park, organisers of the annual Leeds Festival have revealed the stage times for Leeds 2011.

So it's now time to get that clashfinder updated and plan out your weekend.

The line up and stage times are listed below.

Note: Leeds stage times are different to those for the Reading site.
