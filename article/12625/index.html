The Cribs to appear on the Main Stage at Leeds Festival 2010

This article was published in 2010.

The Leeds 2010 headliners and the first line up announcement was published last night and now that the dust has settled, we've taken a look at the initial band list.

The notable news of local interest is the inclusion of The Cribs on the Main Stage. The Yorkshire band will appear on the bill alongside The Libertines and headliners Arcade Fire.

The headliners also include Guns N Roses and Blink 182 and the full Leeds line up can be read on our Leeds festival mini site.

Tickets for Leeds Festival are selling fast with Ticketmaster now fully sold out and limited availability left at See Tickets.

In an effort to avoid ticket touts, festival organisers Festival Republic have once again partnered with the UK's leading fan-to-fan ticket exchange, Viagogo. Viagogo operate as the only official ticket exchange for Leeds 2010, and tickets sold via Viagogo are guaranteed.

A spokesman for Viagogo said to Leeds Music Scene earlier today, "Tickets listed on any other ticket exchange could potentially be fraudulent" and confirmed that Viagogo would offer "100% guaranteed tickets".
