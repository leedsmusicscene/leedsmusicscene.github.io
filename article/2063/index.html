Wind-up Birds, The

This is an archive of the band profile for Wind-up Birds, The.

"Life is awful and we are The Wind-up Birds"

Leeds based standard four-piece (bass, drums, guitar, sarcasm) seek like-minded citizens who will love us, then forget us, then pretend that they never liked us in the first place. Single number one is released by Sturdy Records on 7" and iTunes on 26 April 2010. It features two "tracks". "Tyre Fire" is "I Will Survive" in reverse. "There Won't Always Be an England" takes on football, patriotism, and a thousand years of history and loses..
