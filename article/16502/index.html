This Many Boyfriends release a new single

This article was published in 2012.

Fresh from sellout UK tours with The Cribs and Allo Darlin', Leeds indie-pop band This Many Boyfriends have returned with their toe-tappingly catchy new single '(I Should Be A) Communist' which they say is about "our bass player, money and cardigans".

The single was produced by The Cribs' frontman Ryan Jarman and comes out on May 21st through Angular Recording Corporation.

You can watch the video for the single below:



'(I Should Be A) Communist' will be released on limited red 7" vinyl and digital download.

They will also be hosting a single launch party at Oporto in Leeds on 20th May. There will be no entry charge.
