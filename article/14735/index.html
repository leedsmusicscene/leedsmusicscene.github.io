Installment scheme for Leeds Festival tickets announced

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds Festival organisers have announced a payment program for festival fans to spread the cost of weekend tickets over 2 instalment payments.

An initial payment of £103.25 per ticket will be paid immediately, leaving a balance due of £96.25. The balance, plus a registered post charge of £5.25, will be taken on 29th July.

You can buy your ticket via the Instalment Scheme from See Tickets.

Leeds Festival takes place over the August Bank Holiday weekend, 26-28th August 2011.

The official line up for Leeds 2011 was announced in March, with the headliners at Leeds being Muse, My Chemical Romance, and Pulp.

Leeds band Pulled Apart by Horses will play on the NME / Radio One Stage.
