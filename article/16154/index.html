Leeds trio I Swim With Sharks announce new single 'Build Me Up'

This article was published in 2012.

Leeds trio I Swim With Sharks have announced the release of a new single 'Build Me Up' on 16th April.

'Build Me Up' is the second installment from I Swim With Sharks, who have already been heralded as ones to watch by the Leeds Music Scene.

With their self-titled fusion of indie-aggro-pop, I Swim With Sharks are a cut above so many bands vying for attention in today's scene. They have already seen support from the likes of XFM, 6 Music, NME TV, BBC Introducing, Soundblab, Q Radio and more.

The strength of 'Build Me Up' lies in the instrumentation, mindfully elevating lyrical content and an infectious catchiness that sticks with you.

With a string of festival appearances secured for this summer, I Swim With Sharks are steadily building a place as underground favorites for 2012 and beyond.
