Jon Gomm releases new single 'Ain't Nobody'

This article was published in 2012.

The 3rd single in Jon Gomm's The Domestic Science Singles Series is released today, Monday 13th February, and is an instrumental guitar version of Chaka Khan's classic 'Ain't Nobody'. You can watch the video below:



The song and guitar tab are available from the Jon Gomm website on a Pay What You Want basis. A percentage of every sale goes to STEPS, a charity that supports people with clubfoot, hip dysplasia and other lower limb conditions.
