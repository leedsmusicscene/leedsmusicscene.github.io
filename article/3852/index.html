Leeds Festival 2005 update: Jimmy Eat World cancel; The Wedding Present added!

This article was published in 2005.

Jimmy Eat World have cancelled their scheduled appearance at this year's Leeds Festival, so that they can tour the USA with Green Day. Their replacement is yet to be announced. In other news Finch now appear on the Main Stage on the Saturday, with All American Rejects and The Wedding Present added for the Sunday.

As always check Leeds Festival for the latest line up and ticket information.
