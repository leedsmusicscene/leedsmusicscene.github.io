Heads We Dance release a limited edition gold vinyl 7" single

This article was published in 2008.

Heads We Dance are to release their debut single on Monday, 16th June. The single is a double A-side and will be released on limited edition 7" gold vinyl exclusively through Pure Groove Records, with a free download of the tracks also available exclusively from the Pure Groove site.

The tracklisting is ‘My Heart Is Set On You’ and ‘Love In The Digital Age’ and the tracks were produced by Heads We Dance and mixed by Grammy award winning mix engineer Simon Gogerly (U2, New Order, Gwen Stefani).

The single is available for pre-order now. Remixes of the tracks are also available for download now on the band's MySpace.

Heads We Dance will play live at their single launch party at The Library on 19th June.
