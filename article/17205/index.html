Feeds release new music video 'Lights'

This article was published in 2012.

Feeds have released their new music video, Lights. The third single to be taken from their March release 'Words Of A Fool'.

The video sees the 5 piece prog-pop outfit, now based in Leeds, silhouetted within an animated landscape. Created by P.J.Scott and Rob Skene, they take the viewer to a world somewhere between Kasabians 'Shoot The Runner' and an episode of Futurama.
