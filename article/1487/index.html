Instant Species top Radio 1 OneMusic charts...

This article was published in 2003.

Instant Species have been selected for the Radio 1 Onemusic website and currently preside at the top of the chart. Rick from the band said "It's great to be selected and hopefully this will bring us some further exposure. We're playing so many gigs in London and around the country with our new management company at the moment and so we're hoping it will help continue to build our fan base down there."
