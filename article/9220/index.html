Oui Bee

This is an archive of the band profile for Oui Bee.

London Tourdates professed Oui Bee 'one to watch' in a feature article back in 2007.

The years to follow have seen the addition of clarinet, bass, bazouki and a splash of harmonium to her traditional guitar and velvet vocal.
Alone, Bee was always capable of seducing even the most apathetic ear with her unexpectedly large, lilting voice. Now with the addition of the affectionately named 'Jazzbags' it looks like she's enjoying it even more.
