The Music to release "Live At The Blank Canvas" on DVD...

This article was published in 2003.

The Music release a DVD of their live performance at Leeds Blank Canvas on September 1st through Hut Recordings. Filmed on 28th September 2002 during their breakthrough debut UK headline tour, the DVD features the entire twelve song set.

The Music headline the NME stage at T In The Park this weekend.
