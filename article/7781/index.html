Voltage Union to release their debut single in January

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds four-piece Voltage Union have announced the release of their début single on Monday 15th January. Available via the Dance To The Radio record label on 7” and Digital Download formats, “On Your Marks” follows on from the band's recent main support to The Pigeon Detectives at their sold-out homecoming show at Leeds Metropolitan University and various other high profiles show with bands as diverse as Forward Russia and Two Gallants.

"On Your Marks" comes backed with “Zombie #2”.

During the past year the band's love for angry pop assaults has seen them play at Leeds Festival and Nasty Fest, and their music has received national radio airplay from Lamacq. Early in 2006 Voltage Union were featured on the DTTR compilation CD “What We All Want”.

Catch Voltage Union live at Escobar in Wakefield (19th January) and at Brudenell Social Club (25th January).
