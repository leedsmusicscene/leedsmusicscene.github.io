Northern Rokk

This is an archive of the band profile for Northern Rokk.

We are a four piece rock covers band playing all styles of rock and also adding a rock twist to some not so rocking songs.

We play music from all genres of rock such as AC/DC, Metallica and ZZ Top via The Clash, Green Day and The Kinks as well as some more diverse tracks by artists such as Nina Simone and Johnny Cash.

If you like your rock music a little different from your average covers band then we are the band for you.

Northern Rokk enjoy nothing more than playing live and this this shows in our energetic live shows..
