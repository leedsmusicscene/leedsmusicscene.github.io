Larry The News Guy

This is an archive of the band profile for Larry The News Guy.

I'm Larry The News Guy.

I'm also known as Loz Budd, Laurence Budd, and Laurence Edwin 'Bundle' Budd to my Dad. And if that doesn't help my Google search results, I don't know what will.

I'm a singer songwriter from Leeds, and I'm also a newsreader on 96.3 Radio Aire and Magic 828. And if that shameless plug doesn't help my career prospects, I don't know what will.

On stage, I'm joined by Jo The Drummer Boy - he plays all forms of percussion, is a sound engineer, plays guitar, does a bit of live programming-type-stuff. And if that doesn't make him sound pretty damn cool, I don't know what will..
