FAQ

This is an archive of the band profile for FAQ.

Skipton/Farnhill-based, F.A.Q. started out in early 2002, playing short fast punk rock, to short people who liked to dance fast. In the small space of 2 years or so, the band has matured greatly and now plays a sound which covers a range of genres but would probably best be described as "emo-punk-metal" or something like that! Influences are Iron Maiden, A.F.I., and Alkaline Trio. The band play a very energetic live show and like to keep a healthy stage presence. Kick ass..
