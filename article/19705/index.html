Glass Caves release new single 'Do you have a name'

This article was published in 2016.

Pontefract's prodigal sons Glass Caves release their brand new single, 'Do you have a name', on 9th December.

The track follows hot on the heels of their sell-out tour of the same name and is accompanied by a lavish new video produced by long-term collaborator Andy Little. The promo features 'Lass Caves', a fictitious all-girl version of the band, performing the number in the flock and velvet bedecked confines of a northern working men's club setting, Glass Caves themselves completing the performance as the WMC transforms into a more salubrious concert venue.
