On The Bone to release a 19-track compilation album of Leeds bands

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds gig promoters / record label On The Bone have announced the release of their first compilation album, suitably titled “Compilation One”, that will hit the shops on 12th February.

The limited edition (500 copies only) CD is available now for pre-order and features 19 tracks, many of which are previously unreleased.

Bands that feature on the album are This Et Al, The Lodger, That Fucking Tank, Wild Beasts, The Acutes, Downdime, Quack Quack, Samsa, Mother Vulpine, Benjamin Wetherill, It Takes Bridges, The Somatics, Fran Rodgers, The Scaramanga Six, Mickey Charbagz, Worriedaboutsatan, Smokers Die Younger and Champion Kickboxer.

“Compilation One” will be launched on Friday 2nd February at Brudenell Social Club, with live performances from Quack Quack, Massive Heron, The Folk Theatre Partisans, Samsa, Smokers Die Younger and Mother Vulpine.

Following the album’s release On The Bone intend to put out a 7” and CD single.
