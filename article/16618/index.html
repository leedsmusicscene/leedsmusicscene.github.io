Feeds release new music video 'Willow'.

This article was published in 2012.

Huddersfield based Feeds have released a new music video, the second single 'Willow' from their new EP 'Words of a Fool'.

With their first single 'SleepyHead' having already notched up over 2000 hits on youtube, this second video looks to scream past this count as it reaches 1000hits in less than 48hrs.

By working alongside the new production company Pictures&Noise, the film shows what can be done with the DIY ethic and limited budget.

The video sees the band play with the illusion of a one-shot take, as the camera moves around their house watching the weird and wonderful events that unfold before it.

It really is a video that must be watched multiple times, just to work out how they did it!
