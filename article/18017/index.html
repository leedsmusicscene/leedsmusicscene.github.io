Formes announce new single 'Inside I've Flown'

This article was published in 2013.

Formes release their new single called 'Inside I've Flown' on iTunes, Spotify and Amazon MP3 through emubands on the 6th November.

The single, mixed and mastered by Steve Whitfield who has worked with The Cure and more recently, Fossil Collective, is the first song from the current 3 piece line up. Brothers Steve and Jordan McNamara join the masked 'Alchemist' to create another slice of psychedelic space rock drawing in from their influences which include Pink Floyd, Hawkwind, Brian Jonestown Massacre and Tool.

To celebrate the release, Formes are giving away 100 free downloads from soundcloud in the run up to the release.
