The debut EP from Soul Circus will be released in October

This article was published in 2010.

Soul Circus, runners up in the Futuresound Competition 2010, are to release their debut EP "Artists and Artisans" in October.

The five track EP is the first release from the Leeds based indie rock band and will be available on CD and digitally from all major online music retailers.

To promote the release of the EP the five-piece will set off on their first UK the week that the recording is released, kicking off with the official launch party at Milo on 15th October with special guests to be announced neared the time.

The EP was recorded in Leeds at Soundworks and The Soundmill.
