i concur to release their debut album in October

This article was published in 2009.

Leeds band i concur will release their debut album – ‘Able Archer’ - on 19th October. The 10-track album was produced in Bridlington by James Kenosha (Grammatics, Duels) and is released through London label Club AC30 (Swervedriver, Exit Calm).

'Able Archer’ will be available to buy in CD digipak form on Monday 19th October. You can also pre-order the album now from the label’s website at www.clubac30.com and you’ll get a free MP3 (of ‘Lucky Jack’) if you do.

The album includes the following tracks: ‘Iterate This’, ‘Sobotka’, ‘Grandeur’, ‘Lucky Jack’, ‘Able Archer’, ‘Build Around Me’, ‘Oblige’, ‘Your Words, Your Dialect’, ‘Decimal Places’, and ‘Exits Are Blockades’.

As a prologue to the album the band will be releasing ‘Sobotka’ as a single on Monday 5th October. The track will be available on 7” vinyl and Digital Download formats and includes an extended version of ‘Iterate This’ as its B-side.

To support the release i concur will be touring the UK, with a date with Jeniferever at the Brudenell Social Club on 23rd September.
