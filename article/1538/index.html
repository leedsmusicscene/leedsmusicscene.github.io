sammyUSA sign to Universal Island...

This article was published in 2003.

sammyUSA have announced this week (21st August) that they have signed a five album deal with Universal Island. Check out www.umusic.com for the full Universal roster and more.

In other news, their Fierce Panda single release date has slightly altered. Doorway Of A Dancehall will now be released a week later than previously announced. It will now be released on 22nd September. Check out www.fiercepanda.co.uk for latest details.
