Hannah Trigwell - Not Enough EP Out Monday 8th August!

This article was published in 2011.

Hannah Trigwell's eagerly anticipated second EP will be available for worldwide download from Monday 8th August.

'Not Enough' is comprised of four tracks, including 'Headrush', a track that was released as Hannah's debut single earlier this summer to a great response from fans as well as the media. Off the back of its success, Hannah was nominated for, and won, the Best International Unsigned Act at the 2011 St. Helier Cider Online Music Awards.

The EP showcases Hannah's distinctive acoustic-pop sound and songwriting, but also indicates a natural growth in her abilities as both a musician and lyricist.

Hannah will also be playing a live online gig to celebrate the release of 'Not Enough' with her ever-growing worldwide fanbase.

The show will be streamed live on Sunday 7th August at 11.30pm (GMT) via BlogTV. (http://www.blogtv.com/people/hannahtrigwell)
