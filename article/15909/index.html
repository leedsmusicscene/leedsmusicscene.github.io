La Machine release 'Spirit Level EP'

This article was published in 2011.

La Machine is the new project from Lee Morris and Natalie Bolton, formerly of electro-goth duo Lovebites.

The duo have recently released their debut single available as a free download from Bandcamp and Soundcloud.

The EP consists of the title track, 'Spirit Level' and three remixes by active musicians within the Leeds music scene.

The 'Spirit Level EP' is to be one of several free download EPs to be released by the duo over the next few months.

The duo can be seen live for the first time on January 21st @ The Cockpit 3 w/ FOE.
