Fungal Ashtray

This is an archive of the band profile for Fungal Ashtray.

Fungal Ashtray come from Halifax, West Yorkshire and have built up a large following through regular gigging throughout the region and beyond. Their self produced EP The Girl With Green Eyes has created a lot of interest and caught the eye of the University of Lancaster who have booked the band alongside signed bands at their end of term ball.

It is nearly three years since Dale Castell 20 (Drums), and Ben Longworth 19 (Guitar) joined the existing trio of Arran Harrison 23 (Bass), Danny Keast 22 (Guitar) and Rick Hand 22 (Vocals) to complete the line up and since this date the band have grown into an original and inventive indie rock pop outfit..
