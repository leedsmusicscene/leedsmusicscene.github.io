Working With Kenzi to release their debut single on GrooveStealer Records

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds four-piece Working With Kenzi release their debut single 'Magpie' on 4th December through the GrooveStealer Records label.

'Magpie' is taken from their forthcoming self-titled EP, scheduled for release in the New Year, and is a sinister yet instantly likable song led by a distinctive, brooding rhythm section.

B-side 'Love on a Shoestring' is a stripped-down acoustic affair, with contemplative lyrics and a folk-esque tone throughout.

You can watch Working With Kenzi at Leeds' Atrium venue on Wednesday 29th November and again at Dr Wu's on Monday, 11th December.
