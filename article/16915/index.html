The Mavis Seed debut EP launch

This article was published in 2012.

Saturday 1st September is the date of the official launch party for the debut EP from The Mavis Seed.

The free-entry event takes place at The Black Bull in Birstall and the support acts are Amy Cherryholme and The Matt Burnside Band, plus guest artists contributing throughout the night.

Fans of the band can grab themselves a free copy of the EP at the launch party (there are 50 to give away). To reserve your free copy of the EP please email management [ at ] themavisseed.com.
