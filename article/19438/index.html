Allusondrugs release live-in-the-studio video of 'Nervous'

This article was published in 2016.

Quintet Allusondrugs celebrated their co-headline 'ScuzzTV presents: UK Throwdown Tour' with the release of a live-in-the-studio video of live fan favourite 'Nervous'.

Allusondrugs kicked off 2015 supporting Enter Shikari on their Mindsweep Tour dates, before heading into an extensive UK headline jaunt of their own. A smattering of festivals, the release of a video for 'Sunset Yellow', the 'Magic College / Her Crown' double-A-side 7" on Too Pure, a limited CD EP release, and a co-headline tour with Brawlers all helped to keep the band occupied throughout the year.

The band has just completed a 16-date, revolving-headline run of shows that featured Press To Meco and Max Raptor.
