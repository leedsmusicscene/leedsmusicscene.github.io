Valkyrie

This is an archive of the band profile for Valkyrie.

We are a five piece rock outfit evolving from a long line of blues and rock bands. Music is varied but essentially in the rock genre. Chuck Berry, Hendricks, Stones, Primal Scream and the Clash are just some of the artists we appreciate. The secret tom our success is performance, come along and listen. Hearing is believing..
