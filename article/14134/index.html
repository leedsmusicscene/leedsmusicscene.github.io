White Swallow

This is an archive of the band profile for White Swallow.

We are White Swallow, a relatively new addition to the Leeds music scene.

We are a noisy two-piece, consisting of Bobby Mambo on Guitars and Vocals and Sam Swallows on Drums.

Here is what an Ark Magazine Review had to say about us: "The sound in the Mad Ferret has always been exceptional and White Swallow took full advantage of this; playing a set that left the audience dying to hear more. In White Swallow, you have a duo that sound like the result of hundreds but you would be forgiven for expecting something minimalist, in the vein of the White Stripes. You'd be wrong though. Opening with No Horizon is a brave yet brilliant choice, slithering its way to its finale increasing speed all the while, before launching into the sort of heavy outro that Biffy Clyro or Muse would be jealous of. Members of the audience exchange glances with their friends across the head of their pints; impressed.".
