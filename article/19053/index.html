Live At Leeds 2015 schedule released and special guests announced

This article was published in 2015.

With only a few weeks to go until Live At Leeds 2015, organisers have announced special guests Prides will be appearing this year.

The Scottish synth-pop trio, who performed at the 2014 Commonwealth Games, will appear at The Wardrobe at 9.30pm.

The full schedule breakdown with all bands, venues and stage times for Live At Leeds 2015 can be viewed online at www.liveatleeds.com/schedule.

Official venues this year are Belgrave Music Hall, Brudenell Social Club, Faversham, HiFi Club, Holy Trinity Church, Leeds College of Music, Leeds Beckett University, Leeds University, Leeds Town Hall, Nation of Shopkeepers, O2 Academy, Oporto, The Key Club and The Wardrobe.

There will also be fringe events taking place at other venues around Leeds, including Black Swan, Hirst's Yard, Milo, Sela Bar and Trinity Kitchen.
