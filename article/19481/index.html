The Brazen to release debut album on Friday 13th May

This article was published in 2016.

Leeds-based rockers The Brazen will release their mischievous new single Laser Lips & debut album on Friday 13th May. The single is the second track to be taken from their debut album of the same name and is a dancefloor-filling future indie-pop anthem sure to get feet moving this summer.

Since forming in early 2015, the five-piece have been tearing up venues around the UK and picking up fans at every gig. United by shared influences & musical cornerstones The Stone Roses, Queen & The Who, their combination of classic rock sounds and Britpop melodies has also gained national and regional radio airplay and glowing online press.

The boys' debut album was recorded with talented producer Ed Heaton (production credits include Jon Gomm, Fold, Daniel Pearson, NARCS & more) in January and channels the glory days of 90's indie into a vibrant set of guitar-led pop rock tunes. With a string of sold out shows and a performance at Elland Road already under their belt, 2016 will be a great year for The Brazen.

The band will be celebrating the release of the new album with the following headline live dates, including a launch show in Leeds on 27th May:

12th May - London, Dublin Castle
27th May - Leeds, Milo
23rd July - Birmingham, Actress & Bishop
30th July - Manchester, The Live Room
