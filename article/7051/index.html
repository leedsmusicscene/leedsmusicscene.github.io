Leeds webzine HeathenAngel makes a welcome return...

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds based webzine -HeathenAngel- has been re-launched this month under the tutelage of LMS writer Luke Ramsden and Nick Young. The 'zine was established three years ago and has been a prominent supporter of the local online landscape.

Luke said, "We will be looking to continue to push forward and support the local scene, together with coverage of the biggest releases from that vary vague world of rock".
