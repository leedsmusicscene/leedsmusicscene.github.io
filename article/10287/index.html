Fran Rodgers to release her 'The Green Room EP' on 23rd February

This article was published in 2009.

Fran Rodgers has this week revealed that her 'The Green Room EP' will be released on Monday 23rd February. Recorded and produced by James Kenosha, the new EP features Bruce Renshaw on drums, Lindsay Wilson on bass and a special guest appearance from Tom Fleming of Wild Beasts on lead track 'I See Horses Flee'.

The full track-listing is: 'The Lighthouse', 'I See Horses Flee', 'To This Land Of Mine', 'A Place To Lay Your Head' and 'The Protestor'.

The digipak will be available to pre-order soon, and the songs will be available for download from all major sites.

To celebrate the release Fran Rodgers will be having a launch gig at The Library in Leeds on Wednesday 25th February. Support comes from The Rosie Taylor Project, Sketches and Yonderboy.
