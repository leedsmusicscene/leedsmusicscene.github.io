After The Departure

This is an archive of the band profile for After The Departure.

Phil Dearman - Clean Vocals
Greg Forbes - Screams
Tom Wiseman - Lead Guitar
Jordan Saggu - Guitar, Vocals
Ben Hewitt - Bass
Luke Saggu - Drums

After The Departure are a passionate, talented and driven 6-piece from West Yorkshire. Having being together only a small number of years, they have created a plethora of melodic, yet powerful songs that grab the attention of both existing fans and new listeners. They continue to play shows all around the UK, entertaining crowds with spectacular live performances of their vast repertoire.

"If you are looking for something exciting, strap on those headphones and prepare yourself for what I have no doubt will be a top shelf, non stop, super hero of a band known simply as " After The Departure"."

Bands we've played with: Annotations Of An Autopsy, Martyr Defiled, TRC, With One Last Breath, Hand In Heart, Sworn Amongst, Nightshade, Cyclamen, Marmozets, The Ocean Between Us, The Boneyard and many more..
