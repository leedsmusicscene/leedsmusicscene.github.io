The Rosie Taylor Project to release their debut single in September

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds label Bad Sneakers Records have revealed that their next single release will be the debut single from The Rosie Taylor Project. Released on Monday, 3rd September, “Black And White Films” is available on limited edition 7” vinyl and Digital Download formats. The B-Side will be “Maps For Lost Lovers”.

The release follows hot on the heels of the label's 2007 releases by Wild Beasts, Middleman and The Sugars, and will be followed in the new year by the band's debut album.

The Rosie Taylor Project will appear live at the HiFi Club on 7th September for their single launch party.
