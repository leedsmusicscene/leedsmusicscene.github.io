Vessels to headline at Glastonbury 2011

This article was published in 2011.

Vessels have announced that they will be appearing at two festivals this summer, with the first being a headline slot at Glastonbury 2011.

The Leeds band will be headlining the BBC Introducing Stage at Glastonbury on Sunday 26th June. They will be on stage at 7.30pm.

Vessels have also revealed that they will be playing at the 2000Trees Festival in Gloucestershire on Friday 15th July, joining the likes of Zun Zun Egui, Three Trapped TIgers and Frightened Rabbit.
