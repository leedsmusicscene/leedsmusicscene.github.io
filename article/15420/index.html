Post War Glamour Girls release double A-side single

This article was published in 2011.

Post War Glamour Girls have announced the release of their debut single which will be available from Monday 10th October via Sturdy Records.

The limited edition single is available as a double A-side heavyweight vinyl and comes presented in a matte sleeve finish. The release features the tracks 'Ode To Harry Dean' and 'Spitting Pearls'.

Only 200 copies are available so get your order in now. You can buy your copy from Rough Trade or via the band's page on bandcamp.com. All orders come with a free digital download.

The band will be promoting the single via a UK tour, including a couple of dates in the region. They will appear at a headlining show at the Brudenell Social Club on 12th October, with support from Heart Ships and Moody Gowns, and again a week later at The Hop in Wakefield on 19th October where they will be supporting John Cooper Clarke.
