Fulc EP release and tour update

This article was published in 2002.

To coincide with Fulc's first national release, The Fallen EP, the band's website www.fulc.co.uk now has exclusive samples from the EP.

Fulc are also set to appear in Kerrang in the October 10th issue, with a full feature on the band following their live review from the Garage (London) three weeks back.

The band end their national summer tour in Leeds at Joseph's Well on Monday 30th September.
