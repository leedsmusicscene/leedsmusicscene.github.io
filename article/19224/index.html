Fizzy Blood announce new single release in September

This article was published in 2015.

Following the release of their long-awaited debut EP 'FEAST', Leeds quintet Fizzy Blood have announced the release of a brand new single and Autumn headline tour.

'I'm No Good' will be released via DMF Digital on 20th September and the band will tour the UK to coincide with its release.

A new pressing of 300 limited edition vinyl copies of the 'Feast' EP will be available exclusively at the upcoming tour dates.

A launch party on 20th September will take place in Leeds at Milo. Tickets are available now through the Leeds Gig Tickets online store.
