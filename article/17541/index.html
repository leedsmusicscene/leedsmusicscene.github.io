Icarus

This is an archive of the band profile for Icarus.

Icarus are a four piece from Leeds playing indie-pop. They recently recorded a two track EP with Edward Turner (Pegasus Bridge) which has been received very positively,as well as supporting The 1975 at The Cockpit. They'll be sure to put on a good show and are not a band to be missed.

"The youthful group's lyrics are romantic yet mature sung by the soulful and relaxing voice of lead singer Eliott Parsons. Along with the spot on instrumentation from the band, Icarus. have started out on a really strong stand." - Who.What.Where..
