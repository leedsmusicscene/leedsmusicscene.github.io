To The Trees

This is an archive of the band profile for To The Trees.

A family of four, To The Trees are a cozy little set up with Becky taking the lead vocals and man does she take them!! Joel is ballsy on bass, Bush bangs the drums (beautifully) and Griff is that much on lead guitar he can get a side smile out of the most miserable of buggers!! Hailing from practice rooms on the back streets of Brighouse, To The Trees deliver a whole lot more than expected when I tag along to one of their jam sessions. Brightening up a dark and dingy Wednesday night with only The Apprentice to look forward to I leave with the hairs on the back of my neck stood to attention. I stayed for the whole set, left feeling excited and had missed The Apprentice - like I cared! This is a good sign, a very good sign of a band about to burst of out of the pub wood work and on to the unsigned act gig circuit - watch this space!!! Emma Foster (Band Manager).
