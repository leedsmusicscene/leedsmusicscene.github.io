Soulfracture

This is an archive of the band profile for Soulfracture.

Soulfracture initially came together as a 4 piece during 2003 mixing together their eclectic blend of thrash and death metal. After a few line up changes, many hours of intensive rehearsal followed throughout 2004. 2005 saw the 4 piece line up consisting of Glen, Chris, Nathan and Arron begin their storm throughout the Leeds scene. Their first performance being with Swedish death metallers Visceral Bleeding and more recently with The Red Chord to a sold out venue.

Towards the latter end of 2005 new guitarist Nads brought his own magic into the mix helping them graduate into a very profound 5 piece.

As they progress into 2006 more gigs are lined up promising to continue the raging storm that they reinforce with every performance, leaving the future of metal in very safe hands..
