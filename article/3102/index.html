Grace

This is an archive of the band profile for Grace.

Grace are a four piece acoustic rock band. They draw influence from contemporary American bands like The Goo Goo Dolls, Lifehouse And Sister Hazel and consist of Joe Moynihan on vocals, guitar, mandolin, harmonica and percussion, Sam Moynihan on backing vocals, guitar and percussion, Tom Sheen on piano, and Richard Price on drums and percussion..
