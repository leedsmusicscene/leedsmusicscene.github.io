The Outlines

This is an archive of the band profile for The Outlines.

The Outlines are a four-piece band based in Leeds who have played the local live circuit for the past year. Forming in 2001 the line up consists of Matt Worrall, 25 (vocals/bass), Keith Williams, 25 (rhythm guitar/vocals), Ian Sugden, 23 (lead guitar) and Jonnie Borsay, 24 (drums).
