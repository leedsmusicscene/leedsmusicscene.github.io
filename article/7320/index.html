Yes Boss to collaborate with Tom Woodhead (¡Forward, Russia!) on their next single

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds grime/hip-hop duo Yes Boss have this week announced the release of their debut album, “Look Busy”, on Monday 30th October.

The band have also revealed details of a new single - “Tongues In Knots” - to precede the album on 23rd October. “Tongues In Knots” is a collaboration with Tom Woodhead (¡Forward, Russia!) and will be available on multiple formats. The first of two 7” vinyl copies will feature “Don’t Think” on the flipside, whilst the second will have remix versions of the title track and Yes Boss’ summer single “More Or Less“.

A maxi-CD version will include the video for “Tongues In Knots”.

Following a week after the single, Yes Boss’ 12-track album will be available on CD, vinyl and digital download formats, and will include the singles "More Or Less”, “Indie Kids“ and “Tongues In Knots”.

Both single and album are released on the Dance To The Radio label.

Catch Yes Boss live in Leeds at Faversham (30th September), Leeds University (18th October) and at Nasty Fest (Faversham, 18th November).
