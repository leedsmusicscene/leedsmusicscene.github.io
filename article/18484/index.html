Fifty more bands added to Leeds Festival 2014 line up

This article was published in 2014.

Organisers of the annual Leeds Festival have today announced the addition of 50 bands to the line up for Leeds 2014.

Ex-My Chemical Romance front man and previous festival headliner Gerard Way will appear on the NME/Radio 1 Stage for what will be his first ever solo UK show. Also playing will be Schoolboy Q, Maverick Sabre, DZ Deathrays and emotive screamo titans La Dispute.

The Dance Stage at Leeds 2014 sees the addition of Sigma, Indiana, RÜFÜS, Panama, Kove, MØ, Lxury and Billion.

Over on the Lock Up Stage, organisers have added We Came As Romans, Crosses, Muncie Girls, Brutality Will Prevail, Lesser Key, The HELL, Beasts, Masked Intruder, Emily's Army and Vales.

The Festival Republic has King Charles, Southern, We Are Scientists, The Amazing Snakeheads, American Authors, Little Matador, The Minutes, Leaders Of Men, The Sunshine Underground, The Pains Of Being Pure At Heart, Sivu, Drowners, Lucius and Saint Raymond all joining its line-up.

Added as a new stage in 2013, this year sees the return of the BBC Radio 1Xtra Stage. Dedicated to all things hip-hop, urban and grime, and already boasting the likes of Giggs, Pusha T, Danny Brown and Joey Bada$$, the stage is joined by MistaJam, Raleigh Ritchie, Dirty Dike, Edward Scissortongue, Dominique Young Unique, Tempa T, Little Simz, Newham Generals, Adian Coker, Kwabs, Elli Ingram, Hawk House and My Nu Leng alongside the other previously announced names.

Leeds Festival takes place between the 22nd-24th August at Bramham Park. Weekend Tickets are on sale now priced at £202.50 and Day Tickets are available at £92.50.
