Rhode Island

This is an archive of the band profile for Rhode Island.

Rhode Island are Will Turner, Jeff Johns and Dave Brown, and are based around the North of England, but tend to call Leeds home. Loosely speaking they play pop-rock.

At the moment Will sings and play keys, Jeff drums and Dave plays guitar and sings. They have made three releases this year: an album called The Third Person, an EP called Internecine, and a double-a-side-type-thing called Staffa & Fingal/The Old People. All of these releases, along with much more smart and melodic music, can be listened to in full or downloaded free from rhodeisland.org.uk.
