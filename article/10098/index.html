Jonjo Feather to release a new single 'Taxi' on 17th November

This article was published in 2008.

Local musician Jonjo Feather will release his hotly anticipated follow up single ‘Taxi’ on 17th November 2008 via the Dead Young Records label. The single follows his 2008 debut ‘I Suppose’ and will itself be followed by Jonjo’s debut album which is set for release in early 2009.

Jonjo Feather’s output to date has earned him comparisons to a diverse range of artists from Pixies to PJ Harvey.

Jonjo Feather will play live at Jakes Bar in Leeds on 30th October.
