Allegro Fall

This is an archive of the band profile for Allegro Fall.

John Paley - Vocals / Bass
Mike Paley - Drums
Rob Triffett - Guitar
Will Grinder - Guitar

We originally formed in October 2005 and recorded our first demo in May 2006. Our first demo is a 5-track EP of rough recordings which we used to get numerous gigs to get our name around and in April 2007 we headed into HD1 Studios with Steve Whitfield to record a 4 track studio EP titled "They Burn Tonight"¬ù which became demo of the month in September 2007 Issue of Metal Hammer. Since then we have received many reviews and with a couple of line-up changes along the way we are now complete..
