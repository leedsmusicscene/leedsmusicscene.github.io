The Sugars to release their debut single on new Leeds label Bad Sneakers Records

This article was published in 2006.

It's a double debut announcement today as brand new Leeds based record label Bad Sneakers Records reveal details of the debut single from Leeds three-piece The Sugars. The double-edged feast of pleading, fuzz blues duets comes in the form of “Doo-Wop (Sugar So Sweet)” and AA-side flip "TV", and is released on limited-edition pink vinyl on Monday 20th March.

The Sugars will launch the single on Saturday 18th March at the Faversham, with support from The Motorettes and a DJ set from Sheffield’s finest The Long Blondes.

Bad Sneakers Records is the brand new record label from the acclaimed weekly Saturday Leeds club night of the same name at The Faversham.
