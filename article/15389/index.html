Venificus Hora

This is an archive of the band profile for Venificus Hora.

Venificus Hora formed in Halifax, West Yorkshire to bring about a death metal revival. Ignoring the current trends of deathcore and inspired by the late 80's and early 90's florida metal scene, Venificus Hora want to create crushing death metal with an emphasis on memorable riffs and chest pounding beats.

Chris - Vocals
Danny Mitchell - Bass/backing vocals
Peej Mitchell - Drums
James Greenwood -Rhythm Guitar
Lucas Spooner - Lead Guitar.
