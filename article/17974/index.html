The Truth About Frank - The Carrion EP / No Gloss Film Festival

The first physical release by The Truth About Frank since 2011's acclaimed Cannibal Work Ethic album will be available from December 16th. The Carrion EP (LYFCD004) features 3 tracks by the duo, 'About Frank', 'Organism Dying In Transit' and 'SmoKing'. The opener 'About Frank' was originally commisioned by and broadcast on the Crow Versus Crow radio programme. For further information contact thetruthaboutfranknews(at)yahoo.co.uk.

Additionally, two short films by The Truth About Frank, 'Give Culture Man Back' and 'SmoKing' will be featured as installations at the No Gloss Film Festival (http://www.noglossfilmfestival.co.uk/the-experimental-videos-and-sounds-of-the-truth-about-frank/) in Leeds on 20th October.
