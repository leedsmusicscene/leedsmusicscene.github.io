New MonMon EP available now

This article was published in 2012.

Leeds trio MonMon have released their latest EP - Sheep Hanger Blues.

The EP includes three tracks, with "Punch Drunk" having already received airplay on Tom Robinson's BBC 6Music show after becoming a "Fresh Fave" on www.freshonthenet.co.uk

The EP is available for download over at http://monmon.bandcamp.com and includes a plethora of bonus items. The tracks can also be downloaded via iTunes and other digital retailers.

The band will officially launch the record and have a limited number of physical copies to sell at their upcoming gig at The Packhorse on SATURDAY 18th AUGUST, where they will be joined by special guests ESPER SCOUT and the DJ skills of SNAKEPAN FANNY.
