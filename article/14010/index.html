Ross James Day

This is an archive of the band profile for Ross James Day.

"Ross Day is an 19 year old local boy from Leeds.
He fits in perfectly with the singer/songwriter stereotype. Foppish hair and personality, plaid shirts and a sheer persona of awkwardness."

"Ross's music is a combination of folk, blues and rockabilly, albeit in a pop format."

"Toting his guitar and heart on sleeve, influenced by famous Johnny's both Cash & Flynn the husky voiced young troubadour is making great bounds into the vast and illustrious Leeds acoustic scene and it won't be long before we can't contain him" - Communion Records".
