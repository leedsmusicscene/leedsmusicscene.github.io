Post War Glamour Girls announce second single

This article was published in 2011.

Post War Glamour Girls have announced the release of their second single 'Suburban Barbarian' on Monday 12th December.

The band are releasing the single as a free download and a strictly limited run of 50 CDs. The CD version will come with various remixes of the track by the band and their friends.

The title track was recorded, engineered and produced by Ben Siddall (The Lodger) at Tamla Moortown Studios.

To celebrate the release of their new single Post War Glamour Girls will appear live at Nation Of Shopkeepers on Saturday 10th December. Support will come from Hey Sholay and Little Parades.
