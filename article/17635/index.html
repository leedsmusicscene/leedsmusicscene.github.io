Chic feat. Nile Rodgers confirmed for Bingley Music Live 2013

This article was published in 2013.

It's official. Good times are expected at Bingley Music Live 2013 with living legend CHIC featuring Nile Rodgers.

Organisers were this week thrilled to announce the undisputed champions of disco funk CHIC featuring Nile Rodgers, who will be bringing their unique and revolutionary sound to this year's Bingley Music Live.

They will appear at Myrtle Park in Bingley alongside Primal Scream, The Cribs, The Human League, and many more.

Now in its seventh year, Bingley Music Live 2013 will take place between Friday, 30th August and Sunday 1st September 2013.

Three-day weekend tickets are on sale via Yorkshire Gig Tickets.
