Runaround Kids

This is an archive of the band profile for Runaround Kids.

Runaround Kids are a Wakefield band consisting of George Garthwaite, Jack Winn and Rob Burnell. They are signed to Philophobia Music.

In August 2011 they released their debut album 'Linked Arms' to critical acclaim. Drowned In Sound praised it as "A noisy, assertive piece of work... energetic bursts all sharing infiltrating melodies and a fuzzy lo-fi aesthetic. (An) excellent debut album", whilst Vibrations have declared them "One of The UK's most promising acts". The album has led to them being playlisted on Amazing Radio as well as numerous plays on BBC 6Music, Radio 1 (including being named Huw Stephens' 'Tip Of The Week') and XFM..
