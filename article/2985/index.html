6 Fingered Man

This is an archive of the band profile for 6 Fingered Man.

6 Fingered Man are a melodic indie Rock band consisting of four members all hailing from the Bradford area. Fronting the band we have Drew on rhythm guitar and vocals, On lead guitar Ian weaves a intricate web of notes, off set to Wallz's pounding bass line and keeping the beat is Lee on drums. Originally forming in 2003 they completed their line up at the start of 2004. With over 20 original songs they have their sights firmly set on the gig scene. Taking their influence from bands such as Snow Patrol, Radio head and The Strokes. 6 Fingered Man deal in melodic riff driven rock and perform their songs with a wild and raw enthusiasm..
