Superintendent announce debut album 'First Offence'

This article was published in 2012.

Leeds alternative rock band, Superintendent, are due to globally release their debut album, First Offence, on 24th September 2012 via their independent label, Mighty Man Bear Records and online distributor Tunecore.

The band, which formed in the Winter of 2010/11, comprises:
Ad Phillips (vocals)
Dan Westbrooke (bass)
Andrew Poskitt (guitar)
Ric White (drums).

The opening track and lead single from the album - Are We Having Fun Yet? - is available to download free via the bands website at www.superintendentband.com

You can catch the Superintendent live in Leeds on 7th September at the Fox and Newt, along with Envoys (Leeds) and Dead Party Scene (Wakefield).
