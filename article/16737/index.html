Suzy Blu

This is an archive of the band profile for Suzy Blu.

Suzy Blu is back with some of the best electro beat driven layered pop songs you'll hear all year!

A harder, rawer approach whilst still retaining the pop sensibilities and luscious layers of noise that led Insect Guide to a Marc Riley Session, remixing with Sonic Boom (MGMT, PandaBear) and releasing two critically acclaimed albums: 'album of the month' - UNCUT..
