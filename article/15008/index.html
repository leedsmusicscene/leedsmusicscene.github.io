Cut Out Shapes Debut EP now up for free Download

This article was published in 2011.

Cut Out Shapes are letting people download their debut EP 'Sea of Tranquility' for free for a limited time, from their new bandcamp site.

Their new single 'Never Change' will be released through the same website on 15th August 2011 through a 'pay what you want' download.

Keep checking www.facebook.com/cutoutshapes for more news.
