Be in Black Wire's video and get on MTV...

This article was published in 2004.

Black Wire are shooting the video for their upcoming second single Hard To Love Easy To Lay and YOU can star in it! The band have been given guaranteed MTV airplay - along with several other music channels on Sky and terrestrial TV - and all you have to do to star in it is the following:

Be available next Tuesday [25th May] from 2pm onwards [estimated finish time 8pm at latest].

Dress up real nice/weired/like a lunatic.

Be able to get drunk, or be outgoing enough to dance and generally have a good time. [Large amount of free booze available]

There will be an aftershow party at PIGS at Hi-Fi club, and anyone needing accommodation will be catered for.

If this sounds appealing please e-mail the band at vonblackwire@aol.com with your name and contact details (only if you can definitely attend).
