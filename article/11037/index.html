DeadSeaColour

This is an archive of the band profile for DeadSeaColour.

Drawing influence from late 80's/early 90's shoegaze indie as well as 60's psychadelia, their hard edged, reverb soaked guitar tones and atmospheric vocal lines show influences from bands such as The Velvet Underground, The Jesus & Mary Chain and Joy Division among others.

'Dead Sea Colour's "Addicted to Life" encompasses an array of gorgeous melodic tones, mixed with blasts of distorted, energetic musical mastery. With undertones of the 80/90's shoe-gazey indie scene, this album shows influential sparks from the likes of The Smiths and The Big Pink...' - Ark Magazine.
