Headliners announced for Leeds Festival 2004...

This article was published in 2004.

Line up for Leeds Festival has been announced, headliners being The Darkness, The White Stripes and Green Day. Other bands include 50 Cent, The Offspring, Ash, Morrissey, The Libertines, Franz Ferdinand, The Hives, Lost Prophets and The Streets.

Most of these were already known or self-confirmed, check out our new mini-site for up to date news, and to buy tickets: Leeds Festival.
