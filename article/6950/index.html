Danny Laycock

This is an archive of the band profile for Danny Laycock.

Just a music student who would rather have his work available to public. No point writing the things if nobody is going to hear it. As you may expect this student is in need of work, any offers would be appreciated. I've been playing guitar and bass for about 10 years now, performing for probably just as long. I can do (and enjoy) the solo acoustic thing and occasionally I could get together a cracking band. I'm available for session work of any kind, have no worries about musicianship, gear - professional all round. That's not to say I'm an overly serious person....
