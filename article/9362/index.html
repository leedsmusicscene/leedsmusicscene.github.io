Primal Scream to headline Wax:On Live music festival in Leeds

This article was published in 2008.

Primal Scream have today been revealed as the headliners for the first ever Wax:On Live music festival in Leeds. The festival, which takes place at Lotherton Hall on Saturday 31st May, will feature over 60 artists across its six arenas.

Other stage headliners include Reverend And The Makers who will headline the Wall Of Sound arena.
