Eureka Machines to release a new single in February

This article was published in 2011.

Leeds-based band Eureka Machines have announced the release of a new single, the first to be taken from their upcoming second album 'Champion The Underdog".

The single is called 'These Are The People Who Live In My House' and will be released via the Wrath Records label as a Digital Download on 7th February 2011.

It comes backed with 'Arnald', taken from the recent Tim Smith (Cardiacs) tribute album 'Leader Of The Starry Skies'.

You can watch the video for the single below:



The album is expected in May 2011 and will be the follow up to the band's 2008 debut 'Do Or Die'.

Eureka Machines will tour the UK in May, appearing at the Brudenell Social Club in Leeds on the 27th.
