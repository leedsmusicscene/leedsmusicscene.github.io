The Music release their second album in September 2004...

This article was published in 2004.

The Music release their second album, Welcome To The North through Virgin Records on September 20th. Recorded with producer Brendan O'Brien (Stone Temple Pilots, Bob Dylan, Soundgarden, Pearl Jam) in Atlanta in the early part of 2004, the 11 track album will be preceded by a single, Freedom Fighters on September 6th.

The full tracklisting is: Welcome To The North, Freedom Fighters, Bleed From Within, Breakin', Cessation, Fight The Feeling, Guide, Into The Night, I Need Love, One Way In, No Way Out and Open Your Mind.

The band will also make the track Welcome To The North available for download on 19th July 2004 via their website, and have announced a full UK tour in support of the album release.
