The Anonymous Groove Band

This is an archive of the band profile for The Anonymous Groove Band.

The Anonymous Groove Band play a selection of hard hitting funk, reggae, jazz and groove classics with the occasional rock moment when called for.

The band can play as a 3 piece to 10 piece line up, depending on the gig. Our usual line up is as a 4 piece, where we can perform gigs in a more background jazz band style, or a full on get up and dance groove act.

We play a large selection of covers from artists including James Brown, Stevie Wonder, Tower of Power, Meters, Led Zepplin, Toots and the Maytals, Billy Cobham and Bill Withers..
