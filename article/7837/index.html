The Hair to release their debut single on new Fierce Panda label Cool For Cats Records

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band The Hair have this week announced that their debut single will be released on the new Cool For Cats Records label (Fierce Panda) on Monday, 12th March.

With “Ghosts” on the A-side and live favourite “Left Foot, Right Foot” on the B-side, the single will be available on limited edition 7” vinyl (distributed via Pinnacle) and Digital Download formats. Remixes will be available from London DJ duo and new V2 Records signings Uncle Buck and upcoming South London grime-clash producer Goldielocks.

You can watch The Hair live in Leeds on 6th March when they headline at the Faversham as part of the Red Stripe Awards sponsored by Music Week & The Great Escape. The official single launch takes place at Old Blue Last in London on 13th March.
