Rosie Doonan to release single and album in November

This article was published in 2010.

Wakefield-based singer songwriter Rosie Doonan has announced the release of her new single, 'Fall For Me', which will be released on 1st November through the Silvertop Records label.

The single is the first to be taken from her new album, 'Pot of Gold', which is released a fortnight later on 15th November.

To coincide with the releases Rosie Doonan will be playing live at The Adelphi in Leeds on 30th October and again at the New Roscoe on 20th November.
