The Truth About Frank announce new album

This article was published in 2011.

Cannibal Work Ethic, the new album by The Truth About Frank, will be released (in digipak-CD and DL formats) on 4th July 2011 on L.Y.F. Recordings.

Tracklisting as follows;

1. Love Is A Cage
2. Channelling Static
3. A Butterfly Mind
4. Teddy Hop
5. A Savage Invitation
6. Swimming Over Mountains
7. Shadow Sex
8. Taritakoom
