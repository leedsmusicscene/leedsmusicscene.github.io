The Pigeon Detectives to release "You Know I Love You" on July 17th

This article was published in 2006.

The Pigeon Detectives release their second single on Leeds’ Dance To The Radio label on Monday, 17th July. Initially planned as download-only “You Know I Love You” will now be released as a limited edition 7” vinyl single. The B-Side is “Wait For Me”.

The single is available to pre-order now from HMV.

The Pigeon Detectives play a single launch party at Cockpit on July 11th, with support from The Holloways.
