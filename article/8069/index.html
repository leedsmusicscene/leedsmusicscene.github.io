Join in the biggest ever survey of Yorkshire's music producers, labels, promoters, agencies and musicians...

This article was published in 2007.

The Hub is calling out for all of Yorkshire's music producers, record labels, event promoters, venue owners, music agencies, bands and musicians to take part in what is being billed as the largest every survey of the music sector in the region.

Working with Arts Council England and Yorkshire Forward The Hub aim to find out more about the region's music businesses with the ultimate aim of finding out how they can help them grow.

So, if you are a musician or otherwise involved in the region's music industry, this is your chance to get heard and your opportunity to tell these two key agencies just what you think they should be doing.

You can complete the survey online at www.thehubuk.com/yorkshiremusicsurvery.pdf. The deadline for feedback is 11th March 2007.
