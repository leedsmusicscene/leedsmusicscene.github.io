Leeds band become the first ever to stream an entire pre-release album on a dedicated iPhone app

This article was published in 2009.

Leeds-based electronic trio, Heads We Dance, were the first ever act to stream an entire album pre-release on a dedicated iPhone application.

The band’s debut record 'Love Technology' – officially available from today, June 1st, as a Digital Download – was available to hear through the application a full two weeks ahead of its release.

The application also includes exclusive photos, updates and a fan wall to chat to the band direct.

The record will have a physical release on June 15th.

Heads We Dance have recently topped the Hype Machine chart twice in a week, were featured as the top breakthrough artist on wearehunted.com, and their single 'When The Sirens Sound' (released 18th May) was named a Popjustice 'Song of the Day'.

Most recently, the band's version of Kraftwerk's 'Computer Love' received more than a million downloads as part of the Buffet Libre DJs 'Rewind 2' project.

Heads We Dance's debut album 'Love Technology' is available today.
