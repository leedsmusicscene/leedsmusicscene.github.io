Candid Squash

This is an archive of the band profile for Candid Squash.

Formed late summer 2006 as a two-piece jam band consisting of Damian Hughes & Joe Scotcher.

Found suitable bass player in September 2006 - Luke Hay.

Decided the sound was lacking something - Enter Mikey Cook on 2nd guitar.

Played gigs under the name Lethargy.

Band changes name to Candid Squash.

Member change September 2007 - Andrey Pavlovic replaces Joe Scotcher on Drums.

New Image - New Songs..

Current line up:

Damian Hughes - Vocals/Guitar
Andrey Pavlovic - Drums
Luke Hay - Bass/Vocals
Mikey Cook - Guitars.
