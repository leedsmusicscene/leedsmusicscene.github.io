Micky P Kerr

This is an archive of the band profile for Micky P Kerr.

Michael Kerr (aka Micky P Kerr) has been developing as an artist for the best part of a decade. Over the course of around 300 live gigs he has managed to turn himself into a poet, folk musician, comedian, rapper, indie kid, punk and a rock n roller. In Jan 07 he officially formed 'The Dudes' as his backing band and they have been growing in stature ever since. Their live shows usually contain a mixture of spoken word, comedy, pop music, folk pop and hip hop..
