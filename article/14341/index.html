Set Your Goals and Hit The Lights added to Slam Dunk 2011 line up

This article was published in 2011.

Slam Dunk organisers have announced that Set Your Goals will appear at the Slam Dunk Festival this year, along with pop-punk legends Hit The Lights, fast-rising British rockers Francesqa and dub-step act, Tek One.

Festival Promoter Ben Ray says, "I am extremely pleased to have two of the star bands from last year, Set Your Goals and Hit the Lights return for this year's event. They said they had so much fun last year, it was hard to keep them away!"

The bands will join headliners Less Than Jake and Reel Big Fish, along with a number of bands previously announced for Slam Dunk 2011.
