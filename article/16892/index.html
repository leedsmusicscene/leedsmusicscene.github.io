White Heat debut 7'' single released Monday 20th August 2012

This article was published in 2012.

This exciting debut 7'' single release by White Heat - 'Smiles' b/w 'Hymn' - is limited to 500 x 7'' singles and will be self-released on 20th August 2012.

Received 4/5 stars from Leeds Music Scene.

You can purchase this limited single from Jumbo, Piccadilly or Rough Trade East and West. Or alternatively, you can buy the digital download from whiteheatmusic.bandcamp.com.

Single tour dates to follow.
