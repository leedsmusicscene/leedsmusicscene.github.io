Dance Stage headliners announced for Leeds Festival 2005...

This article was published in 2005.

Mean Fiddler have today announced the headliners for the Dance Stage at this year's Leeds Festival at Bramham Park. The festival organisers have also announced additions to the Main Stage and Carling Stage line ups.

On Friday the Dance Stage will be headlined by Mylo and Audio Bullys whilst Saturday's headliners will be Lemon Jelly, aka Fred Deakin and Nick Franglen.

Those other Sunday afternoon additions include Fall Out Boy - who have been confirmed for the Main stage - and Be Your Own Pet and Puggy who have both been added to festivities on the Carling Stage.
