Further line up announcements made for Leeds Festival 2005...

This article was published in 2005.

Mean Fiddler have today announced additions to the line up for Leeds Festival 2005.

Friday's Main Stage line up now includes Alkaline Trio, Turbonegro and Bullet For My Valentine.

Friday on the NME Radio One Stage sees the addition of British Sea Power, Willy Mason, The Duke Spirit and Maximo Park, with Saturday now featuring The Cooper Temple Clause, My Chemical Romance, Fightstar and The Subways. Hot Hot Heat, The Kills and The Others have been added to Sunday's line up.

Weekend tickets are now sold out, although day tickets are still available. Visit Leeds Festival for the latest line up, news and ticket information.

Leeds bands are reminded to get their Futuresound application in for this Friday (22nd April) to be in with a chance of playing at this year's festival.
