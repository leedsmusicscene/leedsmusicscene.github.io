Live at Leeds 2016 reveal 70 new names

This article was published in 2016.

Organisers of the annual Live at Leeds festival have this week revealed a mammoth 73 additional acts who will be playing at their tenth anniversary event in April.

Building on December's announcement that featured 2015's biggest pop sensation, Brit Award-nominee Jess Glynne, fast rising Liverpool four piece Circa Waves and indie legends We Are Scientists, music fans attending Live at Leeds 2016 will also be able to see Mystery Jets, Ghostpoet, Blossoms, Los Campesinos! and Blood Red Shoes.

Leeds bands also featured in the announcement on Thursday, with Vitamin, Fighting Caravans, Forever Cult and TUSK all set for Live at Leeds 2016.

The full list of new additions is:

Jones / Kagoule / Kleine Schweine / Kloe / Meilyr Jones / Miamigo / Milk Teeth / Mullally / Natalie McCool / Pumarosa / Raphaella / Rhys Lewis / Samm Henshaw / Seramic / Slutface / Strange Bones / The Indigo Project / The Jacques / Trash / Tusk / Viola Beach / Vitamin / Where Fires Are / White / Willie J Healey / Zuzu.

Live At Leeds 2016 takes place across multiple venues around Leeds on Saturday 30th April 2016. Tickets are available now via Leeds Gig tickets.
