Eiger join up with In At The Deep End Records...

This article was published in 2003.

In At The Deepend Records have announced their latest local addition. Joining the likes of Buzzkill and Send More Paramedics are Eiger who describe themselves as 'cultured rock for fans of metal and hardcore who prefer the understated stab in the heart to the full-on catfight in the street'. There's a great MP3 on the site - www.inatthedeependrecords.co.uk - and expect some releases soon.
