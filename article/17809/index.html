Eden Junction

This is an archive of the band profile for Eden Junction.

The culmination of a vision from a very long time ago, Eden Junction ties together self taught musicianship, a love of catchy hooks, and soaring guitar driven rock music. It attempts to deliver an assault on your auditory canal, while at the same time sticking around in your head like any other pop song out in the market today.

Lyrically, Eden Junction takes on the issues of the everyman, from having no money in the bank to falling in love, from being torn apart by someone you chose to trust to getting back on your feet and declaring you'd make it without them...

Eden Junction is a meeting point for anyone looking for good wholesome and real rock music with an uplifting sentiment that doesnt take itself to seriously. Get involved, have a listen, spread the word, and enjoy the show..
