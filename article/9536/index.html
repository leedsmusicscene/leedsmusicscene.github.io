The Hokes

This is an archive of the band profile for The Hokes.

A storming Liverpool/Leeds three piece with musical origins rooted in the Mississippi Hill blues through to English classic rock n roll. Formed due to rearrangements to a previous line up the band started their gigging with a new set and a great new vibe in 2006, all members have over 4 years experience in bands and performing live. They have a great energy and a good eye for melody and rock n roll attitude. The band is made up of Chris Dorgan lead guitarist and singer, Jon Rymer drummer and backing vocals and Tom White Bass player and lead vocals. It's guaranteed they'll get you singing along and your feet stomping. Their first official demo is now on sale..
