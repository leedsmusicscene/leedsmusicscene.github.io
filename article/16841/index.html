The Mexanines to release their debut single

This article was published in 2012.

The Mexanines have announced the release of their debut single later this month.

The single - Shimmer - is the Q Magazine "single of the week" and also features in the next issue of Artrocker.

In 2011 The Mexanines secured a spot on the BBC Introducing Stage at Leeds Festival after winning the Centre Stage 2011 competition and this year has also seen recognition for the Halifax three-piece when they were a unanimous choice at the Musicians Benevolent Fund Songwriting Awards.

The band impressed some of the biggest names in the business, walking away with the main award of the night - Songwriter of the Year.
