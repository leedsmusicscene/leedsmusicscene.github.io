Lost Winter

This is an archive of the band profile for Lost Winter.

Lost Winter was born in the long, dark and near endless winter of 2012-13 by a bored 33 year old guitarist living in Northern England. The original idea was a simple one: to release four electro-inspired DIY EPs with the help and support of friends, musicians, artists and designers.

Each EP will be released during a season of the year: the first Lost Winter EP was released in the winter of 2013 and will be followed by the Summer EP in September 2013. All being well, the Spring EP and Autumn EP will follow in 2014.

I want to give something back to the artists that inspired Lost Winter, and these songs are what I have to offer.

Richard-Lost Winter
June 2013.
