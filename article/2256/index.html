Black Wire to release 7" single "Attack Attack Attack" in February...

This article was published in 2004.

Black Wire head out on tour shortly to promote their single Attack Attack Attack. The limited edition 7" will be released on 16th February on Wrong Crowd Records, and is backed with Very Gun. It was produced by Choque Hosein (Black Star Liner) at The Leopard Lounge.
