MonMon to release Sheep Hanger Blues EP

This article was published in 2012.

"Sheep Hanger Blues" is the upcoming serving of music power from Leeds three piece MonMon. Renowned for combining noise and melody in a patented ratio, this latest offering has all the trademark intricate musicianship you've come to know and love.

Tuned in somewhere between the frequency of black light and a small earthquake, this EP builds upon the rhythm-driven urban tales of previous releases, and adds the touches of a band who seem to be growing up disgracefully and starting to really enjoy themselves.

Including the tracks "Punch Drunk", "Something;Anything" and "Black Light" the EP will be released digitally and on CD on August 6th 2012.

All tracks were recorded at Greenmount Studios and mastered by Hippocratic Mastering.
