Giants Causeway

This is an archive of the band profile for Giants Causeway.

Formed from the remnants of a indie/goth/metal outfit on one side and folk/ irish duo on the other (with a touch of punk circa '78), the Giants Causeway sound is a blend (mess?!) of all of these featuring covers and original material from each style. Usually to be found gigging (staggering) on the West Yorkshire UK pub scene, the band's first CD, Hoarse, is available now and another is planned for recording this year..
