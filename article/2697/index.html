Concrete Jungle headliners announced for Leeds Festival 2004 (and other stage additions)

This article was published in 2004.

More bands have been announced for Leeds Festival 2004, including A and Sick of it All as Concrete Jungle Stage headliners on Sunday 29th August.

On the Radio One Stage on Friday, the line up has seen these additions: The Fiery Furnaces, Futureheads and Eastern Lane.

On the Saturday the latest Radio One stage additions include The (International) Noise Conspiracy, The Walkmen and The Open, and on the Sunday the line up now includes Stereolab.
