The Pigeon Detectives to release a follow-up to the charts-topping single "I Found Out"

This article was published in 2007.

On Monday, 26th February, Leeds band The Pigeon Detectives will release their next single, the follow-up to November 2006’s chart-topping single ‘I Found Out‘. Their previous single topped the UK Indie Charts and reached #39 in the UK Top 40.

Produced by Will Jackson and mixed by Cenzo Townshend, the new single will be ‘Romantic Type’ and is set for release on 7" vinyl, CD and Digital Download formats.

Pre-order the single now from Amazon or from iTunes.

The Pigeon Detectives will support Kaiser Chiefs on their recently announced UK tour, playing at Manchester Apollo on Wednesday, 21st February and at Newcastle Academy on the day of the release of ‘Romantic Type‘ (26th). The band will also return to Leeds for a headlining show at Leeds Metropolitan University on Sunday, 25th February.

All tickets are now sold out.
