Lost in Olympus

This is an archive of the band profile for Lost in Olympus.

Lost in Olympus are a heavy rock band from Leeds. Consisting of 5 lads that have a wealth of experience, playing well over 100 gigs between them in different bands up and down the country L.I.O are dedicated to creating original music they love to play. With catchy riffs and big choruses they're not content to just sit back and be like every other band in the British music scene.

L.I.O are finally getting the chance to perform the songs they've been working on for over a year and so far they've gone down a storm. The next step will see them head into the studio to record there debut EP. They hope to be rewarded for there patience, as the saying goes "the juice is worth the squeeze" and 2013 looks set to see all their efforts come to fruition..
