New album from A-Sun Amissa is out today

This article was published in 2013.

The new A-Sun Amissa album 'You Stood Up For Victory, We Stood Up For Less' is out now.

The album is available from Monday 18th March through Gizeh Records and is available on limited edition LP (300 copies) and as a Digital Download.

All LP orders from the Gizeh Records web store come with a free instant download of the album in lossless or MP3 form. If you are buying elsewhere you will find a download code inserted into the sleeve.

A-Sun Amissa is a project formed out of Leeds, UK in 2011 by members of Glissando and The Rustle of the Stars. They next play live in Leeds in April supporting Barn Owl at Wharf Chambers.
