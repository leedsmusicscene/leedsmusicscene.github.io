Sounds of UK to release a DVD volume of Leeds bands

This article was published in 2007.

Available soon, ‘Sounds of UK - Volume Leeds’ is a fantastic DVD collection of 25 of the best bands from the area, presented as live video recordings, promo videos, and band interviews.

Featured on the DVD will be The Pigeon Detectives, The Sunshine Underground, The Hair, Black Wire, Peacewise and Homestyle feat. BTI, Yorkshire Terrierz, Dan Audio, The Sugars, iLiKETRAiNS, !Forward, Russia!, Wild Beasts, 2020 Soundsystem, The Bazaars, Napoleon IIIrd, Embrace, Minus 3, Dead Disco, Sky Larkin, Royal Vendetta, The New Mastersounds, The Call, Bianca Gerald, Shut Your Eyes And You’ll Burst Into Flames, Breaking The Illusion and Yes Boss.

On top of this fine selection of videos, the DVD includes interviews with Danny McNamara (Embrace), Yes Boss, The Sugars, The Hair, iLiKETRAiNS, BTI and Leeds By Example, and The Sunshine Underground.

The Sounds of UK Launch party will happen at the Faversham on 15th May, featuring live sets from The Hair, Breaking The Illusion, Napoleon IIIrd and Metro Riot. The event will include selection of music videos on the venue's big screen.
