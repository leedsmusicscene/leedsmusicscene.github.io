The Frugal

This is an archive of the band profile for The Frugal.

Pete Donnelly - Guitar / Vocals
Logan Helps - Bass / Backing Vocals
Iain Bamford - Drums / Percussion

The Frugal were formed in 2010 as a folk / indie three piece determined to create a stripped down yet energetic acoustic sound.
The plan was to create a style which embodies the ethics of traditional blues and folk music, simple and meaningful storytelling to an infectious and addictive melody.

After being in various line ups together Pete Donnelly (vocals / guitar) and Iain Bamford (drums / percussion) were lucky enough to discover the expertise of local Bass player Logan Helps. Logan completed the line up, immediately contributing to the melting pot of influences which make up The Frugal's interpretation of modern folk music.

Having just finished the recording of the new self titled record, The Frugal look forward to continuing to write music, play music and... most importantly have loads of fun doing it..
