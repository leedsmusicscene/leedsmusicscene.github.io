Leeds bands to feature on Charity Compilation CD to be released in August...

This article was published in 2005.

Several Leeds bands are set to feature on a charity compilation CD being put together by local record label and promotions company, Collateral Music. The CD, in aid of Banardo's, is released on 8th August and features no less than 18 bands!

West Yorkshire bands with songs included on the CD include Ten Seconds Of Chaos, Zealous, Ali Whitton, Kram and All My Friends Are Dead. The full tracklisting, and further information can be found at www.collateralmusic.co.uk.
