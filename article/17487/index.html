Ben Blue Waters

This is an archive of the band profile for Ben Blue Waters.

I've been playing music and gigging since the 70's, Sometimes in bands, sometimes solo, and various genres from R&B to punk to avant garde, but I've always maintained a deep love of the simple country blues of artists like Son House and Robert Johnson, and the urban blues of Howling Wolf, Muddy Waters and John Lee Hooker.

My first official show as a solo blues artist was in 2010 at Weymouth's annual Lupus Festival. Since then I have been gigging regularly, mainly around West Yorkshire, where I'm based. Looking for more gigs in this area and beyond..
