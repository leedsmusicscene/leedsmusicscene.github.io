Someday Maybe

This is an archive of the band profile for Someday Maybe.

Kieran Douglas - vocals / bass
Scott Mincher - guitar
Simon Gordon - drums

From early 2002 Someday Maybe has been on and off trying to write and progress in the music they know they are capable of making. Now Someday Maybe are generating enthusiasm and creativity in their songs Which are going to make people stand up and take note.

Someday Maybe have just come out of the studio and are really fulfilled with what they have created in there, their musical talent really shines through in "The Storm" and the very anthemic "The Perfect Scene".
