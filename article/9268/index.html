Pushbike Army to release their debut single on 14th April 2008

This article was published in 2008.

‘Four From Alexandra Grove’ is the debut single from Leeds punk rock 3-piece Pushbike Army. It is also the first release for new indie label A590 Records. Its B-sides include ‘Farewell To My Fascist Friend’ and an exclusive piano version of the title track. This debut release follows the success of the trio’s one and only demo to date ‘Sleeping In The Ditch’ which received the Demo Of The Week accolade from Steve Lamacq on BBC 6Music in February 2007. The single is released on 14th April 2008.
