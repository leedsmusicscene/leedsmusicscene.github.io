Soul Definition

This is an archive of the band profile for Soul Definition.

Soul Definition are a function band who specialise in performing covers from the Motown era alongside modern chart hits. Formed at Leeds College of Music the band built up a reputation playing in bars and pubs around Leeds City Centre, slowly expanding out to encompass more of the UK at functions and corporate events.

With a diverse repertoire of Soul, Blues and Popular Music, Soul Definition aspire to make your event unforgettable by tailoring their repertoire specifically to your needs - after all it's your party!

This female fronted band offers a versatile group of musicians with more than just the lead vocalist singing on various numbers. Each member of the band has years of session experience playing music in and around the UK for various other bands and solo artists.

Combining 3 part harmonies with infectious grooves, the pieces are arranged to allow multiple solos and prolonged enjoyment. Keen to provide entertainment and customer service that is second to none, Soul Definition try to portray the essence of 'fun' in function band by having you dancing and singing all night long..
