Third Scene, The

This is an archive of the band profile for Third Scene, The.

The Third Scene are a covers band that started at the end of 2006 / start of 2007 by Graham Solley from Batley and Chris Faruggia from Keighley. Both play guitar and have a love of popular British music from the 60's through to present. Along the way they picked up a great bass player called Matt Barbor from Dewsbury, an excellent solid drummer from Grassington by the name of Bill Mallinson and a great singer from Heckmondwike called Simon Moore (aka Simmi).

The band have performed locally around the Leeds and Bradford area playing The Gassienda club in Keighley to Dewsbury Moor Rugby league club and many in between.

Performing Mod classics to Britpop and some alternative British rock bands..
