The Hair to release their debut album in Japan

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band The Hair are set to release their debut album in Japan on December 5. The four piece will release “Indecisions” on Kurofune Records exclusively for the Japanese market after the success of their first two singles in the country.

The album will comprise of 12 tracks recorded by James Kenosha in Fraisthorpe near Bridlington. Tracks on the album include the singles “Ghosts” and “Disco/Retro”, live favourites such as “Left Foot Right Foot” and a number of new songs.

Both singles have already made a big impact in the clubs of Tokyo and beyond, with the band set to head east to tour the country in the new year.

The album comes after a successful year for the band, which saw “Ghosts” spend 3 three weeks in the Indie Chart top 20, the “Disco/ Retro” video played on the Main Stage at Leeds Festival, numerous festival performances and national airplay.

The Hair are supporting The Whip on November 16th at the Cockpit.
