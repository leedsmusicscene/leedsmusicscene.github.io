Laura Groves to release her debut single

This article was published in 2007.

Laura Groves has announced the release of her first single, "I am Leaving", which is to be released on 15th October on XL/Salvia Recordings. Released on 7" vinyl the single is limited to just 500 copies. Laura's single launch party will take place upstairs at The Beehive, Bradford on Saturday 13th October with support from Train Chronicles and Fran Rodgers.

Laura's projects include a contribution to the "50minutes" charity compilation (Excercise1 Records) alongside artists such as Daniel Johnston, Ladyfuzz and Emmy the Great. And her song "Coast" made an appearance on the Dance To The Radio compilation album "Something I Learned Today".
