I Like Trains announce DVD and CD double release

This article was published in 2011.

I Like Trains have this week announced the release of 'This Skin Full of Bones', a DVD & CD package release that marks the end of their 'He Who Saw the Deep' album campaign.

'This Skin Full of Bones' includes a DVD with videos of the band performing acoustic versions of 'A Father's Son', 'Sirens', 'Progress is a Snake' and 'Hope is Not Enough' at the Left Bank in Leeds.

It comes in a beautiful gate-fold sleeve and will be shipped in the first week of November.

The DVD also includes a trilogy of music videos produced by The Progress Film Company for 'He Who Saw the Deep'; 'A Father's Son', 'Sirens' and a brand new video for 'Sea of Regrets.

Accompanying the DVD is a CD that features a recording from the Left Bank session along with the B-sides from the 'He Who Saw the Deep' singles.

You can pre-order the package from the I Like Trains online shop.
