Slam Dunk Festival add 8 new names

This article was published in 2016.

Yellowcard, Cancer Bats and Mayday Parade will be joining Panic! At The Disco as organisers of Slam Dunk Festival last week announced 8 more bands for the festival in Leeds this coming May.

Bands added were:

YELLOWCARD // MAYDAY PARADE // HIT THE LIGHTS // ROAM // NORTHLANE // HACKTIVIST // CANCER BATS // NORMA JEAN

The additions follow the previous announcement in January.

Slam Dunk Festival is celebrating its tenth anniversary and takes place across Leeds city centre venues on Saturday 28th May 2016.
