Superelectric release album and DVD...

This article was published in 2004.

Superelectric have released a new album titled Nine Bar Blues, which is available from gigs and Sugalump (Corn Exchange) for just £5. The nine-track CD features previous single "I Like It Too Much", as well as "Old Photo", "Don't Know What I'm Doing Anymore", "Weeping Willow", "No More Fights", "Sing (your heart out for me)", "Second In Time", "Better Way" and "The Lonely One".

The band have also released a DVD, available for £3. The DVD includes two versions of "I Like It Too Much" and many extras, including photos, hidden tracks and more.

Superelectric next play Leeds on September 15th at The Vine.
