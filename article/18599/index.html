Pulled Apart by Horses to release new single 'Lizard Baby'

This article was published in 2014.

Pulled Apart by Horses have announced details of the next release to be taken from their hotly anticipated third album 'Blood'.

The single, 'Lizard Baby' is out on 1st September, which is the same date as the album release. You can listen to the track below:



A three minute hypnotic rock sleaze fest, 'Lizard Baby' is the follow up to 'Hot Squash' which received amazing responses, spent four weeks on the Radio 1 playlist, and caused the NME to proclaim "Pulled Apart By Horses are now a serious rock band".
