Tsienna

This is an archive of the band profile for Tsienna.

T.S.I.E.N.N.A are an electro indie dance band from Leeds, UK.

T.S.I.E.N.N.A have won rave reviews from their live gigs, where influences can be heard from as far wide as The Music, Soulwax, Primal Scream, Dreadzone, Sunshine Underground, Sigur Ros, Soulwax, Chemical Brothers, Faithless, Prodigy and more.

Following 2 UK tours & a brief deal with SoundPeople, T.S.I.E.N.N.A went alone and have recently worked with break beat DJ/producer Glen Nicholls (Future Funk Squad, Strangers) who has mixed Muse, UNKLE, Prodigy, Snow Patrol, Florence & The Machine and more.

T.S.I.E.N.N.A are available on all social media websites including Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, Soundcloud, Myspace, Reverbnation and more.

T.S.I.E.N.N.A's big live sound has seen them appear at venues alongside Sunshine Underground, Vessels, Utah Saints, The Courteeners, Reverand & The Makers, amongst others.

Singles, EP's, Remixes and more are available at gigs.
