Forever Cult announce new single release 'Antonio Banderas' on Monday 6th July

This article was published in 2015.

Leeds based, grunge-pop trio Forever Cult will be releasing their new single 'Antonio Banderas' in July, following on from the acclaimed single 'Winter's Glow' which is still available as a free download. With festivals including Leeds & Reading, Tramlines, Live At Leeds, Long Division & more under their belt, the band hit the road for their second UK tour this year.

Having received plaudits for their "flawless, exuberant performance" (Bearded Magazine), Forever Cult head out on the road for their first full headline tour which includes a single launch party at Leeds' now legendary Brudenell Social Club on 17th July.

As well as a number of impressive festivals appearances, Forever Cult have also played alongside Eagulls, Brawlers and DZ Deathrays, earning high-praise from the likes of CLASH Magazine, Drowned in Sound, Artrocker, BBC Introducing & Amazing Radio.

"Antonio Banderas" is released courtesy of Clue Records (Allusondrugs/NARCS) on Monday 6th July and will be free to the first 100 downloaders. It will also be made available on limited edition CD.

As well as their Leeds single launch party, the band will be making an appearance on 13th June at the Long Division Festival in Wakefield.
