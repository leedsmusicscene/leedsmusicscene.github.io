Nicola's Cage

This is an archive of the band profile for Nicola's Cage.

Nicola's Cage are a female fronted rock/metal/alternative 5 piece band from the West Yorkshire area. They are fast becoming popular in the West Yorkshire music scene with memorable songs ranging from the fast-paced metal anthem "Dead in an Hour" to the heart-felt "Eternal Spark".
Formed in Early 2009 the Cage began as four school friends messing about in a shed with beer and guitars... Chris showed up shortly after and Nicola's Cage was complete.
Summer 2009 was spent writing, practicing and partying and by Autumn three top-quality bedroom-recorded demos were ready.
Since January Nicola's Cage have been playing local gigs at every oppurtunity and are currently in and out of the studio recording the debut album..
