Dance to the Radio give away free Leeds Festival digital EP

This article was published in 2010.

Leeds independent record label Dance To The Radio have this week announced that they are giving away a free digital EP featuring tracks from all the bands playing the DTTR Stage at Leeds Festival 2010.

The EP includes an exclusive Chickenhawk track, 'Son Of Cern'.

Other bands featured on the EP are Get Cape. Wear Cape. Fly, Bear In Heaven, Spectrals and The Neat.

The EP is free and can be downloaded now from Dance To The Radio.

All bands will appear on the BBC Introducing Stage at the opening of the Leeds Festival on Thursday 26th August.
