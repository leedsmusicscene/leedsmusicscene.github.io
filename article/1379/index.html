Catylyst take coach to Sheffield for King Adora support

This article was published in 2002.

Catylyst have another couple of support slots to add to their already impressive list of Therapy?, Crackout, Utah Saints, Span, Spear of Destiny, Alkaline Trio and more: the band are supporting Britrock punk popsters King Adora at the Sheffield Barfly on Sunday 3rd November. The 'Faith No More meets Faithless' (Y.E.P, 7/2/02) foursome are putting a bus on which will be leaving Joseph's Well at around 5.30pm and returning back for 11.30pm. Return coach fare and ticket price is £13. Also on the bill are Little Nikita.

For more information, mail chris@catylyst.com or call 07092 306412. Catylyst also support Danko Jones at the Cockpit in Leeds on Wednesday 23rd October, tickets are selling fast at £5 from Jumbo and Crash. For a full list of the band's upcoming 22-date tour, plus news and downloads, check www.catylyst.com.
