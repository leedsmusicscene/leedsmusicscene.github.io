Steer

This is an archive of the band profile for Steer.

Steer are the collaboration of brothers Craig and Warren Steer. We formed in 2000 and have been busy writing and recording our music for the last three years. We currently have two albums recorded which we recorded totally at home.

We have recently been performing live in Leeds with Chris Sykes and Neil Gibson as our drum and bass section respectively.

Our music is a mix of heartfelt ballads and alternative rock, driven by meaningful lyrics and tight harmonies. MP3 audio clips from our debut album 'A Song Fir Gil' are available on our website, any feedback would be much appreciated.

The new website is still under construction with further MP3 files to add and information on the next album 'Loved and Lost'..
