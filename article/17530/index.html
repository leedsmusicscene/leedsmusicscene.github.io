Lucy Harriet Grace

This is an archive of the band profile for Lucy Harriet Grace.

Lucy Harriet is a Singer/Songwriter from Bradford. Her original style is built from a wide variety of sounds ranging from country and western to Brit Pop and blues. Citing everyone from Oasis to Brandi Carlile and Etta James to The Smiths as her influences, Lucy's style can only be described as unique. Her individualized covers range from Rihanna to Mungos Hifi and The Specials.

Lucy's EP containing all original songs is set to be released in 2013..
