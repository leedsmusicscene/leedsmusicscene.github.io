i concur

This is an archive of the band profile for i concur.

i concur are a Leeds-based guitar band built on a love of music Americana and delicate post-rock. Bands such as The National, Mogwai, Wilco and Low come high on their list of influences.

The band are Tim Hann (vocals/guitar), Toby Page (bass), Chris Woolford (guitar) and James Brunger (vocals/drums).

While Hann's lyrics touch on peculiar subject matters - from the unscrupulous mistakes of South Korean stem cell scientist Woo Suk Hwang (Decimal Places) to the barbaric extinction of the prehistoric crocodile (Exits Are Blockades) - their music takes intertwined guitar melodies and drives them into crescendos perhaps more familiar to instrumental post-rock enthusiasts than traditional listeners of alternative indie..
