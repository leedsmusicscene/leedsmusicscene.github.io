The Gullivers

This is an archive of the band profile for The Gullivers.

The Gullivers are a 6-piece Folk-Pop band from Wakefield, West Yorkshire. Formed in December of 2011, The group have been writing original music in hopes of breaking into the music scene with their eccentric and unique sound.

The Gullivers aim to re-ignite the music scene in wakefield with their tasteful music, and fill the gap left by the other bands in west yorkshire. The band are currently writting material and are in the process of recording their EP which will be released at their debut concert in April/May.

All the members of The Gullivers attend the same college course which lead to the formation of the band, which consists of front-woman & guitarist Brook Gulliver, 2nd vocals & guitar Sophie Folletti, pianist Helen Brownbridge, lead guitarist Robert Hemingway as well as bassist Beverley Brough & drummer Sam Holmes completing the line-up..
