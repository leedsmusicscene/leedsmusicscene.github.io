Voyeurs, The

This is an archive of the band profile for Voyeurs, The.

The Voyeurs are five men from Leeds who wish to play music and have a ball. To this purpose we intend to write, record, release and play live music to as many people that are willing to listen to us. We write songs about what it's like to be us and sometimes people can think it's them too. That's what makes people like us, like us. It may also be something to do with the hypnotic keyboards, the melodic basslines, funky beats and lots of guitars too..
