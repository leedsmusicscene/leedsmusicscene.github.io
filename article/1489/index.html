Jeepster to play special acoustic set at Homelands festival...

This article was published in 2003.

It has been announced today that Leeds' Jeepster will be playing a special acoustic set in the VIP tent at this years Homelands Festival, on Saturday 24th May. The band, who Sara Cox proclaimed to be "the best new band she's heard in ages" on Radio 1 this week following a show at London's Heavenly Social venue, are managed by Dave Beer and have been keeping leedsmusicscene.co.uk informed of their progress over the past six months. While there is no public news as yet, keep your eyes and ears open for announcements in the future.
