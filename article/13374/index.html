Jack's aTTic

This is an archive of the band profile for Jack's aTTic.

Joe Matthewman - Lead Vocals
Ryan Hughes - Lead Guitar/Backing vocals
Daniel Greaves - Rhythm Guitar/Backing Vocals
Jack Clayton - Drums & Percussion / Backing vocals
Dan Barber - Bass Guitar

Jack's Attic are a five-piece, West Yorkshire indie-rock band who have headlined sold-out shows and played festivals all across the UK.

They have supported some of the biggest names in indie rock - including the Sunshine Underground, Young Knives, Twisted Wheel, Steve Cradock (Ocean Colour Scene/Paul Weller) among others - on their respective tours and have an incredible live following to match their impressive catalogue of songs.

Having released two acclaimed EPs, Jack's Attic have been described as a great festival band with a sound that's modern yet retro, catchy yet melodic; and far out with a commercial appeal..
