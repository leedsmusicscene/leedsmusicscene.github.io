I Like Trains release the Beacons EP on October 15th

This article was published in 2012.

I Like Trains release the Beacons EP on October 15th through ILR and will be on tour from 13th October.

Visuals make a return to the I Like Trains show and they are launching a fan participation element in one of the songs. They plan to set a theme and fans will email photos within the theme which will then be added to the visuals.

The tour kicks off on Saturday 13th October at Leeds City Museum with support from Post War Glamour Girls and Sam Airey.
