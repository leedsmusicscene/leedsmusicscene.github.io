Worriedaboutsatan to release an EP on Birmingham label Iapetus Recordings

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds duo Worriedaboutsatan have revealed that they will release a new four track EP with Birmingham based label Iapetus Recordings later on in the year. The label is also the home of Leeds singer songwriter Paul Marshall, one half of Concentration Champ.

Paul features as one of the guest vocalists on the Worriedaboutsatan EP. Also contributing vocals is Jamie Sidebottom, one time Johnny Poindexter frontman and ambient tunesmith behind He Waits By The Tree.

A track from the EP, 'The Butterfly Effect', is also available on the On The Bone Records compilation, released on Monday, 12th February.
