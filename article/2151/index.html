The Bazaars

This is an archive of the band profile for The Bazaars.

The Bazaars are Jonny Pugh, Jonny Woolnough, Paul Brown, and Tom McGeorge.

Formed in 2003, The Bazaars have always delivered the one thing which sparked their existence and kept them going though changing and challenging times - songs.

Line-up changes may have altered the bands makeup over time but thankfully a full circle brings the linchpin maverick Jonny Pugh back into the fold and the addition of Paul Brown, drummer from ex-touring partners The Blueskins has The Bazaars buzzing again..
