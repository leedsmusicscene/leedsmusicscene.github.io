The Plight to release a mini album - 'Black Summer' - on 8th October 2007

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band The Plight will release a new mini album, the aptly titled 'Black Summer', through the Visible Noise record label on 8th October 2007.

Tracklisting for the album is 'Clarendon', 'Ball And Chain', 'Shadow Boxer', 'It Only Gets Worse', 'Lifestyle', and 'Pull The Trigger' and it promises to be ram packed with the band's trademark mash-up of old school riffs and infectious aggression. Album artwork comes from Visible Noise favourite Dan Mumford (Gallows, Architects, Johnny Truant).

Catch The Plight live at the Fenton on 19th October.
