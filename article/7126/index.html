Bilge Pump and Pifco release a split single on Run Of The Mill records

This article was published in 2006.

Leeds bands Bilge Pump and Pifco will release a split 7” single as part of Run Of The Mill Records' “They Do Them” series. Available from September 4th, this time around the series with feature both bands covering Jonathan Richman with Bilge Pump performing a rock'n'roll cover of “Government Centre” and Pifco opting for “She Cracked”.

For more details visit the Run Of The Mill website at www.runofthemillrecords.co.uk.
