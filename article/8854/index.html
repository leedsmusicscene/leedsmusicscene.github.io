Dirty Fakirs

This is an archive of the band profile for Dirty Fakirs.

Dirty Fakirs are a 6-piece from Hebden Bridge who have been bringing their unique style of super heavy northern grooves to audiences since August 2006. In this short space of time the band have already amassed a massive fan base in the local area and their songs currently available on MySpace for download, "Freaky Dreaming", "Party's Over, Rory", "Charles Bronson" and "Looking for a Fight" have been met with glowing feedback.
