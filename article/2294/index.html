The Small Note taken over by local music photographers...

This article was published in 2004.

The Small Note (formally The CD Centre) record shop on the corner of the Merrion Centre has allowed its window to be taken over by a collaboration of two local music photographers.

Ruth Stanley (Depix Music Photography) and Mat Dale are using the window space to display their work. The pictures are of both local and touring bands that have played in Leeds. The current collection includes Therapy?, Nashville Pussy, Pretty Girls Make Graves, Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, The Cribs, The Kills, Sweep the Leg Johnny, Lungfish, Les Savy Fav, The Band... Convinced and Blood Brothers.

The pictures will be changed regularly so you might see some familiar faces up there, and will also be available to purchase. So if there are any that take your fancy just enquire in the store. For more details contact info@depix.co.uk.
