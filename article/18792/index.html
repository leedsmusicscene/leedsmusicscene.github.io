Pulverise

This is an archive of the band profile for Pulverise.

Jojo - Vocals
Pat - Guitars
Daz - Bass
Butt - Drums

If you smoosh together elements of the hip-hop, rap, metal, hardcore music genres, an insane dreaded female screamer, nutty guitar riffmesiter, a most excellent bass basher and a psycho female drum monster...... You'll get Pulverise!

Pulverise are just over a year old and are based in Leeds/Bradford. With many influences from the metal and hip hop scenes and experience in many other bands their energy, passion and insanity is high! Fun is the aim with these guys, guaranteed to bring the party but most of all THE NOISE!

Influences are Biohazard, Rage Against The Machine, Slipknot, Stuck Mojo, Madball, Limp Bizkit, Vision of Disorder, Hatebreed, Meshuggah, (Hed)pe,Cypress Hill, Walls of Jericho & Pantera.

After beavering away in the studio for the past year, they're about to release a music video of their first single, are aiming to gig the Hell out of the UK and are working on finishing their EP..
