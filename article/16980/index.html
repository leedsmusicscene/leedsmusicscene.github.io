The Gonzos charity single goes on iTunes

This article was published in 2012.

After launching their charity single in aid of St. Gemmas cancer hospice on Friday (14th September), The Gonzos now have their single 'Insult to injury' available on iTunes, Amazon and most other distribution sites online. The record is also available, with a B-side of 'Sucking on a shotgun', from the band on hard copy from their gigs, crash records or jumbo records in Leeds. The single is online for only 79p while the single (with the B-side) is available for Â£2. Their will be donations made from the band as soon as a respectable amount of money is achieved for St. Gemmas cancer hospice. The hospice is a brilliant place that does great work for the victims of cancer so please donate generously and buy the record.
