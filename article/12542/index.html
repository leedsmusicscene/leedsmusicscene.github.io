Cut Out Shapes

This is an archive of the band profile for Cut Out Shapes.

Cut Out Shapes are singer/bassist Chris Ward and guitarist Chris Wood. They have produced one EP, two singles and one album since forming in November 2009.

The band have had radio support from BBC Introducing West Yorkshire, BBC Lancashire, BBC Radio Scotland, Amazing Radio and have played 3 sessions on BBC WY. The EP, singles and album have received great reviews from local press both online and in print.

The biggest gig the band have played so far was when they supported Chase & Status as main support at Bradford Universities Party on the Amp event in 2011.

After playing many gigs in the North of the UK and Scotland between 2010 and summer 2012 the band headed into Chairworks Studios with Simon Humphrey (The Clash, David Byrne) to finish work on their debut album.

The album was released in April 2014 to universal praise from online sources such as Gigslutz, Leeds Music Scene and the 24Our Music website based in Canada..
