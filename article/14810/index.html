GOLD

This is an archive of the band profile for GOLD.

GOLD are a four piece dream pop band hailing from Halifax, originally starting out as a two piece ambient outfit, they then took upon two friends to join their music making. They've been together for 6 months and in the time have created danceable grooves but still maintaining their dreamy ambience. At the moment they are perfecting songs before recording an EP which is yet to be named, although is expected to be completed around late spring. In the winter of 09/10 the two founding members wrote their first two ambient adventures (Echoes and Fantasy's Dream) after receiving a reverb pedal from a friend, and ever since have not looked back. They intend to make your knees sway and your troubles disappear in a dreamlike state, all they ask, is for you to listen, look and embrace..
