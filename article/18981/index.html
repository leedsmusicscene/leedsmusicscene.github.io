Second wave of bands announced for Live At Leeds 2015

This article was published in 2015.

Just one month after announcing the first 100 acts performing at Live at Leeds for 2015, organisers of the festival last week confirmed a further 50 bands ... and there's still more to come!

Joining the already-impressive line up are Lauren Aquilina, Raleigh Ritchie, Lucy Rose, The Strypes, Rhodes and many, many more ... full list of additions below:

Two exciting opening parties will take place on Friday 1st May. Both are separately ticketed, so you need to act fast if you want to attend.

Firstly, Pulled Apart by Horses will play their biggest hometown show yet at Leeds University [buy tickets] with support from Dinosaur Pile up and Allusondrugs. Secondly, over at the Brudenell Social Club, the party kicks off with Darlia, Sundara Karma and The Hearts [buy tickets].

The main event for Live At Leeds 2015 is Saturday 2nd May with over two hundred bands across twenty stages and many Leeds music venues, all accessed through a single wristband.
