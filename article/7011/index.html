Flux Capacitor

This is an archive of the band profile for Flux Capacitor.

Formed in late 2004, members of The Mingers, Sparks and Sicknote, Ninepoundnote, and Kissing Toast joined together through a mutual respect for good music and started writing. We currently have an EP recorded which is available to buy at any of our gigs and we have got some brand new tracks awaiting to be recorded with the new line up that we hope will show the true direction that Flux want to take. Enjoy..
