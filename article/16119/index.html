Compilation EP, 'Spectemur Agendo' out 5th March

This article was published in 2012.

'Spectemur Agendo' EP is released through Philophobia Music on 5th March 2012. The EP will feature 2 songs each by The Do's, Fur Blend and Clandestines and is released on a limited edition 3x mini CD package (100 copies) and digital download.

Tracks from the EP have already picked up plays from John Kennedy (xfm), Steve Lamacq (BBC 6music) and Alan Raw (BBC Introducing WY).
