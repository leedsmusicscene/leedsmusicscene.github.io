Jesus and his Judgemental Father

This is an archive of the band profile for Jesus and his Judgemental Father.

Danny - Vocals, Guitar
Danger - Guitar
Sof - Bass, Backing Vocals
Liz - Drums

Formed in October 2010 through a mutual love of pop punk, Jesus and his Judgemental Father first got to know each other through the Leeds' underground gay scene.

Frequenting gigs by the likes of the Delorean Drivers, Fanny DiWanko, Tiny Tin Lady and Amazon Molly, they bonded over their need for delicious hooks and sweet, sweet pop punk harmonies.

And so, Jesus was born..
