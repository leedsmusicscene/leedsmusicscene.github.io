The Chiara L's

This is an archive of the band profile for The Chiara L's.

Chiara Lucchini - vocals / guitar
Jonny Lee Hart - guitar / vocals
Tim Oldfield - bass
Dan Stretton - drums / vocals

The Chiara L's are a female fronted four piece guitar combo based in Leeds but with roots in Piecenza, Beeston, Churwell and Leicester.

The band were formed in June 2005 and performed their first gig in November 2005. They have since gigged regularly around Leeds taking in all the main venues, current highlights included supporting NME darlings The Organ @ Joseph's Well and performing @ Faversham with Lamacq favourites The Whip and The VC's. The band have impressed audiences with their mix of direct guitar sounds, infectious basslines, punchy rhythms and sweet vocals melodies..
