Cut Out Shapes album sampler now on Youtube

This article was published in 2014.

Cut Out Shapes have released a compilation video of all twelve tracks from their debut album to be released on the 21st of April 2014 via D.I. Records.



The album was recorded by Simon Humphrey (The Clash, David Byrne)at Chairworks Studios Castleford and Will Jackson (The Music) at Soundworks Studios Leeds between 2009-2013.

The band produced and mixed the album in their home studio during the summer of 2013.

The album was then mastered by Pete Norman (Public Image Ltd, Mansun) at Finyl Tweek in London in October of that year.

The album is the third official release on D.I. Records.The band will be taking part in an interview to promote the album on BCB radio on the 22nd of April with Michelle Dalgety on The Yorkshire Music Collective Show.

The album is initially limited to 100 CD copies in gatefold sleeves which you can purchase direct from the band on their website below.
