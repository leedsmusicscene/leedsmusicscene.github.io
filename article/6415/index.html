Corinne Bailey Rae at Number 2 in the UK Top 40...

This article was published in 2006.

Corinne Bailey Rae hit the UK Charts this Sunday (at number 2) with her second single "Put Your Records On".

The Leeds-based musician's single, released on Monday, followed her 2005 debut "Like A Star" and was playlisted across many of the UK's radio stations. She also appeared on Top Of The Pops in early February.

Corinne Bailey Rae self-titled debut album is released tomorrow (Monday, 27th February).
