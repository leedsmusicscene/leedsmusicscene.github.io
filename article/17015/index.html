Debut EP from William Stead released for free

This article was published in 2012.

My debut EP - "St. Christopher Protects Us EP" - has been released for free on bandcamp.

Give my tunes a listen, and then maybe download of even donate! Then you can share on facebook or tweet about it! I hope you enjoy, just follow the link!

The EP includes the songs St. Christopher, The History, One Last Dance and Fade Away.
