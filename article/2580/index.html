0800 Ragu

This is an archive of the band profile for 0800 Ragu.

0800 Ragu are a four piece hailing from Wakefield. With influences including Alkaline Trio, Ash, Green Day and Weezer, the overall result is post-punk with melodic overtones (or "punk-pop" if you will.) With regular ventures to venues in and around West Yorkshire, the band now look to spread their name further. Look out for upcoming gigs and additions to the existing two demos..
