iLiKETRAiNS to release a new single and album in the summer

This article was published in 2006.

iLiKETRAiNS have this week announced that the follow up single to their "A ROOK HOUSE FOR BOBBY" CD will be "TERRA NOVA".

Scheduled for release on the Fierce Panda record label on May 29th, "TERRA NOVA" will be released as a limited edition (1,000 copies) 7" Vinyl. The B-Side will be "NO MiLiTARY PARADE".

The single will be followed by the band's debut album, titled "PROGRESS, REFORM" (also on Fierce Panda) in late June.

iLiKETRAiNS play at Leeds' Brudenell Social Club on April 27th, with support from Bikini Atoll. The gig is the first of a monthly SiGNAL FAiLURE event organised by the band at the venue.
