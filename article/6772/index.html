Dance Stage line up announced for Leeds Festival 2006...

This article was published in 2006.

With the Main Stage and Radio One / NME Stage line-ups now pretty much confirmed and only a very limited amount of day tickets available for both sites, Leeds Festival organisers Mean Fiddler have this week announced the line up for the Dance Stage.

The Dance Stage is for one night only this year, and for Leeds this means Friday 25th August when the stage will be taken over by the clubbing phenomenon that is Radio Soulwax.

Headlining on the live front for Radio Soulwax fittingly are Soulwax, who will perform a bespoke Nite Versions set, and joining the live section of the Radio Soulwax evening of dance genius is Vitalic.

Before, between and beyond the two live sets will be 2 Many DJs and Erol Alkan (the creator of the infamous Trash Club in London).

Also appearing on the Dance Stage at Leeds 2006 will be Coldcut, Lady Sovereign, Sway, Spank Rock, Shitdisco, and Superthriller.

As always, check www.leedsmusicfestival.com for the latest line up and ticket info.
