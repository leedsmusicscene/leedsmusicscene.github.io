The Big Blue

This is an archive of the band profile for The Big Blue.

Richard Wheater (Vocals / Harmonica)
Allan Stelmach (Guitar / Vocals)
Mark Kingston (Keyboards / Accordian)
Charlotte Nicol (Drums / Percussion / Viola)
Richard Larder (Trumpet / Cornet / Flugel Horn)
Pete Hill (Bass)

The Big Blue has had several incarnations over the years. After the original guitar band line-up split, Richard and Allan continued to write and play the occasional gig as a two-piece acoustic act. They met Mark in 2003, forming the basis of the band as it is today.

With the addition of brass and strings, the band's songs have been given the life they deserve. Their first EP was released early 2005, and momentum is building..
