Vessels release 'The Sky Was Pink' and announce UK tour

This article was published in 2012.

Vessels have returned with a new single in the form of a cover of Nathan Fake's 'The Sky Was Pink', which you can listen to below:



Vessels have been writing new music this year that will mark a significant change in direction and this autumn they will head out to roadtest their first new material since 2011's Helioscope

The band will appear at Wharf Chambers in Leeds on Friday 23rd November with support from Runners and People In Jars.

In other news, Vessels' Lee J. Malcolm has a new single on EPM, including a Vessels remix out now under the moniker 'Lee J. Malcolm presents Terrestrial'. A album will follow in November.
