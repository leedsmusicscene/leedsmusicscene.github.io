Mother Vulpine to release their debut single in May

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band Mother Vulpine return home from their national tour with Eagles of Death Metal and have announced the release of their debut double A-side single. Set for release on 16th May through the local On The Bone Records label, the single will include the songs Keep Your Wits and For A Friend. The release will be available on limited-edition (500 copies) 7" green vinyl and on Digital Download formats.

Catch Mother Vulpine live in Leeds when they play the Dance To The Radio compilation launch event at the Cockpit on Good Friday (6th April). The band are due on-stage at 3.30pm.
