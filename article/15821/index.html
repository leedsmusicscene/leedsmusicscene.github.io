Joseph & David to release EP on Hide & Seek label

This article was published in 2011.

The Hide & Seek label have announced details of the second EP from talented Leeds duo Joseph & David.

David Henshaw and Joseph Lawrenson have been writing music together for two years and are joined live by a host of friends who provide additional piano, guitar, vocals, accordion, drums and violin to reconstruct the full, powerful sounds of their records.

The release will be a five track EP entitled 'Rise Up The Sun'. It was recorded, produced and mixed by James Kenosha (Pulled Apart By Horses/Dry the River) in Bridlington during the summer of 2011, and mastered in Leeds by Tom Woodhead (Forward, Russia).

The 'Rise Up The Sun' EP will be available via CD, with artwork hand drawn by David, and download from 19th December.

To launch the EP, Joseph & David will be appearing live at the Holy Trinity Church in Leeds with support from Beccy Owen and Jonathan Woodhead.
