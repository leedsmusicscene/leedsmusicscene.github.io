Thousands expected to watch Leeds bands for Breeze On Tour

This article was published in 2008.

A number of young Leeds bands will appear across the city this summer as part of the Leeds City Council Breeze events.

Breeze On Tour, a large-scale event for under 19s, tours Leeds parks putting on sports and arts activities to over 16,000 people. There is a performance zone for young people with talent to display their work and young local bands are provided with an excellent way to gain experience and raise their profile.

Bands confirmed as playing include I Call Shotgun, The Phubus, Left Right Goodnight, Inner Sense, Zero Blessing, Saving Time and Behaviour Order.

For more info on Breeze On Tour, including full dates and venues, go to www.breezeleeds.org/breezeontour.
