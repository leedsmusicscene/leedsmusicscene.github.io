Steph Stephenson

This is an archive of the band profile for Steph Stephenson.

Steph started writing songs when she picked up her sisters Fender Tele. It was on her 15th Birthday when she received an acoustic guitar of her very own. 8 years and a few hundred songs later Steph continues to share her songs with audiences in West Yorkshire and beyond. 2011 proves to be exciting, Steph supported Ellen & The Escapades at the Holmfirth Folk Festival in May and she has just released her new EP "The Girl Who Has Everything" which can be listened to and downloaded to on bandcamp..
