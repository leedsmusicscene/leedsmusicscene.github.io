Richard Bower

This is an archive of the band profile for Richard Bower.

Here you'll find sounds with the feel of Exile on Main Street if those late night basement recording sessions had actually been done on one microphone in Keith's spare bedroom. You'll find lyrics that love and explore Yorkshire's sometimes harsh, wild landscape then twist and burn those scenes in time into something much more personal. You'll find songs that dream of being off the grid but are shackled by paranoia and doubt.

A distinctive voice that once opened up, rips through the words until there's nothing left. With the enduring influence of troubadour singers, songwriters and bands such as; The Tallest Man on Earth, Bob Dylan, Laura Marling, Fleet Foxes, First Aid Kit, Bruce Springsteen, Ben Howard and many more Richard is pushing hard to make the break from his home and venture further and further out in to the world..
