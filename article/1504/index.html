Attention Hyde Park bands: apply to play Unit Day Festival 2003...

This article was published in 2003.

Attention all Hyde Park bands! Now is the time to apply to play the Main Stage at Unity Day 2003 - a free festival held annually on Woodhouse Moor. This year's festival will take place on Saturday 2nd August and applications to play are welcomed from bands (of any genre) resident near the Hyde Park, LS6 area...

Applications need to be completed and sent in with demos, to be received no later than Friday, 6th June. You can pick up an application form at Joseph's Well and around venues and shops in the Hyde Park area.

In addition, organiser are looking for assistance with fundraising and the day in general (volunteers needed for main stage and stewarding). Visit www.unityday.freeserve.co.uk for more details.
