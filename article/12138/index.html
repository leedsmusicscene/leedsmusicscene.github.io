The Heaviside

This is an archive of the band profile for The Heaviside.

Formed in 2009, The Heaviside are a Leeds based band, the six members came together to make music that they wanted to hear in the UK Hip-Hop scene. Formed from other well established bands and projects within Leeds, the sound and popularity of The Heaviside has been growing from strength to strength.

Based in Hiphop, and influenced by a large wealth of artists from varied genres. All instrumentals are performed entirely live and sample free, The Heaviside's music is diverse and hard hitting.

With Anokh and Nathan on vocals, Joe on bass, Ed on guitar, Rajesh on percussion and Rob on drumkit, this tribe of musicians create high energy, live shows. Their performances are guaranteed to impress and will definitely be memorable.

Insightful lyrics, heavy beats, impressive playing and intense live shows. The Heaviside are a new take on "Live Hiphop", with their debut EP "What Lies Above?" scheduled for April 2010, be sure to check out this new sound..
