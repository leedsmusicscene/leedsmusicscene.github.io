Glass Caves release new single 'Swim' on 7/4 (updated release date)

This article was published in 2017.

Glass Caves release the wonderful 'Swim' on 7/4 as follow-up single to 'Do you have a name'. 'Swim' is one of a brace of fine new tunes suggesting a departure from the more frenetic 'indie rock' vibe of the first album, towards a fuller, more mature and accomplished sound, whilst retaining the passion, ambition and optimism of perennial favourites such as 'This Road' or 'Breaking Out'. 'Swim' is anthemic, as many of Glass Caves' songs are, with infectious chant and Matt Hallas' laid back vocal delivery, ascending throughout and reaching a tumultuous climax that ends so timely, so cleverly, that you just need to spin it again!

Catch the band live at The Wardrobe on April 22nd.
