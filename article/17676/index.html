Baltimore Gun Club

This is an archive of the band profile for Baltimore Gun Club.

In 2011, producer J Fowkes (aka DJ Zemion) met guitarist Geoff Smith. From this moment on, a musical bromance blossomed, resulting in the formation of Baltimore Gun Club in 2012.
Having both worked in the music industry for a number of years, the aim with BGC was simple; get out of your comfort zone and start making music for the sheer fun of it again.
Most of the time, the result is a mess of rock guitar, screeching dance leads, trip-hop beats and atmospheric effects that is tons of fun to play. Thankfully for us, the very talented Dave Sattar (live drums) and Steve Faulkner (live vocals) seemed to enjoy playing it too, so more often than not you'll find them onstage as part of the BGC line-up as well.
We hope you enjoy listening to it as much as we enjoy making it....
