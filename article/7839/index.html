Unexploded Shells release "Made In The Same City As You"

This article was published in 2007.

Leeds band Unexploded Shells have today revealed that their second CD, “Made In The Same City As You” is available to buy now from Radish, Jumbo Records and Crash Records. As well as the title track, the single is backed with “A Room" and "Brush Against My Arm“.

Alternatively, the CD is available to purchase on mail order from the band’s website for £3 (or £5 if purchased along with a copy of their first EP).

Catch Unexploded Shells live at their first gig of 2007 when they appear at Primrose on Saturday 3rd February.
