Last Temple Orchestra to release their debut EP

This article was published in 2009.

Last Temple Orchestra have announced that their debut EP will be available early Spring 2009 through Ivolv distribution. The self-titled release will be available worldwide as a Digital Download from all major outlets.

The band will be appearing live throughout the coming months with gigs confirmed at The Cardigan Arms (March 6th) and The Fox & Newt (April 3rd).

You can preview the first single, 'Inside', and the rest of the EP online at the band's MySpace page.
