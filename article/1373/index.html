Xi to add more tracks to CD give away

This article was published in 2002.

With a few home-town gigs and a tour to come before Christmas, Xi are set to complete their busiest year so far. The 2K2 demo tracks continue to be well recieved, and will continue to be available free at all Xi gigs, or in mp3 format via www.xi-uk.com.

Not wishing to lose momentum, the band are already working on three new tracks which will be added to the tracks on the gig night give-away CD's. The band play live in Leeds next on Wednesday, 16th October at the Bassment.
