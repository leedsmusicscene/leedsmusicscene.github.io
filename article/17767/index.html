Brett Lee To Release Debut E.P "For The Taking" Sat 17th August

This article was published in 2013.

Brett Lee is Set to Release his Debut E.P "For The Taking".

After The Succesful release of his Debut Solo Single "Mistakes" in June, Airplayed by BBC Introducing and the release of his new music video - its onwards and upwards for this new Solo Artist. After the break up of his band Northern Torch, Brett Lee has bounced back with his own Style of music and after a successful period of writing, hes back with his Debut E.P " For The Taking" Recorded at DPS Studios produced by Liam Stokes (Elmaroe Production)

This Successful time in the studios was summed up by Brett Lee who said "Me and Liam Stokes have very similar styles when it comes to writing music, he really understands the honesty in my music and how i would like it to be portrayed, its beens a joy to work on the E.P with him and i am more then happy with the outcome."
The result from this time in the studio is a E.P packed with honest lyrics, style and individuality, a very old school way of writing music with emotion, feeling and meaning, yet brought upto date with a strong depth in vocals and very catchy rhythms, vocal lines and lyrics that will be stuck in the listeners head for years to come,
Brett Lee has has stuck to his guns in believing in writing songs that not only people can relate to and enjoy music wise but a story behind each track that people can relate with and connect emotionally with.
"For The Taking" Will be Released via major Digital Stores, I Tunes, Spotify and Bandcamp on Saturday 17th of August. To Celebrate the release of the E.P, Brett Lee' will be having an E.P Lauch Event at 360 Club - The Library Pub - Leeds on the same day.
