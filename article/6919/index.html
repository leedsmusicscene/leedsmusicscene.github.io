Wilful Missing

This is an archive of the band profile for Wilful Missing.

We are a 5-piece band from Bradford. The first of our music to get an official release was the Vast Atlantic EP in 2009, although we had been gigging for a couple of years by then, and had released a home-made CD of demos in 2007. The album, Molehills out of Mountains, was released in December 2011.

In 2010 we had our tunes played by Steve Lamacq on both Radio 2 and 6Music, and we have performed a couple of live acoustic sessions for BBC Radio Leeds. Our love-in with the BBC continued in 2011 as 4 of our songs featured in Waterloo Road on BBC Television.

Live, we have supported The Duke & The King, Jeffrey Lewis, The Wave Pictures, Neil McSweeney and The Miserable Rich..
