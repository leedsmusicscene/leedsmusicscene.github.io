Eagulls to release split 7" with Mazes

This article was published in 2011.

Eagulls have this week announced the release of a limited-edition split 7" single with Mazes. The single will be released on 24th October 2011 via the Italian Beach Babes Records label and will be limited to 500 copies on vinyl, with digital copies available through iTunes.

The tracklisting for the single is:

A1 Mazes: Farewell Summer
A2 Mazes: Messenger (Wipers cover)

AA1 Eagulls: Possessed
AA2 Eagulls: Mystery (Wipers cover)

You can pre-order the single now from the Italian Beach Babes shop.
