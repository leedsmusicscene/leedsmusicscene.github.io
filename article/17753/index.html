King No-One win Centre Stage 2013 to play Leeds Festival

This article was published in 2013.

King No-One were this week declared the winner of Centre Stage 2013 and in so doing booked their place on the line up for the annual Leeds Festival this coming August bank holiday weekend.

Centre Stage 2013 heats took place in April 2013, from which 11 finalists were confirmed for the Centre Stage final.

The final took place at the O2 Academy Leeds on Wednesday night.

Each of the bands that took part in the event have been fundraising towards Martin House Children's Hospice, who cares for children and young adults, with life limiting illnesses, and also for their families. A perfect example of young people helping young people.

Congratulations to King No-One and good luck at at Leeds Festival next month.
