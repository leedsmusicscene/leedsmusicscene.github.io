King Kananga

This is an archive of the band profile for King Kananga.

Dirty, Driven, Hook filled music, King Kananga play an alternative mix of Rock, Punk, Grunge and Metal influences.

Mid summer 2009 recorded and produced their own EP, released digitally in all forms Sept 09:

- Stalling
- Eugene
- PenosVfu

Jan 2010 finished recording a short cut:

- Murderer
- PenosVfu
- On My Shoulders

CD artwork, recording, mastering and anything else was done by King Kananga and Ed Dickinson.

It is available to anyone who wants to hear it, check the myspace -

http://www.myspace.com/kingkananga

CD's available at Gigs

looking to launch the website early 2010 and continue recording and putting out our tracks.

Some reviews some people wrote:

King Kananga - "Three piece grungy number 'King Kananga' from Huddersfield opened the night with an awesome developed set. A raspy-voiced front-man delivered hook filled choruses and a confident performance. Edgy rhythm changes, hard hitting yet quirky riffs and driving drums gave the band a slight Queens of The Stoneage feel. A truly professional band, even a string repairing minute was filled with some drum and bass jamming"
From Surface Unsigned Festival

"King Kananga - Although new to the rock scene , King Kananga are quickly making a name for themselves, blending Grunge, Punk, Metal and straight ahead rock, they are worthy of the hype."
From the Parish Venue.
