¡Forward, Russia! to release a double A-side single in July on White Heat Records...

This article was published in 2005.

Following the sell out of the band's debut release, a split single with This Et Al, Leeds four-piece ¡Forward, Russia! will release a double A-side single - "Thirteen" / "Fourteen" - on White Heat Records on 25th July. The single comes in two formats, 7" and CD with 1,000 and 300 copies available respectively. It will also be available through iTunes.

For more details about the release, visit www.whiteheatmayfair.com

Prior to the release ¡Forward, Russia! will perform a live session from the legendary Maida Vale studios for Lamacq Live on Radio 1 (4th July), with a John Kennedy XFM session expected later that month.

Finally, in other news, the band can be seen live at Truck, Leeds and Reading Festivals and at NastyFest and they also play a Transmission event at the Packhorse on July 15th.
