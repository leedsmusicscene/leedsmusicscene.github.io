NotNow

This is an archive of the band profile for NotNow.

An exciting new Alternative Rock band from Halifax.

They've previously played such venues as The Cockpit Leeds, The Parish and HudLive to a huge crowd with DJ Fresh and BBC Radio 1's Huw Stephens

They have had songs played on BBC Radio and have signed with Music publisher "Maori Music Publishing" they have just released their second EP "After The High" which is available for purchase at any NotNow gig.
