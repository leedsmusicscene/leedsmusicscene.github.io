Jack's aTTic release their debut single on Dead Sober Records

This article was published in 2011.

Dead Sober Records has this week announced the release of the debut single from Jack's aTTic.

Titled 'Playing Cards and Special Brew' the single was recorded at 2fly studios with Alan Smyth (Arctic Monkeys, Richard Hawley).

The two-track release is backed with 'Island Living'.

Dead Sober Records specialises in under-21 bands and are "proud to release another of the region's freshest, bright young bands".

Jack's Attic are a five piece mellow psychedelic indie rock band from Pontefract and recently headlined a sold-out show at the Cockpit in Leeds.

You can buy the single now from iTunes.
