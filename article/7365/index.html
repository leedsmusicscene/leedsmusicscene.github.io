New internet radio show targets the Leeds music scene

This article was published in 2006.

Based in Glasgow, Radio Magnetic has long been at the forefront of new media and new music and has this week announced a new monthly on-demand show specialising in Leeds’ local music and clubbing scene.

Titled “Odylic Force“ and recorded in Leeds by local DJ John Macbeth, the show presents a relaxed insight to the local scene and features promos, live gig recordings and guest mixes from Leeds-based artists and events. Listeners to the first show can expect a 40 minute mix of top electroclash tunes; a selection of unreleased and independent tracks; and a recording live from Unity Day in Hyde Park.

Future shows promise recordings from clubs such as Northern Light, Mint and Wire and will showcase music from 20:20 Soundsystem, The Scratch Technician and many other local acts.
