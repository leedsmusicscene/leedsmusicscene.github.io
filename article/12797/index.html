Cor Amor

This is an archive of the band profile for Cor Amor.

Hello, we're Cor Amor. A band devoted to creating music to entertain! Our music is for you: for you to enjoy, for you to come to our shows to have an awesome time! We are a band with a huge live sound combined with bright vocals bringing you a fusion of sound perfect to shake your ass to, to nod your head to, or if it suits you, to throw your body into a pit to!

We're currently self-producing our debut album at Kiwi Studios, whilst continuously uploading previews for you - with the aim of playing as many shows as possible, in as many different places as possible, showcasing this music. This is our passion - so support us, and give yourself a good night by getting yourselves down to a show and having a party!
