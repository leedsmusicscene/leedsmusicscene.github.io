Fifth Goodbye announce plans for an album release...

This article was published in 2003.

Fifth Goodbye have announced details of their forthcoming album, to be called Revenge for Broken Ribs. The release will include the tracks Bandstand and This is my Impression taken from their recent EP, as well as seven additional tracks that are still to be recorded. The band play Joseph's Well on March 8th.
