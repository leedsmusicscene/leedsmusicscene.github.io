Mercia Drift

This is an archive of the band profile for Mercia Drift.

MERCIA Drift were formed in the city of Wakefield/Leeds. They are a band with a unique sound, that produce stadium sized anthems of euphoria that take the listener to the next level. MERCIA Drift have played numerous venues including Manchester Academy 2 & 3, The Cavern Liverpool, Barrowlands 2 in Glasgow to name a few and many other venues around the country. "All I Want To Be Is All I want To Be" provides a convincing mix of stadium sized sounds, smart keyboards and jangling, strutting guitars which all underpin the exuberant voice of "Noz" - Manchester Music Scene Review!.
