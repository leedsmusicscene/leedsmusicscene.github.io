Geoff Watson

This is an archive of the band profile for Geoff Watson.

Geoff Watson (The Hebden Bridge 'Hurricane') A great singer / songwriter / guitarist producing highly original guitar sounds in the acoustic power-rock and contemporary range, creating a solid foundation of his own original songs. It has been said that hearing Geoff is like 'being swept away by a hurricane' such is the intense energy of his presence and style. So with new aptly name CD album "The Hurricane Effect" available on itunes or online at: www.geoffwatson.com www.myspace.com\GeoffWatsonhome.
