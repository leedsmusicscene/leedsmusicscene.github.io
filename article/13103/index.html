Pre-order the next Printed Circuit LP

This article was published in 2010.

Printed Circuit have announced that they are aiming to raise enough money to press 300 copies of a limited edition coloured vinyl LP with new and exclusive material.

There are currently no vinyl LP releases by Printed Circuit and plenty of new songs without a home to go to.

You can make a pledge here and pledging gives you the option to be credited in the sleeve or receive a signed copy.

If Printed Circuit make any additional cash they will also release a 7" or 10" (depending on the amount) as a sister release.

Pledging towards the cost of making album involves entering your card details and choosing your goodies - a copy of the LP, a download, t-shirt, tape or something else.

Your card will not be charged unless the project hits the 100% target.
