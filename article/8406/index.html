Highflyer

This is an archive of the band profile for Highflyer.

Highflyer are an original 4-piece indie/funk outfit based in Leeds. All 4 are music students at Leeds University and met through their course. Their sound has been described as similar to 'Athlete', 'Muse' and 'the Dears' and features dual lead vocals, with backing vocals.

Since forming, the band have played an impressive number of gigs, including packed-out dates at the Cavern in Liverpool, the Cockpit in Leeds, the Anson Rooms 2 in Bristol, and the Grand in Clapham. .
