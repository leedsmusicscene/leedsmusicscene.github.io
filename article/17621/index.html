Dinosaur Pile-up unveil new music video for single 'Derail'

This article was published in 2013.

Dinosaur Pile-up have unveiled the music video for their new single 'Derail', which comes out on Sunday 16th June.



Inspired by two of the most iconic Hollywood directors of all time, the latest Dinosaur Pile-up video is part Tarantino and part Hitchcock with a hint of kitsch.

'Derail' is released the day before the band's new album, 'Nature Nurture', which hits the shops on Monday 16th June 2013. The album was recorded by Matt Bigland at Courtyard Studios with Ian Davenport (Band Of Skulls).

Dinosaur Pile-up have been confirmed for the Crooked Ways festival this summer and will appear at the event in Pontefract on Saturday 1st June 2013.
