Lasse Brawn

This is an archive of the band profile for Lasse Brawn.

Lasse Brawn were born in the dysfunctional mind of lead mouth Scott Lewis for over a year. Recruits were... er... recruited & off they popped.

Their sound is a raw, visceral & passionate attack on certain senses. A sort of punk/alternative twist on East Seventeen.

Three months into things they knew they had reached a high point when a bottle of tomato ketchup was hurled at them from a passing Skoda Octavia. Not your average drive-by.

They are the sound of your divorced parents masturbating.

Cave in & commit to their cause!.
