Redemtion

This is an archive of the band profile for Redemtion.

Established during the Easter of 2008 Redemtion have gathered a loyal fan base across the north of England and played with some amazing bands. making new friends in different bands Redemtion make the most of playing music and loving life! there is always a party on after a Redemtion Gig and everyone is usually invited! 2010 sees a new line up for Redemtion, another studio session booked and a hand full of shows already booked! a busy year but one to keep an eye on!.
