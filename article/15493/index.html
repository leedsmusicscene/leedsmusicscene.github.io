The Come Down

This is an archive of the band profile for The Come Down.

Dominic Healey
Anthony Dorgan
Luke Doran

A three piece punk rock band started in late 2010, hailing from Bradford & Middlesbrough, The Come Down includes Dominic Healey on guitar & vocals/Anthony Dorgan on bass & vocals/Luke Doran on drums..
