The Roman Empire

This is an archive of the band profile for The Roman Empire.

The Roman Empire are based in Leeds and have been invading hearts and minds for the last 3 years, conquering all they find with three acoustic guitars and vocals.

Most of our lyrics involve subjects that we feel you need to know more about: tattoos, cavemen, zombies, pies etc (a forthcoming song about a zombie caveman with tattoos, eating a pie is always a possibility) and whilst we intend that the songs are light-hearted, they usually turn out either sad or violent (although all wrapped up in a jaunty style). After a successful 2005, with gigs and a new single "Commander Alan Shepard", we are now writing new material for our 2nd album, hopefully being recorded in the summer. By the way, if you'd like to know more and hear some tunes, have a look at our website or email us - we are sometimes friendly and always reply to everyone..
