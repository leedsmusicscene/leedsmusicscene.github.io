Hadouken! to release a new single and album in January 2010

This article was published in 2009.

Hadouken! have this week unveiled the video for their new single 'Turn The Lights Out', which is scheduled for release on 18th January 2010.

The single will include remixes by Spor and Kitch'n'Sync and will be followed by the band’s second album on January 25th. The album is titled 'For The Masses' and can be pre-ordered now via the band’s website. All albums pre-ordered through the official store will be signed by the band and if you order before December 6th your name will also be included on an album thanks list on a bonus artwork poster included with the album.

The tracklisting for the album is Rebirth, Turn The Lights Out, M.A.D, Evil, House is Falling, Mic Check, Ugly, Bombshock, Play The Night, and Lost.
