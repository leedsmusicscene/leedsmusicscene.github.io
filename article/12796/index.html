Natural Impulse

This is an archive of the band profile for Natural Impulse.

Natural Impulse are great four piece band consisting of two guitarists with backing vocals, percussion with backing vocals, fronted by female vocalist.

They have a unique knack of stripping classic covers down to basics and adding their own influence to the song, creating a very laid back feel to their performance. The style of music is best described as smooth, cool with hints of jazz, blues, soul, rock and pop. Along with other classic acoustic sounding artists such as, Dusty Springfield, Alison Krauss and Minnie Ripperton and other up beat artists such as Tina Turner, Doobie Brothers, Blondie, The Buzzcocks and The Eurhythmics, the band create a real ambient atmosphere and are suitable for any type of venue but are ideally suited for weddings, corporate functions and private functions/parties..
