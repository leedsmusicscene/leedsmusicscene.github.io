Send More Paramedics looking for Leeds' zombified youth to take part in video shoot...

This article was published in 2004.

Leeds' zombie-core band Send More Paramedics will be shooting a video for their forthcoming single Zombie Crew on Sunday 5th September at Joseph's Well, and are looking for video extras!

In particular, they inform us that they are “hoping to gather together a bunch of zombified hardcore kids to make up the crowd that will appear in the video”. The theme of the video being “hardcore / metal zombie kids hanging out at a Send More Paramedics show”. Simple.

If you would like to take part, and can get to Joseph's Well for noon (4pm finish) then drop an e-mail to video@sendmoreparamedics.com.

As an incentive, the band are also offering a free shirt, CD or vinyl to the top 5 zombie make-overs!
