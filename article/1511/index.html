Royal Park Cellars secure national music magazine showcase...

This article was published in 2003.

LOGO have chosen the Royal Park Cellars for their first Northern showcase in their campaign to bring the magazine, already claiming a circulation larger than NME in the South, to Northern audiences.

Titled "A Girls Night Out" the show on Friday 20th June will feature four female, or female fronted, acts that have appeared in LOGO's pages.

Headlined by Leeds' own gentle rockers Bodixa, the show will also feature bands Fleeing New York, Bruise and solo acoustic artist Rachel Dadd.

Steve Kind, of Panama Promotions said: "We're really excited to have been asked by LOGO to stage this show and feel that it is a vindication of all the work the Panama team have put in to turn the Royal Park Cellars into a professional standard venue. We look forward to future collaborations with LOGO and Uglyman Media".
