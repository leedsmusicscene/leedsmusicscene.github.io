Let's Rock Leeds! The retro festival returns in June 2016

This article was published in 2015.

Following another great event this summer, we are delighted to confirm that Let's Rock Leeds! will return for a third year to Temple Newsam on Saturday 18th June 2016. With a fabulous line‐up of artists to bring you the very best of the 80s and an action packed day of general 80s madness, there is plenty to do for everyone at this Family Friendly Festival.
The following acts, are just a handful of the artist announced for the 2016 festival:

SOUL II SOUL, JIMMY SOMERVILLE, CHINA CRISIS, JOHNNY HATES JAZZ and TOYAH

Let's Rock Leeds! offers the perfect day out for all the family, including circus workshops with face painting, crafts, tricycles and the "Disco Shack"! There is a super funfair as well as children's entertainers. This year there will be multiple bars, plus a wide range of quality food outlets selling food from around the world.

VIP tickets allow access to our funky 80s themed VIP enclosure with comfy chill out zone or enjoy the outdoor seating area, including a full bar serving quality brands and 80s cocktails, plus of course the all important VIP luxury loos - essential at any festival! VIP tickets include entry to the festival plus a souvenir programme, meal and welcome drink.

For further details and to book tickets visit:
www.letsrockleeds.com
