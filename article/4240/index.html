Loqui to release their debut album on October 3rd...

This article was published in 2005.

Loqui are to release their debut album “I Can’t Believe It’s Not Better” on Monday 3rd October. The album represents the compilation, and in many cases completion, of much of the band’s work over the past 7 years, both in their current form and as their previous incarnation, Vertigo Green. Pre-release copies will be available at the bands forthcoming gigs at the Love Apple, Bradford (30th September) and the Mixing Tin, Leeds (1st October).
