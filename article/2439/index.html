Huddersfield talent wanted for a forthcoming compilation CD release...

This article was published in 2004.

A newly formed Community Music Group is on the look out for Huddersfield talent for a forthcoming CD release.

Timeless Music Project follows on from last year’s successful Timeless Music Festival, held in Ravensknowle Park, Huddersfield, which treated the local music scene to such talent as The Selecter, The Freestylers, The Bays, Kava Kava, Fingathing etc alongside showcases for local talent. The festival was delivered by Chocolate Fireguard in association with DRAM and the Young Peoples Service.

The Huddersfield compilation will be ready for the beginning of May and sold initially via the internet and in outlets throughout Yorkshire. Additional promotional copies will sent out to music industry contacts with the aim of generating interest for Huddersfield’s music scene.

Material should be sent in to:
