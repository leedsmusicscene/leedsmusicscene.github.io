Post War Glamour Girls reveal video for 'Tremor'

This article was published in 2012.

Post War Glamour Girls have revealed their new video, Tremor, which is taken from the band's forthcoming EP 'Tragic Loss; He Had Such A Lovely House'.

The EP will be released on Monday 6th August as a strictly limited run of 250 x 10" singles (including free digital download). There will also be an equally limited run of CDs, and you can get the tracks via the usual digital outlets - iTunes, 7Digital, Spotify and the rest.

The video is shot and directed by Leeds' Anthologies and can be views below.

Post War Glamour Girls were recently announced as the winners of Futuresound 2012 and will play on the NME / Radio One Stage at both Reading and Leeds Festival sites.
