Leeds Festival line up announced

This article was published in 2013.

BBC Radio One announced the line up for Leeds Festival live on air at 7pm this evening (Monday 11th March).

In a break from the norm, the organisers had already leaked a few bands from the Leeds 2013 line up. Already confirmed were Main Stage headliners Eminem and Biffy Clyro, and NME / Radio One Stage headliners Alt J.

Announced this evening were the third Main Stage headliner Green Day, along with a vast supporting cast including Nine Inch Nails

Leeds Festival takes place at Bramham Park between 23rd-25th August 2013 and Leeds Festival tickets are already on sale.

Prices are £202.50 for a weekend ticket and £90 for a day ticket and all ticket types can be bought via the major online ticket agencies Ticketmaster and See Tickets.
