Audit Control

This is an archive of the band profile for Audit Control.

Huddersfield based 5 piece Audit Control formed in June 2008 after years of acoustic medleys, boozy nights and travelling. Working and rehearsing from of all places a church basement in the moors, the band performed a series of intimate sets at venues across West Yorkshire.

The band hit the studio in late 2008 to record their first demo. With an atmospheric live sound, they mix big soaring guitars and fiendish lyrics with soothing feedback with influences as far ranging as Lou Reed and Joy Division to the Yeah Yeah Yeahs and KOL. The 5 piece have a busy year with gigs across the North England and are set to release their first EP proper titled "To The Universe" November 7th..
