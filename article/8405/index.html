Rob Ashford

This is an archive of the band profile for Rob Ashford.

Rob Ashford is a singer/songwriter from Leeds who plays guitar and keyboards. Rob has played in a number of Leeds bands and is currently looking for a band for live gigs and recordings. All Robs songs have been recorded at his home studio in Leeds and have all been produced by Rob. He is currently working on a new album due to be finished this summer and is hoping to attract the attention of record companies. Rob has allready had excellent feedback from London based record companies including Columbia/Sony and Virgin Records. Rob also writes material for other recording artists and any one interested should send a message to Rob. Rob says "I am currently playing around Leeds at various accoustic and open mic nights. Hope to see you sometime!".
