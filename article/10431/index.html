Pocket Of 3

This is an archive of the band profile for Pocket Of 3.

With catchy melodies and insightful lyrics based on front man Oli Pluck's travels around the world as a renowned flair bartender, coupled with his rhythmical guitar playing; this sets the tone for unusual and interesting blend of musical styles. James Willoughby on drums and Steve Gligorovic on bass provide a tight groovy rhythm section, with the sensual tones of Lucy Scott's saxophone, giving the music a fresh new dynamic.

Taking influences from international bands and artists such as the Dave Matthews Band and the John Butler Trio, Pocket of 3 have set out to fill a void in the UK music scene. Pocket of 3 are tricky to tie down to one genre, yet appeal to a wide range of people.
What is certain however is that they have an exciting and vibrant live show, earning praise from critics and their audiences alike.

Currently in the process of recording their debut album due for release in Autumn 2011; the follow up to last years eponymous self titled EP, the band are gaining a faithful following of all ages and backgrounds.

With a summer of festival appearances ahead, make sure Pocket of 3 are on your list of new bands to catch..
