@follow the Leeds Festival 2009

This article was published in 2009.

The internet is a frenzy of excitement for Leeds Festival this week and many music fans are using the social site Twitter to talk about it. To aggregate the comments, our Leeds Festival mini-site has published a Leeds Festival Twitter page where you can follow all the latest festival tweets.

We're monitoring for anything we can determine to be festival related but use of hashtags #leedsfest or #leedsfestival will ensure we pick them all up.

We'll also display all your festival images if you use TwitPic - so make sure you take photos from the festival site, share them and we'll display them.

The Leeds Festival Twitter page can be found at twitter.leedsmusicfestival.com.
