Arizona Rifles

This is an archive of the band profile for Arizona Rifles.

Steve McNamara - Guitar
Andy Cooper - Bass
Sam Jones - Vocals
Craig Thomspon - Drums

Arizona Rifles are a Leeds 4 piece rock band formed in March 2013. Since formation, they have had three separate songs played by Alan Raw on BBC introducing who he describes "as a great band".

No Title magazine describe the bands sound as being similar to bands such as Foo Fighters and Pearl Jam. They also state 'The band play like a tight compact unit that centres around the husky vocals of lead singer Sam Jones'.

The bands sound is instantly recognisable due to Sam Jones unique voice whom The Guide describe as a "gravelly and whiskey soaked"

A little louder blog describe the band as "..a great raw, gritty garage rock band".
