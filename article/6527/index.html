The Lodger to release third single on 1st May

This article was published in 2006.

The Lodger release their third single "Let Her Go" through Angular Records on 1st May. The single is backed with "We Come From the Same Place" and is available on limited 7" single (1000) only.

The release follows the previous two singles "Many Thanks For Your Honest Opinion" on Dance to the Radio and "Watching" on Double Dragon.

The band play a single launch show on Saturday April 29th at The Packhorse.
