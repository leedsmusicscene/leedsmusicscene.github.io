Kaiser Chiefs and The Cribs to release a limited edition tour single...

This article was published in 2005.

Kaiser Chiefs and The Cribs are to release a limited split 7" single, with each band covering the other's music. The single - available at their live dates between 7th and 15th April - features The Cribs covering "Modern Way" and Kaiser Chiefs covering "Another Number" and is limited to just 1,000 copies.
