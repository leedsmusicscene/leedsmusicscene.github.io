Repeating Mistakes launched for digital download

This article was published in 2013.

'Repeating Mistakes', the new CD from Martin Plock, is now live for download via his Facebook music page, CD Baby and all the usual digital music shops.

A limited edition of CDs are available from Martin at gigs in a shiny digipak format. It features live classics like Red Letter and Kiss & the Bullet.

You can listen for free from his Facebook page now.

'Repeating Mistakes' features contributions from many other local artists such as Hayley Gaftarnick, Adam Richards, Kiernan O'Mallet, Scott Wainwright and Matt Burnside.
