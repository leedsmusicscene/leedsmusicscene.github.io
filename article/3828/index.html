The Glitterati release a new single in May...

This article was published in 2005.

Leeds five-piece The Glitterati release their new single “Heartbreaker” through Atlantic Records on May 23rd. This is the band's second single from their recent self-titled album - the first being Top 40 hit “You Got Nothing On Me”. The album was recorded in LA with producer Mike Clink at the helm.
