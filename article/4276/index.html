Bonsai Kittens to release a new CD titled "The Shortbread Agreement"

This article was published in 2005.

Bonsai Kittens, Leeds' premier (and only!) Nintendo electro j-pop duo, return from a lengthy absence proudly brandishing their brand new 4-track CD, 'The Shortbread Agreement', which will be celebrated via the medium of song and dance on a 7-date mini-tour in October. This will include a Leeds' date at the Packhorse on Tuesday 11th October, with special guests Monster Killed By Laser, Keith Burton And His Beef Curtain, and Unicorn Love.
