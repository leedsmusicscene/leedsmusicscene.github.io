Dinosaur Pile-up album to be released on 4th October

This article was published in 2010.

The debut album from Leeds trio Dinosaur Pile-up will be released on Monday, 4th October.

The album - titled 'Growing Pains' - will be preceded by the single 'Mona Lisa', which is released through 'Friends Versus Records / Big Brain Records' on 27th September 2010.
