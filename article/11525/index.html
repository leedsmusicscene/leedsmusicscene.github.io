Jon Gomm to play a free in-store session at Crash Records

This article was published in 2009.

Following on from the success of the instore gig by The Sunshine Underground in their newly refurbished basement, Crash Records have announced this week that Jon Gomm will be doing a live set this Saturday 5th December at 3pm to promote his brand new album 'Don't Panic'.

The store has a maximum capacity of 50 - so either get down to the shop in good time or email crash [ at ] crashrecords.co.uk to reserve your place.

The album is officially released on 7th December 2009 through the Performing Chimp Records label. However, if you attend the in-store then the CD will be available to purchase for Â£9.99 after the gig.

The official album launch party will be at the Brudenell Social Club on 4th December 2009. Tickets for the launch party are available now from We Got Tickets.

Crash hope to make in-store gigs a regular feature, so look out for more announcements soon.
