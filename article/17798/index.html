Allusondrugs single 'Plasters' is available to stream and watch online now

This article was published in 2013.

Castleford based Allusondrugs have announced their debut single release. Titled 'Plasters', it will be released via Clue Records on August 19th on a very limited edition physical CD.

The video for the single is available to watch below.



You can hear the 2-track single here:



The limited edition physical release will include:

- 100 limited edition CDs with individual sleeve artwork, hand-drawn by the band
- An Allusondrugs badge
- A unique photo taken by a member of the band with a personalized message

You can get 'Plasters' from Clue Record store from August 19th.
