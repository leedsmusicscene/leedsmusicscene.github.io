Marsicans and The Scandal win BD1 LiVE to play at Bingley Music Festival 2012

This article was published in 2012.

In May Bradford Theatres launched their annual search for local music talent to open Bingley Music Live's Main Stage in September.

Over 50 bands entered the competition and the final was held on Friday 20th July. The winners were The Scandal and Marsicans who will be appearing at the festival on Saturday 1st and Sunday 2nd September respectively.

It's been a good few weeks for Marsicans, who were recently confirmed for Leeds Festival 2012 as well. Well done to both bands!
