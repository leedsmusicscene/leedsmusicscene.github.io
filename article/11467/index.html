The Phoenix Fall release their second single 'Tearing Me Apart'

This article was published in 2009.

Leeds four-piece The Phoenix Fall released their second single this week. The single – titled ‘Tearing Me Apart’ – was available from 23rd November via iTunes and Amazon stores.

The single is the band’s second release and their debut single was downloaded over 1,000 times.

The single takes the listener on a journey of longing, desire and passion. And with the help of a 67-piece choir, it will entertain, satisfy and make you want to listen to more.
