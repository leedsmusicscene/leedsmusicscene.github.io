X-Roads

This is an archive of the band profile for X-Roads.

We are a relatively new project playing rock & blues covers from the likes of Dr Feelgood, Delbert McClinton, Georgia Satellites, Fabulous Thunderbirds, Wilko & Daltrey, Bonamassa & Rodgers, Blackberry Smoke, as well as some old standards John started the band because he wanted to build something with a few aims in mind.

Originally a drummer, John wanted to stick to vocals and harmonica.

He wanted to focus on the sort of material that always appealed to him.

He wanted to play for the sheer enjoyment of it all.

He decided to pull together a band of like-minded people who would enjoy the same type of music.

We've all been involved in bands of all descriptions over the years and saw this as an opportunity to just have fun.

John - lead vocals and harmonica
Tony - lead guitar
Derek - rhythm guitar and vocals
Fin - bass
Elle - drums

There are no egos in this band, we play, we enjoy and we stick together..
