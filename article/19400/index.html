Volcanoes release seventh EP "Radiogeist"

This article was published in 2016.

After a three year absence the illusive Yorkshire quartet Volcanoes are poised to release their seventh EP - RADIOGEIST.

Not much needs to be said about this band who have danced on the fringes of success for years. Volcanoes have proved time and time again that they can write witty, thrilling and wonderful music and this EP is no exception.

Despite being 11years old, Volcanoes will prove with this EP that they are more relevant than ever.

Radiogeist comes pre-haunted with five deadly songs:

China Shop
Bowler Hatter
Radiogeist
Noir
Join the Dots

Volcanoes music is hard to find, but there is wonderful music out there if you are willing to dig a little.
