Leeds Music Scene historical archive containing profiles, articles, news, and reviews from 1998-2017.

Leeds Music Scene was the primary music resource for bands and musicians based in Leeds and West Yorkshire. We've restored the following archive of our original site.

Thank you to everyone that contributed to Leeds Music Scene over the years, and to everyone that visited our site to read about bands and musicians from Leeds.
